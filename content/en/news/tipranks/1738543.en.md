The latest update is out from Kafrit ( [(IL:KAFR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:kafr) ).

Kafrit Industries Ltd. has announced a general meeting to be held on March 20, 2025, to discuss several key agenda items including the appointment of a new director, Daniel Ben-Shushan, effective April 5, 2025, and a management agreement with a controlling shareholder. The meeting will also cover the approval of a new compensation policy for directors, the engagement of external auditors, and transactions with interested parties. Notably, the agenda includes the approval of a merger with a foreign corporation, pending regulatory and shareholder approvals. These decisions could significantly impact Kafrit’s governance structure and strategic direction.
