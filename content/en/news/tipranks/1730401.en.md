These updates, which include figures and JORC Code table revisions, enhance the company’s exploration data, potentially strengthening its position in the mineral exploration industry.

**More about Kingsrose Mining Ltd**

Kingsrose Mining Limited is a sustainability-conscious and technically proficient mineral exploration company listed on the ASX. The company focuses on acquiring and exploring critical mineral deposits and has projects in Finland and Norway. Kingsrose was also selected for the BHP Xplor exploration accelerator program.
