China Telecom ( [(HK:0728)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:0728) ) just unveiled an announcement.

China Telecom Corporation Limited announced the resignation of Mr. Liang Baojun from his positions as Executive Director, President, and Chief Operating Officer due to a change in work arrangement, effective February 10, 2025. Mr. Liang was instrumental in implementing the company’s Cloudification and Digital Transformation strategy, contributing to high-quality development, and his departure is acknowledged with gratitude by the Board.

**More about China Telecom**

China Telecom Corporation Limited is a major telecommunications company in China, focusing on offering fixed-line and mobile telecommunication services, internet access, and digital solutions. The company plays a significant role in China’s telecommunications industry, emphasizing cloudification and digital transformation.
