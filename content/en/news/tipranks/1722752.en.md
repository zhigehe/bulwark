An update from Australian Critical Minerals Ltd. ( [(AU:ACM)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:acm) ) is now available.

Australian Critical Minerals Limited has reported significant progress in their Shaw and Cooletha Projects in the Pilbara region, revealing high-grade iron results and substantial channel iron deposits. The Shaw Project identified high-quality direct shipping ore with low impurities, while the Cooletha Project shows promising prospects for high-grade iron ore development, supported by new expertise from their recently appointed Technical Advisor. These developments are expected to enhance the company’s market positioning and create substantial value for shareholders.

**More about Australian Critical Minerals Ltd.**

Australian Critical Minerals Limited is a company operating within the critical minerals industry, focusing primarily on iron ore exploration and development. The company is engaged in projects located in the Pilbara region of Western Australia, specifically targeting high-grade iron and channel iron deposits.
