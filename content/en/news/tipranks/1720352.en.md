Grupo Aeroportuario Del Centro ( [(OMAB)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/omab) ) just unveiled an announcement.

In February 2025, OMA reported a 9.9% increase in passenger traffic for January 2025 compared to the previous year. This growth included a 7.3% rise in domestic and a significant 24% surge in international traffic, highlighting a robust start to the year for the company. The announcement underscores OMA’s strong operational performance, potentially enhancing its positioning within the sector and indicating positive implications for stakeholders.

**More about Grupo Aeroportuario Del Centro**

Grupo Aeroportuario del Centro Norte, S.A.B. de C.V., known as OMA, operates 13 international airports across nine states in central and northern Mexico. It serves major areas such as Monterrey and tourist destinations like Acapulco. OMA also manages hotels in key airports and is listed on both the Mexican Stock Exchange and the NASDAQ Global Select Market.
