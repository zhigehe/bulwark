**More about Daqo New Energy**

Daqo New Energy Corp. is a leading manufacturer of high-purity polysilicon for the global solar PV industry. Founded in 2007, the company supplies high-purity polysilicon to manufacturers of photovoltaic products, which are further processed into solar power solutions. Daqo New Energy boasts a polysilicon nameplate capacity of 305,000 metric tons and is recognized as one of the world’s lowest cost producers of high-purity polysilicon.
