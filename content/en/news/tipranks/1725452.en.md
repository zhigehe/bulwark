Cannabotech has announced that Shai David Zohar has become an interested party in the company due to an increase in securities held as part of a creditors’ arrangement. As a result, Zohar now holds 162,056 ordinary shares, representing a 10.57% stake in the company. This development is significant for Cannabotech, a company listed on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange, as it may impact its shareholder dynamics and influence future corporate governance decisions.
