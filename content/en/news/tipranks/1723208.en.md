This change in ownership may influence the company’s governance and strategic decisions, as voting power is a significant aspect of shareholder influence.

**More about Brooks Macdonald**

Brooks Macdonald Group PLC is a UK-based company operating in the financial sector, providing wealth management services. They focus on managing investment portfolios for individuals, charities, and trusts, aiming to deliver strong financial performance and client satisfaction.
