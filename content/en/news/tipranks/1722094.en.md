MA Financial Group Limited ( [(AU:MAF)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:maf) ) has provided an update.

MA Financial Group Limited announced the early closure of its Initial Public Offering for the MA Credit Income Trust after raising an oversubscribed $330 million, which was upscaled by 10% from its original target. Scheduled to list on the ASX on March 5, 2025, the fund offers investors access to MA Financial’s private credit strategies, including a diversified portfolio of 165 private credit investments exceeding $3.7 billion. This development not only highlights strong investor demand but also reinforces MA Financial’s robust market positioning in private credit strategies, potentially enhancing its reputation and influence in the alternative asset management industry.

**More about MA Financial Group Limited**

MA Financial Group is a global alternative asset manager that specializes in private credit, real estate, and hospitality sectors. The company provides lending solutions to property, corporate, and specialty finance sectors, alongside corporate advisory services. MA Financial manages investments worth A$9.9 billion and is responsible for A$128 billion in managed loans, with a significant presence across Australia, China, Hong Kong, New Zealand, Singapore, and the United States.
