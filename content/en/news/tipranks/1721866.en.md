BAIT VE GAG REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT LTD ( [(IL:BVGG)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:bvgg) ) has provided an update.

Bait Ve Gag Real Estate Development Ltd. has announced a special general meeting scheduled for February 19, 2025, to discuss the appointment of an external director and other matters. The proposed resolution includes appointing Mr. Yokir Drikort as an external director with compensation set at 25% of the regular fee, subject to the approval of the general meeting. This move aligns with the guidelines of the Israel Securities Authority and has been approved by the company’s Board of Directors.
