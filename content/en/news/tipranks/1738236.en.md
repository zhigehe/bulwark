Eneco Energy Limited has been granted an extension to meet the financial exit criteria after being placed on the SGX-ST’s financial watch-list. The company is collaborating with Union Steel Holdings to expand its core logistics business and explore new market opportunities, following significant share purchases by Union Steel.

**More about Eneco Energy**

Eneco Energy Limited, incorporated in Singapore, operates in the logistics industry with a focus on enhancing its services to maintain high organic volumes and sustainability.
