The latest update is out from Wynnstay ( [(GB:WYN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:wyn) ).

Wynnstay Group reported a challenging financial year ending October 2024, with revenue dropping to £613.1m from £735.9m due to commodity deflation. Despite adverse weather and falling commodity prices affecting operations, the company maintained a strong balance sheet with a net cash position of £32.8m. The launch of ‘Project Genesis’ aims to transform the company’s operations for better margins and future growth, with initial benefits expected in FY25.

**More about Wynnstay**

Wynnstay Group PLC is an agricultural supplies and services group that operates primarily in the agricultural industry. It focuses on providing a range of products and services including feed, grain, fertiliser, and seed to the farming sector.
