The latest update is out from Warpaint London ( [(GB:W7L)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:w7l) ).

Warpaint London PLC announced the successful application for admission of 103,422 new ordinary shares to the London Stock Exchange’s AIM following its acquisition of Brand Architekts Group plc. This move will increase Warpaint’s total issued capital to 80,787,321 ordinary shares with voting rights, enhancing its market presence and providing shareholders a new basis for tracking their ownership interests.

**More about Warpaint London**

Warpaint London PLC is a cosmetics company that sells branded products under names like W7 and Technic. W7 is distributed in the UK to major retailers and internationally to local distributors and retail chains, while Technic focuses on the gifting market in the UK and continental Europe, targeting high street retailers and supermarkets. The company also offers other brands such as Man’stuff, Body Collection, and Chit Chat, each appealing to different demographics.
