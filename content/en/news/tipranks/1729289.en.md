SATS ASA has announced the implementation of a Share Investment Program aimed at aligning the interests of its employees and board members with those of its shareholders. The program will offer up to 2,500,000 shares at a discounted price, subject to a three-year lock-up period, reflecting the company’s commitment to long-term stakeholder engagement and share-based remuneration policies.
