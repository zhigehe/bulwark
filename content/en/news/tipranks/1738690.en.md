XPS Pensions Group Plc has announced a change in the voting rights held by BlackRock, Inc., which has acquired or disposed of both voting rights and financial instruments. As of February 12, 2025, BlackRock’s total voting rights in XPS Pensions Group have increased to 10%, reflecting a significant rise from its previous position. This development may impact XPS Pensions Group’s operational decisions and stakeholder engagement, as BlackRock is a major global investment management corporation.

**More about XPS Pensions Group Plc**

XPS Pensions Group Plc is a UK-based company operating in the pensions industry, providing pensions consulting, pensions administration, and investment consulting services. The company focuses on delivering comprehensive pension solutions to organizations across the UK.
