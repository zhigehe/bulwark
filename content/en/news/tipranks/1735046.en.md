First Andes Silver Ltd., a company in the mining industry, has appointed Ian Stalker as Executive Chairman and James Hutton as an advisor. Stalker brings over 50 years of experience in resource development, having raised substantial capital and directed significant mining projects across various minerals. Hutton adds extensive capital markets experience to the team. The company also announced a private placement financing aimed at raising $1,000,000, which may involve paying fees to registered finders.
