Verbrec Ltd ( [(AU:VBC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:vbc) ) has issued an announcement.

Verbrec Limited has announced the release of its half-year FY2025 results scheduled for 27 February 2025. An investor webinar will follow on 6 March 2025 to present these results and provide a business update, hosted by CEO Mark Read. This announcement demonstrates Verbrec’s commitment to transparency and engagement with stakeholders, potentially impacting its market perception and investor relations.

**More about Verbrec Ltd**

Verbrec Limited is a leading provider of engineering, asset management, project delivery, operations & maintenance, training, and mining technology services, supporting customers in the energy, infrastructure, and mining industries across Australia, New Zealand, Papua New Guinea, and beyond. The company is listed on the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX: VBC) and offers comprehensive services throughout the asset lifecycle, including specialties in asset management, automation and control, pipelines, power, process plant, and training.
