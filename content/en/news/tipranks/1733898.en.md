Clal Insurance has announced the submission of an agreed request for withdrawal in a [class action](https://www.tipranks.com/class-action-lawsuits) lawsuit filed against it. The lawsuit concerned the alleged improper cancellation of policies, and Clal Insurance has agreed to provide additional disclosure and pay nominal fees and compensation. This request is pending court approval, which remains uncertain.
