Skin Elements Limited ( [(AU:SKN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:skn) ) has issued an announcement.

Skin Elements Limited announced an extension of its non-renounceable entitlement issue closing date to 28 February 2025, allowing shareholders more time to consider participation. This extension reflects the company’s focus on shareholder engagement and could potentially impact its market operations and shareholder value positively.

**More about Skin Elements Limited**

Skin Elements Limited is an Australian natural biotechnology company that focuses on developing its proprietary SE Formula Biotechnology. The company specializes in creating plant-based and organic sourced ingredients, and has developed a portfolio of natural formulations with global applications. Their products include SuprCuvr, a TGA-registered hospital-grade disinfectant, ECO-Nurture plant bio-stimulant, Soléo Organics natural sunscreen, PapayaActivs skincare products, and Elizabeth Jane Natural Cosmetics.
