illimity Bank S.p.A. reported strong growth in its 2024 financial results, with a 21% year-over-year increase in operating results and a solid capital position. The bank has undergone strategic repositioning to enhance its focus on credit services for SMEs, while also investing in new initiatives with future growth potential. The recent takeover offer by Banca Ifis has impacted some of illimity’s operations, delaying certain projects and necessitating a thorough evaluation of strategic options with shareholder interests in mind.

**More about illimity Bank SpA**

illimity Bank S.p.A. is a financial institution focusing on credit and investment banking services for Italian small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). It operates in sectors such as performing, restructuring, and relaunching, providing a strong growth and development platform.
