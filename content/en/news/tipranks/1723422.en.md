Tristate Holdings Limited ( [(HK:0458)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:0458) ) just unveiled an announcement.

Tristate Holdings Limited announced a strategic review of its licensed brands portfolio, particularly regarding its Reebok business strategy in its operating regions. The review aims to optimize resource allocation for long-term growth, although no changes to the Reebok license agreement have been finalized yet. The announcement underscores the company’s commitment to adapting to market dynamics and ensuring sustainable brand performance.

**More about Tristate Holdings Limited**

Tristate Holdings Limited is involved in garment manufacturing and brand business. It has a licensing agreement with Authentic Brands Group for distributing Reebok branded products in Mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.
