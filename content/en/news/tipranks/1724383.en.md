Fevertree Drinks ( [(GB:FEVR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:fevr) ) has shared an announcement.

Fevertree Drinks PLC has announced a change in the voting rights held by Lindsell Train, a significant shareholder in the company. The notification indicates that Lindsell Train’s voting rights have decreased from 11.99% to 10.66%. This shift in voting rights might impact the company’s shareholder dynamics and decision-making processes, potentially affecting its strategic direction and governance structure.

**More about Fevertree Drinks**

Fevertree Drinks PLC is a UK-based company operating in the beverage industry, primarily known for its premium mixers. The company focuses on the production and distribution of high-quality tonic waters, ginger ales, and other mixer products, catering to a market that values quality and sophistication in drink mixers.
