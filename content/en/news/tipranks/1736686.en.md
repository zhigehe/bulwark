Shoval Engineering and Construction Ltd. announced the issuance of a new series of bonds (Series B) to the public, based on its shelf prospectus. This issuance, executed via the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange, marks a change in the company’s securities register, increasing the total quantity of the bonds to 131,655,000. The move is expected to enhance the company’s financial position and provide additional capital for its operations.

**More about Shoval**

Shoval Engineering and Construction Ltd. operates in the engineering and construction industry, focusing on providing comprehensive construction solutions. It is involved in the issuance and management of securities, including bonds and options, as part of its financial operations.
