This initiative aims to strengthen employee involvement and ownership, potentially impacting the company’s internal stakeholder engagement positively.

**More about XP Factory PLC**

XP Factory PLC operates in the financial services industry, offering a Share Incentive Plan for its employees, which allows them to purchase company shares through salary deductions. The company focuses on engaging and rewarding its employees by providing them with opportunities to invest in the company.
