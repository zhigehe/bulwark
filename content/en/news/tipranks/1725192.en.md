Vanadiumcorp Resource ( [(TSE:VRB)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:vrb) ) just unveiled an announcement.

VanadiumCorp Resource Inc. announced the appointment of Mr. Brian Gusko as an Independent Director, replacing Mr. Christian Paradis. Mr. Gusko brings over 15 years of capital markets experience and a strong background in mining and green energy, which is expected to bolster the company’s capabilities as it aims to lead in vanadium electrolyte production in Canada.

**More about Vanadiumcorp Resource**

VanadiumCorp Resource Inc. operates in the mining and green energy sectors, focusing on the production of high-quality vanadium electrolyte. The company’s primary market is the international sector for long-duration Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries (VRFB), with its initial manufacturing facility located in Val-des-Sources, Québec.
