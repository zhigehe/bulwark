On February 12, 2025, Beeline Loans, Inc., a subsidiary of Beeline Holdings, announced a partnership with RedAwning to enhance the real estate investment process for buyers. By integrating Beeline’s mortgage application into RedAwning’s platform, the collaboration aims to offer a faster and more intuitive experience for investors, particularly targeting Millennials who are increasingly driving demand in real estate. This strategic move positions both companies to capitalize on and streamline the investment property market.

**More about Eastside Distilling**

Beeline Holdings is a technology-driven mortgage lender and title provider that operates a fully digital, AI-powered platform, aimed at simplifying and accelerating the home financing process. Headquartered in Providence, RI, the company is committed to transforming the mortgage industry through innovation and customer-focused solutions.
