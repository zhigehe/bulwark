ECARX Holdings ( [(ECX)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/ecx) ) has issued an update.

On February 11, 2025, ECARX Holdings Inc. announced the launch of the first intelligent cockpit developed under its strategic partnership with FAW Group in the Hongqi Tiangong 05 all-electric sedan, marking a significant milestone in their collaboration initiated in April 2024. This development, featuring ECARX’s Antora 1000 Pro computing platform and AutoGPT integration, sets a new industry benchmark and will be scaled across future Hongqi models, enhancing ECARX’s industry positioning and showcasing their technological strengths in AI-driven solutions for next-generation mobility.

**More about ECARX Holdings**

ECARX Holdings Inc. is a global automotive technology provider specializing in delivering turnkey solutions for next-generation smart vehicles. The company offers products ranging from system on a chip (SoC) to central computing platforms and software, aiming to enhance user experiences while reducing complexity and cost. Founded in 2017 and listed on Nasdaq in 2022, ECARX operates in major locations worldwide, including China, UK, USA, Sweden, Germany, and Malaysia. The company collaborates with notable automakers, such as FAW and Dongfeng Peugeot-Citroën, and has products in over 7.3 million vehicles globally.
