The latest update is out from SPC Nickel ( [(TSE:SPC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:spc) ).

SPC Nickel Corp. held its Annual and Special General Meeting on February 11, 2025, where shareholders re-elected several directors and approved the company’s Omnibus Equity Incentive Compensation Plan. McGovern Hurley LLP was re-appointed as the auditor for the upcoming fiscal year. The meeting’s outcomes reinforce SPC Nickel’s governance and strategic planning, potentially impacting its operational focus and financial oversight in the mining sector.

**More about SPC Nickel**

SPC Nickel Corp. is a Canadian public corporation focused on exploring for nickel, copper, and platinum group metals (Ni-Cu-PGMs) within the Sudbury Mining Camp and Nunavut. The company is actively developing its 100% owned Lockerby East project in Sudbury and holds additional projects across Canada, including the Muskox Project in Nunavut, the Aer-Kidd Project, and the Janes Project near Sudbury.
