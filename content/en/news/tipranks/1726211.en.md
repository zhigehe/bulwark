**More about Xanadu Mines Ltd**

Xanadu Mines Ltd is an exploration company listed on the ASX and TSX, operating primarily in Mongolia. They focus on large-scale copper-gold discoveries and low-cost inventory growth, with a notable project being their jointly controlled Kharmagtai copper-gold deposit alongside Zijin Mining Group.
