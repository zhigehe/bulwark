An announcement from TVI Pacific ( [(TSE:TVI)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:tvi) ) is now available.

TVI Pacific Inc. has appointed Davidson & Company LLC as its new auditor following the resignation of its former auditor, PricewaterhouseCooper LLC, due to unresolved issues related to whistleblower allegations and a tailings incident at the Siana gold mine. The board and audit committee have approved the new appointment, which will be ratified at the next shareholder meeting, and TVI has ensured a smooth transition between the auditors by authorizing full cooperation between them.

**More about TVI Pacific**

TVI Pacific Inc. is a Canadian resource company focused on mining projects in the Philippines, known for its rich mineral deposits. TVI holds a significant stake in TVIRD, operating the Balabag gold/silver mine and the Siana gold mine, and has interests in other projects like the Mapawa and Mabilo projects, as well as Agata Mining Ventures Inc.
