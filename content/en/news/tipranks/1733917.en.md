Zacatecas Silver ( [(TSE:ZAC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:zac) ) has provided an announcement.

Zacatecas Silver Corp. has announced a new drill program to expand its current resource base at its silver projects in Zacatecas, Mexico. The program targets high-grade silver intercepts identified at the Panuco Deposit and El Cristo Vein System, with additional high-priority exploration targets identified at San Gill Breccia Zone and Muleros Zone. This initiative aims to enhance the company’s exploration footprint and industry positioning by unlocking further mineral potential at its key sites.

**More about Zacatecas Silver**

Zacatecas Silver Corp. is a company in the mining industry, primarily focused on silver exploration and production. It operates in the Zacatecas region of Mexico, targeting significant silver and base metal deposits through advanced exploration techniques.
