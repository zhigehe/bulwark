An update from South32 Ltd. ( [(AU:S32)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:s32) ) is now available.

South32 Limited has received Federal environmental approval for the Worsley Mine Development Project, allowing it to access bauxite resources to sustain production at Worsley Alumina. This project, commencing mining in Q4 FY25, is expected to support production until at least FY36, benefiting the local economy and workforce.

**More about South32 Ltd.**

South32 Limited is a company focused on developing natural resources with operations across the Americas, Australia, and Southern Africa. It produces minerals and metals critical to the global energy transition and aims to leave a positive legacy through meaningful relationships with partners and communities.
