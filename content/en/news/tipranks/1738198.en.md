Vext Science ( [(TSE:VEXT)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:vext) ) has shared an announcement.

Vext Science has announced the relocation and expansion of its Herbal Wellness Center dispensary in Jackson, Ohio, to a more central location, doubling its retail space and enhancing customer convenience with a drive-thru window. This strategic move aligns with Vext’s plan to capture more market share in Ohio by maximizing its state dispensary cap of eight licenses, thus reinforcing its market position and optimizing its footprint for sustainable growth and shareholder value.

**More about Vext Science**

Vext Science, Inc. is a U.S.-based cannabis operator with vertical operations in Arizona and Ohio. The company operates in the cannabis industry and offers a wide range of products through its retail dispensaries, cultivation, and manufacturing facilities. Vext focuses on non-urban locations with limited competition and has a strong presence in Ohio, which is a key area for its growth strategy.
