Xin Yuan Enterprises Group Ltd. has announced the closure of its register of members in preparation for an extraordinary general meeting (EGM) scheduled for March 1, 2025. This meeting will address purported requisitions, and shareholders must register their share transfers by February 24, 2025, to participate. The company will send relevant documents to shareholders soon.
