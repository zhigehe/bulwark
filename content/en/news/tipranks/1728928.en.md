Carasso Motors Ltd has announced a special general meeting for shareholders on March 19, 2025, to discuss and approve key financial and governance proposals. The agenda includes the approval of the annual financial statements for 2024, reappointment of the auditor, remuneration policy for directors and officers, a special bonus for the CEO, and changes to the company’s articles of association. Shareholders can participate in person or by proxy, with the necessary arrangements in place for access to proposed resolutions.
