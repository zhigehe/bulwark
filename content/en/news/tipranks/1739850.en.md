[Cryptocurrency](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency) exchange Coinbase Global [(COIN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/coin) has reported fourth-quarter 2024 financial results that [crushed Wall Street’s targets](https://www.tipranks.com/news/the-fly/coinbase-reports-q4-adjusted-ebitda-1-29b-vs-324m-last-year) as trading in [Bitcoin](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency/btc-usd) [(BTC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/btc) and other digital assets hits new heights.

The company posted earnings per share of $4.68, which was more than double the $2.11 [consensus expectation](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/coin/earnings) of analysts who track the company’s progress. Revenue in the final quarter of last year totaled $2.27 billion, which was far ahead of the $1.88 billion estimate on Wall Street. Sales were up 130% from a year earlier and it was the company’s biggest quarterly revenue in three years.

[Transaction revenue](https://www.tipranks.com/news/ratings/positive-outlook-for-coinbase-global-buy-rating-backed-by-strong-q4-expectations-and-crypto-trends) at Coinbase more than doubled from last year to $1.56 billion, and topped estimates that called for $1.29 billion. Total trading volume on the company’s platform during Q4 2024 was $439 billion, up 185% year-over-year. Consumer (retail investor) trading volume rose 224% from the same period a year ago, while trading volume among institutional investors increased 176%.

**Crypto Rally**
----------------

The management team at Coinbase said the [exceptionally strong quarter](https://www.tipranks.com/news/analysts-raise-price-targets-on-coinbase-global-coin-stock-ahead-of-earnings) was due to a raging postelection rally that pushed cryptocurrency prices to record levels at the end of last year. Bitcoin and other [crypto](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency) have surged since U.S. President [Donald Trump](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/djt) won re-election last November, promising to usher in a new era for digital coins and tokens.

Coinbase, which runs the largest U.S. marketplace for buying and selling crypto, also credited the continued popularity of spot BTC exchange-traded funds (ETFs), which first launched nearly a year ago, for its stellar results. Despite the exceptional print, COIN stock was trading flat in after hours trading. Over the last 12 months, the company’s [share price has risen 86%.](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/coin)

**Is COIN Stock a Buy?**
------------------------

The stock of Coinbase Global has a consensus Moderate Buy rating among 16 Wall Street analysts. That rating is based on six Buy and 10 Hold recommendations issued in the last three months. The [average COIN price target](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/coin/forecast) of $317.64 implies 6.55% upside from current levels. These ratings are likely to change after today’s financial results.
