Brainbees Solutions Limited ( [(IN:FIRSTCRY)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/in:firstcry) ) has shared an announcement.

Brainbees Solutions Limited announced the resubmission of its unaudited financial results for the quarter and nine months ended December 31, 2024, due to an arithmetical error in the computation of ‘Other Comprehensive Income’ and ‘Total Other Comprehensive Loss.’ The corrected figures did not affect the overall financial results for the specified period. This move highlights the company’s commitment to transparency and accuracy in its financial reporting, ensuring stakeholders are well informed about its financial standing.

**More about Brainbees Solutions Limited**

Brainbees Solutions Limited operates in the retail industry, primarily focusing on children and baby products. The company is known for its e-commerce platform, FirstCry, which caters to the needs of parents and offers a wide range of products for infants and young children.
