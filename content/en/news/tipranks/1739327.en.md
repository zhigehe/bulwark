The latest announcement is out from Sikri Holding AS ( [(DE:57U0)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/de:57u0) ).

Spir Group ASA has announced the implementation of a new 3-year share option program for its management and key employees, mirroring the terms of the previous year’s program. The 2024 Option Program grants a total of 1,146,987 options, with no performance conditions, and options vesting over three years. The announcement reflects the company’s commitment to incentivizing its leadership and aligns with its strategic goals, potentially impacting stakeholder interests through enhanced employee retention and motivation.

**More about Sikri Holding AS**

Spir Group is a Nordic software provider specializing in mission-critical software and data solutions for the real estate and public administration sectors. The company aids in streamlining complex real estate processes and offers software solutions for case management, archiving, and quality management to the public sector in Norway. Its diverse clientele includes municipalities, government agencies, real estate professionals, and other industry stakeholders.
