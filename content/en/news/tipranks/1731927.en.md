The latest announcement is out from Flutter Entertainment PLC ( [(FLUT)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/flut) ).

Flutter Entertainment PLC has executed a repurchase of its ordinary shares as part of a previously announced buyback program. This move, facilitated by Goldman Sachs & Co. LLC, aims to repurchase up to $350 million worth of shares by March 31, 2025, and is part of a larger $5 billion buyback strategy announced in September 2024. The repurchase reflects Flutter’s strategy to optimize its capital structure and return value to shareholders, potentially impacting its market position and investor relations.

**More about Flutter Entertainment PLC**

Flutter Entertainment PLC operates within the gambling industry, offering a variety of betting and gaming products. The company focuses on expanding its reach in the global betting market, leveraging technology to enhance its offerings and customer experience.
