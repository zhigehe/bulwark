Gulf Energy Development ( [(SG:TGED)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/sg:tged) ) has issued an announcement.

Gulf Energy Development has announced the termination of its SDR program in light of its upcoming amalgamation with Intouch Holdings, creating a new public limited company. This strategic move will result in the delisting of both companies from the Stock Exchange of Thailand, impacting shareholders and requiring conversions of SDRs to NVDRs by March 2025.

**More about Gulf Energy Development**

Gulf Energy Development Public Company Limited is a major player in the energy sector based in Thailand. It focuses on power generation, and its operations are pivotal in the energy market, engaging in significant corporate activities to maintain its competitive positioning.
