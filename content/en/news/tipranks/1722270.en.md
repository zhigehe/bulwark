An update from Pilot Energy Limited ( [(AU:PGY)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:pgy) ) is now available.

Pilot Energy Limited is set to conduct its Annual General Meeting on February 12, 2025, at 11 am (AWST), offering a livestream option for shareholders unable to attend in person. However, the virtual broadcast is non-interactive, meaning shareholders cannot ask questions or vote online, as proxy voting has already closed.

**More about Pilot Energy Limited**

Pilot Energy Limited operates in the energy sector, focusing on providing sustainable energy solutions with a commitment to innovative practices. The company is involved in various energy projects, aiming to contribute to cleaner and more efficient energy supply.
