Idi Insur ( [(IL:IDIN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:idin) ) has provided an announcement.

Idi Insur is facing a [class action](https://www.tipranks.com/class-action-lawsuits) lawsuit filed in the Be’er Sheva District Court over allegations of discriminatory practices based on geographical location. The lawsuit accuses the company of imposing additional security requirements and higher premiums on insurance candidates from certain areas without statistical justification. The claimant seeks financial compensation, an injunction against the alleged practices, and a declaration that such discrimination is invalid.

**More about Idi Insur**

Idi Insur operates in the insurance industry, focusing primarily on providing car insurance policies to individuals. The company is known for its comprehensive insurance offerings tailored to meet the specific needs of its clientele.
