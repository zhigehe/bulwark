Shanaya Limited ( [(SG:SES)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/sg:ses) ) has provided an announcement.

Shanaya Limited announced an extension of the long stop date for the disposal of its Kian Teck facility to Arina International Holding Pte. Ltd. due to pending approval from JTC. The new deadline for the approval has been extended by three months to 14 May 2025, reflecting the company’s ongoing efforts to finalize the transaction and its commitment to stakeholders involved in the sale.

**More about Shanaya Limited**

Shanaya Limited is a company incorporated in Singapore, primarily engaged in environmental services through its wholly owned subsidiary, Shanaya Environmental Services Pte. Ltd. The company operates within the environmental services industry, focusing on providing sustainable solutions and services in Singapore.
