TasFoods Limited has announced a biosecurity update following the detection of the H7N8 avian influenza strain at a layer egg farm in Victoria. While this outbreak currently does not affect TasFoods’ operations in Tasmania, the company has increased biosecurity measures across all its poultry sites. The company reassures stakeholders that it continues its supply activities without disruption, under the guidance of enhanced biosecurity policies and monitoring by the Tasmanian Government.

**More about TasFoods Limited**

TasFoods Limited is a company listed on the ASX under the ticker TFL. It operates in the food industry, focusing on poultry production with operations including broiler growing sites, processing plants, and hatcheries in Tasmania. The company supplies both local and mainland markets.
