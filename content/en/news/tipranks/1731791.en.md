Spackman Entertainment Group Ltd ( [(SG:40E)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/sg:40e) ) has provided an update.

Spackman Entertainment Group Limited has announced significant changes to its Board of Directors and Board Committees, effective from February 12, 2025. Mr. Na Kyoungwon has resigned as CEO and been re-designated as a Non-Executive Non-Independent Director, while Mr. Anthony Wei Kit Wong has been appointed as the Interim CEO. Additionally, Mr. Lau Rui Sheng, Ian has been appointed as a Non-Executive Independent Director. These changes are expected to influence the company’s strategic direction and governance, potentially impacting its operations and stakeholder relationships.

**More about Spackman Entertainment Group Ltd**

Spackman Entertainment Group Limited is a company incorporated in Singapore, primarily engaged in the entertainment industry. It focuses on the production of films, television shows, and other forms of media content, aiming to capture a significant market share within the entertainment sector.
