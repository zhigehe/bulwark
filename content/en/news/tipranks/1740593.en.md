Empress Royalty ( [(TSE:EMPR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:empr) ) has shared an update.

Empress Royalty Corp. announced it is in advanced discussions to acquire a new gold and silver stream from an operating mine in the United States. This strategic move aims to enhance Empress’s portfolio and strengthen its market position, with the transaction expected to close in the second quarter of 2025, pending customary conditions and regulatory approvals.

**More about Empress Royalty**

Empress Royalty Corp. is a global royalty and streaming creation company that offers investors a diversified portfolio of gold and silver investments. The company focuses on partnering with mining companies at the development and production stages to provide non-dilutive capital. With strategic partnerships with Endeavour Financial and Terra Capital, Empress leverages global investment opportunities and mining finance expertise to enhance value for shareholders.
