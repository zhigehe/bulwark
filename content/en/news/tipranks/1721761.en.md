Rotem Energy Mineral (REM), a company active in the energy sector, announced changes to its capital status and securities registry involving the exercise and expiration of Series 7 warrants. This adjustment, executed through the Stock Exchange Clearing House, resulted in an increase in the number of Rotem Energy Yesh securities and a decrease of Rotem Energy Opt 7 securities. The changes reflect ongoing adjustments in the company’s securities portfolio and have implications for their capital structure and market positioning.
