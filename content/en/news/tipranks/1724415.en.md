Convergenze SpA Societa Benefit ( [(IT:CVG)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/it:cvg) ) has issued an update.

Convergenze S.p.A. has repurchased 500 shares as part of a share repurchase and disposal program, bringing its total ownership to 133,500 shares, or 1.781% of the share capital. This move reflects the company’s strategic financial management and commitment to enhancing shareholder value while maintaining compliance with relevant legal and regulatory standards.

**More about Convergenze SpA Societa Benefit**

Convergenze S.p.A. Società Benefit is an integrated technology operator in the Telecommunications and 100% green Energy sectors, offering services through a patented EVO network and a proprietary fiber optic network using XGS-PON technology. Founded in 2005, the company has evolved from providing internet and fixed-line services to becoming a national energy trader, focusing on renewable electricity and natural gas. As a Società Benefit and B-Corp, Convergenze emphasizes sustainability, innovation, and social responsibility.
