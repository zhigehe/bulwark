The latest announcement is out from Delta Brands ( [(IL:DLTI)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:dlti) ).

Delta Brands has announced a dividend distribution totaling 30,291,637 NIS to be paid on March 9, 2025, for shareholders on record as of February 18, 2025. The company’s board of directors approved the dividend after confirming its compliance with profit and solvency tests as per the Companies Law, 1999. The distribution is not expected to adversely affect the company’s financial condition or operations, and no court approval is required for this payout.
