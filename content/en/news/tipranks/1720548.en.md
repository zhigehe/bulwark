Arctic Star Exploration ( [(TSE:ADD)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:add) ) has provided an announcement.

Arctic Star Exploration Corp. announced a change of auditor from A Chan & Company LLP to SHIM & Associates LLP, effective February 6, 2025. The transition was seamless with no reservations or reportable events noted in prior audits, suggesting stability in the company’s financial reporting practices. This change is procedural and aligns with regulatory compliance standards, with all necessary documentation filed according to National Instrument 51-102 requirements.

**More about Arctic Star Exploration**

Arctic Star Exploration Corp. is primarily engaged in diamond exploration and has recently made significant discoveries of five new kimberlites in the Lac De Gras kimberlite field, an area known for supporting large-scale kimberlite mining complexes. The company holds an exploration permit for diamond-bearing kimberlites in its Timantti project in Kuusamo, Finland, and continues to seek other diamond opportunities.
