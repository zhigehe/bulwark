The latest announcement is out from Windward Ltd. ( [(GB:WNWD)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:wnwd) ).

Windward Ltd. has announced that its shareholders have approved a cash acquisition by Octopus UK Bidco Limited, a subsidiary of FTV VIII, with 96.96% of votes in favor during an Extraordinary General Meeting. This acquisition is expected to complete in March 2025, pending the satisfaction of certain conditions. Additionally, Windward has applied for a tax ruling with the Israel Tax Authority to allow non-Israeli shareholders to avoid withholding tax on the merger consideration, with further announcements to follow.
