Ichiyoshi Securities Co. announced the completion of its treasury share acquisition through the Tokyo Stock Exchange Trading Network System for pre-market trading, acquiring 245,000 shares at a total cost of 201,880,000 yen. This move is intended to enhance the company’s capital management flexibility in response to changing business environments, reflecting a strategic decision by the Board of Directors to optimize its financial operations.

**More about Ichiyoshi Securities Co**

Ichiyoshi Securities Co., Ltd. is a company listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange, operating primarily in the securities industry. It focuses on providing financial services, including brokerage and trading, investment banking, and asset management.
