Tokai Carbon Co. has launched its ‘Vision 2030’ initiative, aiming to reshape its strategic direction by 2030 through structural reforms, expansion in growth markets, and sustainable value creation. The company plans significant restructuring, including its graphite electrode and smelting businesses, while investing in new facilities globally. By focusing on enhancing shareholder value and improving communication with stakeholders, Tokai Carbon seeks to achieve a Price Book value Ratio of over 1.0x, positioning itself for sustained growth and market competitiveness.

**More about Tokai Carbon Co**

Tokai Carbon Co., Ltd. operates in the materials industry, specializing in the production of carbon-based products such as graphite electrodes, carbon black, and fine carbon. The company is focused on expanding its market presence in growing sectors and is committed to sustainable value creation.
