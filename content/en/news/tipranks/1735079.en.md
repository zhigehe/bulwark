An update from Metaplanet KK ( [(JP:3350)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/jp:3350) ) is now available.

Metaplanet Inc. announced a change in the use of funds from the issuance of its 6th Series Ordinary Bonds. Instead of allocating funds entirely for [Bitcoin](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency/btc-usd) purchases, JPY 4 billion will now be designated for bond redemption, with the remaining funds continuing to support their strategic Bitcoin acquisition plan. This adjustment reflects the company’s financial strategy to manage currency risks and enhance corporate value in Japan’s challenging economic environment.

**More about Metaplanet KK**

Metaplanet Inc. operates in the financial industry, focusing on strategic financial management with a particular emphasis on Bitcoin as a primary reserve asset. The company aims to mitigate risks associated with currency depreciation and [inflation](https://www.tipranks.com/economic-indicators/inflation-rate) while leveraging Bitcoin’s appreciation potential.
