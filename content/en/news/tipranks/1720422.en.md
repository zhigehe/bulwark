The latest announcement is out from Manulife Financial ( [(TSE:MFC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:mfc) ).

Manulife Financial Corporation announced the details for its upcoming annual meeting, set to take place on May 8, 2025. The record date for notice and voting is March 12, 2025. Common shareholders are entitled to receive notice and vote at the meeting, with notice-and-access provisions applicable to both registered and beneficial holders. The company will cover the cost of sending proxy-related materials to objecting beneficial owners through intermediaries, signaling its commitment to facilitating shareholder participation.

**More about Manulife Financial**

Manulife Financial Corporation is a prominent financial services company based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The company provides a wide range of financial products and services, including insurance, wealth management, and investment solutions, focusing on both individual and institutional clients globally.
