OxPay Financial Limited has entered into a revised convertible loan agreement with a lender, replacing a previous agreement. The Securities Industry Council has granted a whitewash waiver, allowing the company to proceed with the transaction without obligating the controlling shareholder and lender to make a mandatory general offer, subject to specific conditions, including shareholder approval.

**More about OxPay Financial Limited**

OxPay Financial Limited, incorporated in Singapore, operates in the financial industry, focusing on providing financial solutions and services, including loan agreements, to its clients.
