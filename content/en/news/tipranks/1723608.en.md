Differ Group Auto Limited has announced the withdrawal of its application to convene a Scheme Meeting for creditors to consider a Revised Scheme. This decision, approved by the Hong Kong High Court, indicates a shift in the company’s approach to its proposed restructuring. Stakeholders are advised to remain cautious, and further updates will be provided as the restructuring progresses.

**More about Differ Group Auto Limited**

Differ Group Auto Limited is a company incorporated in the Cayman Islands, operating in the automotive industry. It is listed on the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong and focuses on providing automotive products and services.
