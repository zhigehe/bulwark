Primaris Real Estate Investment Trust announced a distribution of $0.0717 per unit for February 2025, equating to an annualized distribution of $0.86 per unit. This strategic financial decision underscores Primaris’ robust capital position, enabling it to capitalize on evolving market opportunities within Canada’s retail property sector.

**More about Primaris Real Estate Investment Trust**

Primaris Real Estate Investment Trust is Canada’s only REIT focusing on enclosed shopping centres, primarily owning interests in leading centres located within growing mid-sized markets. The REIT’s portfolio encompasses 15 million square feet valued at approximately $4.6 billion, benefiting from economies of scale through a fully integrated management platform.
