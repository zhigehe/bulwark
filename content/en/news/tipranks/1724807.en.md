Willas-Array Electronics (Holdings) Limited has announced the resignation of its independent auditors, Deloitte & Touche LLP, without any issues of concern reported to shareholders or creditors. The company plans to change auditors, pending approval from the Accounting and Financial Reporting Council and a special general meeting for shareholder consent, with the transition expected to have no material impact on its upcoming financial audit.

**More about Willas-Array Electronics (Holdings) Limited**

Willas-Array Electronics (Holdings) Limited operates in the electronics industry, focusing on the distribution of electronic components. It serves various sectors, including consumer electronics, telecommunications, and industrial markets.
