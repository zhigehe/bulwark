Umdoni Exploration, Inc. ( [(TSE:ZEUS)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:zeus) ) has shared an announcement.

Zeus North America Mining Corp. has announced a comprehensive update on its 2024 exploration activities at the Cuddy Mountain project in Idaho. The company has undertaken extensive geophysical and sampling work, including staking additional claims, conducting 3D-DCIP Induced Polarization and resistivity surveys, and collecting soil and rock samples. This work has confirmed the presence of stratigraphy similar to that of the nearby Hercules Metals discovery, suggesting potential for significant mineralization. The results from these efforts are expected to advance the company towards drilling and further development of this promising site, potentially enhancing its position in the mining sector.

**More about Umdoni Exploration, Inc.**

Zeus North America Mining Corp. operates in the mining industry, focusing on the exploration and development of mineral resources. The company’s primary products are copper, gold, and silver, with a significant emphasis on porphyry deposits, as evidenced by their recent activities at the Cuddy Mountain project in Idaho.
