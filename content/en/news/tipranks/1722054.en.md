The latest announcement is out from Sands China ( [(HK:1928)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:1928) ).

Sands China Ltd. has announced the filing of its controlling shareholder, Las Vegas Sands Corp.’s annual report for the year ended December 31, 2024, with the SEC under Form 10-K. The report includes financial results and operational statistics, especially concerning Macao operations, which are a significant part of Sands China’s business. The financial information is prepared under US GAAP, which differs from Sands China’s reporting standards, IFRS. This disclosure might impact investor decisions regarding Sands China due to the differences in accounting standards and the insights provided into Macao operations.

**More about Sands China**

Sands China Ltd. is a company incorporated in the Cayman Islands, primarily involved in the gaming and hospitality industry. The company operates within the Macao region and its controlling shareholder is Las Vegas Sands Corp., which owns approximately 72% of its issued share capital.
