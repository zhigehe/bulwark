Japfa Ltd continues to hold a majority stake of 55.43% in the company, indicating stability in its ownership and potentially reassuring stakeholders of consistent strategic direction and control.

**More about Japfa Ltd.**

Japfa Comfeed Indonesia Tbk operates in the agribusiness sector, focusing on the production of animal feed, poultry breeding, and processed food. The company is a significant player in Indonesia’s growing agribusiness industry, serving both domestic and international markets.
