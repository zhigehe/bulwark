An announcement from Ten Sixty Four Limited ( [(AU:X64)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:x64) ) is now available.

Ten Sixty Four Limited, listed on the ASX under the ticker X64, has successfully effectuated its Deed of Company Arrangement (DOCA), ending its period of external administration. This development, confirmed by the Australian Securities and Investments Commission, returns full control to the company’s board of directors. Following this transition, Ten Sixty Four is actively engaging with the ASX for the reinstatement of trading in its securities, marking a significant step in its recovery and operational stabilization.
