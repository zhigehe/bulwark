Daiwa ( [(JP:8601)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/jp:8601) ) has issued an announcement.

Daiwa Securities Group Inc. has been recognized as a global leader in climate change initiatives by being named to CDP’s highest rating ‘A List’ for the second time since 2022. This accolade reflects Daiwa’s commitment to achieving net zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2030 and 2050 for its direct operations and investment portfolios, respectively, as well as its alignment with international climate-related financial disclosure frameworks. This recognition underscores the company’s leadership and dedication to sustainability, potentially enhancing its reputation and influence within the industry.

**More about Daiwa**

Daiwa Securities Group Inc. operates within the financial services industry, primarily focusing on securities and investment banking. The company is listed on the TSE Prime and NSE Premiere, highlighting its significant market presence.
