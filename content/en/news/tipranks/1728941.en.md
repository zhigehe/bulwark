IMPACT Silver Corp. reported significant drill results from the Kena Vein South discovery at its Guadalupe Mine in Mexico, highlighting high-grade silver intersections. The discovery, which is close to existing mine workings, allows for rapid and cost-effective development, with plans to begin mining imminently. These results underscore the company’s aggressive exploration strategy, which aims to expand its high-grade silver resources and potentially enhance its market position.

**More about IMPACT Silver**

IMPACT Silver Corp. operates in the mining industry, focusing on the exploration and production of high-grade silver, along with gold, lead, and zinc. The company’s operations are primarily based in the Royal Mines of Zacualpan Silver-Gold District, Mexico.
