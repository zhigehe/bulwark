This issuance, amounting to 1,062,080,000, marks a significant adjustment in the company’s securities register, following a shelf prospectus and offering report. The impact of this bond issuance is likely to enhance Delek Group’s financial flexibility and potentially strengthen its market position, as it increases the total balance of the securities issued by the company.
