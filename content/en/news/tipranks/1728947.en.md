Stolt-Nielsen ( [(SOIEF)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/soief) ) has provided an announcement.

Stolt-Nielsen Limited has announced that its Board of Directors recommends a final dividend of $1.25 per common share for 2024, subject to shareholder approval at the upcoming Annual General Meeting. If approved, this will result in a total dividend of $2.50 per share for the year, highlighting the company’s commitment to returning value to its shareholders.

**More about Stolt-Nielsen**

Stolt-Nielsen Limited is a company that invests in and manages businesses primarily involved in logistics, distribution, and aquaculture. Their portfolio includes global bulk-liquid and chemicals logistics businesses such as Stolt Tankers, Stolthaven Terminals, and Stolt Tank Containers, as well as Stolt Sea Farm. The company is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange.
