On February 12, 2025, Keith Sadler, a Non-executive Director of Warpaint London plc, purchased 2,500 ordinary shares at 400p per share, increasing his beneficial interest to 44,899 shares. This transaction is part of Warpaint’s ongoing business operations, reflecting confidence in the company’s performance and potentially influencing investor sentiment positively.

**More about Warpaint London**

Warpaint London plc is a specialist supplier of color cosmetics, known for its W7 and Technic brands. The company primarily operates in the UK and international markets, selling its products to major retailers and local distributors. It focuses on the gifting market through its Technic brand, catering to high street retailers and supermarkets. Warpaint also markets other brands like Man’stuff, Body Collection, and Chit Chat, each targeting distinct consumer demographics.
