Tera Light Ltd. announced a successful partnership in the Tera Light Energy Storage Limited Partnership to establish availability tariffs for energy storage facilities interfacing with the high voltage grid. This achievement positions the company strategically within the energy sector, enhancing its operations and offering significant implications for its stakeholders by potentially increasing market competitiveness and operational efficiency.

**More about Tera Light**

Tera Light Ltd. operates in the energy sector with a focus on energy storage solutions. The company is involved in the development and integration of energy storage facilities that connect with the high voltage grid. Its securities are listed on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange, and it was previously known as Menora Mivtachim Energy Holdings Ltd.
