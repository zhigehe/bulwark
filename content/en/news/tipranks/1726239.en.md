**More about IXUP Ltd.**

IXUP Limited is a pioneering technology company that specializes in secure data collaboration. It offers a suite of software products designed to enable the secure sharing and analysis of sensitive information, addressing concerns around privacy, cyber security, and compliance. The company is also responsible for developing and operating BetStop, the National Self Exclusion Register in Australia, under a contract with the Commonwealth Government.
