In the second quarter of fiscal 2025, Electromed, Inc. reported record financial performance with a net revenue increase of 18.7% to $16.3 million and a net income rise to $2.0 million. **More about Electromed**

Electromed, Inc., based in New Prague, Minnesota, is a leader in airway clearance technologies, producing and marketing products like the SmartVest® Airway Clearance System for patients with compromised pulmonary function.
