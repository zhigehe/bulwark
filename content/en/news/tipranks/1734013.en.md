Monumental Energy Corp. has provided updates on its collaboration with New Zealand Energy Corp., highlighting the progress of the Tariki-5A well, which has shown promising potential for gas storage. Despite initial technical challenges in production, efforts are underway to enhance output. The company is advancing the first stage of the Tariki Gas Storage project, which is expected to address New Zealand’s growing demand for gas storage due to market tightness and price volatility. This project is anticipated to commence gas injection by Q4 2025, aiming to leverage existing infrastructure while adapting to increasing energy needs.

**More about Monumental Gold**

Monumental Energy Corp. is a company in the energy sector, focusing on investment and partnerships in the realm of gas exploration and storage. Its primary involvement is in collaboration with the New Zealand Energy Corp., targeting gas storage development and energy resources.
