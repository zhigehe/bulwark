Rapid7 Inc. is a prominent player in the cybersecurity sector, specializing in extended risk and threat detection, with a focus on simplifying cybersecurity for over 11,000 global customers through innovative solutions. In its latest financial report for the fourth quarter and full-year 2024, Rapid7 announced a year-over-year revenue increase of 9%, reaching $844 million, with product subscriptions contributing $809 million. The company’s annualized recurring revenue grew to $840 million, marking a 4% rise, while it achieved a non-GAAP operating income of $164 million and generated $154 million in free cash flow. Key highlights from Rapid7’s financial performance include a 9% increase in product subscription revenue and a significant contribution from North American and global markets. The company also won the ‘Security Vendor of the Year’ award and expanded its platform capabilities to enhance threat detection and response. Looking forward to 2025, Rapid7 aims to continue its focus on growth, innovation, and customer engagement to strengthen its position as a leading security operations platform.
