CVC Limited ( [(AU:CVC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:cvc) ) just unveiled an update.

CVC Limited has announced a projected net loss after tax ranging from $1.6 to $2.1 million for the half-year ending December 31, 2024, compared to a $0.5 million loss in the previous year. Due to future cashflow commitments, the board has decided not to pay an interim dividend for this period, with further business performance details expected with the release of the Half Year accounts in late February 2025.
