**Building DeFi Bridges Between Old and New Finance**
-----------------------------------------------------

At its heart, DeFi Technologies is on a mission to bring the best of both worlds together. The company specializes in blending digital asset innovations with established finance frameworks. Its business spans across several areas, including Asset Management, Infrastructure, Ventures, and DeFi Alpha trading strategies, according to *marketscreener.com*.

So, what does this mean? Think of it as DeFi Technologies setting up the infrastructure that lets both big institutions and everyday users dip their toes into blockchain without needing a degree in cryptography.

**Defi Tech Partners with Giants to Drive Innovation**
------------------------------------------------------

One of the keys to DeFi Technologies’ strength is its strategic partnerships. The company doesn’t just go it alone; it partners with some serious players to expand its reach.

* Professional Capital Management Partnership: In September 2024, DeFi Technologies joined forces with Professional Capital Management to explore the lucrative U.S. ETF market. Their goal? Capture opportunities in a sector that’s growing faster than a blockchain transaction. (*prnewswire.com*)

* Zero Computing Collaboration: Over the summer, DeFi Technologies teamed up with Zero Computing to improve its DeFi Alpha trading desk by integrating advanced zero-knowledge proof technology. This means faster, more secure trades—essentially, a major upgrade to the backend of [crypto](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency) trading infrastructure. (*newswire.ca*)

* AsiaNext and SovFi MOU: As part of its expansion strategy, the company’s subsidiary, Valour Inc., signed a deal with AsiaNext and SovFi to offer digital asset products in the Asia-Pacific region, tapping into the enormous institutional demand in Singapore. (*stocktitan.net*)

These partnerships highlight the company’s focus on scaling its operations globally and positioning itself as a DeFi powerhouse.

**Launching Products That Push Boundaries**
-------------------------------------------

DeFi Technologies isn’t just talking about digital asset innovation; it’s walking the walk. Through its subsidiary Valour, the company has been busy introducing financial products that make traditional finance players pay attention.

Yield-Bearing [Bitcoin](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency/btc-usd) ETP: In a game-changing move, Valour launched a physically-backed, yield-bearing Bitcoin ETP in November 2024. This product gives investors exposure to Bitcoin, along with a 5.65% fixed yield, according to globenewswire.com. It’s a rare offering, and it’s a clever way to attract investors who want more than just price appreciation.

This kind of innovation isn’t just about keeping up with trends—it’s about setting new ones. DeFi Technologies is essentially saying, “Hey, traditional finance, we’ve got better tools now.”

**Strong Financials Anchor Future Growth**
------------------------------------------

Now, let’s talk numbers. As of September 2024, DeFi Technologies reported assets under management (AUM) of about $1.02 billion. That’s a solid foundation for a company operating in an industry that’s still finding its feet.
