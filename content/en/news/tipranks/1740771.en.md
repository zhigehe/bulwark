An update from Emerge Gaming Limited ( [(AU:SP8)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:sp8) ) is now available.

Streamplay Studio Limited has announced the completion of issuing 130,000,000 fully paid ordinary shares to advisors for acquiring Noodlecake Studios Inc. and an additional 740,741 shares for employees and consultants in lieu of salaries and bonuses. This strategic move confirms compliance with the Corporations Act and positions Streamplay to leverage Noodlecake’s innovative capabilities, enhancing its market presence in the casual and indie gaming sectors.

**More about Emerge Gaming Limited**

Streamplay Studio Limited (ASX:SP8) is a prominent entity in the competitive casual gaming and eSports sector, providing game development and publishing, music and sports streaming services, and telco value-added services across Africa, the UAE, and Pacific Islands. The company owns Noodlecake Studios, a North American indie game studio known for its innovative IPs and collaborative publishing projects.
