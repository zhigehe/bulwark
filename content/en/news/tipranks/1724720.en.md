The latest announcement is out from Alta Global Group Limited ( [(MMA)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/mma) ).

On February 10, 2025, MMA.inc announced the launch of the first-ever fully integrated community and commerce platform designed for the global martial arts industry. This innovative platform aims to unify fans, fighters, coaches, gym owners, and brands into a single, monetized ecosystem, promising to redefine how the martial arts community connects and engages. The platform, currently in closed beta, is expected to launch officially in the second calendar quarter and will target the vast martial arts market, which generates over $18 billion in annual revenue in the US alone. This strategic move by MMA.inc is positioned to consolidate the fragmented digital landscape of the martial arts industry, offering a diverse range of features from social community engagement to business tools for gyms and coaches. By leveraging its existing user base and social media reach, MMA.inc anticipates rapid user adoption, ultimately aiming to become the global social and economic hub for martial arts and combat sports.

**More about Alta Global Group Limited**

Mixed Martial Arts Group Limited (MMA.inc) is revolutionizing the MMA industry by fostering increased participation and engagement across fans, athletes, coaches, and gym owners. The company operates four core business units: TrainAlta, Hype, MixedMartialArts.com, and BJJLink, which together serve as platforms for transforming MMA fans into active participants, growing revenue for gym owners and athletes, and managing gym operations. With a substantial presence on social media and a wide network of gyms and user profiles, MMA.inc continues to transform the martial arts landscape.
