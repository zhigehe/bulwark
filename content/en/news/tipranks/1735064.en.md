Mare Engineering Group SpA ( [(IT:MARE)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/it:mare) ) just unveiled an update.

Mare Group has acquired a 70.6% stake in La SIA S.p.A., marking a significant step in its strategic growth plan. This acquisition aims to enhance Mare Group’s innovation capacity and digital transformation opportunities, propelling it into the top 10 independent engineering firms in Italy. The move also facilitates the integration of new predictive [AI](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/artificial-intelligence) models and strengthens the company’s leadership position in the engineering sector.

**More about Mare Engineering Group SpA**

Mare Group S.p.A. is a digital engineering company listed on Euronext Growth Milan, operating both in Italy and internationally. The company focuses on innovation through enabling technologies, particularly within the fields of critical infrastructure, civil construction, smart cities, and sustainable mobility. Mare Group aims to redefine digital engineering boundaries by integrating [artificial intelligence](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/artificial-intelligence) and extended reality.
