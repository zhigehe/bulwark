On February 11, 2025, ARMOUR Residential REIT announced it will host a real-time webcast for equity analysts to discuss its fourth quarter 2024 operating results on February 13, 2025. The earnings release for this period will be issued after trading closes on February 12, 2025, with the webcast available online and for replay on the company’s website for one year.

**More about ARMOUR Residential REIT**

ARMOUR Residential REIT, Inc. is a company that invests in fixed rate residential, adjustable rate, and hybrid adjustable rate residential mortgage-backed securities. These securities are issued or guaranteed by U.S. Government-sponsored enterprises or the Government National Mortgage Association. ARMOUR is externally managed by ARMOUR Capital Management LP, a registered investment advisor with the SEC.
