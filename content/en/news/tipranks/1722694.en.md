Olidata S.p.A. ( [(IT:OLI)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/it:oli) ) has shared an update.

Olidata S.p.A. has announced a share consolidation, converting 192,859,584 existing ordinary shares into 19,285,958 new ordinary shares at a ratio of 1 new share for every 10 existing shares. This move is part of a strategic decision approved by the Extraordinary Shareholders’ Meeting and aims to streamline the company’s share capital structure, potentially enhancing its market position and shareholder value.

**More about Olidata S.p.A.**

Olidata S.p.A. is a leading system integrator in the IT sector based in Italy. The company operates as part of the Olidata Group and has its shares listed on the Euronext Milan market, managed by Borsa Italiana.
