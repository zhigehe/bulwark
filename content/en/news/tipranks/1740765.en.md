Blender Bites Ltd. has appointed Mr. Mike Iannone as the interim Chief Financial Officer and Corporate Secretary, succeeding Mr. Geoff Balderson. Mr. Iannone, who has been with the company for three years, brings over 12 years of experience in financial management and transaction advisory services. His expertise in scaling financial operations and implementing strategic initiatives is expected to support Blender Bites’ continued growth and operational efficiency in the competitive functional food and beverage industry.

**More about Thoughtful Brands**

Blender Bites is a multi-award-winning Canadian company that specializes in the development and marketing of premium frozen beverage products with an emphasis on functionality. Founded in 2017, the company leads in the ‘easy smoothie’ category across North America, offering organic, non-GMO, gluten-free, dairy-free, and soy-free products. Their offerings, like the 1-Step Smoothies and Frappes, are available in over 7000 stores including major retailers such as Walmart and Whole Foods.
