On February 5, 2025, Kingstone Companies, Inc. announced that its subsidiary has entered into a contract to sell its headquarters building and an adjacent mixed-use property in Kingston, New York, to Ulster County for $3,600,000. The sale is expected to close in February or March 2025, subject to certain conditions being met. This transaction reflects a strategic move by Kingstone Companies, potentially impacting its operations and market positioning, while stakeholders are advised that this information is not necessarily material for investment decisions.
