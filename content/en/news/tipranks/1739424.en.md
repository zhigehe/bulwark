Greenheart Gold Inc. has significantly expanded its exploration rights at the Majorodam project in Suriname, increasing the project area by 150% to 251.5 square kilometers through new agreements. The company has commenced a 1,500-meter reverse circulation drilling program at the Heuvel target, marking a critical step in its exploration efforts. This expansion and drilling initiation are poised to enhance Greenheart Gold’s industry positioning and potential resource base, presenting new opportunities for stakeholders.

**More about Greenheart Gold Inc.**

Greenheart Gold Inc. operates in the mining industry, focusing on gold exploration and development. The company is engaged in acquiring and exploring mineral properties, with a significant focus on expanding its operations in Suriname.
