Elbit Imaging ( ($IL:EMITF.M) ) has provided an update.

Elbit Imaging has announced the outcomes of a special meeting held on September 2, 2025, which included key decisions on executive appointments and compensation. The meeting approved the appointment of Mr. Eran Mazor as CEO effective January 1, 2025, and granted a discretionary bonus to the current CEO, Mr. Ron Hadassi, for his work in 2024. Additionally, it approved the update of Mr. Hadassi’s employment terms as an external consultant post his tenure as chairman, and the granting of option warrants to certain directors.
