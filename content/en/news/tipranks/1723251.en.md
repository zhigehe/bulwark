Raffles Medical Group has appointed Mr. Phua Tien Beng as the Deputy Managing Director of Raffles China Healthcare. With over 20 years of experience in the healthcare sector in Singapore and China, Mr. Phua is expected to lead the development and operational management of the business portfolio in Greater China, enhancing the group’s presence and strategic positioning in the region.

**More about Raffles Medical Group**

Raffles Medical Group is a healthcare provider that focuses on delivering a range of medical services. It operates within the healthcare industry, with a particular emphasis on expanding its operations in the Greater China region.
