Cemex SAB ( [(CX)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/cx) ) has provided an update.

On February 10, 2025, Cemex announced significant senior-level organizational changes following the retirement of CEO Fernando Gonzalez and the appointment of Jaime Muguiro as the new CEO, effective April 1, 2025. These organizational adjustments, which include new appointments for key regional president positions, aim to strengthen Cemex’s leadership development and organizational success, reinforcing its strategic positioning in the construction materials industry.

**More about Cemex SAB**

Cemex is a global company specializing in construction materials, offering products such as cement, ready-mix concrete, and aggregates. The company is committed to sustainability and carbon neutrality, utilizing innovative technologies to advance the circular economy within the construction value chain. Cemex operates in growing markets worldwide, driven by a multinational workforce enhancing customer experience through digital technologies.
