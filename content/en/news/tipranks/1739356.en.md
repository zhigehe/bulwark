An announcement from CyberArk Software ( [(CYBR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/cybr) ) is now available.

CyberArk Software announced record financial results for the fourth quarter and full year 2024, with total annual recurring revenue reaching $1.169 billion and surpassing $1 billion organically. The company reported a total revenue of $1.001 billion for the year, driven by strong demand for its identity security solutions and the acquisition of Venafi. CyberArk’s strategic execution enabled it to return to the Rule of 40 a year ahead of schedule, reflecting robust revenue growth and a strong free cash flow margin. The acquisition of Zilla Security further enhances CyberArk’s identity security platform with AI-powered identity governance and administration capabilities, positioning the company to capitalize on its significant market opportunities.

**More about CyberArk Software**

CyberArk Software is a global leader in identity security, specializing in solutions that protect organizations from cyber threats through identity management and privileged access security. The company focuses on delivering a comprehensive identity security platform that secures both human and machine identities with advanced privilege controls.
