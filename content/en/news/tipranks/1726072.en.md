BSP Financial Group Ltd. ( [(AU:BFL)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:bfl) ) has issued an update.

BSP Financial Group Ltd. announced that its 2024 Full Year Financial Results presentation will take place on February 24, 2025, via webcast. The event will be hosted by the Group CEO and Acting Group CFO and include a Q&A session. The results will be released on February 19, 2025, on the PNGX, ASX, and SPX, with the company encouraging stakeholders to submit questions in advance.

**More about BSP Financial Group Ltd.**

BSP Financial Group Ltd., incorporated in Papua New Guinea, operates within the financial services industry. It offers a range of banking products and services and is listed on the ASX and PNGX, focusing on the Pacific region.
