The latest announcement is out from Kumagai Gumi Co ( [(JP:1861)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/jp:1861) ).

Kumagai Gumi Co., Ltd. has announced the introduction of an interim dividend system, aiming to provide more frequent profit returns to shareholders by shifting from an annual to a biannual dividend payment. This decision, pending shareholder approval, reflects the company’s intention to enhance shareholder value and could potentially strengthen its market positioning by attracting more investors seeking regular income.

**More about Kumagai Gumi Co**

Kumagai Gumi Co., Ltd. operates in the construction industry and is listed on the Prime Market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange. The company is involved in various construction projects, focusing on infrastructure and building development.
