The latest announcement is out from Comal S.p.A. ( [(IT:CML)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/it:cml) ).

Duferco Solar Projects S.r.l. has received approval from CONSOB for its voluntary total public purchase offer on the ordinary shares of Comal S.p.A., aiming to acquire all shares except those held by Duferco Travi e Profilati S.p.A. The offer, set at 4.80 euros per share, will be open from February 17 to March 7, 2025, with potential extensions and reopenings. This move indicates a strategic expansion effort by Duferco in the renewable energy sector, potentially affecting stakeholders’ interests and market dynamics.
