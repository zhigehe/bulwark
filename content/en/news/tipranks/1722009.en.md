Fosun Tourism Group has announced a court meeting scheduled for March 4, 2025, to consider a proposed scheme of arrangement with its shareholders. This is part of a legal process under the Grand Court of the Cayman Islands and will require the approval of the Scheme Shareholders, with implications for the company’s corporate structure and shareholder agreements.

**More about Fosun Tourism Group**

Fosun Tourism Group is a company incorporated under the laws of the Cayman Islands, operating primarily in the tourism industry.
