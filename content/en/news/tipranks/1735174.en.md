CITCO TRUSTEES (UT) LIMITED, as Trustee of 3D Endeavor Master Fund II, has initiated a tender offer for investment units of Hankyu Hanshin REIT, Inc. without prior consultation. Hankyu Hanshin REIT is currently reviewing the offer and will announce its official stance after examining the relevant details, potentially impacting its stakeholders and market positioning.

**More about Hankyu Hanshin REIT, Inc.**

Hankyu Hanshin REIT, Inc. operates in the real estate investment trust (REIT) industry, focusing on managing and investing in a diversified portfolio of real estate properties. The company aims to provide stable income and growth potential for its investors through strategic asset management.
