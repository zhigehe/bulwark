Mr. Qian brings extensive investment experience across sectors such as information technology, healthcare, and energy, with a notable background in managing investment companies. His appointment is expected to enhance the Group’s project promotion and business development activities. Mr. Qian holds a significant shareholding in the company, owning approximately 19.90% of its issued share capital. The company’s board welcomes Mr. Qian, anticipating his strategic impact on their operations and growth prospects.
