Wise PLC Class A ( [(GB:WISE)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:wise) ) just unveiled an update.

Wise PLC, a financial technology company, announced a series of share transactions involving its Class A ordinary shares. These transactions were undertaken by entities associated with Alex Rampell, a Non-Executive Director of the company. The shares were distributed from various funds to the Rampell Family Revocable Trust, with all transactions occurring on February 7, 2025, outside of a trading venue. This movement of shares, conducted at zero cost, highlights internal shifts in shareholding among closely associated parties within the firm.
