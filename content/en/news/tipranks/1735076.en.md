Swift Networks Group Limited has issued 7,716,007 fully paid ordinary shares following the conversion of vested performance rights, as announced on February 13, 2025. This move, executed without disclosure under Part 6D.2 of the Corporations Act 2001, signifies Swift’s compliance with its statutory reporting and disclosure obligations, reinforcing its commitment to transparency and operational integrity.

**More about Swift Networks Group Limited**

Swift Networks Group Limited is a specialist technology company that provides a premium entertainment and engagement platform through proprietary technology and network infrastructure. The company offers an end-to-end solution, including consulting, design, and installation services, catering specifically to sectors such as Mining and Resources, Residential Aged Care, Retirement Living, and Government.
