Ensurge Micropower ( [(ENMPF)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/enmpf) ) just unveiled an update.

Ensurge Micropower held an electronic Extraordinary General Meeting where it was decided to issue 20,000,000 warrants to participants in a recent private placement, each with an exercise price of NOK 1. This development is part of Ensurge’s strategic initiatives to strengthen its financial position and expand its presence in the microbattery market, potentially enhancing its operations and stakeholder interests.

**More about Ensurge Micropower**

Ensurge Micropower is a company focused on innovating energy solutions with its ultrathin, flexible, and reliable solid-state lithium microbattery. These microbatteries are ideal for wearable devices, connected sensors, and IoT solutions, particularly in applications where space is limited, such as hearables, digital and health wearables, sports and fitness devices. The company operates a cutting-edge manufacturing facility in Silicon Valley, leveraging advanced technology and materials innovation to produce energy solutions for both established and growing markets.
