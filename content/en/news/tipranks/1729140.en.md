Anteros Metals Inc. ( [(TSE:ANT)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:ant) ) has issued an update.

Anteros Metals Inc. has initiated 3D modeling at its Havens Steady VMS Property in Newfoundland, which is rich in critical minerals including lead, zinc, silver, copper, and gold. This strategic move aims to enhance exploration efficiency by identifying zone extensions and higher-grade areas, potentially boosting the company’s resource base and strengthening its position in the mining industry. The property, located in a prolific mining region, benefits from existing infrastructure and has significant historical drilling data to support further exploration.

**More about Anteros Metals Inc.**

Anteros Metals Inc. operates in the mining industry, focusing on critical mineral deposits. The company specializes in Volcanogenic Massive Sulphide (VMS) deposits containing lead, zinc, silver, copper, and gold. Anteros is strategically positioned in Newfoundland, Canada, and its operations are centered around exploring and developing mineral-rich properties in established mining districts.
