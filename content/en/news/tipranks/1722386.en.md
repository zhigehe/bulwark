These shares have been issued under an employee incentive scheme, indicating efforts to align employee interests with the company’s performance. This move may positively impact Pureprofile’s market positioning by enhancing employee motivation and potentially improving operational outcomes.

**More about Pureprofile Ltd.**

Pureprofile Ltd operates in the technology industry, focusing on data and insights services. The company is primarily known for its work with consumer data to provide insights and analytics to businesses, aiming to enhance marketing strategies and consumer engagement.
