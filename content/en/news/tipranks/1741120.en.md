Greencape Capital Pty Ltd has announced that it is no longer a substantial holder in Sigma Healthcare Ltd, as revealed in a recent notice. This change is attributed to a reduction in the percentage of shares held by Greencape due to Sigma’s issuance of new securities, which has implications for the company’s shareholder structure.

**More about Sigma Healthcare Ltd**

Sigma Healthcare Ltd operates in the healthcare industry, focusing on the distribution and wholesale of pharmaceutical products and services. The company is a significant player in the Australian market, providing pharmacy services and retail pharmacy support services across the region.
