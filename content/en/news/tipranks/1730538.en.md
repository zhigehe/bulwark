The latest update is out from AnteoTech Ltd ( [(AU:ADO)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:ado) ).

AnteoTech Ltd announced the release of its Half Yearly Report for the six months ending December 2024, scheduled for February 26, 2025, alongside a Results & Operational Update Presentation. CEO David Radford will host an investor call to discuss the company’s operational status and upcoming business opportunities, which may impact AnteoTech’s market positioning and stakeholder interests.

**More about AnteoTech Ltd**

AnteoTech Ltd is a revenue-stage company that provides solutions in the clean energy and life sciences markets through its proprietary applied materials technology. The company is involved in the clean energy sector with its lead product, Anteo X™, which enhances anode performance for lithium-ion batteries, and has partnered with global suppliers in the industry. In the life sciences domain, AnteoTech focuses on point-of-care and in vitro diagnostics markets, offering bioconjugation advantages through its AnteoBind™ technology.
