**More about German American Bancorp**

German American Bancorp is a community-focused financial services organization operating in Indiana, Kentucky, and Ohio. It has $8.3 billion in total banking assets and $3.7 billion in investment and trust assets under management, with 94 banking offices and approximately 1100 team members.
