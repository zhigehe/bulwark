Trendlines Group ( [(SG:42T)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/sg:42t) ) has provided an update.

The Trendlines Group Ltd. has announced an expected net loss between US$9 million and US$11 million for the fiscal year ending December 2024. This financial downturn is attributed to increased expenses from portfolio companies raising funds for development and a decline in portfolio value due to write-offs and write-downs. These financial adjustments do not impact the company’s cash position. Shareholders are advised to exercise caution, with further financial details to be provided by March 1, 2025.

**More about Trendlines Group**

The Trendlines Group Ltd., incorporated in Israel, operates within the financial sector. The company is involved in managing a portfolio of companies focused on raising funds and further development, particularly in Singapore.
