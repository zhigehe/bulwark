AF Gruppen ASA ( [(AGRUF)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/agruf) ) has shared an update.

AF Gruppen ASA has been awarded the detailed design and construction of the new E6 Roterud-Storhove highway project by Nye Veier, following a successful conceptualization phase. The project, valued at NOK 6,350 million, will see the construction of a 23 km four-lane highway, with a focus on environmental considerations, expected to be completed by the summer of 2030, bringing significant benefits to local and regional stakeholders.

**More about AF Gruppen ASA**

AF Gruppen ASA is a prominent company in the construction and civil engineering industry, known for its involvement in major infrastructure projects. They specialize in designing and building large-scale projects, often collaborating with partners to ensure environmental considerations are integrated into their work.
