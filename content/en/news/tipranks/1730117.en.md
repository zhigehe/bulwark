ROXX, Inc. has announced insights from its Q1 financial results briefing for the fiscal year ended September 30, 2025. The company observes a strong labor market in Japan’s non-desk worker segment, with employers easing hiring criteria due to worker shortages. ROXX is piloting Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) services to deepen client engagement and stabilize performance, affecting its gross profit margin. Additionally, a mass media campaign is enhancing brand awareness and job seeker acquisition for its ‘Z Career’ platform, which faces intense competition. ROXX plans to leverage AI-powered products and its brand to support partner recruitment agencies.

**More about ROXX, Inc.**

ROXX, Inc. is engaged in the recruitment industry, focusing on non-desk worker segments in Japan. The company provides recruitment services and is exploring Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) to enhance its offerings.
