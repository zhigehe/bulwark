An update from Ashoka Buildcon Limited ( [(IN:ASHOKA)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/in:ashoka) ) is now available.

Ashoka Buildcon Limited reported a decline in its financial performance for the quarter and nine months ended December 31, 2024, with a 16% decrease in total income for Q3 FY25 compared to the same quarter the previous year. Despite the decrease in total income and profit, the company saw an improvement in EBITDA margin and a 5% year-over-year increase in toll collection for its BOT division. The company’s significant order book, primarily in road EPC and power transmission and distribution, suggests ongoing opportunities for growth despite current financial challenges.

**More about Ashoka Buildcon Limited**

Ashoka Buildcon Limited is an integrated Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) and Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT) player in the infrastructure sector. The company focuses on developing key infrastructure projects across sectors like roads, power transmission and distribution, and railways, with a significant portion of its order book in road EPC and power T&D.
