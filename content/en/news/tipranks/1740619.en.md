ispace, inc. and King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals (KFUPM) have signed a memorandum of understanding to explore collaborations in lunar exploration and spacecraft development. This partnership aims to advance Saudi Arabia’s space exploration capabilities and inspire future generations through joint research and development initiatives, highlighting ispace’s role in pioneering lunar missions and technology.

**More about ispace,inc.**

ispace, inc. is a global lunar exploration company focused on designing and building lunar landers and rovers. The company aims to expand human life into space by providing sustainable and cost-effective lunar missions.
