Siemens Energy ( [(DE:ENR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/de:enr) ) has shared an update.

Siemens Energy AG, based in Munich, Germany, has issued a voting rights announcement detailing changes in voting rights associated with its shares. Morgan Stanley, a major shareholder, has adjusted its voting rights due to the application of a custody exemption at the subsidiary level, resulting in a decrease in the total voting rights percentage from 9.25% to 8.37%. This change could impact Siemens Energy’s shareholder dynamics and influence within the energy sector.
