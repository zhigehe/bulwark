IWS Group Holdings Limited has announced an Extraordinary General Meeting to be held on March 6, 2025, in Hong Kong to approve the 2025 Master Services Agreement. The agreement, which outlines services transactions for a three-year term starting April 2025, aims to establish annual caps and authorize directors to make necessary amendments. This meeting is significant for shareholders as it involves decisions that will impact the company’s future operations and strategic directions.

**More about IWS Group Holdings Ltd.**

IWS Group Holdings Limited is incorporated in the Cayman Islands and is listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. The company operates primarily in the services industry, though specific details about its products or services are not provided in the notice.
