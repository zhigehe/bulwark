The latest announcement is out from Yibin City Commercial Bank Co., Ltd. Class H ( [(HK:2596)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:2596) ).

Yibin City Commercial Bank Co., Ltd. has announced a proposed appointment of Confucius International CPA Limited as its international auditor for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2024. The decision was made by the Board after an evaluation process and is subject to approval at an extraordinary general meeting. Previously, PricewaterhouseCoopers served as the bank’s auditor, and there were no disagreements regarding the change. This strategic move aims to enhance the bank’s financial reporting and internal controls, reflecting the bank’s commitment to maintaining transparency and robust governance.

**More about Yibin City Commercial Bank Co., Ltd. Class H**

Yibin City Commercial Bank Co., Ltd. is a joint stock company incorporated in China, engaged in the financial sector. The bank focuses on providing banking services in the region but is not an authorized institution under the Hong Kong Banking Ordinance and does not operate under the supervision of the Hong Kong Monetary Authority.
