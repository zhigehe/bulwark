Ascent Bridge Limited ( [(SG:AWG)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/sg:awg) ) just unveiled an announcement.

Ascent Bridge Limited announced it will start quarterly financial reporting from the fourth quarter of the financial year 2026. This requirement follows a Disclaimer of Opinion from its auditor, Ernst & Young LLP, on the financial statements for the year ending March 2024, necessitating increased transparency and compliance with exchange regulations.

**More about Ascent Bridge Limited**

Ascent Bridge Limited, incorporated in Singapore, operates within a group structure and is subject to the rules of the Singapore Exchange Securities Trading Limited.
