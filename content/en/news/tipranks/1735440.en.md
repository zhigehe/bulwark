Heartland Group Holdings Ltd., a company listed on the New Zealand Stock Exchange, has seen the Accident Compensation Corporation (ACC) acquire a substantial holding in its ordinary shares. The ACC now holds 5.026% of Heartland’s total shares, marking a significant investment move between October 2024 and February 2025, with ACC purchasing over 4.6 million shares while selling around 854,000 shares. This development indicates ACC’s strategic interest in Heartland, potentially impacting the company’s market dynamics and stakeholder interests.
