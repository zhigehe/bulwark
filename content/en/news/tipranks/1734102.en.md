SMX Public Limited Company ( [(SMX)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/smx) ) just unveiled an announcement.

SMX Public Limited Company held its Annual General Meeting on February 12, 2025, where shareholders voted on five key proposals. These included the re-election of directors Zeren Browne and Roger Meltzer, the election of Pebble Sia Huei-Chieh, the re-appointment of BDO Israel as the statutory auditor, and the approval of the auditor’s remuneration. All proposals passed with a majority vote, indicating strong shareholder support for the company’s current governance and operational strategies.

**More about SMX Public Limited Company**

SMX (Security Matters) Public Limited Company operates in the technology sector, focusing on digital tracking solutions. The company specializes in providing security and traceability services for supply chains, aiming to enhance transparency and accountability in various industries.
