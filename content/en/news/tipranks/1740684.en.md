This outcome supports the company’s strategic objectives in advancing its exploration projects and could positively impact its operational capabilities and industry positioning by potentially accelerating project development and enhancing stakeholder confidence.

**More about Alderan Resources Ltd.**

Hawk Resources Limited specializes in the exploration of critical and precious metals, with projects in copper and gold located in Utah, USA, as well as eight lithium projects in Minas Gerais and Bahia, Brazil. The company aims to rapidly discover, delineate, and develop critical metal and gold deposits for mining, leveraging its position in under-explored geological belts that are geologically similar to neighboring mining districts.
