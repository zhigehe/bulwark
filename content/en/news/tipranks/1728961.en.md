Rotork plc has announced transactions by its key managerial personnel, namely Ross Pascoe, Chief Technology Officer, and Metin Gerceker, Managing Director of Water & Power, under the company’s Share Incentive Plan. These transactions, conducted on the London Stock Exchange, involve purchasing shares at a specified price, reflecting compliance with regulatory obligations, and demonstrate the executives’ continued investment in the company, potentially affecting stakeholder perceptions positively.

**More about Rotork plc**

Rotork plc operates within the industrial sector, focusing on the production and supply of flow control solutions such as actuators and associated equipment. The company serves various industries, including oil and gas, water and wastewater, and power generation, providing essential components for the automation of valves.
