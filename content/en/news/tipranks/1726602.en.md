Clal Insurance ( [(IL:CLIS)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:clis) ) has shared an announcement.

Clal Insurance’s subsidiary, Max It Finance Ltd, has signed a correction to its agreement with Bank Leumi, extending the agreement period until January 31, 2031, and adjusting conditions for exercising options and income distribution. This update, which includes a commitment from the bank to maintain minimum issuance volumes, is not expected to materially impact Max’s business results.

**More about Clal Insurance**

Clal Insurance Enterprises Holdings Ltd is a prominent player in the insurance industry, offering a wide range of insurance products and services. The company operates through subsidiaries, including Max It Finance Ltd, and is listed on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange.
