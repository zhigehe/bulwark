An announcement from Mapletree Logistics ( [(SG:M44U)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/sg:m44u) ) is now available.

Mapletree Logistics Trust has announced the issuance of S$50 million 3.298% Green Notes due 2032 under its S$3 billion Euro Medium Term Securities Programme. The proceeds will be used to finance or refinance eligible green projects in line with the company’s Green Finance Framework, supporting its commitment to sustainable development.

**More about Mapletree Logistics**

Mapletree Logistics Trust is a company based in Singapore that operates in the logistics sector. It primarily focuses on managing and investing in logistics properties across Asia-Pacific, enhancing its market presence through strategic acquisitions and developments.
