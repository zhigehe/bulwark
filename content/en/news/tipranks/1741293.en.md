Siemens Energy ( [(DE:ENR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/de:enr) ) just unveiled an announcement.

Siemens Energy AG, headquartered in Munich, Germany, has announced a change in voting rights associated with its shares. The Ministry of Finance on behalf of the State of Norway has adjusted its shareholding, resulting in a new total position of 2.98% voting rights, down from a previous total of 3.33%. This adjustment indicates a slight reduction in the influence of the Norwegian Ministry of Finance on Siemens Energy’s shareholder decisions.
