**Recent Updates on Alphabet Inc. Class A stock**

Alphabet Inc. Class A recently reported strong financial performance for Q4 2024, with annual revenue reaching $350 billion, a 14% increase from the previous year. This growth was primarily driven by advancements in [AI](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/artificial-intelligence) and cloud services, with Google Cloud revenue rising by 30% to $12 billion. Despite facing a 4% decline in network advertising revenue and challenges in cloud capacity, the company’s overall sentiment remained positive. YouTube and Google Services also performed well, with YouTube advertising revenue growing by 14%. Alphabet anticipates potential revenue challenges in 2025 due to currency fluctuations and the leap year effect but plans to invest $75 billion in capital expenditures, focusing on AI and technical infrastructure to sustain growth. CEO Sundar Pichai highlighted the company’s leadership in AI as a key driver of future growth.
