Luzon Group ( [(IL:LUZN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:luzn) ) has issued an update.

Luzon Group announced a corrective report regarding an error in their securities register. The company made an adjustment due to the incorrect deletion of option letters following their exercise, reinstating them in the register with a current amount of zero. This change involves the allocation of 577,557 ordinary shares against the exercise of option letters, reflecting an update in the company’s securities and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.
