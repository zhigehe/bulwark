The latest announcement is out from Ourgame International Holdings Ltd ( [(HK:6899)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:6899) ).

Ourgame International Holdings Limited has announced a continued suspension of trading in its shares on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange due to unresolved issues that have led to the suspension. The company faces the risk of having its listing canceled if it does not comply with the resumption guidance and resolve the suspension issues by the deadline of September 27, 2025. The board advises shareholders and potential investors to exercise caution, noting significant uncertainty regarding the resumption of trading.

**More about Ourgame International Holdings Ltd**

Ourgame International Holdings Limited is a company incorporated under the laws of the Cayman Islands, operating in the gaming and entertainment industry. It primarily focuses on providing online card and board games, and has a significant presence in the Asian market.
