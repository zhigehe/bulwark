Great Boulder Resources Ltd ( [(AU:GBR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:gbr) ) has issued an update.

Great Boulder Resources Ltd has announced a change in its company auditor, appointing Criterion Audit Pty Ltd to replace RSM Australia Partners. This decision follows a review and tender process undertaken by the company’s board to assess external audit arrangements. The appointment of Criterion will be recommended for ratification in the upcoming General Meeting. This change reflects Great Boulder Resources’ commitment to maintaining robust financial oversight, which is essential for its continued development and exploration activities in the competitive mineral exploration sector.

**More about Great Boulder Resources Ltd**

Great Boulder Resources Ltd is a mineral exploration company based in Western Australia, focusing on gold and base metals assets. The company is primarily engaged in the exploration and development of the Side Well Gold Project at Meekatharra in the Murchison gold field. Great Boulder is also involved in early-stage exploration at the Wellington Base Metal Project. With a strong technical team and a portfolio of promising assets, the company is well-positioned for future growth and success in the mining industry.
