The latest update is out from StrongPoint ASA ( [(DE:PGT)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/de:pgt) ).

StrongPoint ASA is set to release its financial results for the fourth quarter and full year 2024 on February 13, 2025, with presentations available via webcast and a live Q&A session. This announcement underscores StrongPoint’s commitment to transparency and its ongoing efforts to keep stakeholders informed about its financial health and market performance.

**More about StrongPoint ASA**

StrongPoint is a grocery retail technology company headquartered in Norway, providing solutions to enhance shopping experiences and efficiency in online grocery shopping. It operates in over 20 countries with approximately 500 employees across Norway, Sweden, the Baltics, Finland, Spain, the UK, and Ireland. The company offers end-to-end e-commerce solutions, in-store technologies, and various pickup solutions, and is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange.
