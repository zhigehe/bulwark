U.S. futures remained steady on Friday morning, following a strong session on the previous day. The market rally was fueled by U.S. President Donald Trump’s decision to delay the implementation of new reciprocal tariffs. Futures on the [Nasdaq 100](https://www.tipranks.com/index/nasdaq-100) ([**NDX**](https://www.tipranks.com/index/nasdaq-100)), the [Dow Jones Industrial Average](https://www.tipranks.com/index/dow-jones) ([**DJIA**](https://www.tipranks.com/index/dow-jones)), and the S&P 500 ([**SPX**](https://www.tipranks.com/index/spx)) were up 0.09%, 0.01%, and 0.07%, respectively, at 3:18 a.m. EST, February 14.

On Thursday, all three major indices ended the day higher. The Dow Jones, the S&P 500, and the Nasdaq Composite [(NDAQ)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/ndaq) gained 0.77%, 1.04%, and 1.5%, respectively. It must be noted that an [encouraging Producer Price Index (PPI) report](https://www.tipranks.com/news/january-ppi-report-what-investors-need-to-know-about-rising-inflation) helped boost market sentiment.

In major stock market news, Applovin [(APP)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/app) gained 24% on [impressive Q4 results](https://www.tipranks.com/news/company-announcements/applovin-reports-strong-growth-in-2024-financial-results). Also, Crocs [(CROX)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/crox) stock was up 23.9% following the release of [better-than-expected Q4 results](https://www.tipranks.com/news/top-analysts-praise-crocs-stock-crox-post-strong-q4-results). In contrast, Reddit [(RDDT)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/rddt), Deere [(DE)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/de), The Trade Desk [(TTD)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/ttd), and Datadog [(DDOG)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/ddog) declined as investors expressed disappointment over their earnings reports.

Moving to the extended trading session, GameStop [(GME)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gme) stock was up 7.4% on news that the company is [looking to invest in cryptocurrencies](https://www.tipranks.com/news/gamestop-stock-gme-soars-as-bitcoin-speculation-heats-up). Also, Airbnb [(ABNB)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/abnb) jumped 14.2% after [reporting strong fourth-quarter results](https://www.tipranks.com/news/abnb-earnings-airbnbs-stock-surges-15-on-strong-print).

Looking ahead, investors are focused on the release of U.S. Retail Sales and Industrial Production reports for January, due today. These reports will provide insights into consumer spending and overall economic activity.

On the corporate earnings front, a few companies are scheduled to release their quarterly numbers such as Moderna [(MRNA)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/mrna), Magna [(MGA)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/mga), Fortis [(FTA)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/fta), and AMC Networks [(AMCX)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/amcx).

Meanwhile, the U.S. 10-year treasury yield was up, floating near 4.546% as of writing. Simultaneously, WTI crude oil futures are trending higher, hovering near $71.65 per barrel as of the last check.

Elsewhere, European indices opened lower on Friday after a strong session yesterday. Further, traders are assessing the impact of U.S. tariffs on European growth after [Trump](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/djt) signed an executive order for reciprocal tariffs but delayed its implementation.

**Asia-Pacific Markets Traded Mixed on Friday**
-----------------------------------------------

Asia-Pacific indices were mixed today amid hopes of easing trade tensions after Trump’s delay on reciprocal tariffs. Further, the People’s Bank of China’s promise to ease monetary policy as necessary to support economic growth helped lift market sentiment.

Notably, the Hang Seng Index rallied 3.53%. Further, China’s Shanghai Composite and Shenzhen Component indices gained 0.43% and 1.16%, respectively. However, Japan’s Nikkei and Topix indices were down 0.79% and 0.23%, respectively.
