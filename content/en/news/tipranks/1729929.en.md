This initiative is part of their strategy to optimize capital structure, potentially enhancing shareholder value and signaling confidence in the company’s financial health.

**More about Webjet Limited**

Web Travel Group Limited operates in the travel industry, offering services related to booking and managing travel accommodations and experiences. The company focuses on enhancing travel experiences through online platforms, catering to a diverse market interested in travel planning.
