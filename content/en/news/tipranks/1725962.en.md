An announcement from Endur ASA ( [(DE:4ZT0)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/de:4zt0) ) is now available.

Endúr ASA announced a successful placement and conditional allocation of 4,861,111 shares at a fixed subscription price of NOK 72.00, raising gross proceeds of approximately NOK 350 million. This Private Placement is subject to approval in an extraordinary general meeting and may lead to a subsequent offering. The announcement reflects Endúr ASA’s strategic move to strengthen its financial position, potentially impacting its market standing positively and offering new opportunities for existing shareholders.

**More about Endur ASA**

Endúr ASA is a leading supplier of construction and maintenance projects and services for marine infrastructure, including facilities for land-based aquaculture, quays, harbours, dams, bridges, and other specialized concrete and steel projects. The company is headquartered in Lysaker, Norway.
