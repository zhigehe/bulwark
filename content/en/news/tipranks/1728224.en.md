Banco Bradesco SA ( [(BBD)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/bbd) ) has provided an update.

On February 10, 2025, Banco Bradesco S.A. completed a significant transaction with John Deere Brasil S.A., acquiring a 50% stake in Banco John Deere S.A. This strategic partnership aims to leverage financial solutions for clients and dealerships in agribusiness and construction. Despite this acquisition, the transaction is not expected to materially impact Bradesco’s capitalization rate.

**More about Banco Bradesco SA**

Banco Bradesco S.A. is a prominent financial institution in Brazil, providing a wide range of banking and financial services. The company serves various sectors, including agribusiness and construction, through its financial solutions.
