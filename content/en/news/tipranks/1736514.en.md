IWS Group Holdings Limited has announced a delay in the dispatch of a circular related to its continuing connected transactions and the renewal of the Master Services Agreement. The circular, originally intended to be sent to shareholders by mid-January, is now expected to be postponed until February 19, 2025, due to additional time required for its publication.
