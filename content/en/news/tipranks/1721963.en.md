The latest update is out from Fosun Tourism Group ( [(HK:1992)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:1992) ).

Fosun Tourism Group has announced the dispatch of a Scheme Document related to a proposed share buy-back and withdrawal of its stock listing, under a scheme of arrangement as per the Companies Act. The Scheme Document includes detailed proposals and recommendations from the Independent Financial Adviser, who has deemed the terms fair and reasonable, urging disinterested shareholders and share incentive holders to vote in favor of the resolutions at upcoming meetings.

**More about Fosun Tourism Group**

Fosun Tourism Group, incorporated under the laws of the Cayman Islands, operates within the tourism industry. It focuses on providing travel and leisure services, with a market emphasis on enhancing tourism experiences and offering various travel-related products.
