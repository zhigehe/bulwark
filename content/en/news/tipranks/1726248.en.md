Myer Holdings Limited ( [(AU:MYR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:myr) ) has issued an update.

Myer Holdings Limited has announced a change in the director’s interest, specifically for Jacquie Naylor. The change involves the acquisition of a significant number of shares, both directly and indirectly, due to an in specie distribution related to Myer’s acquisition of Just Group from Premier Investments. This adjustment in director’s interests reflects a strategic move following the recent corporate acquisition, potentially impacting Myer’s shareholder dynamics and market positioning.

**More about Myer Holdings Limited**

Myer Holdings Limited is a prominent company in the retail industry, primarily offering a wide range of products including fashion, beauty, and homewares. The company is focused on serving the Australian market with a strong emphasis on providing quality merchandise and a positive shopping experience for customers.
