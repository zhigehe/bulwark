EFFI CAPITAL NADLAN LTD has released an immediate report concerning the results of a recent tender offering of securities. The company issued 1,000,000 NIS bonds during the tender, with 314,526 NIS bonds sold at a weighted average price of 314.5 million NIS, representing a 6.69% share of the issued bonds. The significant shareholder has provided commitments under the tender terms, as per the Land Law and securities law.
