Akastor ASA ( [(AKKVF)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/akkvf) ) has issued an update.

Akastor ASA has completed the transfer of 8.3% of its shares in AKOFS Offshore to Mitsui O.S.K. Lines, Ltd., altering the ownership structure so that Akastor now controls two-thirds and Mitsui O.S.K. Lines one-third of the shares. This strategic realignment is part of Akastor’s efforts to optimize its investment holdings in the oil and gas sector, potentially impacting its market positioning and stakeholder interests.

**More about Akastor ASA**

Akastor ASA is a Norway-based oil-services investment company with a portfolio of industrial holdings and other investments, focusing on active ownership and long-term value creation. AKOFS Offshore, a subsidiary, provides vessel-based subsea well installation and intervention services to the oil and gas industry, operating three specialized offshore vessels contracted to major companies for projects in Brazil and on the Norwegian Continental Shelf.
