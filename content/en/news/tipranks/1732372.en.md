An update from China Partytime Culture Holdings Ltd. ( [(HK:1532)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:1532) ) is now available.

China Partytime Culture Holdings Limited has announced an extraordinary general meeting to discuss and potentially approve the conditional sale of its entire equity interests in Zhejiang Zhongpai Enterprise Management Co., Ltd to Zhejiang Bestway Costume & Accessory Co., Ltd for RMB80,000,000. This decision is part of the company’s strategic realignment and could significantly impact its market positioning by refocusing its operational assets. Stakeholders will observe how this transaction aligns with the company’s long-term goals and affects its competitive stance in the industry.
