Smith & Nephew Snats ( [(SNN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/snn) ) just unveiled an announcement.

On February 12, 2025, Smith & Nephew plc released its six-monthly block listing return for several share plans, covering the period from August 1, 2024, to January 31, 2025. The report indicates that there was no increase in the block schemes and limited securities were issued across various plans, with a notable decrease in the unallotted securities for some schemes. This report reflects the company’s ongoing efforts to manage its equity-based compensation schemes proactively.

**More about Smith & Nephew Snats**

Smith & Nephew plc is a multinational company based in Watford, England, specializing in medical devices and healthcare products. The company is focused on providing solutions in orthopedics, sports medicine, and advanced wound management.
