CodeLab Capital AS held an extraordinary general meeting electronically, where key decisions were made to address board changes and improve the company’s share pricing. The meeting approved the resignation of board member Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, electing Anton Lorenz Bondesen as the new chairman. Additionally, a reverse share split was approved to consolidate shares in a 50:1 ratio, aiming to enhance share value and meet Euronext Growth Oslo’s minimum price requirements.
