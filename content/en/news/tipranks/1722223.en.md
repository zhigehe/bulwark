Ventia Services Group Limited ( [(AU:VNT)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:vnt) ) just unveiled an announcement.

Ventia Services Group Limited has appointed Amy Jackson as an additional Company Secretary, effective from February 10, 2025. Amy, who joined as Group General Counsel, brings significant expertise in corporate, commercial, and compliance areas. Jill Hardiman will continue in her role as Group Company Secretary. This appointment strengthens Ventia’s communication with the ASX and supports its operational and strategic objectives.

**More about Ventia Services Group Limited**

Ventia Services Group Limited is a prominent provider of essential infrastructure services in Australia and New Zealand, employing over 35,000 people across more than 400 sites. The company operates in various sectors, including defense, social infrastructure, water, electricity, gas, resources, telecommunications, and transport, with a focus on client-centered, innovative, and sustainable service delivery.
