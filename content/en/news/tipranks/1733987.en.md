Singularity Future Technology Ltd. has announced a delay in filing its Form 10-Q (Quarter Report) for the financial period ending December 31, 2024. The primary reason for this delay is the additional time needed to finalize the quarterly financial statements and complete their review by the company’s independent accounting firm. The company expects to file the report within the five calendar days following the prescribed due date. No significant changes in financial results are anticipated. Singularity Future Technology is committed to maintaining compliance, as indicated by the signature of CEO Jia Yang on the notification.
