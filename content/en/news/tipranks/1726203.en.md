Yatra Online Limited ( [(IN:YATRA)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/in:yatra) ) has issued an update.

Yatra Online Limited reported a robust performance for the third quarter of fiscal year 2024-25 with significant growth across key financial metrics. The company achieved a 113% year-on-year increase in revenue, while EBITDA grew by 207% and net profit soared by 845%. Key growth drivers included the Hotels and Packages business and contributions from the MICE segment. The acquisition of Globe All India Services Limited has begun to show early synergies, aiding operational efficiency and supplier consolidation. Additionally, Yatra onboarded a record 50 new corporate accounts, further cementing its leadership in the corporate travel sector.

**More about Yatra Online Limited**

Yatra Online Limited is India’s largest corporate travel services provider and one of the largest online travel companies among key online travel agency players.
