Schumann’s extensive experience in mining operations, capital markets, and uranium project development is expected to enhance the company’s capabilities as it embarks on a busy work program for 2025, focusing on North American projects.

**More about Okapi Resources Ltd.**

Global Uranium and Enrichment Limited operates in the mining industry, focusing on uranium resource development through project development and targeted mergers and acquisitions. The company aims to establish a globally significant high-grade uranium resource, primarily targeting the North American market.
