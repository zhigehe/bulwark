Netanel Group Ltd has announced a public issuance of their bonds, Series 15, which involves a change in the number and registration of security holders. The public offering, executed through the stock exchange clearing, has impacted Mizrahi Tefahot Company for Registrations Ltd as a registered holder. The issuance resulted in an increase of 200,000,000 securities, fully paid and considered in full, without involving rights to purchase shares.
