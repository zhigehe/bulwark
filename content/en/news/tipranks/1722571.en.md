**More about VinaCapital Vietnam Opportunity Fund Limited AccumUSD**

VinaCapital Vietnam Opportunity Fund Limited (VOF) is a closed-ended investment company incorporated in Guernsey, focusing on investment opportunities primarily in Vietnam. It is authorized by the Guernsey Financial Services Commission and operates under the regulatory framework for collective investment schemes.
