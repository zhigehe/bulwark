The latest update is out from AMTD IDEA Group Class A ( [(SG:HKB)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/sg:hkb) ).

AMTD IDEA Group, through its subsidiary WME, plans to expand the footprint of its fashion magazine L’Officiel by launching in new markets such as Australia, Canada, Mexico, and Taiwan in 2025. This expansion is part of a broader strategy to strengthen L’Officiel’s global presence and enhance its role in the luxury fashion industry, aiming to connect diverse regions through enriched content.

**More about AMTD IDEA Group Class A**

AMTD IDEA Group operates in the media and entertainment industry, focusing on digital and print media through its subsidiary, World Media and Entertainment Group (WME). The company engages in the global expansion of media assets, demonstrated by its ownership of the century-old fashion magazine L’Officiel.
