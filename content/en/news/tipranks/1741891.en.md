The latest update is out from Montepino Logistica SOCIMI SA ( [(FR:MLMTP)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/fr:mlmtp) ).

Montepino Logística SOCIMI SA has announced a distribution to its shareholders, which will be paid from the unrestricted voluntary reserves account, following the resolution from the General Shareholders’ Meeting. This distribution, amounting to a total gross dividend of €26,500,000, will be executed proportionally among shareholders, with payments facilitated by BANKINTER, S.A. The move reflects the company’s ongoing commitment to shareholder returns and may enhance its market positioning.
