**More about Roland Corporation**

Roland Corporation operates in the music industry, specializing in the development and manufacturing of electronic musical instruments, equipment, and software. The company is renowned for its innovation and focus on high-quality sound production technology, serving a global market of musicians, recording artists, and audio professionals.
