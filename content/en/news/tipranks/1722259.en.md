LGI LTD ( [(AU:LGI)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:lgi) ) has issued an announcement.

LGI Limited has announced the appointment of Ms. Priyamvada (Pia) Rasal as the new Company Secretary, effective immediately. This change aims to streamline communications between the company and the Australian Securities Exchange, potentially enhancing operational efficiency and stakeholder engagement.

**More about LGI LTD**

LGI is a leading company in the Australian market focused on the recovery of biogas from landfill sites, converting this biogas into renewable electricity, and producing environmental products. The company is engaged in engineering and managing landfill gas infrastructure to promote renewable electricity generation and carbon abatement, addressing key environmental challenges in waste disposal.
