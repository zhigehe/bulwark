Ai Holdings Corporation announced a revision to its dividend forecast for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2025, due to extraordinary income recorded by its wholly owned subsidiary, Iwatsu Electric Co., Ltd. The company plans to pay a year-end dividend of 55 yen per share, with 45 yen as a regular dividend and an additional 10 yen as a special dividend, reflecting the exceeded profit expectations.

**More about Ai Holdings**

Ai Holdings Corporation operates in the technology sector, primarily focusing on providing innovative solutions through its subsidiaries. One of its significant subsidiaries, Iwatsu Electric Co., Ltd., contributes to its market presence through its operations.
