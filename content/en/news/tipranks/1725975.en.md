Infomedia Ltd ( [(AU:IFM)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:ifm) ) has shared an update.

Infomedia Ltd is set to release its half-year financial results for the period ending December 31, 2024, on February 18, 2025. The announcement will be accompanied by a webinar hosted by CEO Jens Monsees and CFO Chantell Revie, aimed at analysts and investors, signaling transparency and engagement with stakeholders.

**More about Infomedia Ltd**

Infomedia Ltd is a global provider of Data as a Service (DaaS) and Software as a Service (SaaS) solutions, catering to the automotive industry. Their offerings support OEMs, NSCs, dealerships, and third-party partners in managing vehicle and customer lifecycles, enhancing customer journeys, improving dealer efficiencies, and boosting sales. Their solutions are utilized by over 250,000 professionals across 50 OEM brands in 186 countries.
