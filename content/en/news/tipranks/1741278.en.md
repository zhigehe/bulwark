DroneShield Limited ( [(AU:DRO)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:dro) ) has shared an update.

DroneShield Limited has announced the issuance of 9,455,000 unquoted equity securities under an employee incentive scheme, with the securities not intended for public trading on the ASX. This move is part of the company’s strategy to incentivize its workforce and align employee interests with corporate goals, potentially enhancing operational performance and improving its market position in the drone defense industry.

**More about DroneShield Limited**

DroneShield Limited operates in the defense and security industry, specializing in the development and production of advanced drone detection and mitigation solutions. The company focuses on providing innovative technologies to protect against drone threats in various sectors, including military, law enforcement, and commercial markets.
