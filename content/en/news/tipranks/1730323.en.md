The latest update is out from United Co RUSAL ( [(HK:0486)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hk:0486) ).

United Company RUSAL has entered into a new heat supply contract with Baikalenergo, an associate of En+, to secure heat energy for its operations until the end of 2025. This agreement is part of a series of continuing connected transactions aimed at ensuring stable and efficient energy supply for the company’s facilities, reflecting RUSAL’s ongoing efforts to optimize its operational costs and maintain its competitive position in the market.

**More about United Co RUSAL**

United Company RUSAL is an international public joint-stock company, incorporated under the laws of Jersey and continued in the Russian Federation. It operates primarily in the aluminum industry, focusing on the production and supply of aluminum and its related products. The company has a significant presence in the global aluminum market.
