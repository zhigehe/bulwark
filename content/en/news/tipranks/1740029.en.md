Flinders Mines Limited ( [(AU:RHK)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:rhk) ) just unveiled an update.

Flinders Mines Limited’s substantial holder, Fortescue Ltd, along with its associated entities, has increased its voting power in Red Hawk Mining Limited from 88.43% to 89.44% as a result of an off-market takeover bid and on-market purchases of shares. This acquisition, part of FMG Pilbara’s strategic consolidation efforts, strengthens Fortescue’s control over Red Hawk Mining, potentially influencing future corporate strategies and stakeholder interests.
