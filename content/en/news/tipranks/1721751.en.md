Queenco ( [(IL:QNCO)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:qnco) ) has shared an announcement.

Queenco has reported that Eran Dov Raif is no longer an interested party in the corporation following the sale of 3,325,685 regular shares. This change affects the company’s shareholder structure and could impact its market positioning, as Raif is no longer a holder with significant influence over corporate decisions.

**More about Queenco**

Queenco is a company with securities listed for trading on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange. It has undergone several name changes in the past, including Sagi Basket Assets (A.T.) Holdings (1993) Ltd. and Oki-Dok Investments Ltd., and is based in Bnei Brak, Israel.
