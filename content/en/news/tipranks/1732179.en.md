Pharmocann ( ($IL:PMCN.M) ) has shared an announcement.

Pharmocann Ltd. has announced a special meeting of its shareholders for February 5, 2025, to discuss a private placement of its securities to raise capital and an employee rights plan. The company has signed with investors for a private placement worth 20 million NIS in exchange for 40% of its shares, with the process to be completed by August 12, 2024. The meeting will also address the issuance of shares to employees as part of an employee rights plan, with options exercisable for 30 days and subject to a tax exemption for capital gains. These strategic initiatives aim to strengthen Pharmocann’s financial positioning and incentivize its workforce.
