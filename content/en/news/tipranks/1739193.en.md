DLSI SA ( [(FR:ALDLS)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/fr:aldls) ) has shared an update.

Thierry Doudot has reclassified his participation in DLSI by transferring 1,648,750 shares to his personal holding, Untitled 57, which now holds over 50% of the capital and voting rights. This move, approved by the Financial Markets Authority, is seen as a strategic reallocation within the same group, potentially impacting DLSI’s operational control and market position.

**More about DLSI SA**

The DLSI Group operates a network of over 70 employment agencies across France, Luxembourg, Germany, and Switzerland, offering comprehensive employment solutions including permanent, fixed-term, and temporary placements. The company is listed on the Euronext Growth market of Euronext Paris and reported a turnover of 204.5 million euros in 2023.
