Ansell ( [(AU:ANN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:ann) ) has shared an update.

Ansell Limited has announced a new dividend distribution, with a payment of USD 0.222 per share for its ordinary fully paid security. The dividend relates to a six-month period ending December 31, 2024, and will be paid on March 6, 2025, with an ex-dividend date of February 14, 2025. This announcement reflects Ansell’s ongoing commitment to providing value to its shareholders, potentially strengthening its financial positioning and reinforcing investor confidence.

**More about Ansell**

Ansell Limited operates in the manufacturing industry, specializing in protective solutions and safety gear, including gloves and protective clothing, primarily serving a global market.
