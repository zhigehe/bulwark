Ansell ( [(AU:ANN)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:ann) ) has issued an update.

Ansell Limited announced a change in the director’s interest, with Nigel David Garrard increasing his indirect shareholding by acquiring 1,000 ordinary shares through Kaben Nominees Pty Ltd as Trustee for The Garrard Family Trust. This acquisition, valued at $36,700, reflects confidence in the company’s performance and could influence investor sentiment positively.

**More about Ansell**

Ansell Limited operates in the protective solutions industry, providing a wide range of products including gloves and protective clothing. The company focuses on healthcare and industrial markets, aiming to enhance safety and protection standards globally.
