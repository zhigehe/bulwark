This collaboration is expected to enhance value creation for clients and expand Sidetrade’s market reach, while reshaping working capital management with transformative digital solutions.

**More about Sidetrade SA**

Sidetrade is a global leader in AI-powered Order-to-Cash applications, providing a SaaS platform that enhances cash flow processes using advanced [AI](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/artificial-intelligence) technology. The company operates in over 85 countries and serves a diverse range of industries with clients such as Bidcorp, Engie, and Veolia. Sidetrade’s AI platform processes $6.1 trillion in B2B transactions daily, offering insights and automation to optimize working capital and productivity.
