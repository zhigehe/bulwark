Treatment.com International ( [(TSE:TRUE)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:true) ) has shared an announcement.

Treatment.com [AI](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/artificial-intelligence) Inc. has announced a definitive agreement to acquire Rocket Doctor Inc., a digital health company that empowers doctors to establish virtual practices in the U.S. and Canada. This acquisition will enhance Treatment.com’s capabilities by integrating Rocket Doctor’s AI-powered platform, boosting the network to over 300 doctors and increasing services billable value to C$7.1 million. The deal, valued at approximately C$11 million in shares with potential additional consideration based on performance, aims to drive innovation in digital healthcare services across North America, offering a proof of concept for future commercial sales of Treatment’s software.

**More about Treatment.com International**

Treatment.com AI Inc. operates in the digital health industry, focusing on innovative healthcare solutions. The company develops AI-powered platforms to enhance medical practices and patient care, leveraging a Global Library of Medicine to support healthcare professionals.
