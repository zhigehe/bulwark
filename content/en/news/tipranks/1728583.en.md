An announcement from Resaas Services ( [(TSE:RSS)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:rss) ) is now available.

RESAAS Services Inc. and the San Francisco Association of REALTORS® have launched ‘SF Commercial,’ a new platform tailored for commercial real estate brokers in the San Francisco Bay Area. This subscription-based service, priced at $99 USD per month, aims to centralize commercial listings, improve visibility, and streamline 1031 exchange transactions, enhancing the efficiency and growth of broker operations within the industry.

**More about Resaas Services**

RESAAS Services Inc. is an award-winning technology company that serves the global residential and commercial real estate industry. It provides unique real estate data, international referrals, and data exchange solutions to over 600,000 residential real estate agents in 160 countries, as well as major commercial brokerages and institutions.
