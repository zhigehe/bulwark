**Recent Updates on Amazon.Com, Inc. stock**

Amazon.com, Inc. recently reported robust Q4 2024 results, with record revenue of $187.8 billion and operating income of $21.2 billion, showcasing significant growth in AWS and advertising segments. However, challenges such as foreign exchange headwinds and AWS capacity constraints were noted. Analysts have mixed views on Amazon’s stock, with Phillip Securities downgrading it due to valuation concerns, while others like Deutsche Bank and DA Davidson raised their price targets citing strong fundamentals and growth potential in [AI](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/artificial-intelligence) and cloud services. Despite some soft Q1 guidance attributed to FX issues, analysts remain optimistic about Amazon’s position in e-commerce, cloud, and advertising sectors.
