Alliance Aviation Services Limited announced a change in the indirect shareholding interests of their director, James Jackson. The director acquired 9,500 ordinary shares through an on-market trade, increasing his holdings in Mistover Pty Ltd, while maintaining the same number of shares in Federal Pacific Holdings Pty Ltd. This change reflects a strategic adjustment in the director’s investment portfolio, which could indicate confidence in the company’s future performance or a shift in personal investment strategy.
