An update from XPS Pensions Group Plc ( [(GB:XPS)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:xps) ) is now available.

XPS Pensions Group has appointed Deutsche Numis as a joint Corporate Broker to work alongside Canaccord Genuity. This appointment is expected to enhance XPS’s market presence and strengthen its brokerage capabilities, potentially leading to improved stakeholder engagement and operational efficiency.

**More about XPS Pensions Group Plc**

XPS Pensions Group is a leading UK consulting and administration business focusing on the pensions and insurance sectors. As a FTSE 250 company, XPS leverages advanced technology and analytics to serve over 1,400 pension schemes, providing administration services for over one million members and advisory services to schemes of all sizes. The company also offers support to insurance companies in the life and bulk annuities sector.
