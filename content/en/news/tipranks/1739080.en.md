Storage Drop ( [(IL:STRG)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/il:strg) ) has issued an announcement.

Storage Drop Storage Technologies Ltd. has announced the postponement of a shareholder meeting originally scheduled for February 16, 2025, to March 2, 2025. The delay is attributed to ongoing discussions with shareholders. The meeting will address the granting of exemption and indemnification letters to Ms. Einav Razon Cohen and the re-appointment of Dr. Tzvika Ben Porat as an external director, reflecting significant governance and leadership considerations pivotal to the company’s strategic direction.

**More about Storage Drop**

Storage Drop Storage Technologies Ltd. operates in the storage technology industry, focusing on providing innovative solutions for data storage. The company’s primary services include storage management and technology solutions aimed at optimizing data storage for businesses and individuals.
