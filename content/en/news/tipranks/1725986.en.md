The latest announcement is out from VGI Partners Global Investments Ltd. ( [(AU:VG1)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:vg1) ).

In January 2025, VG1 reported a strong portfolio return of 8.4% due to its diversified investment strategy across sectors and geographies. The company’s UK and European financials, as well as Asian [semiconductor](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/chips-stocks) stocks, contributed positively to this performance. Despite volatility in global equities, VG1 remains optimistic about global markets, supported by favorable economic conditions such as low [interest rates](https://www.tipranks.com/economic-indicators) and robust earnings growth. The company also announced an update to its dividend policy, increasing the target dividend yield, which could enhance shareholder returns.

**More about VGI Partners Global Investments Ltd.**

VGI Partners Global Investments Limited (VG1) is a listed investment company that provides investors with access to an actively-managed, concentrated portfolio of long and short positions in global listed securities. The company employs a fundamental, bottom-up investment approach and leverages the expertise of Regal Partners, its investment manager, which specializes in alternative investments with a wide range of strategies including long/short equities and private markets.
