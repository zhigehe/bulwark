Treasure Factory Co., Ltd. ( [(JP:3093)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/jp:3093) ) has provided an update.

Treasure Factory Co., Ltd. reported a notable increase in net sales for January 2025, with existing stores achieving 110.6% and all stores reaching 121.3% compared to the previous year. This growth is attributed to strong sales of winter apparel, home appliances, and various other categories like brand items and sports goods, driven by colder temperatures. The company also opened a new Treasure Factory Style store in Gotokuji and closed a general store in Narashino, reflecting ongoing adjustments in its store portfolio.
