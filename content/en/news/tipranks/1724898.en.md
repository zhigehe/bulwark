Argo Group Limited has announced the successful passage of all resolutions at its Annual General Meeting, including the decision to cancel the trading of its Ordinary Shares on the AIM market. This move, supported by over 75% of shareholder votes, will be finalized on February 19, 2025, and includes a Tender Offer detailed in the company’s circular, impacting shareholder engagement and the company’s public market presence.
