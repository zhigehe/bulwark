Ind-Swift Ltd. ( [(IN:INDSWFTLTD)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/in:indswftltd) ) has provided an announcement.

Ind-Swift Limited has adopted a new Code of Fair Disclosure, Internal Procedures and Conduct for regulating, monitoring, and reporting of trading by designated persons and their immediate relatives, as well as a Legitimate Purpose Policy. This move aims to ensure compliance with the Securities and Exchange Board of India’s (SEBI) Prohibition of Insider Trading Regulations, 2015, enhancing transparency and governance standards within the company.
