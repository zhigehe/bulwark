Conquest Resources ( [(TSE:CQR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:cqr) ) has issued an update.

Conquest Resources Limited has successfully completed the first phase of its drilling program at the Belfast-TeckMag Project in southern Ontario, in partnership with VerAI Discoveries Inc. This initiative has identified a potential Volcanogenic Massive Sulfide (VMS) system at the Eaglerock target, indicating significant copper, zinc, and gold mineralization. The collaboration with VerAI has enabled Conquest to uncover potential mineralized systems in three out of four AI-generated targets, opening new areas for future discoveries and strengthening their exploration capabilities.

**More about Conquest Resources**

Conquest Resources Limited is a mining exploration company based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The company focuses on identifying and developing mineral resources, with a particular emphasis on copper, zinc, and gold-bearing systems. It operates projects in southern Ontario, partnering with technology firms to leverage advanced [AI](https://www.tipranks.com/compare-stocks/artificial-intelligence) for exploration.
