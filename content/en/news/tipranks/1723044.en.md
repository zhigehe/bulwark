This financial update underscores the company’s strong market positioning and operational growth, potentially impacting investor confidence and stakeholder interests positively.

**More about Pou Sheng International (Holdings)**

Pou Sheng International (Holdings) Limited is a subsidiary of Yue Yuen Industrial (Holdings) Limited, which is part of Pou Chen Corporation. The company operates within the sporting goods industry, focusing on the distribution and retail of sportswear and footwear, and is listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.
