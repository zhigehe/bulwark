Aptitude Software Group plc ( [(GB:APTD)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:aptd) ) just unveiled an announcement.

Aptitude Software Group plc announced a share buyback transaction, purchasing 18,000 ordinary shares at a price of 330 pence each to be held in treasury as part of its ongoing Share Buyback Programme. This move reduces the total number of shares in issue to 55,912,298, impacting shareholder calculations under the Financial Conduct Authority’s rules and potentially signaling confidence in the company’s financial stability.

**More about Aptitude Software Group plc**

Aptitude Software Group plc is a market-leading provider of finance transformation software solutions. It specializes in delivering fully autonomous finance solutions to enable growth, efficiency, and sustainability for its clients. Its platform, Fynapse, is designed to enhance productivity and reduce costs for finance teams by offering a comprehensive view of finance and business data, performance automation, and user-friendly features.
