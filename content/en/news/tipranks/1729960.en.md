Critical Resources Ltd ( [(AU:CRR)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/au:crr) ) just unveiled an update.

Critical Resources Limited has announced promising petrographic results from its Mayview Prospect in New South Wales, confirming the presence of significant quartz-sulphide veining and antimony mineralization. These findings, coupled with high-grade assay results, highlight the potential of Mayview as a significant Hillgrove-style orogenic Sb-Au system, which could enhance the company’s exploration and development prospects in the region.

**More about Critical Resources Ltd**

Critical Resources Limited is a company in the critical minerals exploration and project development industry. It focuses on the exploration of critical minerals, with a particular emphasis on antimony and gold mineralization.
