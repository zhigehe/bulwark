In the fourth quarter of 2024, Nordic Mining ASA reported significant progress on its Engebø project, achieving key milestones with the first ore and mineral concentrate production. Despite facing unforeseen challenges during the production ramp-up, the company has implemented temporary solutions to minimize impact on plant throughput, though revenue forecasts have been shifted by a quarter. Additionally, Nordic Mining completed its transition to trading on Euronext Oslo Børs, indicating strong financial positioning with sufficient funding until reaching positive cash flow.

**More about Nordic Mining ASA**

Nordic Mining ASA is a resource company specializing in high-end industrial minerals and metals, with significant assets located in the Nordic region. The company is involved in a large-scale construction project at Engebø in Norway, focusing on the extraction of a substantial eclogite deposit containing rutile and garnet. Nordic Mining also has interests in other ventures such as new technology for alumina production and high purity quartz exploration. The company is publicly traded on Euronext Oslo Børs under the ticker symbol ‘NOM’.
