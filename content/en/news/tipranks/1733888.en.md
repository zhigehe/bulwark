BH Macro Shs GBP ( [(GB:BHMG)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/gb:bhmg) ) has provided an announcement.

On February 12, 2025, BH Macro Limited announced the acquisition of 250,000 of its ordinary shares from J.P. Morgan Securities plc on the London Stock Exchange, at a weighted average price of 389.8047 pence per share. These shares will be held in treasury, and following this transaction, the company reports a total of 522,760,103 voting rights. This move reflects BH Macro’s strategic operation to manage its share capital and potentially enhance shareholder value.

**More about BH Macro Shs GBP**

BH Macro Limited is a closed-ended collective investment scheme established in Guernsey, focusing on investment opportunities on the London Stock Exchange. The company deals primarily in ordinary shares denominated in Sterling and US Dollars.
