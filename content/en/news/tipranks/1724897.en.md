An announcement from Endur ASA ( [(DE:4ZT0)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/de:4zt0) ) is now available.

Endúr ASA has announced a significant underwritten equity issue to fund acquisitions of several companies, including Total Betong AS and others, which aim to enhance its market presence in marine infrastructure construction and maintenance. The company plans a private placement of new shares to raise up to NOK 350 million, with pre-commitments from Kverva Finans AS, to support these acquisitions and meet general corporate expenses, which could impact shareholder interests and the company’s strategic positioning.

**More about Endur ASA**

Endúr ASA (OSE: ENDUR) is a leading supplier of construction and maintenance projects and services for marine infrastructure, which includes facilities for land-based aquaculture, quays, harbours, dams, bridges, and other specialized projects.
