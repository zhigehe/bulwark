The latest update is out from Hon Hai Precision Industry ( [(HNHPF)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hnhpf) ).

In a recent interaction with the media, Hon Hai Precision Industry Co., Ltd. addressed reports regarding its financial expectations for 2025. Chairman Young Liu was mentioned in Economic Daily as stating that the company’s revenue for the year would exceed NT$7 trillion. However, Hon Hai clarified that it has not released any official estimates on its financial and business information, reiterating that such disclosures will be made through the official market observation post system.

**More about Hon Hai Precision Industry**

Hon Hai Precision Industry Co., Ltd., also known as Foxconn, is a Taiwanese multinational electronics contract manufacturer. It is the world’s largest provider of electronics manufacturing services and the third-largest information technology company by revenue. The company is primarily involved in manufacturing and assembling a wide range of products for major international brands, including consumer electronics, telecommunications, and computer hardware.
