An announcement from Optiva Inc ( [(TSE:OPT)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/tse:opt) ) is now available.

Optiva Inc. has launched a new Agentic AI-powered Business Support System (BSS) for the telecom industry, using Google’s Gemini models. This integration aims to enhance operational efficiency, reduce costs, and improve customer experiences for communication service providers globally. The technology enables CSPs to automate intelligent processes, personalize customer engagement, and expedite market solutions, positioning Optiva as a forward-thinking leader in telecom transformations.

**More about Optiva Inc**

Optiva Inc. is a leading provider of mission-critical, cloud-native revenue management software for the telecommunications industry. Its products are delivered globally on private and public clouds, helping service providers maximize digital, 5G, IoT, and emerging market opportunities. Established in 1999, Optiva Inc. is listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange.
