Comal S.p.A. ( [(IT:CML)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/it:cml) ) just unveiled an announcement.

Comal S.p.A.’s Board of Directors has approved a communication regarding a voluntary total public purchase offer by Duferco Solar Projects S.r.l. for up to 97.86% of Comal’s share capital. The offer values each share at €4.80, deemed financially fair by the board and independent experts. This development may significantly impact Comal’s operations by potentially changing its ownership structure and market positioning.

**More about Comal S.p.A.**

Comal S.p.A. operates in the engineering sector, focusing on the production of energy from solar sources. The company, based in Montalto di Castro, specializes in constructing high-power photovoltaic plants using EPCM and O&M formulas. Since its founding in 2001, Comal has engaged in the design, construction, and maintenance of photovoltaic plants, as well as maintenance of conventional energy production plants. Comal is also recognized for pioneering photovoltaic plants in grid parity.
