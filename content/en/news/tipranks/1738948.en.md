Investment bank H.C. Wainwright [lifted its price target](https://www.tipranks.com/news/the-fly/coinbase-price-target-raised-to-330-from-255-at-h-c-wainwright) on COIN stock to $330 a share from $255 and reiterated a Buy rating on the shares. Analysts at H.C. Wainwright expect Coinbase to easily beat its fourth-quarter 2024 earnings and revenue targets due to “strong tailwinds” for [crypto](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency) trading.

Citigroup [(C)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/c) is also [bullish on Coinbase Global](https://www.tipranks.com/news/ratings/positive-outlook-for-coinbase-global-buy-rating-backed-by-strong-q4-expectations-and-crypto-trends) heading into the company’s Q4 print. Analysts at Citigroup say that Coinbase, which is the largest U.S. cryptocurrency exchange, is well-positioned to capitalize on investor enthusiasm for digital assets, and as crypto transitions to a new era under the friendly administration of U.S. President Donald Trump.

Citigroup raised its price target on COIN stock to $350 from $275 and also maintained a Buy rating on the shares.

**Big Growth Ahead?**
---------------------

Coinbase Global’s financial results come a day after rival exchange Robinhood Markets [(HOOD)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/hood) reported [blowout Q4 2024 earnings](https://www.tipranks.com/news/robinhood-markets-green-surge-bold-moves-and-big-earnings), driven by 700% growth in its crypto trading revenues. The consensus forecast on Wall Street is for Coinbase Global to report earnings of $2.11 a share, up 103% from $1.04 a year earlier. Revenue in the quarter is expected to grow 93% year-over-year to $1.84 billion.

Trading in cryptocurrencies such as [Bitcoin](https://www.tipranks.com/cryptocurrency/btc-usd) [(BTC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/btc) has been frenetic since [Trump](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/djt) won re-election last November, promising to usher in pro-crypto legislation. However, COIN stock has declined about 15% since hitting a 52-week high in mid-December of last year. [The stock is up 7%](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/coin) today (Feb. 13), hours before the company is due to report its quarterly results.

**Is COIN Stock a Buy?**
------------------------

The stock of Coinbase Global has a consensus Moderate Buy rating among 16 Wall Street analysts. That rating is based on six Buy and 10 Hold recommendations issued in the last three months. The [average COIN price target](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/coin/forecast) of $317.64 implies 8.46% upside from current levels.
