Embelton Limited announced a 23% increase in net profit attributable to members, despite a 25.4% decline in revenue for the half-year ending December 31, 2024. The company maintained its interim dividend of 15.0 cents per share, fully franked, indicating stability in shareholder returns despite revenue challenges.

**More about Embelton Limited**

Embelton Limited operates within the industrial sector, focusing on the production and distribution of flooring and acoustic products. The company serves various market segments with a strong emphasis on quality and innovation in its product offerings.
