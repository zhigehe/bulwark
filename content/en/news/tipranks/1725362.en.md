STAR7 reported a 15% organic revenue growth for 2024, reaching approximately €120 million. The company’s strategic acquisitions of CAAR and STI enhance its consolidation efforts, particularly strengthening its presence in the Defence and automotive sectors and expanding in Brazil. STAR7’s international strategy and focus on innovation are driving its global expansion and sustainable value creation, as reflected in its increased market share in high-margin foreign markets.

**More about Star7 S.p.A**

STAR7 S.p.A is a company that provides an integrated range of product-information services. Its offerings include product and process engineering support, creation and management of technical and marketing content, translation, printing, and virtual experience services. The company is focused on expanding its market reach, particularly in the USA and Brazil, by leveraging its expertise in Engineering and Product Knowledge Service Lines.
