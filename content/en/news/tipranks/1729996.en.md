Super Micro Computer, Inc. has announced a delay in filing its Form 10-Q (Quarter Report) for the financial period ending December 31, 2024. The delay is attributed to an ongoing internal review by a Special Committee, which was formed to evaluate internal controls following the resignation of the company’s previous accounting firm. The company expects to file the delayed reports, including the Form 10-Q (Quarter Report), by February 25, 2025, as granted by Nasdaq. Preliminary financial information for the quarter has been released, indicating potential significant changes in operational results. Super Micro Computer, Inc. is actively working to comply with Nasdaq’s listing rules, with the notification signed by CEO Charles Liang.
