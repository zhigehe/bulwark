Bastide le Confort Medical ( [(FR:BLC)](https://www.tipranks.com/stocks/fr:blc) ) just unveiled an update.

Bastide le Confort Medical reported an 8.4% organic revenue growth for the first half of the 2024-2025 fiscal year, driven by a 10.2% increase in home healthcare services and a return to growth in the homecare business. The company has confirmed its revenue target of at least €560 million and aims to maintain a recurring operating margin of 8.7% by focusing on higher value-added businesses and cost control. The company plans to reduce debt by disposing of non-core assets.

**More about Bastide le Confort Medical**

Bastide le Confort Medical operates in the healthcare industry, focusing on home healthcare services and medical equipment. The company’s primary products and services include respiratory care, nutrition-perfusion-stomatherapy services, and homecare solutions, with a strong market presence in France, England, Canada, and Italy.
