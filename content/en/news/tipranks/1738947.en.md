Golden Spike Resources Corp announced the final assay results from their 2024 diamond drilling program at the Gregory River VMS property in Newfoundland. The drilling, conducted at the Steep Brook, Lode 9, and Vein Zone targets, revealed significant copper and gold mineralization, which could enhance the company’s resource base and strengthen its position in the mining sector.

**More about Golden Spike Resources Corp**

Golden Spike Resources Corp is a company involved in the mining industry, focusing on the exploration and development of mineral properties, particularly volcanogenic massive sulphide (VMS) deposits.
