On February 6, 2025, S&P Global Ratings upgraded the long-term local and foreign currency debt ratings of Transportadora de Gas del Sur S.A. from ‘CCC’ to ‘B-‘. This upgrade reflects a revision of Argentina’s transfer and convertibility risk assessment, potentially enhancing the company’s financial standing and market perception.

**More about Transportadora De Gas Sa Ord B**

Transportadora de Gas del Sur S.A. is a company operating in the energy sector, primarily focused on the transportation of natural gas. The company serves the Argentine market, providing essential gas distribution services to various stakeholders.
