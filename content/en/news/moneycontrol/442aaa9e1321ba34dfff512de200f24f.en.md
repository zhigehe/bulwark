Craft Emerging Market Fund PCC - Elite Capital Fund bought 63,600 shares in Vertexplus Technologies for an average price of Rs 91.5 via a bulk deal on February 7.

The shares of Vertexplus Technologies closed around 3.8 percent lower at Rs 86.55

Navarthanmal Anil Kumar sold 2.5 lakh shares in Eco Hotels for an average price of Rs 20.43. As of December 31, Anil Kumar had a 1.16 percent stake in Eco Hotels.  The shares for Ecohotels closed nearly 5 percent higher at Rs 20.50.

Aegis Investment Fund sold 1.3 lakh shares for an average price of Rs 191.82 in Sumuka Agro Industries. The shares of Sumuka Agro Industries closed 11.7 percent lower at Rs 185.95.
