The sectoral index [Nifty Bank](https://www.moneycontrol.com/indian-indices/NIFTY-BANK-23.html) was down nearly 700 points or 1.37 percent on February 11 as bank stocks tumbled up to 3 percent amid a broader market downturn. Nifty Bank stood at 49,296.85, as seen as 2.32 pm. It even went below its 20-day moving average of 49,245.5.

All stocks were trading in the deep red, with shares of [Punjab National Bank](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/banks-public-sector/punjabnationalbank/PNB05) (PNB) being the top loser on the index. PNB shares fell over 3 percent to trade at Rs 95.33 apiece. The shares of Canara Bank, [Kotak Mahindra Bank](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/banks-private-sector/kotakmahindrabank/KMB) and IDFC First Bank followed, trading nearly 3 percent lower than their respective previous closing prices.

The shares of Federal Bank were trading 2.23 percent lower at Rs 182.40 apiece, while those of [Bank of Baroda](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/banks-public-sector/bankbaroda/BOB) were down 2.16 percent to trade at Rs 210.58 apiece. IndusInd Bank and AU Small Finance Bank shares were trading over 1 percent lower.

Heavyweight [HDFC Bank](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/banks-private-sector/hdfcbank/HDF01) also recorded strong losses, being down nearly 1.23 percent to trade at Rs 1,694.65 apiece. Shares of [State Bank of India](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/banks-public-sector/statebankindia/SBI) (SBI), [Axis Bank](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/banks-private-sector/axisbank/AB16) and ICICI Bank meanwhile were trading with losses less than 1 percent.

The Bank Nifty index has been in the red for the third consecutive session now. The Nifty PSU Bank index was down nearly 2.18 percent or 133.90 points, while the Nifty Private Bank index was down 1.49 percent or 370 points.

Union Bank and Punjab & Sind Bank (PSB) shares were down 4 percent each, being the top losers on the Nifty PSU Bank index. City Union Bank (CUB), [Bandhan Bank](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/banks-private-sector/bandhanbank/BB09) and RBL shares were the top losers on the Nifty Private Bank index, falling over 3 percent each.

Notably, Nifty Bank was the best performer among the sectoral indices despite being in deep red. This comes as broader markets recorded strong losses. Nifty Financial Services was the worst performers, being down nearly 3.65 percent.
