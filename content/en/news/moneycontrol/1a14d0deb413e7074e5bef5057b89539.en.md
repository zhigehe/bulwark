Ajax Engineering IPO allotment status is likely be finalized by the official registrar of the company Link Intime after the issue got fully subscribed on the last day of bidding on February 12, ending the three-day share sale with 6.45 times subscription.

The initial public offer of concrete equipment manufacturer [Ajax Engineering Ltd](https://www.moneycontrol.com/ipo/ajax-engineering-ltd-ael08-ipodetail) received bids for 9.12 crore shares against 1.41 crore shares on offer. The Bengaluru-headquartered company's Rs 1,269-crore initial share sale had a price band of Rs 599-629 per share.

The investors who subscribed to the issue can check their Ajax Engineering IPO allotment status on the official website of the registrar, Link Intime India Private Limited, using their application number or PAN details. The status can also be accessed on the BSE and NSE websites.

**Ajax Engineering IPO allotment date today: Steps to check share allotment status on Link Intime**

**Step 1:** Open the link to the official registrar of the issue on this URL: https://linkintime.co.in/initial\_offer/

**Step 2:** Select the company from the dropdown menu.

**Step 3:** You check your allotment status by filling in details like PAN, application number or DP client ID.

**Step 4:** Press the Submit button

**Ajax Engineering IPO allotment status check on NSE**

**Step 1:** Open the website of NSE by clicking on this direct link (https://www.nseindia.com/products/dynaContent/equities/ipos/ipo\_login.jsp).

**Step 2:** Register by signing up using your details and then Submit.

**Step 3:** Select 'Ajax Engineering'

**Step 4:** Investors can check the allotment status by filling in details like their IPO Application number.

**Step 5:** Press the Submit button

**Step 6:** The allotment status will be shown in the window.

**Ajax Engineering IPO allotment status check on direct link of BSE**

**Step 1:** Open the website of Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

**Step 2:** Click on 'Investors' option.

**Step 3:** On the 'Investor Services' dropdown, click on 'Status of Issue Application'.

**Step 4:** Click on 'Application Status Check'.

**Step 5:** Select Equity in the issue type.

**Step 6:** Fill in the required details including the 'Issue Name'.

**Step 7:** Enter PAN number and click on Search to view the status
