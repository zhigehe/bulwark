Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund bought 1.5 percent stake in Medi Assist Healthcare Services via a bulk deal on February 12.

The mutual fund completed the transaction in two tranches. It bought 5.89 lakh shares in the company for an average price of Rs 492.86. Then, bought another 5.1 lakh shares in Medi Assist Healthcare Services for an average price of Rs 492.99.

Medi Assist Healthcare Services shares closed 4.04 percent down at Rs 480 on the National Stock Exchange.

Bharat Value Fund bought 6.11 lakh shares in Prataap Snacks for an average price of Rs 1147.48.

Prataap Snacks shares closed 0.93 percent down at Rs 1,107 on the National Stock Exchange.

Promoter Apollo Pipes Limited bought 6 lakh shares in Kisan Mouldings for an average price of Rs 43.86. As of December 31, the promoter had a 53.57 percent stake in the company.

M7 Global Fund PCC - Cell Dewcap Fund bought 3.19 lakh shares in Ken Enterprises Limited for an average price of Rs 85.
