IRCTC Q3 results: Net profit jumps 14% to Rs 341 crore.
-------------------------------------------------------

 

Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) on February 11 reported a 13.7 percent rise in its consolidated net profit to Rs 341 crore for the quarter ended December 31, 2024. The state-run company had posted a net profit of Rs 300 crore in the year-ago period.
