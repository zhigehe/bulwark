Ajax Engineering IPO subscription continues in the primary market.
------------------------------------------------------------------

 

Ajax Engineering initial public offer (IPO) received 39 percent subscription on the second day of bidding on February 11.

The initial share sale of concrete equipment manufacturer Ajax Engineering Ltd got bids for 55.29 lakh shares against 1.41 crore shares on offer, as per NSE data at 2 PM.

The portion for Retail Individual Investors (RIIs) fetched 45 percent subscription while the category for non-institutional investors got subscribed 42 percent. The quota for Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) received 26 percent subscription.
