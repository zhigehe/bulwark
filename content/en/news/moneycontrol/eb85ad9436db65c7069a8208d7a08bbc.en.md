Quality Power IPO subscription began on Friday in the primary market after the company mobilised more than Rs 386 crore from anchor investors.

Quality Power IPO saw 23 percent subscription till 1:30 PM with the quota for Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) garnering 10 percent subscription while Retail Individual Investors (RIIs) part got subscribed 41 percent. The portion for non-institutional investors received 36 percent subscription.
