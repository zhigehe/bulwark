Zaggle share price dropped for the fourth straight session in February 11 trade after the company reported that its profit dipped 2.47 percent on a sequential basis in the December quarter for the current fiscal.

The shares of [Zaggle Prepaid Ocean Services Ltd](https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/it-servicesconsulting/zaggleprepaidoceanservices/ZPO) opened gap-down with a loss of 6.14 percent in Tuesday's trading session. Shortly thereafter, the stock plunged to hit the lower circuit limit of 10 percent to quote at Rs 381.65 per share on the NSE. This was the second consecutive session when the stock hit the lower circuit limit of the day.

The scrip has been declining for the last four straight trading sessions and is down nearly 25 perent.
