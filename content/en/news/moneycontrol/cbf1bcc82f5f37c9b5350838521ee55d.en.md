Small, midcap indices decline up to 2% amid negative global cues; Oil India, Paytm, Tilaknagar among key losers
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

The small and midcap segment declined in February 10 trade amid weak global cues with key stocks of Oil India, Paytm and Tilaknagar among the key losers in the pack. Fresh tariff concerns and rennetless FII selling continued to weigh on the market sentiment. Analyst believe that valuations in India continue to be on the higher side, particularly in the broader market.
