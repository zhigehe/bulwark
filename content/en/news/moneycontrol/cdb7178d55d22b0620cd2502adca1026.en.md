Ajax Engineering IPO sails through on final day of subscription on February 12.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

Ajax Engineering IPO sailed through on the final day of subscription on strong bidding from Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) and Non Institutional Investors (NIIs).

The Rs 1,269-crore initial public offering (IPO) of concrete equipment manufacturer [Ajax Engineering Ltd](https://www.moneycontrol.com/ipo/ajax-engineering-ltd-ael08-ipodetail) got subscribed 1.12 times on the third day of bidding on Wednesday till around 1 PM. The initial share sale received bids for 1.58 crore shares against 1.41 crore shares on offer.

The quota for non-institutional investors garnered 1.61 times subscription, while the quota for retail individual investors (RIIs) got subscribed 66 percent. The category for qualified institutional buyers (QIBs) received 1.54 times subscription. Earlier, Kedaara Capital-backed Ajax Engineering mobilised over Rs 379 crore from anchor investors.
