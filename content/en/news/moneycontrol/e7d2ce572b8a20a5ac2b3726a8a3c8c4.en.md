HDFC AMC, UTI AMC rise in early trade as Prudential Plc considers ICICI Prudential AMC IPO
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

HDFC Asset Management Company, UTI Asset Management Company among others rose in the early trade on Thursday as England-based insurance major Prudential Plc is considering a potential listing of ICICI Prudential AMC.

Prudential plc announced that it is evaluating a potential listing of ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Ltd involving the partial divestment of its shares. It is intended that following the completion of such a divestment, the net proceeds would be returned to shareholders, Prudential plc said in an announcement.

ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company Ltd recorded a net profit of Rs 1,815 crore in FY24 as compared to Rs 1,508 crore in FY23.
