Swiggy shares extended losses in February 10 trade after Q3 results announcement on February 5.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

Swiggy share price fell over 5 percent to hit the fresh low in February 10 trade. The stock has extended its decline to over 18 percent since it declared its quarter third earnings for the current fiscal.

The firm reported widening of consolidated loss to Rs 799.08 crore for the December quarter. The company had incurred a net loss of Rs 574.38 crore in the year-ago period.
