In this episode of the StrictlyVC Download, Nextdoor CEO Nirav Tolia joins Connie and Alex to talk about the challenges of running a public company and his decision to step back into leadership after over five years away from the role. He also discusses why now is the right time for a product overhaul and how he plans to turn the neighborhood social media app around.

He goes over his strategy to improve user engagement and why he’s betting on AI to moderate user-generated content and minimize hate speech. They also talk about what’s next for Nextdoor and whether an acquisition could be on the table.
