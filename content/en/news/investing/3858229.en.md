LONDON (Reuters) - Shares in oil major BP (NYSE:BP) rose as much as 7% to their highest since August in early trade on Monday after Reuters reported Elliott Management had built a stake in the company, citing a source. By 0808 GMT, the shares were up 6.6% to 461.55 pence per share, on track for their biggest daily gain since February 2023.
