[Siemens](https://www.business-standard.com/markets/siemens-ltd-share-price-514.html) India reported financials for the December quarter after a demerger of the company’s energy segment. For the combined entity, revenue was 9 per cent below estimates and net profit was 3 per cent above estimates. The demerger is on track to be completed this calendar year (CY25). Revenue and net profit grew 4 per cent and 22 per cent year-on-year (Y-o-Y), driven by the energy segment. The non-energy segments were impacted by a slowdown.
