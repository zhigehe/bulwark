Media companies saw little relief during the festive season, as the continued shift towards digital platforms and sluggish discretionary spending in key sectors due to economic uncertainty impacted the anticipated ad revenue boost in the October-December quarter (Q3) of 2024-25 (FY25).
 
This comes after media companies reported a decline in their traditional advertising revenue in Q2 (July-September) of FY25, with hopes that the festive season would help revive their advertising revenues.
