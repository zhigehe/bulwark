A government mandate to blend a certain level of sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) with aviation turbine fuel (ATF) might not work in isolation, and a combination of policies is needed in India to incentivise SAF production and consumption, Brian Moran, chief sustainability officer at The Boeing Company, told *Business Standard* on Thursday.
 
In November 2023, the National Biofuels Coordination Committee (NBCC), led by Union Petroleum Minister Hardeep Singh Puri, established “indicative” targets for blending SAF in ATF.
