SAN DIEGO ([KSWB/KUSI](https://fox5sandiego.com/news/local-news/military-plane-crashes-near-shelter-island/)) — Emergency crews are responding to reports of a military plane that crashed into the San Diego Bay near Shelter Island, according to the San Diego Fire-Rescue Department Wednesday.

According to SDFD, the incident happened around 10:16 a.m. PT, close to the Kona Kai San Diego Resort and other neighboring businesses.

Military personnel have confirmed only two pilots were on the plane at the time. Authorities say both pilots on the plane have been rescued.

Nexstar’s KSWB/KUSI has a crew en route to the scene to gather additional information.
