### Mohawk Industries EPS Misses Expectations

Revenue was in line with analyst estimates. Earnings per share (EPS) missed analyst estimates by 4.3%.

The primary driver behind last 12 months revenue was the Global Ceramic segment contributing a total revenue of US$4.23b (39% of total revenue). Notably, cost of sales worth US$8.15b amounted to 75% of total revenue thereby underscoring the impact on earnings. The largest operating expense was General & Administrative costs, amounting to US$1.98b (91% of total expenses). *[Explore how MHK's revenue and expenses shape its earnings.](https://simplywall.st/stocks/us/consumer-durables/nyse-mhk/mohawk-industries/past)*

Looking ahead, revenue is forecast to grow 2.0% p.a. on average during the next 3 years, compared to a 5.6% growth forecast for the Consumer Durables industry in the US.
