Konecranes secured an order from Boluda Maritime Terminals Tenerife to supply four hybrid rubber-tired gantry cranes to the Port of Tenerife in the Canary Islands, Spain.

The Finnish crane manufacturer signed the contract with the Spanish towage and shipping company in December 2024, according to a Friday release.
