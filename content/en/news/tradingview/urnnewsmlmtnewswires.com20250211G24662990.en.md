Q Technology (Group) recorded a 10% year-over-year decline in the total sales volume of camera modules to around 35.9 million units in January, a Tuesday Hong Kong bourse filing said.

The sales volume of fingerprint recognition modules stood at roughly 16.7 million in the month, up 42% from a year prior, according to the camera modules technology company.
