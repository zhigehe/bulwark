Alfa Transformers has received a purchase order worth 12.5 million Indian rupees from TP Western Odisha Distribution in India, according to a Wednesday filing with the Indian stock exchanges.

The contract is for the design, manufacturing, and supply of 63KVA 11/0.433KV transformers to be executed in 67 days.

Shares of the company fell 5% in recent trade.
