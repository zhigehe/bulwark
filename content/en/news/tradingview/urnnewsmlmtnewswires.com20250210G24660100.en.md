Merck KGaA said Monday it is in discussions for the potential acquisition of US drug manufacturer SpringWorks Therapeutics, confirming earlier media reports.

The German science and technology company clarified that a legally binding deal is yet to be signed, noting that "critical conditions have yet to be met." It cautioned that there is no guarantee that a transaction will be carried out.
