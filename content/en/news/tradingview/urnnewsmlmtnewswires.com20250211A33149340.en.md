Energy Transfer reported Q4 earnings late Tuesday of $0.29 per diluted share, down from $0.37 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.37.

Revenue for the quarter that ended Dec. 31 was $19.5 million, down from $20.5 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $21.4 million.
