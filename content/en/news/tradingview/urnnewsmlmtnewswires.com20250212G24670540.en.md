MGM China Holdings' controlling shareholder, MGM Resorts International, recorded a total adjusted EBITDA of HK$2.13 billion for the three months ended Dec. 31, down from HK$2.19 billion a year prior, a Thursday bourse filing said.

Total revenue for the quarter rose to HK$7.92 billion from HK$7.68 billion in the year-ago period, according to the casino operator.
