\*\* Shares of Advance Metals climb 1.9% to A$0.053, and set for five consecutive sessions of gains, if current trend holds

\*\* Stock hits its highest point since mid-December 2023

\*\* The mineral explorer says it has secured firm commitments to raise A$1.52 million ($950,760) at A$0.044/share via placement for diamond drilling at the Myrtleford Project in Victoria

\*\* Says initial drilling at the project to test area around the previous high-yield site, aiming to improve understanding of the mineral distribution and structural controls

\*\* Over 6.2 mln shares traded vs 2.8x the 30 day avg

\*\* Stock last month gained 20.6%

($1 = 1.5987 Australian dollars)
