Lotte Chilsung Beverage swung to a net loss attributable to shareholders of 21.6 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024 from a net income attributable to shareholders of 37.4 billion won.

Sales rose 0.5% year over year to 923.3 billion won from 918.4 billion won, according to a Monday filing with DART.

Shares of Lotte Chilsung Beverage fell nearly 2% at market close on Tuesday.
