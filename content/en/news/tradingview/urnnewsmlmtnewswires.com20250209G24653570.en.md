Hong Lai Huat's wholly owned subsidiary, PH One Development, received a letter of demand for payment from Shanghai Baoye (SBC) for an outstanding payment claim of around $11.0 million.

The group has dispute the claims and asserting them as baseless, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Monday.

SBC was the group's main contractor for its mixed-use development project, D'Seaview in Cambodia.

The company has engaged its legal representatives to send a formal response to SBC, outlining the various failures and breaches of contract by the contractor that delayed the project.

Shares of the construction and development company were down over 5% in recent trading.
