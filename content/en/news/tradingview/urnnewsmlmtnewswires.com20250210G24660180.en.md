China's fixed-asset investment in railways rose 3.7% year over year to 43.9 billion yuan in January as major projects, including the Chengdu-Chongqing central line high-speed railway, were advanced, according to data from the China State Railway Group.

In 2024, fixed-asset investment in railways climbed 11% to 850.6 billion yuan.
