\*\* Swiss inspection, verification, testing and certification services company SGS is up 3.8% after FY results, with FY organic growth broadly as expected and progress made on the group's strategic priorities

\*\* Accelerated M&A, an organic 7.2% hike in North America sales, and improvements in incremental sales in sustainability services and digital trust show progress on the group's strategic priorities, Vontobel says

\*\* Vontobel also notes improvements in profitability amid cost savings and strong FCF generation

\*\* J.P. Morgan highlights a strong Q4 for the group's Connectivity & Products division and says the announcement of a scrip dividend should not come as a surprise to the market

\*\* SGS is second-highest on Swiss midcap market index (.SMIM)
