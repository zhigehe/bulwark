NORDEN

ANNOUNCEMENT NO. 22 – 10 FEBRUARY 2025

In connection with the announced share buy-back program in NORDEN, A/S Motortramp continuously sells shares pro rata and the market is to be informed accordingly – see the attached file and announcement no. 20/2025.

A/S Motortramp participates in NORDEN’s share buy-back program by selling shares pro rata in order to maintain the current ownership of just under 32% of the shares in NORDEN.

Kind regards,

Dampskibsselskabet NORDEN A/S
