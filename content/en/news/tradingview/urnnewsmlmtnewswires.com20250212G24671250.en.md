Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing or TSMC's board approved a capital injection of up to $10 billion into its unit, TSMC Global.

The investment will reduce the chip maker's foreign exchange hedging costs, according to a Wednesday Taiwan Exchange filing.

The capital will be injected in multiple installments, it said.
