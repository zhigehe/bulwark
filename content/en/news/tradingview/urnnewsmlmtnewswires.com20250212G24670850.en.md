Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance posted net income attributable to shareholders of 207.1 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, up 16.4% from 178.0 billion won a year earlier.

Sales jumped 23.1% year over year to 6.297 trillion won from 5.114 trillion won, according to a Wednesday Korea Exchange filing.

Shares of Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance fell nearly 4% in recent trade on Thursday.
