The Saudi Exchange set the fluctuation limits for Anaam International's (SASE:4061) planned reverse split of shares, according to a Thursday release.

The limits will be based on the Saudi Arabia-based food processing company's share price of 26.20 Saudi riyals, with outstanding orders canceled.

The group's shares will be suspended from trading between Thursday and Sunday to reflect the change.
