Monarch Casino & Resort reported Q4 earnings late Tuesday of $1.36 per diluted share, up from $0.93 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $1.10.

Revenue in the three months ended Dec. 31 rose to $134.5 million from $128.2 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $128.6 million.

The company maintained a dividend of $0.30 a share, payable March 15 to holders of record March 1.
