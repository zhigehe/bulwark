Korea Electric Power filed for the listing of $400 million worth of 4.75% senior unsecured bonds due 2028 on the Singapore bourse.

The bonds will be listed and quoted in the Bonds Market on Feb. 14, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Thursday.
