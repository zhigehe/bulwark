TasFoods said the recent avian influenza outbreak in Victoria has currently no direct impact on its operations and it has proactively increased biosecurity measures across all poultry sites, according to a Tuesday Australian bourse filing.

Avian influenza virus, otherwise known as bird flu has been recently detected at a layer egg farm in Victoria, the filing added.

Company shares were up 8% in recent trade.
