Gigasun signed a contract with a Chinese brewery to build a 4.2-megawatt solar power plant in the Shangdong province.

The Swedish solar energy company will invest 18 million kronor to install the rooftop facility and receive 42 million kronor by selling the electricity throughout the 20-year agreement, according to a Tuesday release.

Construction is expected to begin in the second quarter.
