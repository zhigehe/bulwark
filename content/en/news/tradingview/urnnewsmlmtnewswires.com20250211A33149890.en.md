IAC reported a Q4 loss late Tuesday of $2.39 per share, compared with an earnings of $3.70 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.24 .

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $989.3 million, down from $1.06 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $934.3 million.

Shares of the company were up over 3% in after-hours activity.
