Business investment in the United Kingdom declined by 3.2% quarter-on-quarter in the three months to December 2024, reversing a 1.9% growth in the previous period, preliminary estimates showed.

This marked the sharpest drop in business investment since the first quarter of 2021.

On an annual basis, business investment fell 0.7%, following a downwardly revised 4.4% increase in the prior quarter.

For the entire year, business investment rose by 0.8%.
