Fortum launched a two-year feasibility study to assess the construction of new pumped hydro storage plants in Sweden.

The study aims to review commercial, technological, environmental, and regulatory conditions in Lekstjärnen in Trängslet County, and in Bastvålen and Höljessjön in Värmland County, according to a Tuesday release. The study will also look at a potential increase in power for the energy company's existing plants.

Fortum noted that prospective future investment decisions will be made in due course.
