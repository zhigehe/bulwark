Taiwan Business Bank said it was investigated by the Prosecutors Office on Saturday and, according to same-day Taiwan bourse filing.

The reason for the probe was not disclosed but the bank said it will comply with the authorities' requirements.

The investigation has no material impact on its finances or business operations, Taiwan Business Bank said.
