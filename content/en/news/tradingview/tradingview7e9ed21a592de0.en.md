Monolithic Power Systems CEO, Hsing Michael, sold 11,964 shares of company stock on February 7, 2025, at a price of $700 per share, totaling $8,374,800. Following the transaction, Hsing Michael directly owns 842,665 shares and indirectly owns 145,865 shares through the M Hsing 04 Trust and ZH Family 2020 Trust.
