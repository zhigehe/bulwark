Ericsson (ERIC-A.ST, ERIC-B.ST, ) signed a framework agreement with Telecommunication Towers Co, or Tawal, for a fully integrated, vendor-neutral indoor 5G network in Saudi Arabia.

The Swedish telecom equipment maker will provide its Radio Dot System and network infrastructure to help boost capacity and coverage in high-traffic venues such as malls, airports and stadiums, according to a Thursday release.
