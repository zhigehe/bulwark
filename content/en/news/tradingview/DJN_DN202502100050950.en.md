By Denny Jacob

Monday.com shares rise 23% in premarket trading after revenue surged in its latest quarter and the company projected further top-line growth.

Shares were trading around $317.90. The stock is up 22% over the last year.

The work-management software company logged fourth-quarter revenue of $268 million, a 32% increase from a year earlier. This exceeded estimates of $261.4 million from analysts polled by FactSet.

Monday.com also forecast further top-line growth in its first-quarter and 2025.
