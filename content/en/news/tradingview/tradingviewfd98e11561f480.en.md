Kenneth Duda, CTO and SVP Software Engineering at Arista Networks, sold 160,000 shares of common stock on February 6, 2025, for a total sale amount of $18,497,048. The transactions were executed at weighted average prices ranging from $114.4642 to $117.068, with sales conducted both directly and indirectly through trusts and a foundation. Following these transactions, Duda directly owns 12,976 shares and indirectly controls 3,549,772 shares of Arista Networks' common stock through various trusts and a foundation.
