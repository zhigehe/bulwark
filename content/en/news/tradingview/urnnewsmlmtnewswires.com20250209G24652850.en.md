Funai Soken Holdings will record a 3.16 billion yen extraordinary gain in the fourth quarter of 2025 from the sale of its Osaka headquarters, according to a Friday filing with the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Meanwhile, its unit, Funai Consulting, expects a 2.16 billion yen extraordinary loss in the first quarter from the sale of its Gotanda office in Tokyo.

The consultancy firm is relocating its headquarters and will transfer ownership of its Osaka property in December. Funai Consulting is selling its Tokyo office to optimize resources, with the transfer set for May.
