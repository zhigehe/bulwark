\*\* Indian metal stocks drop 2.5%, with 13 of the 15 constituents trading in red

\*\* Metals index is the biggest pct loser among 13 major sectoral indexes; benchmark Nifty 50 down 0.43%

\*\* U.S. President Donald Trump on Sunday said he would impose 25% tariffs on U.S. steel and aluminium imports, adding to existing duties

\*\* JSW Steel and Adani Enterprises are top losers on metals index, down 2.8% and 1.2%

\*\* The index is down about 3% so far this year vs a near-1% loss in Nifty 50
