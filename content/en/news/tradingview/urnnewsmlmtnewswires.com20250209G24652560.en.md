Corem Property Group (CORE-A.ST, CORE-B.ST, CORE-D.ST) filed an application to list its 1 billion kronor senior unsecured green bonds on Nasdaq Stockholm.

The Swedish real estate company expects the bonds to start trading on the bourse's sustainable bond list on Wednesday, according to a Feb. 7 release.
