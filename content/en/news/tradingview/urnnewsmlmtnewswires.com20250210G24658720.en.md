Insource will establish a wholly owned subsidiary, Insource Research Institute Corp., on April 18, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The unit will focus on research, consulting, and industry-academia collaboration. Shigeo Fujimoto will serve as representative director, with Takayuki Funahashi and Yoshio Ohata as directors.
