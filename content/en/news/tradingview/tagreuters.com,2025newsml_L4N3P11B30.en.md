\*\* Shares of Mobileye rise 13.2% to $18.31 in early trading

\*\* Lyft to lauch Mobileye-powered robotaxi to its app "as soon as 2026" in Dallas, Texas

\*\* Shares of ride-hailing platform Lyft rise 5.3% to $14.93 in early trading

\*\* Marubeni, a Japanese conglomerate with experience managing fleets, will own and finance the Mobileye-equipped vehicles that will show up on Lyft app, CEO David Risher said.

\*\* In 2024, LYFT and MBLY shares fell 2.1% and 54.1%, respectively
