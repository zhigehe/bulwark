Industrial production in Sweden advanced by 9% year-on-year in December 2024, up from a 2.6% increase in the previous month.

This marked the second consecutive month of growth in industrial activity and the strongest since July 2021, as output accelerated for manufacturing (10.3% vs 3.2% in November).

At the same time, production declined at a softer pace for mining and quarrying (-9.1% vs -9.4%) and electricity, gas, steam and hot water plants (-0.3% vs -4.9%).

On a seasonally adjusted monthly basis, industrial output rose by 5.7% in December, following an upwardly revised 2.5% gain in the preceding period.
