The yen weakens against other G-10 and Asian currencies in early trade amid mild risk-on sentiment. There has been positive news out of Ukraine, with President Zelensky willing to negotiate a land exchange with Russia, says Matt Simpson, market analyst at FOREX.com and City Index, in an email. Also, U.S. Treasury Secretary Bessent is being dispatched to Ukraine to discuss energy and rare-earth deals, which could point to early signs of an end to the Russia-Ukraine war, the analyst adds. USD/JPY rises 0.3% to 152.90; AUD/JPY gains 0.3% to 96.25; EUR/JPY is up 0.2% at 158.36. (ronnie.harui@wsj.com)
