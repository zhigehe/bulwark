Bellway said Tuesday its housing revenue and completions rose year over year for the six months ended Jan. 31.

The homebuilder's housing revenue for the period was 1.42 billion pounds sterling, compared with 1.27 billion pounds a year ago.

Housing completions in the first fiscal half stood at 4,577 units at an average selling price of 310,600 pounds, compared with 4,092 units at an average selling price of 309,278 pounds in 2024.

For fiscal 2025 ending July 31, Bellway said it is on course to deliver volume output of at least 8,500 homes with an average selling price of 310,000 pounds, compared with 7,654 homes with an average selling price of 307,909 pounds in fiscal 2024.
