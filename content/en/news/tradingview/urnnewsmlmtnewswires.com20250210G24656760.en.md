Innolux reported consolidated revenue of NT$18.7 billion in January, up 13.1% versus a year earlier, according to a Monday Taiwan Exchange filing.

During the month, the company shipped 8 million units of large-sized products, down 12% versus December 2024.

Meanwhile, the shipments for small and medium-sized products rose 1.5% month on month to 24.9 million units, it noted.
