\*\* After Kering's better-than-feared Q4 earnings, analysts pin hope on Gucci's next to-be-appointed creative director given co's "ambitious" 2025 outlook

\*\* French luxury fashion group's crown jewel Gucci, which logged 24% y/y fall in Q4 organic sales, sacked its design chief last week within 2 years of appointing him

\*\* Bernstein says, "a heavyweight Gucci creative director soon is essential for guidance to materialize"

\*\* Expects announcement around Milan show on Feb. 25

\*\* Stifel says announcement "may reignite investor hopes in anticipation of better days for Gucci in 2026"

\*\* Expects visible impact on sales after several quarters

\*\* Says 2025 outlook "not easy" given further wholesale rationalisation and store closures amid delayed impact from new designer

\*\* Deutsche Bank acknowledges near-term excitement over new designer with expectations of sequential sales improvement in 2025; calls H2-weighted recovery ambitious
