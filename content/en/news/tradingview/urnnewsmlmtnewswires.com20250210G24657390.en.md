Arabian Co. for Agricultural and Industrial Investment set the price range for its initial public offering on the Saudi Exchange's Nomu Parallel market at between 46 Saudi riyals and 50 riyals per share.

The final offer price will be disclosed at the end of the bookbuilding period, which started Sunday and will end Thursday, according to a Monday release. All proceeds will go to the Saudi Arabian poultry company's selling shareholder, Arabian Agricultural Services.

SNB Capital was tapped as the lead manager, financial adviser, bookrunner, and underwriter for the IPO.
