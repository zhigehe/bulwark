Shandong Xiantan's sales revenue declined 22% year over year to 352.2 million yuan in January, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

Volume of chicken products sold plunged 21% to 38,600 tons during the month, the filing said.

The decline in sales is attributable to the Spring Festival, the filing said.
