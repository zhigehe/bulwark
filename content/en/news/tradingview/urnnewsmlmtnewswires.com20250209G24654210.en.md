Alchip Technologies purchased one batch of machinery and equipment from Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing for NT$859.1 million.

The deal was conducted between Jan. 24 and Feb. 7, according to a Friday Taiwan Exchange filing.

The purchase will support the company's production operations.
