Vitol currently holds 51,906,699 shares, or a 98.48% stake, in WPU - Waste Plastic Upcycling after concluding its four-week compulsory acquisition offer.

The energy company will proceed with the mandatory acquisition of the remaining shares in the plastic waste recycling company, with WPU to subsequently delist from Euronext Growth Oslo, according to a Tuesday release.

In December 2024, Vitol offered 15 Norwegian kroner apiece for 35,227,994 WPU shares it agreed to purchase.
