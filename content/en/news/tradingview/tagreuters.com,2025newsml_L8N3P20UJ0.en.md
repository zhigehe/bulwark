\*\* Shares in some European airlines move lower, with analysts pointing to Europe's largest travel operator TUI flagging a slowdown in bookings

\*\* "TUI reported this morning and pointed to a slowdown in summer bookings versus last quarter, which is adding fears over UK and German leisure demand," James Goodall from Redburn Atlantic says

\*\* Volodymyr Shkuropat from Trigon brokerage also points to TUI's weaker bookings, adding that the UK and Germany-exposed airlines are falling the most

\*\* TUI's stock slumps 9% at 1004 GMT

\*\* Wizz Air falls 5.6%, Germany's Lufthansa is down 4.5%, Easyjet slips 4.2% and British Airways-owner IAG is down 2.5%
