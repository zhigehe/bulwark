Sparx Group reported assets under management of 1.929 trillion yen at the end of January, down 6.8 billion yen or 0.4% from the previous month, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

By segment, Japan equity assets fell 1.3% to 1.359 trillion yen, while OneAsia increased 8.8% to 112.4 billion yen. Real assets remained unchanged at 281.2 billion yen, and private equity rose 1.1% to 175.8 billion yen.
