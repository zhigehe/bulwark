Several SentinelOne executives have recently sold shares of the company's Class A Common Stock to cover tax withholding obligations related to the vesting of Restricted Stock Units.

Conder Keenan Michael, the Chief Legal Officer & Secretary, sold 1,011 shares at a weighted average price of $23.9499 per share, totaling $24,213. Following this transaction, Michael directly owns 426,166 shares of SentinelOne.

Smith Ric, President of Product Technology and Operations, sold 2,341 shares at the same weighted average price, amounting to $56,066. Post-sale, Ric directly owns 529,118 shares of the company.

Tomer Weingarten, the President and CEO, sold 10,178 shares at a weighted average price of $23.9499 per share, for a total of $243,762. After this sale, Weingarten directly owns 949,504 shares of SentinelOne.
