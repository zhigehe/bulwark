Abu Dhabi Aviation (ADX:ADAVIATION) will temporarily cease trading on the Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange at 1 pm local time on Feb. 20 due to a board meeting.

The aviation services company's shares will resume trading after it discloses the meeting results, according to a Thursday release.
