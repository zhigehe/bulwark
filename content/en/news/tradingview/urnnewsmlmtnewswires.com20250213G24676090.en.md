Skanska (SKA-B.ST) said Thursday it signed a deal with US-based DXC Technology to manage its global IT infrastructure operations, effective July 1.

As part of the contract, the Swedish project development and construction company will restructure its IT organization and transition its global IT operations to DXC Technology, including both services and employees.

The agreement is aimed at improving Skanska's operational efficiency.
