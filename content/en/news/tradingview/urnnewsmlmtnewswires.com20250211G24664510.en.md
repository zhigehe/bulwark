Santos said its 2P (proved plus probable reserves) fell 6% to 1.56 billion barrels of oil equivalent (boe) at the end of 2024, from 1.66 billion boe in the same period a year earlier, according to a Wednesday filing with the Australian bourse.

2C contingent resources were 3.34 billion boe, compared with 3.33 billion boe a year earlier, the filing said.

Total 1P reserves fell to 917 million boe in 2024 from 998 million boe in 2023.

The company's shares were up 1% in recent Wednesday trade.
