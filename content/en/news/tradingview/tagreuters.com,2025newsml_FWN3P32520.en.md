Koninklijke BAM Groep NV :

* BAM DELIVERS ADJUSTED EBITDA OF EUR 333 MILLION OVER 2024
* FY ORDER BOOK INCREASED BY EUR 3.2 BILLION (+33%) TO EUR 13 BILLION
* FOR 2025, BAM EXPECTS TO DELIVER AN ADJUSTED EBITDA MARGIN AROUND 5%.

* FY REVENUE INCREASED BY 3% TO EUR 6.5 BILLION
* ANNOUNCEMENT OF EUR 50 MILLION SHARE BUYBACK
* FY NET PROFIT GROUP SHARE EUR 82.2 MILLION
