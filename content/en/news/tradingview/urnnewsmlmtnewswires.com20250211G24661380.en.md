The Hour Glass bought back 9,100 shares in the open market on Monday for SG$14,246 or SG$1.56 apiece, according to a same-day filing with the Singapore Exchange.

The luxury watch retail group has been authorized to buy back 64.8 million shares under its current mandate. To date, it has bought back 497,800 shares.
