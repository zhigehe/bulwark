Hercules Site Services finalized the sale of its Suction Excavator subsidiary to SNC Holdings (NW).

The UK-based labor supply company divested the unit for 2.4 million pounds sterling in cash, according to a Tuesday release, and expects the transaction to lower its debt and lease payments by 9 million pounds and increase free cash flow.

The deal was announced in January.
