Leah Tate, SVP - HR at Belden, sold 2,400 shares of Common Stock on February 7, 2025, at a price of $114.77 per share, totaling $275,448. Following the transaction, Tate directly owns 23,243 shares and indirectly owns 4,390 shares through a spouse and a 401(k) Plan. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on November 8, 2024.
