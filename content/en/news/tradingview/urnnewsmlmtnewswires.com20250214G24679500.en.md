Crowell Development logged an attributable profit of NT$1.30 billion in 2024, according to a Thursday disclosure to the Taiwan Stock Exchange.

Basic earnings per share for 2024 at the fashion retailer were NT$3.26.

Operating revenue for the year reached NT$5.33 billion, the filing said.
