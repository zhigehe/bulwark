Seazen Holdings logged notable declines in sales revenue and area in January.

Contracted sales revenue fell 72.5% year over year to 1.02 billion yuan, while sales area fell 77.4% to 115,562 square meters, according to a Thursday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

Shares of the property developer rose nearly 5% in recent trade.
