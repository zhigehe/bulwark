Willas-Array Electronics (Holdings) said its independent auditor Deloitte & Touche Singapore tendered its resignation, effective from Monday, a same-day bourse filing said.

The selection of a new auditor is subject to the incoming auditor's internal client acceptance procedures and prior approval by Hong Kong's Accounting and Financial Reporting Council.
