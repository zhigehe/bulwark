The trade surplus in the Netherlands slightly narrowed to EUR 10.29 billion in December 2024 from EUR 10.60 billion in the same of the previous year.

Exports rose 1.9% year-on-year to EUR 65.88 billion, while imports grew at a faster 2.9% to EUR 55.60 billion.

For the whole year, the trade surplus widened to EUR 143.99 billion from EUR 131.81 billion, as exports fell 0.8% and imports by 2.7%.
