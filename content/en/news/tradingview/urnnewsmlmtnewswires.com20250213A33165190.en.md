Telus reported Q4 adjusted earnings Thursday of 0.25 Canadian dollars ($0.17) per share, up from CA$0.24 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected CA$0.22.

Operating revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was CA$5.38 billion, up from CA$5.20 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected CA$5.24 billion.

Telus said it expects 2025 revenue to grow of 2% to 4%.

The company also said it has maintained a quarterly dividend of CA$0.4023 per share, payable April 1 to shareholders of record as of March 11.

Shares of the company were up nearly 3% in recent premarket activity.
