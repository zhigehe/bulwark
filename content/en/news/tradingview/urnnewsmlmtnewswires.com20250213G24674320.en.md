Sentia's SSEA secured a contract with Atrium Ljungberg (ATRLJ-B.ST) to deliver a building for Stockholm University of the Arts in Sweden.

The deal covers the construction of a building with a floor space of 36,000 square meters, slated to begin in 2026, with completion expected in 2030, according to a Thursday release.

Sentia is a subsidiary of Swedish private equity company Ratos (RATO-A.ST, RATO-B.ST).
