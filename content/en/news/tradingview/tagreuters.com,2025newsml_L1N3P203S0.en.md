\*\* Hong Kong shares of Bank of Qingdao jump 4.3% to HK$3.16, highest since March 2023

\*\* Stock on track for biggest one-day pct gain since October 18, 2024

\*\* Chinese bank posts 20.2% y/y rise in 2024 net profit; operating income up 8.2% y/y

\*\* Says its asset quality improved steadily with non-performing loan ratio at 1.14% at the end of 2024, representing a drop of 0.04 percentage point from a year ago

\*\* Bank's Shenzhen-listed stock rises 4.9% to 4.07 yuan; set for biggest one-day pct gain since September 30, 2024

\*\* Hong Kong stock jumped 50.5% last year; Shenzhen shares added 27.2%
