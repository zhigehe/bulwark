Hanatour Service posted net income attributable to shareholders of 40.9 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, up 265.9% from 11.2 billion won a year earlier.

The South Korean travel agency company's sales jumped 19% year over year to 142.2 billion won from 119.5 billion won, according to a Wednesday filing with DART.

Shares of Hanatour Service fell nearly 2% in recent trade on Thursday.
