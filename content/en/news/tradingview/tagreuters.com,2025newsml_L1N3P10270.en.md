\*\* Shares of Dongfeng Motor Group Co Ltd soared as much as 85.8% to HK$6, highest since July 2022, after the Chinese automaker flagged a possible change in the company's controlling shareholder structure amid a restructuring

\*\* Stock last up 21.1% at HK$3.92

\*\* Dongfeng Motor said on Sunday its controlling shareholder Dongfeng Motor Corp is planning a restructuring with another central state-owned enterprise group which may lead to a change in the controlling shareholder structure

\*\* The restructuring will "not result in a change to the actual controller" and is not expected to materially impact the company's normal production and operational activities - Co

\*\* Another key Chinese automaker Chongqing Changan Automobile Co Ltd also says its controlling shareholder is set to change following reorganisation of its indirect controlling shareholder

\*\* Stock of Chongqing Changan jumps as much as 8.6% to 14.7 yuan, highest since Dec. 12
