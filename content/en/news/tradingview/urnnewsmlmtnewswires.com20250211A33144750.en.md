NNN REIT reported Q4 adjusted funds from operations Tuesday of $0.82 per diluted share, unchanged from a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.82.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $218.5 million, up from $216.2 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $216.3 million.

For 2025, the company said it expects adjusted FFO of $3.39 to $3.44 per share.

Analysts polled by FactSet expect $3.40.
