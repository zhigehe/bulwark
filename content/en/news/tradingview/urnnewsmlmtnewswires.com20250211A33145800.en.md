James Hardie Industries' building products subsidiary said Tuesday it will supply siding and trim products for new houses built by M/I Homes under a new, three-year agreement.

The companies said Hardie siding and trim products will be a standard feature on all new construction by M/I Homes where hard siding is installed.

Financial terms were not disclosed

James Hardie shares were 1.1% lower in recent trading.
