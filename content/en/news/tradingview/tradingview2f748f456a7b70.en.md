Gordon Murdo, EVP of Global Commercial Ops at Amgen, sold 8,771 shares at an average price of $294.7947 per share, totaling $2,585,644. Following the transaction, Murdo directly owns 44,186 shares of Amgen. The sale price was an average, with transactions ranging from $294.6101 to $294.885. Murdo's ownership includes 773 Dividend Equivalents from the Amgen Inc. Equity Incentive Plan.
