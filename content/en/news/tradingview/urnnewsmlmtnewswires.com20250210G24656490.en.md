Shanghai Challenge Textile , a subsidiary of Shanghai Jialinjie Textile, plans to sell a 7% stake in Pakistan-listed Masood Textile Mills (PSX:MSOT) to CEO Shahid Nazir Ahmad or a designated party for $7.1 million, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

This transaction will result in a loss in the company's long-term equity investment, and is expected to have an impact on the company's 2025 net profit, the filing said.

Post-sale, Shanghai Challenge Textile will retain an 18.78% stake in Masood Textile Mills.
