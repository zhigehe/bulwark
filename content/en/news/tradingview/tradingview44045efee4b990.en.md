Rene Jacobsen, EVP & Chief Operating Officer of ABM Industries, sold 10,000 shares of common stock on February 3, 2025, under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $53.3778 and $52.6781, with the total sale amounting to $528,369. Following these transactions, Jacobsen directly owns 87,808 shares of ABM Industries.
