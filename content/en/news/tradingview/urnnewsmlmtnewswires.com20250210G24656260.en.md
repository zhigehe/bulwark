Matrix Concepts proposed a bonus issue of up to 625.7 million new shares, based on one bonus share for every two existing shares.

The company will list bonus shares on the Main Market of Bursa Malaysia Securities on Feb. 26 - the next market day after the entitlement date, according to a Friday Malaysian bourse filing.
