GuocoLand's indirect wholly owned subsidiary, GLL B, was awarded a land parcel at River Valley Green by the Urban Redevelopment Authority of Singapore for a bid price of SG$627.8 million.

The 99-year leasehold parcel has a gross plot ratio of 3.5 and a land area of 11,736 square meters, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Thursday.

The company plans to develop the parcel into a high-end waterfront development comprising two towers with commercial shops located at the first story.
