Commercial Bank International (ADX:CBI) shares temporarily paused trading on the Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange from 11 am local time on Thursday due to its annual general meeting.

The Dubai-based lender will resume trading after it publishes the meeting results, according to a same-day release.
