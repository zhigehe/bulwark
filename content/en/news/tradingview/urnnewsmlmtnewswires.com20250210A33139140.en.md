Inspire Medical Systems late Monday reported Q4 net income of $1.15 per diluted share, up from $0.49 a year ago.

Analysts polled by FactSet, on average, expected $0.72.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $239.7 million, compared with $192.5 million a year ago.

Analysts expected $236.8 million.

For 2025, the medical device company is projecting EPS of $2.10 to $2.20, while keeping its prior revenue forecast of $940 million to $955 million.

Analysts are looking for $1.98 and $949.6 million, respectively.

Shares were up 1% in after-hours session.
