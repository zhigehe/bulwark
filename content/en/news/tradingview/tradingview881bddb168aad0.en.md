Meredith J Ching, Exec. VP of External Affairs at Alexander & Baldwin, sold 700 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $17.9059, totaling $12,534. Following the transaction, Ching directly owns 152,449 shares and indirectly owns 994 shares through a spouse and TCESOP. The sales were conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan.
