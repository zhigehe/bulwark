Oversea-Chinese Banking Corp. or OCBC used another 2,500 treasury shares worth around SG$35,736 for its employee share schemes, a Monday Singapore bourse filing said.

Following the transaction, the number of treasury shares decreased to 19,096,606 shares, according to the lender.
