Service International reported Q4 adjusted diluted earnings Wednesday of $1.06 per share, compared to $0.93 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $1.05.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $1.09 billion, up from $1.06 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $1.08 billion.

The company said it expects full-year 2025 adjusted diluted earnings of $3.70 to $4.00 per share.

Analysts polled by FactSet expect $3.93.
