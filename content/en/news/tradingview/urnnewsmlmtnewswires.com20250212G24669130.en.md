The UK Competition and Markets Authority approved the proposed acquisition of research and consultancy services company Preqin by US-based investor BlackRock.

The approval is followed by the regulator's probe into the deal in December 2024 to assess whether the deal would impact competition for goods and services in the UK, according to a Wednesday release.
