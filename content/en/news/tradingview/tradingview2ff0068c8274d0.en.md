Robert Brian Mitchell, EVP, General Counsel, and CRO of Globe Life, sold 30,000 shares of common stock on February 7, 2025, for a total of $3,657,117. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $121.4818 and $122.1097 per share. Following these transactions, Mitchell directly owns 2,520 shares and indirectly owns 54,619 shares through various trusts and a 401(k) plan.
