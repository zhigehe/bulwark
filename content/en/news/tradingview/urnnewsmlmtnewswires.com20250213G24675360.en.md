Sinomax Group expects a minimum profit of HK$160 million in 2024, higher than HK$80.5 million logged for 2023, a Thursday bourse filing said.

The mattress manufacturing company attributed the expected increase to a rise in sales and cost-cutting measures of the company during the reporting period.
