\*\* Shares in some European defence companies slide on hopes of a peace deal between Ukraine and Russia after U.S. President Donald Trump held phone calls with leaders of both countries

\*\* Trump said on Wednesday that Russian President Vladimir Putin and Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskiy expressed a desire for peace in separate phone calls with him

\*\* A gauge of aerospace and defence stocks (.SXPARO) down 0.3%, as of 0814 GMT

\*\* Germany's Hensoldt falls about 5%, Rheinmetall and Renk down 2% and 1%, respectively

\*\* Italy's Leonardo slips 1.8%, while Sweden's Saab , France's Thales and Britain's BAE Systems down between 0.1% and 1%

\*\* French maker of Rafale fighter jets Dassault Aviation down 1%
