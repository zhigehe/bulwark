Kaiyuan Education Technology Group said 1,828,392 shares, or about 0.45% of total equity, held by its controlling shareholder Jiang Yong, were sold in a judicial auction for 6.5 million yuan due to a contract dispute, according to a Wednesday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

The transaction is pending final court approval and share transfer for completion.

If completed, the stake of Jiang and persons acting in concert in the company will drop to 11.84% from 12.29%without affecting control.
