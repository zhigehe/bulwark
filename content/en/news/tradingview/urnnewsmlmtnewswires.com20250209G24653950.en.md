Naver is taking part in LEAP 2025 to present AI solutions tailored to Saudi Arabia's culture and language, the internet giant said last week.

The LEAP 2025 is an event held from Feb. 9, Monday, to Feb. 12, Wednesday, in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

The South Korean company will highlight its AI value chain, covering sectors such as education, healthcare, media, and business productivity. Showcased services include AI tutors, automated medical transcription tools, media scene search AI, and HyperCLOVA X-powered collaboration tools, the release said.

Shares of Naver rose more than 2% in recent trade on Monday.
