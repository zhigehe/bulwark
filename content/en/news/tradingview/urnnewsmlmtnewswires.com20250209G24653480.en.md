New Zealand's total new lending went up to NZ$13.8 billion in December 2024 from NZ$13.6 billion in the prior month, data from the Reserve Bank showed Monday.

New lending fully secured by residential mortgage rose to NZ$8.87 billion from NZ$7.56 billion. Personal consumer new lending grew slightly to NZ$230 million from NZ$229 million.

Loans made for business purposes fell to NZ$3.37 billion from NZ$4.43 billion. Agriculture new lending increased to NZ$832 million from NZ$796 million. Other new lending dropped to NZ$497 million from NZ$582 million.
