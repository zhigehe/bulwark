Dynagreen Environmental Protection Group's waste input volume increased year-on-year by 7% to 14.4 million tons, while its accumulative electricity generation volume rose 8.9% to 5.15 billion kilowatt-hours, a Monday Hong Kong bourse filing said

The accumulated on-grid electricity volume reached 4.26 billion kilowatt-hours, up 9.4% from a year ago, with steam supply volume came in at 563,600 tons, 31% higher than last year.

For the fourth quarter of 2024, the company's waste input volume was at around 3.7 million tons.

Electricity generation volume for the reporting period was around 1.33 billion kilowatt-hours, while on-grid electricity volume was 1.10 billion kilowatt-hours.

The Chinese waste incinerator's steam supply volume for the December 2024 quarter was 191,700 tons.
