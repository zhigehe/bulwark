"Dining Out More Might Actually Save You Money. Here's How," at 4:15pm ET on Feb. 5, contained a number of errors about the price of the Dorsia app membership and the number of Fun Coupons that accompany premium memberships. Dorsia's premium membership comes with 500,000 Fun Coupons (valued at $5,000), not 5,000 as previously stated. Their Premium Plus membership costs $25,000 and comes with 2,500,000 Fun Coupons (valued at $25,000), not $10,000 and 10,000 Fun Coupons as previously stated. Also, the Dorsia app can currently be used in 318 restaurants worldwide, not 400 as previously stated.
