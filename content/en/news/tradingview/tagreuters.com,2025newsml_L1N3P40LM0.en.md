Russia's current account deficit in January 2025 stood at $0.7 billion, compared with a surplus of $2.9 billion in January 2024 and a deficit of $1.6 billion in December 2024, the country's central bank said on Thursday.

The central bank data showed that the foreign trade surplus fell to $4.5 billion, compared with $5.6 billion in the previous month.
