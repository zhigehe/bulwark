\*\* Shares of Graincorp rise as much as 2.9% to A$7.2, their biggest intraday pct gain since December 17, 2024

\*\* Co's shares track positive sentiment in Australian markets, with the S&P/ASX 200 having hit a record high earlier in the day

\*\* Shares hit one-year low in the previous session after co forecast lower-than-expected earnings for FY25

\*\* Macquarie lowers stock's price target to A$8.76 from A$9.85, accounting for lower FY25 earnings estimates

\*\* Six of eight analysts rate the stock "buy" or higher, two "hold"; their median PT is A$8.91 – according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Stock rose 21.7% in 2024
