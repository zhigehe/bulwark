\*\* Shares of trading platform Robinhood surge 14.4% to $63.97 in after-hours trading

\*\* Company reports a surge in fourth-quarter profit, fueled by frenetic activity in equity and crypto markets following Donald Trump's election victory

\*\* HOOD posts a profit of $916 million, or $1.01 per share, in the fourth quarter, up significantly from $30 million, or 3 cents per share, a year earlier.

\*\* Company booked a deferred tax benefit of $369 million, or 41 cents per share, primarily from the release of its valuation allowance on most of its net deferred tax assets - Robinhood

\*\* HOOD shares jumped 192.5% in 2024
