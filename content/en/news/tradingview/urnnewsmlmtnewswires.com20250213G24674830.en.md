Sam Woo Construction Group was recently awarded a construction contract for the foundation works of a project in Yuen Long South, a Thursday Hong Kong bourse filing said.

The contract sum for the project is expected to be at least HK$300 million, according to the construction engineering company.

The construction works is expected to be completed by the end of 2025.
