Philip Morris International's Vice President and Controller, Reginaldo Dobrowolski, sold a total of 8,000 shares of common stock on February 12, 2025. The transactions included sales at weighted average prices of $149.88 and $149.41, totaling $1,197,630. Following these transactions, Dobrowolski directly owns 20,799 shares and indirectly owns 7,596 shares through his spouse. The indirect ownership includes 2,326 shares held outright and 5,270 Restricted Share Units.
