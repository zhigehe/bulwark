\*\* Shares of Australian share registry provider Computershare rise 10% to their record high of A$39.57

\*\* CPU top gainer in the ASX200 benchmark index , which is down 0.2% as of 2330 GMT

\*\* CPU on Tuesday hiked annual EPS view to 135 Australian cents per share, up 15% from last year; the previous view was for a 7.5% growth

\*\* 1H25 net income nearly doubled to A$287.8 million ($181.08 million); it missed Visible Alpha consensus of A$311.2 million

\*\* Declares interim dividend of 45 Australian cents apiece, up 12.5% from last year

\*\* CPU stock up 14.1% this year, including the current session's moves

($1 = 1.5893 Australian dollars)
