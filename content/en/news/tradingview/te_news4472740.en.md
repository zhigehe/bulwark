Industrial production in Turkey climbed 7% year-on-year in December 2024 from 1.5% in the previous month.

This marked the strongest expansion in industrial activity since February, as output accelerated sharply for manufacturing (6.8% vs 1.2% in November), while it rebounded for mining and quarrying (1.8% vs -6.6%).

In addition, production edged higher for electricity, gas, and air-conditioning supply (11.4% vs 11.1%).

On a monthly basis, industrial production rose 5% in December 2024 from 2.9% gain in the previous month, the most since March 2023.
