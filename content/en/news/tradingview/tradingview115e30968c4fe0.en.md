Michael P Hartung, Chief Commercial Officer of FLEX LTD., sold a total of 64,807 Ordinary Shares on February 6, 2025, for a total sale amount of $2,844,537. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $43.7807 and $44.2329, with actual sales prices ranging from $43.17 to $44.39. Following these transactions, Hartung directly owns 233,526 Ordinary Shares of FLEX LTD.
