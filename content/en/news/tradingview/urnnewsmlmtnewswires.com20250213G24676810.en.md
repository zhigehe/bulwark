Abacus Storage King reported Friday that its six-month funds from operations per security rose to AU$0.0329 from the prior year's AU$0.0303.

Diluted earnings per stapled security during the period rose to AU$0.0516 from AU$0.0003 a year earlier, while underlying earnings per security fell to AU$0.0323 from AU$0.0361 a year earlier.

Total revenue and other income for the half year ended Dec. 31, 2024, was AU$160.4 million, up from AU$81.7 million in the same period a year earlier.

The real estate investment trust reaffirmed its 2025 distribution guidance of AU$0.062 per security.

The board declared an interim dividend of AU$ 0.031 per security, up from AU$0.03 in the previously reported period, payable on Feb. 28 to shareholders on record as of Dec. 31, 2024.

The real estate investment trust's shares were up almost 3% in recent Friday trade.
