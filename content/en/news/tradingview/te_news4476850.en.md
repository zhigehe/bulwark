In New York, the Dow Jones Index dropped 225 points or 0.50 percent on Wednesday.

Losses were led by Caterpillar (-2.95%), Home Depot (-2.33%) and Amazon (-1.68%).

Offsetting the fall, top gainers were Boeing (2.91%), Apple (1.75%) and Coca-Cola (1.64%).
