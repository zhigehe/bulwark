Thomson Medical Group's Malaysia-listed unit, TMC Life Sciences , appointed Ahmad Adzuan Abdul Rahman as group chief executive officer.

Rahman takes over the role from Melvin Heng Jun Li, who has been redesignated from acting group CEO to non-executive director, according to a Monday filing with the Singapore Exchange.

Before joining TMC Life, Rahman was a founding member and advisor of Novotec Group. Prior to that, he was a regional managing director and executive vice president at IDS Medical Systems.
