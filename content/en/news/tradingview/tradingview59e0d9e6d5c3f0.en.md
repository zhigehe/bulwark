Orr R Douglas, EVP & Chief Financial Officer of FirstCash Holdings, sold 6,000 shares of common stock on February 5, 2025, at prices ranging from $113.35 to $113.36, totaling $680,120. Following these transactions, Douglas directly owns 100,567 shares and indirectly owns 91,844 shares through a Spousal Trust and a Family Limited Partnership. The sales were part of a 10b5-1 Preset Diversification Program.
