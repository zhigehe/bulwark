Crane NXT reported Q4 adjusted earnings late Wednesday of $1.20, up from $0.99 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $1.20.

Net sales in the quarter ended Dec. 31 rose to $399.1 million from $356.9 million a year earlier. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $405.5 million.

In 2025, the company said it expects adjusted EPS of $4 to $4.30. Analysts in a FactSet poll expect $4.39.

The company increased its quarterly dividend by $0.01 to $0.17 a share, payable March 12 to shareholders of record as of Feb. 28.

Shares of Crane fell 4% in recent after-hours activity.
