Titan Cement International S.A said Thursday it partnered with Indian building material company Jaycee to enter the South Asian market.

The Belgium-based cement and building materials producer will hold a majority stake in Atlas EcoSolutions, the new joint venture formed under the partnership to enhance low-carbon building materials supply.

The partnership will help Titan Cement expand its presence by leveraging the Indian cost-effective supply chain.
