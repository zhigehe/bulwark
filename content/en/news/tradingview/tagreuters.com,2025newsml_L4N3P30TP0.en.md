Restaurant Brands on Wednesday topped market expectations for quarterly revenue, helped by strong demand for value meals at its Burger King chain and strength in its Tim Hortons coffee outlets.

The company reported fourth-quarter revenue of $2.30 billion, compared with analysts' average estimate of $2.28 billion, according to data compiled by LSEG.
