Contract research, development, and manufacturing organization Syngene International has appointed Peter Bains as chief executive officer - designate, with effect from Monday, according to a same-day disclosure to the Indian stock exchanges.

Bains takes over from Jonathan Hunt, who resigned from the position of managing director and chief executive officer, effective Monday, to pursue new opportunities.
