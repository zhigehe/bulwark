One of EDA Group's cornerstone investors, Lemkins Group (Asia), recently increased its shareholding in the company through the open market, a Tuesday Hong Kong bourse filing said.

As of Monday, Feb. 10, Lemkins Group (Asia) held a total of 13,586,000 shares in the supply chain services provider, representing 3.1% of its issued share capital.
