Yunnan Bowin Technology Industry on Monday recovered the full principal of 30 million yuan and earned an investment return of about 233,700 yuan from a trust product.

The product, Ping An Trust Gu Yi Lian 3M-36, had a three-month lock-in period and matured on Feb. 5, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shanghai bourse.
