Deneke J Heath, Chairman, President, and CEO of Summit Midstream Corp, sold 3,000 shares of Common Stock over three days, with transactions dated February 5th, 6th, and 7th, 2025. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices ranging from $41.83 to $43.99 per share, resulting in a total sale amount of $128,649. Following these transactions, Heath directly owns 255,326 shares of Summit Midstream Corp. All sales were made in compliance with a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan.
