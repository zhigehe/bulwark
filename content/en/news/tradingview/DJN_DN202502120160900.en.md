Shares of technology companies ticked down after a surprisingly heated inflation reading drove up Treasury yields.

During the first inflation spike earlier this decade, the tech sector often moved in lockstep with Treasurys. Portfolio managers often adjust exposures to high-risk tech sector based on movements in the lower risk Treasury market.

Micron Technology stock tumbled after the company's chief financial officer said there would be "no change or update" to the memory-chip maker's disappointing second-quarter outlook and signaled a deterioration in margins for the third quarter.

Chinese internet company Baidu is in talks with authorities in the United Arab Emirates to bring its driverless ride-hailing service to the Middle Eastern country, The Wall Street Journal reported.
