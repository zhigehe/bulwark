Beijing Hotgen Biotech recently obtained 35 overseas qualification certifications, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

These certifications cover a range of diagnostic reagents and equipment as well as biochemical tests mainly in the European Union, Indonesia and Cambodia.

Shares of the in vitro diagnostic device manufacturer closed 3% lower Tuesday.
