Waja (SASE:9560) on Wednesday launched a private placement of 10 million Saudi riyals worth of sukuk, with the offering set to run until Feb. 26.

The construction, media, and information technology company said the sukuk has a maturity period of 24 months. The terms will be provided in the offer document.

Manafea Capital serves as the financial adviser and sole arrangement manager for the issuance.
