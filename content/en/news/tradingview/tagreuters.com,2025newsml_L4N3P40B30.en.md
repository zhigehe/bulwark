\*\* Kotak Mahindra Bank gains ~2% to 1,980 rupees, highest since July 21, 2023

\*\* RBI lifts 10-month-old order barring the private lender from onboarding new clients digitally and issuing fresh credit cards

\*\* Ban imposed due to gaps in co's IT infrastructure

\*\* Two analysts raise PT, one upgrades stock after RBI action

\*\* Jefferies says lifting of ban can spur loan growth, improve asset quality

\*\* Raises loan growth estimate by 100 basis points; hikes PT to 2,330 rupees from 2,200 rupees

\*\* Macquarie says withdrawal of ban to improve growth and yields in unsecured loan segment, provide cushion to margins

\*\* KTKM has gained ~7.3% since the ban was imposed on April 24, 2024
