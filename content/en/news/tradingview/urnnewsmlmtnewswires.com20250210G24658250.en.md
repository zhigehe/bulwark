Glass and mirrors manufacturer Raoom Trading (SASE:9529) said Monday its board recommended a capital increase to 125 million Saudi riyals.

The plan will be carried out via a 1-for-1 bonus share issue and using 62.5 million riyals from retained earnings, subject to certain conditions including regulatory and shareholder approvals.
