Power cables manufacturer NKT joined the International Responsible Business Conduct Agreement, or IRBC Agreement, as part of its sustainability commitment.

The agreement, signed with the Social and Economic Council of the Netherlands, is part of the company's efforts to curb environmental and human rights risks in the value chain, according to a Monday release.
