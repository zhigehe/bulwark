Middle East Healthcare (SASE:4009), or Saudi German Health, secured its final regulatory clearance to double the bed capacity at its hospital in Dammam, Saudi Arabia.

The approval, obtained from Saudi's Ministry of Health, will allow increasing the number of beds in the hospital to 300 from 150, according to a Thursday release.

The hospital operator noted that the bigger capacity will increase revenue and streamline its operations.
