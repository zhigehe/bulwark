Theta Edge signed a conditional share subscription deal with Threadstone Capital, according to a Monday Malaysian bourse filing.

Under the deal, Threadstone Capital will subscribe to 35,390,270 new shares in Theta at an issue price of 1.30 ringgit apiece.

The company will apply for the listing and quotation of shares on the Main Market of Bursa Securities.
