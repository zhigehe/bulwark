South Korean food company CJ Cheiljedang swung to a net loss attributable to shareholders of 201.06 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024 from a net income attributable to shareholders of 112.5 billion won.

Sales increased 2.8% year over year to 7.490 trillion won from 7.289 trillion won, according to a Monday filing with DART.

Shares of CJ Cheiljedang fell more than 3% in recent trade on Tuesday.
