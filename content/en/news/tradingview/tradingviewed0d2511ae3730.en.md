MongoDB Director Dwight A. Merriman sold a total of 1,922 Class A Common Stock shares across transactions on February 7 and February 10, 2025, for a total sale amount of $538,938. The sales included shares sold at prices of $279.01, $282.25, and $281.62. Following these transactions, Merriman directly owns 1,112,006 shares and indirectly owns 605,626 shares through the Dwight A. Merriman Charitable Foundation and a trust for his children's benefit. The sales were executed under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan.
