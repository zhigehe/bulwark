Paras Defence and Space Technologies has agreed to acquire an 8% stake in Logic Fruit Technologies for 100 million Indian rupees, according to a Saturday filing with the Indian stock exchanges.

The investment will be made in the form of 1,800 compulsorily convertible preference shares at face value of 10 each.

Logic Fruit provides high-speed digital protocols, SDR IPs, encryption, and AI/ML integration.

The acquisition is aimed at providing technical support to Paras Defence's subsidiary, Paras AntiDrone Technologies, and helping it to enhance revenue generation.
