DBS Group's net profit for the second half of 2024 rose 12% to SG$5.65 billion from SG$5.03 billion a year earlier, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Monday.

Earnings per share rose to SG$3.92 from SG$3.49 in the comparable period. Analysts polled by Visible Alpha estimated earnings per share of SG$1.92.

Total income rose 10% year over year to SG$11.3 billion from SG$10.2 billion, driven by an increase in income from treasury customer sales and net fee and commission income.

Analysts polled by Visible Alpha estimated a revenue of SG$10.8 billion.

Shares of the financial group were up nearly 3% in recent trading.
