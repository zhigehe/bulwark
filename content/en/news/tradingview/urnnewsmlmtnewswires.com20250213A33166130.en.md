DTE Energy reported Q4 operating earnings Thursday of $1.51 per diluted share, down from $1.97 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $1.44.

For 2025, the company said it expects operating EPS in the range of $7.09 to $7.23.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $7.20.

The company's shares were down more than 1% in recent Thursday premarket activity.
