SpareBank 1 Østlandet (Issuer rating Aa3 with stable outlook by Moody's) hastoday issued a new NOK 500 million Senior Non-Preferred bond. Maturity date is 20 February 2030, the bond has a floating rate coupon of3months NIBOR + 90 bps, ISIN: NO0013481259. Settlement date is 20 February 2025.SEB acted as Lead Manager.

Hamar, February 13th 2025

Contact person SpareBank 1 Østlandet: Cathrine Mordal, Head of Treasury, +47 480 02 537

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
