Aker BioMarine will release the fourth quarter 2024 results on Wednesday 12February 2025 at 07:00 CET.

At 09:00 CET on the same day, the management will host a webcast to presenttheresults. Following a presentation, there will be a Q&A session where writtenquestions can be submitted to ir@akerbiomarine.com.

The webcasted presentation will be in English. To join, please use the linkthatwill be available at www.akerbiomarine.com/investor.

A replay of the presentation and Q&A session will be made available soon afterthe presentation at the same web page.

For further information, please contact Christopher Robin Vinter, VP Strategic Finance & IRMobile: +47 911 60 820Email: christopher.vinter@akerbiomarine.com
