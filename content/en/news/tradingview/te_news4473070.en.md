Industrial production in Bulgaria dropped by 3.9% year-on-year in December 2024, slipping from a downwardly revised 2.4% rise in the previous month.

Output tumbled both for manufacturing (-1.2% vs 3.2% in November) and electricity, gas, steam & air-conditioning supply (-28.8% vs 11.7%).

Meanwhile, production rebounded for mining and quarrying (8% vs -5%).

On a seasonally adjusted monthly basis, industrial output fell by 1.6% in December, following a revised 0.4% decline in the preceding period.
