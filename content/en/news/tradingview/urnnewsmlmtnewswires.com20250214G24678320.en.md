Swoop Holdings subsidiary Swoop Telecommunications extended the takeover offer period for Vonex to Feb. 21 from Feb. 14, according to a Friday filing with the Australian bourse.

As part of the takeover, the company intends to offer one fully paid-up Swoop Holdings share for every four Vonex shares at an implied price of AU$0.05 per Vonex share.

The offer remains unconditional, the filing said.

Swoop Holding's shares fell 5% on market close.
