Noel Gifts International reported a breach of certain order information on its online ordering platform on Feb. 6, according to a Wednesday filing with the Singapore Exchange.

The group has informed its customers about the breach and has advised them to take precautionary measures.

Meanwhile, the company has enlisted the services of an external consultant to assess the breach before advising on the next steps.
