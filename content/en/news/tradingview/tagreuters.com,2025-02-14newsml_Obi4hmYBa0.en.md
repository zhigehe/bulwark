North Energy ASA ("North Energy" or "Company") recorded a comprehensive incomeof NOK 9.6 million in the fourth quarter of 2024 compared to a comprehensiveloss of NOK 21.0 million in the same quarter last year. The income reportedthisquarter is mostly driven by increase in the value of financial investments andincome from associated companies. For the full year 2024 North Energy recordedacomprehensive income of NOK 58.1 million compared to a comprehensive incomelastyear of NOK 24.6 million.

At the end of the fourth quarter 2024, North Energy's Net Asset Value (NAV)wasNOK 603.3 million, representing a 2.0% increase from last quarter and,adjustedfor dividends paid, a 54.4% increase from the same quarter last year. In line with the Company's dividend policy, which states that "The Companyintends to distribute an annual dividend that approximates 3% of year end NetAsset Value", the Board intends to propose a dividend of NOK 0.15 per sharefor2024.

The report for the fourth quarter 2024 is attached and available on theCompany's website, www.northenergy.no.
