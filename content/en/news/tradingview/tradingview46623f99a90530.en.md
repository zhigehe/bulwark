Eric Carre, EVP & Chief Financial Officer of Halliburton Co, sold 141,206 shares of common stock on February 11, 2025, at a price of $26.13 per share, totaling $3,689,712. Following the transaction, Carre directly owns 127,101 shares of Halliburton Co. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on November 12, 2024.
