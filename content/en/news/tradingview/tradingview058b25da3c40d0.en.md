Anthony Digiandomenico, Co-Founder and 10% Owner of MDB Capital Holdings, LLC, has directly purchased an additional 2,000 shares of Common Stock. These transactions occurred on February 7 and February 11, 2025, with a weighted average purchase price of $6.22 per share, totaling $12,440. Following these transactions, Digiandomenico directly owns 93,042 shares of MDB Capital Holdings, LLC.
