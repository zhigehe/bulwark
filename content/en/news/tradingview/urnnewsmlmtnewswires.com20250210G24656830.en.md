Panther Securities finalized the divestment of its freehold interest in the Charles House, Premier House, and 78 Darlington Street properties in Wolverhampton in the UK.

The properties were sold for 2.5 million pounds sterling in cash to an undisclosed local developer, according to a Monday release. The British property investor will use proceeds from the sale for its future investments.
