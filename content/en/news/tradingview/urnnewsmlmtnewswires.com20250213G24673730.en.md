Baota Industry secured a tax relief of 47.5 million yuan, according to a Thursday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Subsidiary Northwest Bearing received government approval for a tax reduction that exempts the payment of 47.4 million yuan in land value-added tax and 110,000 yuan in stamp duty related to the transfer of premises and land.

Shares of the ball and roller bearings manufacturer closed 1% higher Thursday.
