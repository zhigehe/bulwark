Max C Wygod, serving as Exec Chairman, CEO & President and a 10% Owner of Forian, has purchased a total of 15,867 Common Stock shares across transactions on February 11th, 12th, and 13th, 2025, for a total amount of $42,249. The purchases were made at weighted average prices of $2.87, $2.66, $2.52, and $2.73, with prices varying within specified ranges for each transaction. Following these transactions, Wygod directly owns 1,328,047 shares of Forian.
