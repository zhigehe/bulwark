Manufacturing production in the UK increased by 0.7% month-over-month in December 2024, rebounding from a 0.3% drop in the previous month and defying market expectations of a 0.1% fall.

Among the sub-sectors, 7 out of 13 saw increases, with the largest positive contribution coming from the manufacture of basic pharmaceutical products (5.1%), machinery and equipment (5.9%) and other manufacturing and repair (2.3%).

Meanwhile, output declined for computer electronic and optical products (-3.6%), coke and refined petroleum products (-2.3%), and rubber & plastic products & non-metallic mineral products (-1.7%).

On a yearly basis, manufacturing output dropped by 1.4%, slipping further from a downwardly revised 1.1% but below market expectations of a 1.9% decrease.
