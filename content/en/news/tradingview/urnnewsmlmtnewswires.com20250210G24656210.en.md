Tiansheng Pharmaceutical Group received approval from China's medical products administrator for the consistency evaluation of its generic drug, ceftizoxime sodium for injection.

Ceftizoxime sodium is used to treat a wide range of bacterial infections, according to a Feb. 8 filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of the pharmaceutical company closed 5% higher Monday.
