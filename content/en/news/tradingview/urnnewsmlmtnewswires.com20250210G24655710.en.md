ANE (Cayman) expects to record a minimum of 830 million yuan in non-HKFRS profit for 2024, higher than 510 million yuan logged for 2023, a Friday bourse filing said.

The express freight network operator attributed the expected increase to a 16% year-on-year increase in its revenue and a 44% year-on-year increase in its gross profits for the reporting year.
