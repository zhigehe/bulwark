Scotiabank said it expected the central bank of the Philippines (BSP) to cut rates again on Thursday.

A 25bps reduction to 5.5% would make cumulative easing an even 100bps, noted the bank.

BSP is also contemplating a large reduction in the reserve requirement ratio for lenders by about the middle of this year, stated Scotiabank.
