Oberon Investments Group (OBE.AQ) said Thursday it raised 2.5 million pounds sterling via an oversubscribed share placing.

The boutique investment management group issued 55,555,554 shares at 0.045 pound apiece. Proceeds were earmarked for its unit expansions and working capital.

Admission of the shares on the Aquis Stock Exchange is expected on Feb 18.
