Poste Italiane (PST.MI) approved a letter of commitment to tender its Anima Holding (ANIM.MI) shares to Banco BPM's (BAMI.MI) Banco BPM Vita, subject to certain conditions.

The postal service provider seeks a higher offer price to align with current market prices, according to a Monday release. The commitment is also conditional upon acceptance by Banco BPM Vita, fulfillment of all legal requirements, and approval by Banco BPM shareholders.
