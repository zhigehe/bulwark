Aker Solutions secured a contract from Dragados Offshore to provide the steel substructure for a converter station for the BalWin1 offshore wind grid connection system in Germany.

The engineering company will execute the fabrication of the substructure for the 2-gigawatt high voltage direct current converter station in Verdal, Norway, according to a Monday release. The deal also includes an option for the BalWin 2 steel substructure.

Procurement and method engineering preparations will begin immediately, with construction planned for early 2026. Deliveries will commence in 2028 and 2029.

Aker Solutions noted that the contract is valued between 1.5 billion Norwegian kroner and 2.5 billion kroner.
