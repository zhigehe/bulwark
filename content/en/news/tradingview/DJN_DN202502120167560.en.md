South Korea's benchmark Kospi rises 0.5% to 2560.67 in early trade, as construction and auto stocks advance. Traders are digesting hotter-than-expected U.S. consumer inflation data for January. While foreign investors are net sellers, institutional and retail investors are net buyers. Excavator maker HD Hyundai Construction Equipment climbed 18%, leading a rally in local construction equipment stocks after U.S. President Trump said he and Russian President Putin would open talks to end the war in Ukraine. Hyundai Engineering & Construction rises 3.6%. Carmaker Hyundai Motor advances 3.2%. USD/KRW is flat at 1,453.05 in Seoul onshore trading. South Korea's 10-year government bond yield is up 3.0 bps at 2.879%.(kwanwoo.jun@wsj.com)
