\*\* Shares of Australian lithium miners tumble following a report that Chinese electric vehicle maker CATL has restarted production at a major lithium refinery in China

\*\* Jiangsu Lopal Tech and CATL are recommencing production at a lithium refinery in China after around a five-month pause, Bloomberg News reported

\*\* Major Australian lithium miners fall, with Liontown Resources down 7.6%, while IGO drops 4.1%

\*\* Pilbara Minerals and Mineral Resources drop 3.2% and 4.8%, respectively

\*\* CATL restarting production at the Chinese operation is set to further threaten any price recovery for the metal used to make batteries for electric vehicles
