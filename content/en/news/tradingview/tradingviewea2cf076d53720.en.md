MAGNITE has seen a flurry of stock transactions by its top executives and a director, as reported in recent SEC Form 4 filings.

David Day, the Chief Financial Officer, sold 52,245 shares across February 6 and 7, 2025, at prices ranging from $18.35 to $20.00 per share, totaling $1,023,045. Post-transaction, Day holds 488,506 shares.

Aaron Saltz, Chief Legal Officer, offloaded 17,947 shares on February 7, 2025, at $19.75 each, amassing $354,453, with his remaining stake at 330,630 shares.

Katie Seitz Evans, President of Operations, sold 12,520 shares on the same day at $20.00 per share, resulting in $250,400, and now directly owns 492,129 shares.

Sean Patrick Buckley, President of Revenue, also sold 18,693 shares on February 7, 2025, at $20.00 per share, totaling $373,860, leaving him with 408,529 shares.

James Rossman, a director, sold 149,936 shares on February 7, 2025, at $19.46 per share, for a total of $2,917,754, and now directly owns 219,920 shares.
