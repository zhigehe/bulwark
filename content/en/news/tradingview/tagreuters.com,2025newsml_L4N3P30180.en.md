\*\* Shares of AGL Energy rise 1.5% to A$11.89

\*\* Stock among top gainers in the benchmark index and hits its highest level since October 9

\*\* Co posts HY underlying profit of A$373 million, down 6.5% from a year earlier, but beats Visible Alpha consensus estimate of A$307.4 million

\*\* However, AGL narrows its fiscal 2025 underlying post-tax net profit outlook to a range of A$580 mln to A$710 mln, compared with its previous expectations of A$530 mln to A$730 mln

\*\* About 2.2 mln shares change hands, 1.5 times the 30-day average of shares

\*\* Stock up 3.7% this year, as of last close
