Morinaga & Co posted a 2.7% drop in profit attributable to owners of parent to 13.6 billion yen for the nine months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Earnings per share stood at 152.94 yen, compared with 152.33 yen a year ago.

Revenue rose 7.8% to 176.58 billion yen.

For the full fiscal year, Morinaga expects attributable profit to rise 10.2% to 16.7 billion yen, with revenue forecast to increase 6.4% to 227 billion yen. EPS is projected at 189.42 yen.

The company will pay a year-end dividend of 60 yen per share, up from 55 yen last year.

Shares of the company were down nearly 4% in morning trade Wednesday.
