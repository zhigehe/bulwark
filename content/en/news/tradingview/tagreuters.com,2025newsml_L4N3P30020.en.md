\*\* Shares of Australian insurer Suncorp rise 4.3% to a record high of A$21.22

\*\* SUN second-biggest gainer in the ASX200 benchmark index

\*\* Reports 1H25 cash earnings of A$860 million ($541.46 million), ahead of Visible Alpha consensus estimate of A$775.1 million

\*\* Announces capital return of A$3 per share from net proceeds of Suncorp Bank sale

\*\* Declares special dividend of 22 Australian cents per share and an interim dividend of 41 Australian cents apiece

\*\* SUN says "robust capital position with capacity for further capital management initiatives, most likely on-market buyback"

\*\* Expects GWP growth in mid- to high-single digits; FY24 growth was 13.9%

\*\* SUN up 12.2% this year, including day's move

($1 = 1.5883 Australian dollars)
