LJUBLJANA (Slovenia), February 14 (SeeNews) - Swiss food group Nestle said that its organic sales in Slovenia increased by 3.8% on the year in 2024.

"Last year we achieved organic growth thanks to very good results in the categories of coffee, food, pet food under the Purina brand, confectionery products and (food-service dedicated group) Nestle Professional," Laurentiu-Florin Dimitriu, Nestle general manager for Croatia, Slovenia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, said in a press release on Thursday.

Nestle's global sales totalled 91.4 billion Swiss francs (101 billion/97 billion euro) last year, down 1.8% compared to 2023. Its organic growth rate was 2.2%.

(1 euro = 0.946 Swiss francs)
