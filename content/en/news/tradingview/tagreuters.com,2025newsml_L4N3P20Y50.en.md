\*\* Shares of portable MRI maker Hyperfine fall 16.2% to $1.40 premarket

\*\* HYPR says it is raising ~$6 mln by selling ~4.5 mln shares and warrants

\*\* Combined offering price is $1.33/shr; representing a 20.4% discount to the stock's last close

\*\* Co says it intends to use the proceeds for working capital and general corporate purposes

\*\* Lake Street Capital Markets is acting as the exclusive placement agent for the offering

\*\* HYPR fell ~22% in 2024
