Empyrean Energy raised 625,000 pounds sterling through a placing to fund its share of additional costs for the Wilson River-1 well in Australia.

The oil and gas development company issued 500 million new shares at 0.00125 pound apiece and will use the proceeds for drilling, logging, and testing costs and general working capital, according to a Tuesday filing.
