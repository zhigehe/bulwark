Jiangsu Feymer Technology signed a strategic cooperation agreement with Yongzhuo Holdings to explore collaboration in water-soluble polymers, functional monomers, and membrane separation products, according to a Saturday filing with the Shanghai Bourse.

The agreement will take effect after Yongzhuo completes its 29.98% equity transfer in Feymer.

The deal does not immediately impact 2024 earnings, with future effects dependent on specific contracts.
