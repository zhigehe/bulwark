India's Bamboo Hotel and Global Centre (Delhi) has accepted bids worth 8.08 billion rupees ($93.1 million) for the reissue of 10.81% January 2028 bonds, three bankers said on Friday.

The company, which is a joint venture between Prestige Group and DB Realty Group, had invited bids from bankers and investors for the issue on Thursday, they said.

The companies did not reply to a Reuters email seeking comment.
