Saudi Arabian Oil's (SASE:2222), d/b/a Saudi Aramco's, Motiva Enterprises reportedly increased the processing capacity at its Port Arthur refinery in Texas, US, to up to 654,000 barrels of crude oil per day.

Bloomberg News reported Tuesday, citing unnamed sources, that the petroleum refiner eliminated bottlenecks in the facility's processes to improve capacity.

Saudi Aramco and Motiva did not immediately respond to requests for comment from MT Newswires.
