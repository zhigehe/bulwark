BUCHAREST (Romania), February 13 (SeeNews) - Romania's gross foreign debt rose to 203.6 billion euro ($212 billion) at the end of 2024, from 183.2 billion euro at the end of 2023, the central bank said on Thursday.

The end-2024 figure includes 154.4 billion euro in long-term foreign debt, up from 137 billion euro at the end of 2023, the central bank, BNR, said in a monthly balance of payments report.

Long-term external debt service ratio came in at 16.5% at the end of last year, down from 16.7% at the end of 2023.
