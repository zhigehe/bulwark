Cosmo Energy Holdings acquired 1.8 million shares for 11.66 billion yen through an off-auction purchase on the Tokyo Stock Exchange (ToSTNeT-3) on Feb. 7, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The buyback is part of a broader repurchase program approved on Feb. 6, allowing for up to 3 million shares, or 3.5% of outstanding stock, at a maximum cost of 18 billion yen.

The company plans additional market purchases until June 30.
