UNIPER SE :

* SUCCESSFULLY CLOSES THE 2024 FINANCIAL YEAR WITH AN ADJUSTED EBITDA OF OVER EUR 2.6 BILLION AND PROVIDES AN OUTLOOK FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2025
* OUTLOOK 2025: EXPECTS AN ADJUSTED EBITDA OF EUR 0.9 TO 1.3 BILLION AND AN ADJUSTED NET INCOME OF EUR 250-550 MILLION
* UNIPER EXPECTS AN ADJUSTED EBITDA OF EUR 2.612 BILLION FOR FINANCIAL YEAR 2024 WHICH IS WITHIN OUTLOOK OF EUR 2.5 TO 2.8 BILLION
* FY ADJUSTED NET INCOME COMES IN AT EUR 1.601 BILLION WHICH IS ALSO WITHIN OUTLOOK OF EUR 1.5 TO 1.8 BILLION
* EXPECTS 2025 ADJUSTED NET INCOME OF EUR 250-550 MILLION
* EXPECTS 2025 ADJUSTED EBITDA OF EUR 0.9-1.3 BILLION
