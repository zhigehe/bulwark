Chen Ke Ming Food Manufacturing's pig sales revenue declined 17% to 40.4 million yuan in January from 48.4 million yuan in the year-ago period, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

Pig sales volume fell 12% to 37,400 heads from 42,600 heads in the year earlier, the filing said.
