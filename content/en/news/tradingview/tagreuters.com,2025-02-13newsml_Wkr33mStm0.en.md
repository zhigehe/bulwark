On 20 December 2024, Creditas Financial Solutions, Ltd. (the "Company") issued senior unsecured fixed rate bonds in an amount of USD 60 million (the "Bonds").

For the purpose of the listing of the Bonds, the Company has prepared a prospectus that has, on 11 February 2025, been approved by the Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority (Sw: *Finansinspektionen*).

The prospectus is available on the Company's corporate website.

Admission to trading of the Bonds on Nasdaq Stockholm is expected to take place on or about 17 February 2025.
