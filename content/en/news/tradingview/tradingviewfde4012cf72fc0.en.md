Derrick B. Mayes, a Director at Patrick Industries, sold 1,820 shares of Common Stock on February 11, 2025, at a weighted average price of $93.2697 per share, totaling $169,750. Following the transaction, Mayes directly owns 10,549 shares of the company. The sale price ranged from $93.08 to $93.45. The reported holdings have been adjusted for a three-for-two stock split on December 13, 2024.
