U.S. crude oil inventories rose 4.1 million barrels to 427.9 million barrels in the week ended Feb. 7, the U.S. Energy Information Administration reported Wednesday.

Crude oil production inched up 3,000 barrels per day to 13.42 million b/d, the EIA reported.

Crude oil imports increased by 46,000 b/d to 6.60 million b/d while crude oil exports fell 43,000 b/d to 4.11 million b/d, the agency said.

Total gasoline stocks decreased by 3 million barrels to 248.1 million barrels, the EIA reported. Distillate stockpiles gained 100,000 barrels to 118.6 million barrels.
