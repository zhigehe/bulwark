SpareBank 1 Østlandet issued a new senior nonpreferred bond worth 500 million Norwegian kroner, maturing Feb. 20, 2030.

The bond carries a floating rate of three-month Norwegian interbank offered rate plus 90 basis points, the Norway-based savings bank said Thursday.

SEB served as the lead manager for the issuance.
