AutoNation reported Q4 adjusted earnings Tuesday of $4.97 per diluted share, down from $5.02 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $4.26.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $7.21 billion, up from $6.77 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $6.80 billion.

The company's shares were up nearly 4% in recent Tuesday premarket activity.
