BELGRADE (Serbia), February 10 (SeeNews) - The blue-chip index of the Belgrade Stock Exchange, the BELEX15, closed virtually flat on Monday, with no blue-chip companies posting gains, bourse data showed.

The BELEX15 inched down by 0.04% to close at 1,112.80 points.

Cookware manufacturer Metalac [BEL:MTLC], Aerodrom Nikola Tesla [BEL:AERO], and insurer Dunav Osiguranje [BEL:DNOS] were the only blue-chip companies that traded on Monday.

Metalac lost 0.35% to close at 1,990 dinars as 1,334 shares traded. Aerodrom Nikola Tesla closed unchanged at 2,000 dinars in a trade of 51 shares. Dunav also close flat, at 1,371 dinars, as 1,825 shares changed hands.

Outside the blue chips, there were also no gainers. Transport firm Jugoprevoz Krusevac [BEL:JGPK], which closed unchanged at 8,000 dinars as 834 shares traded, registered the single largest turnover of 6.7 million dinars for the day.

The broader BELEXline, a free-float market capitalisation-weighted index, also fell by 0.04% to 2,441.79 points at close.

Total turnover on the Belgrade bourse rose to 16.6 million dinars ($146,181/ 141,795 euro) from 13.7 million dinars on Friday. Trading in government securities contributed some 3.4 million dinars of the total on Monday, compared to 9.4 million dinars on Friday.

(1 euro = 117.065 dinars)
