Carolyn M. Burke, a Director at Finward Bancorp, has been actively increasing her stake in the company through a series of stock purchases in February 2025. On February 3, 2025, Burke acquired 1,000 shares at a price of $26.8 per share, totaling an investment of $26,800. This transaction brought her direct ownership to 1,000 shares.

Following this, on February 6, 2025, she purchased an additional 500 shares at $26.85 each, for a total of $13,425, increasing her direct holdings to 1,500 shares.

Burke continued to expand her investment on February 7, 2025, by buying 800 shares at $26.5 each, amounting to $21,200. After this purchase, her direct ownership in Finward Bancorp reached 2,300 shares.
