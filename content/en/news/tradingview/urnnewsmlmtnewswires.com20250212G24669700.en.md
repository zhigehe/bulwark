Enma Alrawabi (SASE:9521) sold a commercial building located in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, to Haifa Al-Tuwaijri for 30.7 million Saudi riyals.

The 3,624.60-square-meter property is leased to retail company Abdullah Al Othaim Markets (SASE:4001). It generated a total income of 3.2 million riyals in the last three years, the Saudi real estate company said Wednesday.
