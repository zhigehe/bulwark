Gift Holdings opened its first E.A.K. RAMEN location in New Jersey at Westfield Garden State Plaza on Feb. 12, according to a Wednesday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The store, situated among major dining and retail brands, aims to replicate the high service and food quality of its existing New York locations.

The mall is undergoing expansion, with new retail, entertainment, and luxury residential developments expected by 2027.
