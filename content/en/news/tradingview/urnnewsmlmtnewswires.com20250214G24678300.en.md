Iconic Labs said Thursday it terminated the potential reverse takeover of In the Style Fashion.

The London-listed media and technology company signed a nonbinding heads of terms with the owners of ITS Holdings 2023 to acquire the online fashion retailer in March 2024.

The company said it was now exploring alternative transactions and requested the UK Financial Conduct Authority to restore its listing on the London Stock Exchange.
