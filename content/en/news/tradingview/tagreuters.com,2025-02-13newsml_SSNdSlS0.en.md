NASDAQ NordicProduct information

Anticipated adjustment due to extraordinary dividend in Mandatum (43/25)

The following information is based on the press release from Mandatum Oyj(Mandatum) published on February 13, 2025 and may be subject to change.

The Board of Directors of Mandatum has proposed that the Annual General Meeting(AGM) scheduled for May 15, 2025, approves an extraordinary dividend of EUR0.33 per share in addition to an ordinary dividend of EUR 0.33 per share. Thescheduled Ex-date is May 16, 2025. Provided that the AGM approves the proposal,NASDAQ Derivatives Markets will carry out a re-calculation of stock options,regular futures, regular and gross return forwards in Mandatum (MANTA).
