The New York Federal Reserve's inflation expectations survey for January showed no acceleration in inflation expectations for the one-year and three-year horizons and an acceleration in the five-year period, putting all three readings at 3%.

Home price growth accelerated modestly, as did price growth for all the measured commodities except for no change in the rate of price gains for gold.
