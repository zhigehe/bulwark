Renova acquired an additional 18% stake in Omaezakikou Biomass Energy G.K., bringing its total ownership to 56% and making it a consolidated subsidiary, according to a Wednesday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The move increases Renova's share of dividends from the biomass power project to 75%.

The 1.1 billion yen acquisition was completed on Feb. 12, following an agreement with Mitsubishi Electric Financial Solutions .

Omaezakikou G.K., based in Shizuoka Prefecture, operates a 74.95 MW biomass power plant that began commercial operations on Jan. 29.
