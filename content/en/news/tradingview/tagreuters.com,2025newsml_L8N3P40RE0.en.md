The Ugandan shilling traded stable on Thursday on the back of thin foreign currency demand, as most large firms and banks reserve their local currency holdings for pending mid-month tax payments, traders said.

At 0849 GMT commercial banks quoted the shilling at 3,675/3,685, the same level as Wednesday's close.
