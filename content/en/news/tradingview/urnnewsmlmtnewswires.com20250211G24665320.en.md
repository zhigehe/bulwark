China's small and medium-sized enterprise or SME development index rose to 89 in January, Xinhua reported Tuesday, citing data from the China Association of Small and Medium Enterprises.

The current index was higher than 88.9 in December 2024, according to the association's data.

The categories of market, investment, and labor are among the six out of eight sub-indices that accelerated during the month. The industries of transport, real estate, retail and wholesale, social service, and information transmission software rose by between 0.1 and 0.3 points, the report said.
