WESCO International reported Q4 adjusted earnings Tuesday of $3.16 per diluted share, up from $2.65 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $3.24.

Sales for the quarter ended Dec. 31 were $5.50 billion, up from $5.47 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $5.41 billion.

For 2025, the logistics and supply chain services company expects organic sales growth of 2.5% to 6.5%. Analysts polled by FactSet expect full-year revenue of $22.37 billion.
