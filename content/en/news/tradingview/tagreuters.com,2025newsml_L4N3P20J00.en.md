India's REC has accepted bids worth 52.80 billion rupees ($608.5 million) for bonds maturing in 15 years and seven days and for the reissue of February 2027 bonds, three merchant bankers said on Tuesday.

The state-run company will pay an annual coupon of 7.28% on the 15-year bond issue and a yield of 7.45% on the reissue, and had invited bids from bankers and investors earlier in the day, they said.

The company did not immediately reply to a Reuters email seeking comment.
