Canmax Technologies will invest more than $200 million for the development of two lithium mines in Nigeria, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

The battery materials supplier signed an agreement with the government of Kebbi state in Nigeria and mining company Three Crown Mines to establish a joint venture, the filing said.

Canmax will hold 85% of the JV while Three Crown Mines will hold the remaining 15%, the filing said.

Shares fell 3% during Tuesday's late morning trading.
