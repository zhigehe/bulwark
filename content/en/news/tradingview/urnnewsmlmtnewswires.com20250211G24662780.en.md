Krafton returned to net income attributable to shareholders of 492.1 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, from a net attributable loss of 12.8 billion won a year earlier.

The South Korean video game publisher's sales jumped 15.5% year over year to 617.6 billion won from 534.6 billion won, according to a Tuesday filing with DART.

Shares of Krafton fell nearly 1% at market close.
