Aleksandar Milovanovic, a 10% owner of Golden Matrix Group, purchased 40,000 shares of common stock on February 5 and 6, 2025, at a price of $1.85 per share, totaling $74,000. Following these transactions, Milovanovic directly owns 77,954,616 shares of the company.
