Kokusai Electric's profit attributable to owners of the parent rose 55.8% to 26.1 billion yen in the fiscal nine months ended Dec. 31, from 16.7 billion yen a year ago.

The semiconductor manufacturer's earnings per share (EPS) increased to 110.03 yen from 70.11 yen a year ago, according to a Tokyo Stock Exchange filing on Feb. 7.

Revenue grew 32.7% to 174.7 billion yen from 131.7 billion yen a year earlier.

It forecasted attributable profit to grow 48.8% to 33.3 billion yen or 142.30 yen per share and revenue to gain 31.6% to 238 billion yen for the fiscal year ending March 31.

The company expects to pay a year-end dividend of 18 yen per share for the current fiscal year.

Kokusai Electric's shares gained 5% in early trading.
