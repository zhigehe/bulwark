Longfor Group Holdings recorded contracted sales of 4.46 billion yuan in January after selling a gross floor area of 338,000 square meters, according to a Wednesday filing with the Hong Kong bourse.

Contracted sales attributable to shareholders amounted to 3.01 billion yuan, with a contracted gross floor sales area of 215,000 square meters.
