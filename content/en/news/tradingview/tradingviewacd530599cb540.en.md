Martina M.D. Flammer, Chief Medical Officer of INSMED, sold 37,828 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, under a 10b5-1 trading plan. The sales occurred at weighted average prices of $77.02, $78.36, and $79.33, totaling $2,973,484. Following these transactions, Flammer directly owns 100,165 shares of INSMED.

Roger Adsett, Chief Operating Officer of INSMED, sold 42,975 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, under a 10b5-1 trading plan. The sales occurred at weighted average prices of $77.02, $78.36, and $79.33, resulting in a total sale amount of $3,378,066. Following these transactions, Adsett directly owns 146,082 shares of INSMED.
