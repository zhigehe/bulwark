Furukawa will cancel 4 million treasury shares, or 9.9% of issued stock, on Feb. 28, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

As of Dec. 31, 2024, the metals and machinery firm had 36.3 million shares outstanding, excluding 4.2 million in treasury.
