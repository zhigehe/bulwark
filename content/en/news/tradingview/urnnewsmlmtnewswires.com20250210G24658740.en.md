Rakus Co. will transfer its internet connectivity and hosting business to Ride through an absorption-type company split, effective April 1, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The company will receive 15 million yen in monetary compensation from Ride. The deal is expected to reduce total assets by less than 10% and sales by under 3% of the previous fiscal year's figures.
