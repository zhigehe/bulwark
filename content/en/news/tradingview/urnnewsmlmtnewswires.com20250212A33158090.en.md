The US Treasury's 10-year auction hit a high yield of 4.632% on Wednesday, down from the 4.680% high in the previous auction.

The bid to cover ratio for the auction was 2.48, below the 2.53 ratio in the previous auction.

Dealers represented 57.62% of the bids, with direct bidders at 9.09% and indirect bidders at 33.29%.

For takedown, dealers took 14.84%, with direct bidders at 13.61% and indirect bidders at 71.55%.
