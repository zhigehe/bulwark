Gorilla Gold found more high-grade gold at the Comet Vale Gold Project in Western Australia following a reverse circulation drilling, according to a Monday filing with the Australian bourse.

Notable results include seven meters at 19.3 grams per tonne grade of gold from 49 meters and four meters at 10.1g/t gold from 27 meters, the filing said.
