Edgewell Personal Care reported fiscal Q1 adjusted earnings Monday of $0.07 per diluted share, down from $0.24 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.12.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $478.4 million compared with $488.9 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $480.1 million.

The company reiterated its fiscal 2025 adjusted diluted earnings guidance of $3.15 to $3.35 per share but expects results to be at the lower end of the range due to negative foreign currency impacts. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $3.18.
