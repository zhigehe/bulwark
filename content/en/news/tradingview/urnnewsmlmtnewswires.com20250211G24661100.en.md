UniCredit (UCG.MI) said Tuesday that its net profit and revenue for full-year 2024 increased year over year.

Net profit attributable to the group for the 12 months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 9.72 billion euros, compared with 9.51 billion euros a year ago. EPS moved to 5.74 euros from 4.71 euros.

The Italian lender's revenue was 24.84 billion euros, compared with 23.83 billion euros earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet were expecting 24.71 billion euros in sales, 9.39 billion euros in net income, and 5.80 euros in EPS.

Meanwhile, the board will pay a preliminary cash dividend of 1.4764 euros per share, bringing the total dividend for the year to 2.4025 euros per share. The dividend will be paid on April 24 to shareholders on record on April 23.

UniCredit provided its net revenue guidance for 2025 to be above 23 billion euros, net profit to be broadly in line with 2024, and a strong growth in EPS.
