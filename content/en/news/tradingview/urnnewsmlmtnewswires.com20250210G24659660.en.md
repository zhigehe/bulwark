Lendlease Global Commercial REIT appointed Guy Alexander Cawthra as its chief executive officer, effective April 1, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Tuesday.

Cawthra succeeds Kelvin Chow who will be leaving the role to pursue other opportunities.

Shares of the REIT were up nearly 2% in recent trading.
