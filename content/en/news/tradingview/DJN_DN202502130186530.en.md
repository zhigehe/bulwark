India's Sensex is flat at 76135.17 as gains in bank and tech stocks help offset losses in industrial shares. ICICI Bank is up 1.0% and HCL Technologies is 0.8% higher, while Adani Ports & Special Economic Zone is down 1.6% and NTPC is 0.9% lower. Among individual movers, Godfrey Phillips India is up 8.1% after third-quarter net profit climbed 49% on year. Earnings remain in focus, with Glenmark Pharmaceuticals and Samvardhana Motherson International set to announce their results later in the day. (kosaku.narioka@wsj.com; @kosakunarioka)
