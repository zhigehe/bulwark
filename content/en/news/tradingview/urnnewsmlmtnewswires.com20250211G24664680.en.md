Food & Life Co.'s profit attributable to owners of the parent soared 88.2% to 6.13 billion yen in the fiscal first quarter ended Dec. 31, 2024, from 3.26 billion yen a year ago.

Earnings per share increased to 53.65 yen from 27.94 yen a year ago, according to a Tokyo Stock Exchange filing on Feb. 7.

Sales rose 15.9% to 99.1 billion yen from 85.5 billion yen a year earlier.

Food & Life forecasted attributable profit to grow 2.5% to 15 billion yen or 132.60 yen per share and net sales to gain 13% to 408 billion yen for the fiscal year ending Sept. 30, 2025.

It expects to pay a year-end dividend of 27.50 yen per share for the current year.
