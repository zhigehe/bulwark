Amanat (DFM:AMANAT) said Thursday it swung to a profit for full-year 2024, while revenue increased year over year.

Profit attributable to equity holders of the company for the 12 months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 115.8 million Emirati dirhams, compared with a loss of 53.2 million earlier. EPS moved to 0.0467 dirham from a loss per share of 0.0214 dirham.

The Dubai-listed healthcare and education investor's revenue was 796.1 million dirhams, compared with 679.8 million dirhams a year ago.
