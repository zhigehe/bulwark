Robert De Rothschild, a 10% owner in PrimeEnergy Resources Corp, has sold a total of 3,472 common shares across three transactions on February 5th, 6th, and 7th, 2025. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $215.179, $204.5722, and $202.1491 respectively, resulting in a total sale amount of $711,696. Following these transactions, De Rothschild directly owns 249,528 common shares of the company.
