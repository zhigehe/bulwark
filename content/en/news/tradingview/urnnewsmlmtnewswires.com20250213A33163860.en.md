Duke Energy reported Q4 adjusted earnings Thursday of $1.66 per share, up from $1.51 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $1.65.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $7.36 billion, up from $7.21 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $7.58 billion.

The energy company said it expects 2025 adjusted EPS of $6.17 to $6.42. Analysts polled by FactSet expect $6.33.
