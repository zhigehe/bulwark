UK industrial production edged up 0.5% month over month in December 2024, after falling by a revised 0.5% in the previous month, the Office for National Statistics said Thursday.

Analysts expected a 0.2% increase for the month.

On a yearly basis, UK industrial output was 1.9% lower, against the revised 2% decline earlier and the consensus estimate of a 2.1% decrease.
