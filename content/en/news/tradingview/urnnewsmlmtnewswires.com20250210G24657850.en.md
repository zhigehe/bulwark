Lansdowne Oil & Gas signed an interest-free convertible loan agreement with existing shareholders totaling 45,000 pounds sterling.

The oil and gas company intends to use the funds for working capital, while it continues talks for potential funding and works on a potential reverse takeover deal, according to a Monday filing.

Trading in Lansdowne's shares will remain suspended until the completion of the reverse takeover.
