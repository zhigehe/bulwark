\*\* Shares of chipmaker NXP Semiconductors rise 3.2% to $217.75 in early morning trading

\*\* Morgan Stanley upgrades NXPI to "overweight" from "equal weight"; hikes PT to $257 from $231

\*\* Brokerage says that cyclical and secular tailwinds are aligning for NXPI

\*\* "With customer inventory digestion largely behind them, we think NXP is set up well to outgrow auto semis in 2025," Morgan Stanley says

\*\* Twenty-three of 32 brokerages rate the stock "buy" or higher and nine "hold"; their median PT is $250

\*\* The stock fell 9.5% in 2024
