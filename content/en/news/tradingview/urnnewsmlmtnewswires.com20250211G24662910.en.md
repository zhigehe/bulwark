Adventure Box Technology signed a binding letter of intent to buy iFaming company Zefr Media's intellectual property.

The online game creation platform will pay $2.5 million for the transaction, which will be settled by cash and shares, according to a Tuesday release.

The deal is expected to close in the second quarter, subject to shareholder and regulatory approvals and due diligence.
