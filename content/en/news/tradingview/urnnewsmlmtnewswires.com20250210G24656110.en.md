Guanghui Logistics has received approval from China State Railway Group to build a railway connecting line between the Hongnao Railway and the Linha Railway in China, according to a Shanghai Stock Exchange disclosure on Saturday.

The state-owned enterprise gave the green light for the connection line, which is managed by Guanghui Logistics subsidiary Xinjiang Hongnao No. 3 Railway.

The Xinjiang Coal Export channel behind the Hongnao Railway will be upgraded from a single channel to a dual channel, the Chinese real estate developer said.

Guanghui Logistics' shares closed 5% higher.
