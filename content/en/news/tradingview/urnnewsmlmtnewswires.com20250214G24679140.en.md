The Global X Physical Gold ETF, operated by Global X Australia under Mirae Asset Global Investments, amassed over 3.6 trillion won in net assets, The Korea Herald reported Thursday, citing a Mirae Asset release on Wednesday.

Global X Physical Gold ETF, originally launched in 2003 by ETF Securities, was Mirae's first exchange-traded fund linked to the gold spot price, the report said.
