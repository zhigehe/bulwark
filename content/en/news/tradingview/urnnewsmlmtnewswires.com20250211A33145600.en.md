StoneX said Tuesday its Payments division has entered into a partnership with Fiserv to provide cross-border payment services to financial institutions.

Community banks and credit unions will be able to use StoneX's infrastructure in conjunction with Fiserv's web-based platform Payments Exchange Services, StoneX said.

Financial details weren't provided.
