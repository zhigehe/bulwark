Pantech Global (KLSE:PGLOBAL) is eyeing 178.3 million ringgit through its listing on the Main Market of Bursa Malaysia.

The manufacturer pipe fittings and welded pipes manufacturer plans to issue 262.2 million shares to the public at 0.68 ringgit apiece, according to a Monday prospectus filed on Bursa Malaysia.

Proceeds will be used for business expansion, capital expenditure, to boost working capital, and to pay off expenses.
