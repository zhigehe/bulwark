Nittetsu Mining Co acquired 900,000 shares for 4.73 billion yen through an off-auction share repurchase via ToSTNeT-3 on Friday, completing its buyback program, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The buyback allowed for the purchase of up to 900,000 shares for a maximum of 4.99 billion yen.

Shares of the company were down over 1% in recent trade.
