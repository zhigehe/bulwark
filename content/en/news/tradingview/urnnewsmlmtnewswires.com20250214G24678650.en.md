China Resources Building Materials Technology Holdings expects a decrease of 62% to 72% in its profit attributable to owners for 2024 compared with 2023, a Thursday bourse filing said.

The company attributed the expected decrease to the impairment losses recognized during the reporting year and a year-on-year decrease in gross profit margins for the group's businesses aside from cement and concrete.
