Consumer stocks were mixed pre-bell Tuesday with the Consumer Staples Select Sector SPDR Fund 0.4% higher and the Consumer Discretionary Select Sector SPDR Fund down 0.7% recently.

Coca-Cola shares advanced by over 4% after the company reported higher Q4 comparable earnings and net operating revenue.

Shopify shares were down more than 1% even after the company reported higher Q4 non-GAAP net income and revenue.

Marriott International shares were over 2% lower after the company reported a decline in Q4 adjusted earnings. The company also issued Q1 and 2025 outlook for adjusted earnings per share below analysts' expectations.
