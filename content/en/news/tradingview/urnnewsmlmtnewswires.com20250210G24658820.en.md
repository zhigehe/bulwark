Meiji Holdings will set up a subsidiary in Singapore to oversee its Asia-Pacific operations, aligning with its 2026 medium-term plan, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The unit, Meiji Food Asia Pacific, will manage regional subsidiaries and build a localized value chain.

Meiji Food Asia Pacific will start with $3 million in capital, which will increase to $31 million through in-kind contributions by March 2028, making it a specified subsidiary.
