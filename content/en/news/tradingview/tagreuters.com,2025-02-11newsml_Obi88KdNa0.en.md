Protector Forsikring ASA, rated bbb+ (Good) Positive outlook by AM Best, hastoday successfully issued a new NOK 800,000,000 Solvency II compliant Tier 2bond with maturity date 20 February 2055 pricing at 3m NIBOR + 225bps. Firstcall date will be on 20 May 2030, 5.25 years after the settlement date.

Settlement date for the new issue is expected to be 20 February 2025.

The new tier 2 bond issue will be applied for listing at Oslo Børs.

Nordea and Pareto Securities acted as Joint Lead Managers for the transaction.

Oslo, 11 February 2025.

For further information, please contact: CFO, Ditlev de Vibe Vanay, mob. +47 934 80 393
