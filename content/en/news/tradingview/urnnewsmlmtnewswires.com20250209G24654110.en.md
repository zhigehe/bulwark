Lana Co. (SASE:9587) received a 1.5 million-Saudi-riyal project from the National Center for Prevention and Control of Animal Pests and Diseases in Saudi Arabia.

The project covers the transport and treatment of veterinary medical waste based on project documentation, specifications, and quantity schedules, according to a Sunday release.

The Saudi medical device company expects to sign the contract on March 1.
