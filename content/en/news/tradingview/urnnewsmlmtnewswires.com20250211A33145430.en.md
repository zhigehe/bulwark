Financial stocks were leaning lower pre-bell Tuesday, with The Financial Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLF) down 0.2%.

The Direxion Daily Financial Bull 3X Shares (FAS) was down 0.6% and its bearish counterpart Direxion Daily Financial Bear 3X Shares (FAZ) was 0.4% higher.

S&P Global shares were up more than 6% after the company reported higher Q4 adjusted earnings and revenue.

Fidelity National Information Services (FIS) stock was more than 10% lower after the company issued Q1 outlook for revenue and adjusted earnings that trail analysts' expectations.

The Carlyle Group (CG) shares were down more than 1% even after the company posted higher Q4 after-tax distributable earnings and revenue.
