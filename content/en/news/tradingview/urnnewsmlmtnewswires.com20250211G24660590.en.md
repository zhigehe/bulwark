Chengdu Dahongli Machinery said its subsidiary, Zhend Ding Machinery, will invest 1.0 million yuan in Chengdu Leidianniao Technology, acquiring a 51% stake, according to a Monday filing with the Shenzhen Bourse.

The capital increase aims to support the growing unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) market. Leidianniao will be integrated into Dahongli's financial statements. The company will closely monitor Leidianniao's performance to mitigate potential risks.

The company's stocks were up by 1% in recent trading.
