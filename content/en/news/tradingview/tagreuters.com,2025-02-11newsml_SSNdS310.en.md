NASDAQ NordicProduct information

Anticipated adjustment due to extraordinary dividend in NCC (38/25)

The following information is based on the press release from NCC AB (NCC)published on February 10, 2025, and may be subject to change.

The Board of Directors NCC has proposed that the Annual General Meeting (AGM)scheduled for May 7, 2025, approves an extraordinary dividend of SEK 2.00 pershare in addition to an ordinary dividend of SEK 9.00 per share, which will bepaid in two installments. The scheduled Ex-date is May 8, 2025. Provided thatthe AGM approves the proposal, NASDAQ Derivatives Markets will carry out arecalculation of stock options, regular and gross return forwards/futures inNCC (NCC).
