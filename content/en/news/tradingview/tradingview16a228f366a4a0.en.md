James Andrea S., Chief Financial Officer of Oncocyte Corp, purchased 97,561 shares of common stock at a price of $2.05 per share on February 7, 2025, totaling $200,000. This transaction increases Andrea's direct ownership to 151,231 shares. The purchase was made through a private placement as per a Securities Purchase Agreement dated February 7, 2025.
