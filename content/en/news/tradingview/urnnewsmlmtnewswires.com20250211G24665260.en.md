South Korean holding company GS Holdings posted a net income attributable to shareholders of 6.85 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, down 94.04% from 114.9 billion won a year earlier.

Sales fell 2.3% year over year to 6.343 trillion won from 6.491 trillion won, according to a Tuesday Korea Exchange filing.

Shares of GS Holdings fell over 1% in recent trade on Wednesday.
