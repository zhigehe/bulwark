NBCC (India) has secured orders of undisclosed amounts from the Irrigation and Water Resources, State Government of Mizoram, India for planning, designing and implementation of three hydro projects, according to a Wednesday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The contracts include harnessing the potential of the Mat River in Serchhip, Mizoram, building a multipurpose rainwater harnessing dam, Ngur-Vapar in the Champhai District, and a dual mini-dam project at Vantawng, Thenzawl.
