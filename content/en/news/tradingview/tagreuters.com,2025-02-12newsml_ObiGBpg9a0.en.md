Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published on January 31,2020, when Nye Veier selected AF Gruppen for the conceptualization phase ofregulation, design, and construction of the new E6 Roterud-Storhove. Nye Veierhas now decided that AF Gruppen will carry out the detailed design andconstruction of this project, subject to the completion of the intentionannouncement according to public procurement regulations.

The project entails a 23 km new four-lane highway from Roterud in Gjøvik toStorhove near Lillehammer. AF Gruppen, along with its consultant Norconsult,hasbeen closely collaborating with Nye Veier since early 2020 in developing andregulating this road. The project has placed a strong focus on howenvironmentalconsiderations can be combined with the realization of a safe road, especiallywhen crossing the Lågen delta.

"It's rare for us as a general contractor to have the opportunity to influencearoad project in its early stages, and it's a patient team that now looksforwardto starting the construction phase. We have collaborated well with Nye Veieronseveral projects and look forward to continued cooperation in Innlandet. Theproject is important for many local and regional stakeholders and will providegreat benefit and value for future users of this vital transport route," saysAmund Tøftum, CEO of AF Gruppen.

The agreement for this project has a value of NOK 6,350 million excluding VAT.Afixed-price agreement for the execution phase is planned to be signed beforeMarch 1, 2025. A relatively quick construction start is planned, withcompletionin the summer of 2030.
