Trade Desk reported Q4 non-GAAP earnings Wednesday of $0.59 per diluted share, up from $0.41 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet, on average, expected $0.57.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $741 million, compared with $605.8 million a year earlier.

Analysts expected $758.9 million.

For Q1, the company is projecting at least $575 million in revenue.

Analysts are looking for $582.2 million.

Additionally, Trade Desk said its board authorized an additional $564 million for its stock buyback program.

Shares of the company were down nearly 25% in after-hours trading.
