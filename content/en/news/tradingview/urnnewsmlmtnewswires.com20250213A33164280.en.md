Genesis Energy reported a Q4 net loss Thursday of $0.58 per common unit, widening from a loss of $0.08 a year earlier

One analyst polled by FactSet expected a loss of $0.31 per unit.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $725.6 million, down from $774.1 million a year earlier.

Analyst estimates for revenue were not available.

The company's shares were up nearly 3% in recent pre-market activity.
