Enechange has entered into a partnership with PORT to enhance its energy platform business, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The agreement covers collaboration on electricity and gas switching services, market expansion, and digital transformation for retail energy firms.

PORT, which holds 4.2% of Enechange shares, has agreed to sell its holdings under specified conditions, ensuring minimal market impact. This includes 6.3 million shares, or 13.5% of fully diluted voting rights, factoring in new shares issued to Itochu Enex .
