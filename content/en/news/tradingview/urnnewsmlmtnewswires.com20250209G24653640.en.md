Contemporary Amperex Technology , or CATL, has restarted operations at its lithium lepidolite mine in China's Jiangxi province, Reuters reported, citing UBS.

The restart comes four months after a production halt sparked a rally in lithium stocks, according to the report.

The move, driven by low inventories and cost-cutting strategies, signals a boost to China's lithium supply, Reuters wrote.
