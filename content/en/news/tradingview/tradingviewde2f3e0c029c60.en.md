Chimera Investment Corporation, a publicly traded real estate investment trust (REIT), has released its financial results for the fourth quarter and full year ending December 31, 2024. The company, which invests in a diversified portfolio of real estate assets, has provided key insights into its financial performance and strategic initiatives.

**Financial Highlights**

For the fourth quarter of 2024, Chimera reported a GAAP net loss of $2.04 per diluted common share. However, earnings available for distribution were $0.37 per diluted common share. For the full year, the company achieved a GAAP net income of $90 million, or $1.10 per diluted common share, and earnings available for distribution of $121 million, or $1.48 per diluted common share. The GAAP book value per common share stood at $19.72 as of December 31, 2024, with an economic return of 4.40% for the year.

**Business and Operational Highlights**

Chimera's portfolio includes residential mortgage loans, Non-Agency RMBS, Agency RMBS, business purpose and investor loans, and other real estate-related assets. The company has made significant progress in building a more durable and diversified portfolio. The acquisition of Palisades is expected to strengthen Chimera's existing business and provide additional opportunities for shareholder value in 2025 and beyond.

**Strategic Initiatives and Corporate Developments**

In 2024, Chimera focused on strategic initiatives to enhance its portfolio. The acquisition of Palisades is a key development, expected to bolster the company's business and create long-term value. Additionally, the company has been actively managing its secured financing agreements and securitized debt to optimize its financial structure.

**Management's Perspective**

Phillip J. Kardis II, President and CEO of Chimera, commented on the company's progress in 2024, stating, "In 2024, we made progress on our initiative towards building a more durable and diversified portfolio. We expect the acquisition of Palisades to strengthen our existing business and provide additional opportunities to add value for our shareholders in 2025 and over the long term."

**Future Outlook**

Looking ahead, Chimera is optimistic about the potential benefits of the Palisades acquisition and its impact on the company's portfolio. The management expects continued progress in 2025, with a focus on creating long-term value for shareholders.
