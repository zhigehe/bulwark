Lazard reported preliminary assets under management Wednesday of $233.02 billion as of Jan. 31, up from $226.33 billion a month earlier.

The asset management firm reported a market appreciation of $6.1 billion, foreign exchange appreciation of $400 million, and net inflows of $300 million for January.
