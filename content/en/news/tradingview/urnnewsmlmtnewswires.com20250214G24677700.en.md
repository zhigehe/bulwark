Wereldhave said Thursday it purchased a shopping center in Luxembourg, while its 67%-owned Wereldhave Belgium acquired another for a total consideration of 167 million euros.

The Dutch real estate investment company bought Knauf Shopping Schmiede, while Wereldhave Belgium purchased Knauf Shopping Pommerloch​, both from property investor Nextensa. The transactions were completed the same day.

The Schmiede shopping center has a total gross leasable area of 41,000 square meters, while the Pommerloch property has a total gross leasable area of 33,000 square meters. The acquisition of the properties both included parking and development land.
