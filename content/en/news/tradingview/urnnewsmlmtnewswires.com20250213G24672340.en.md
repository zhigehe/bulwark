China's anti-graft watchdog is investigating a deputy manager at Chongqing Changan Automobile controlling shareholder China South Industries Group for suspected violations of law and discipline, Reuters reported Wednesday.

"Violations of law and discipline" are a euphemism by Chinese media for corruption.

The investigation comes as China South Industries is planning a restructuring with other government-owned firms, the report said.
