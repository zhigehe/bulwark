JTEKT acquired 24.7 million shares for 28.84 billion yen on Friday through the Tokyo Stock Exchange's off-auction repurchase system, according to a same-day filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The board had authorized repurchasing up to 27 million shares, or 7.87% of outstanding stock, for a maximum of 34 billion yen.
