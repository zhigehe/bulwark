Lorey Brandon Craig, an Executive Officer and Director at Eagle Financial Services, has increased his stake in the company by purchasing 2,343 shares of Common Stock at $32.00 per share on February 7, 2025, for a total of $74,976. Following this transaction, Craig's direct ownership in the company has risen to 38,786 shares.

Cary R Claytor, another Director at Eagle Financial Services, also expanded his investment in the company by acquiring 3,125 shares at a price of $32.00 per share, amounting to $100,000. After this purchase, Claytor directly owns 8,364 shares and indirectly controls an additional 312 shares through family members. This acquisition includes shares from the company's Dividend Investment Plan.
