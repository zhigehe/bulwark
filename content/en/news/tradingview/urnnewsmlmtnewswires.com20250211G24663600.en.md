Workman recorded a net income of 13.0 billion yen for the nine months ended Dec. 31, 2024, 0.5% lower than the 13.1 billion yen logged for the same period a year ago.

The company's basic earnings per share for the reporting period slipped to 159.52 yen from 160.35 yen per share a year ago, according to a Monday filing with the Tokyo Exchange.

Total operating revenue of the specialty retail company came in at 107.6 billion yen, 1.4% higher than 106.2 billion yen a year earlier.

It forecasted a net income of 16.3 billion yen, a net income per share of 200.04 yen, total operating revenue of 138.6 billion yen, and a final dividend of 68 yen per share for the fiscal year ending March. 31.
