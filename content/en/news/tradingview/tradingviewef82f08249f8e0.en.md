Prudential Financial Director, Michael Todman, sold 2,950 shares of common stock on February 7, 2025, at a weighted average price of $112.98 per share, totaling $333,291. Following this transaction, Todman directly owns 375 shares of Prudential Financial. Additionally, Todman holds 1,304 Restricted Stock Units, 12,194 Notional Shares-Mandatory, and 8,877 Notional Shares-Optional.
