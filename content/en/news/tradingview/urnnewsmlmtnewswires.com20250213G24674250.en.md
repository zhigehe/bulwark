Renault said Thursday that Japanese automaker Nissan contributed 211 million euros to its full-year 2024 results.

The figure is lower than Nissan's 2023 contribution of 797 million euros.

In the fourth quarter of 2024, Nissan posted a positive contribution of 59 million euros to the French automaker's net income. Renault owned 35.71% of Nissan as of Thursday.
