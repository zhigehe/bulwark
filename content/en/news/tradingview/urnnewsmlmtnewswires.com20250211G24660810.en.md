ASE Technology's unit Siliconware Precision Industries signed facility engineering deals worth NT$500.1 million.

The deals were signed with Chung Yuang Electrical Consulting, between Dec. 22, 2024, and Feb. 10, 2025, according to a Monday Taiwan Exchange filing.

The contracts will run from Dec. 22, 2024, until Dec. 31, 2026.
