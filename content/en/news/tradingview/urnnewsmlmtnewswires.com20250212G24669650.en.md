Sweden-based aXichem (AXIC-A.ST) secured an order worth 7 million kronor from global distributor Chr. Olesen for its aXiphen animal feed additive.

The natural analogue industrial chemicals company said Wednesday it will deliver the order to the distributor's operations in Brazil by call-off during 2025.
