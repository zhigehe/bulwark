Environmental technology company M Vest Water signed Invest Support Co. as an exclusive agent to sell its technologies in Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, and the United Arab Emirates.

The five-year agreement covers technologies related to the oil and gas market as well as other water treatment sectors, according to a Feb. 7 release.
