Wetteri's Wetteri Auto acquired Hedin Automotive's Kia and Mitsubishi new car sales and maintenance business in Lahti, Finland, for 150,000 euros.

The representation for the two brands will be transferred to the Finnish automotive retailer by March 1, along with its staff, according to a Monday release.
