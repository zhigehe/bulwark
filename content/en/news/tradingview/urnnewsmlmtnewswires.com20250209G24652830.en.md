SG Holdings will acquire Taiwan-based freight forwarder Morrison Express Worldwide for $900 million through its Singapore unit, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The deal with Morrison Express Holdings, set to close in July, expands SG Holdings' air freight business and global logistics reach. Morrison Express, operating in over 100 countries, serves major semiconductor and tech firms.
