Endúr secured 350 million Norwegian kroner in gross proceeds from the oversubscribed private placement of its shares.

The industrial group issued 4,861,111 new shares at 72 kroner apiece, according to a Tuesday release. Share issuance is subject to shareholder approval during an extraordinary general meeting scheduled for March 4, with settlement expected on March 6.

Net proceeds will be used for part of the cash payment for the Totalbetong acquisitions, short-term working capital, general corporate purposes, and a financial buffer.

Arctic Securities and Danske Bank acted as joint bookrunners in the offering.
