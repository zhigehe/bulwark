Hartmann Markus, General Counsel of Mister Car Wash, sold 4,209 shares of Common Stock on February 6, 2025, at a price of $7.95 per share, totaling $33,461. The sale was conducted to cover taxes and fees associated with the vesting of restricted stock unit awards. Following the transaction, Markus directly owns 15,098 shares of Mister Car Wash.
