Insperity reported Q4 adjusted earnings Monday of $0.05 per diluted share, down from $0.75 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.02.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $1.61 billion, up from $1.58 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $1.6 billion.

The company said it expects adjusted EPS of $1.89 to $2.15 for Q1 and $3.10 to $3.95 for full-year 2025. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $2.02 and $3.70, respectively.

The company's shares were up 9.5% in premarket activity Monday.
