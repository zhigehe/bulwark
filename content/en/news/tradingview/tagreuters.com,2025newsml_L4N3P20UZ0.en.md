\*\* Deutsche Bank trims stock rating for Playa Hotels & Resorts to "hold" from "buy"

\*\* "We are downgrading PLYA following announcement that it has agreed to be acquired by Hyatt Hotels for $13.50 per share in an all-cash transaction, as we note that the stock is trading within 2% of the takeout price" - brokerage

\*\* Also cuts PT to $13.50 from $15

\*\* Hyatt on Monday said it will buy PLYA for about $2.6 billion including debt, seeking to boost its presence in Mexico and the Caribbean

\*\* One of 6 brokerages rate PLYA "buy" and 5 "hold"; their median PT is $13.25 - LSEG data

\*\* In 2024, stock was up 46.2%
