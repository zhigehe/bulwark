WisdomTree reported Tuesday assets under management in January of $114.56 billion.

The company said inflows were $1.08 billion in Fixed Income, $175 million in US Equity, $15 million in Cryptocurrency, and $9 million in Alternatives.

Outflows were $262 million in Emerging Market Equity and $36 million in International Dev. Mkt Equity.
