Humana forecast annual profit slightly below Wall Street estimates on Tuesday, signaling that costs from its government-backed plans for older Americans would remain elevated for the rest of the year.

The company sees 2025 adjusted profit per share of about $16.25 compared to analysts' estimate of $16.71 per share, according to data compiled by LSEG.
