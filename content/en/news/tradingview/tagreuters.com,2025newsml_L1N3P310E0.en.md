North Sea Forties crude prices fell after a single trade, while Johan Sverdrup traded higher.

PLATTS WINDOW

\* Indications are free on board (FOB) unless marked as cost, insurance and freight (CIF) or delivered at place (DAP).

\* There were two deals on Tuesday.

\* Forties: Glencore bought a cargo from Equinor at dated Brent minus 20 cents FOB Hound Point, down from a bid at dated Brent plus 20 cents on February 11.

\* Johan Sverdrup: Equinor sold a cargo to TotalEnergies at dated Brent plus $1.70, up from a bid at dated Brent plus $1.65 on February 11.

\* WTI Midland: Gunvor offered a cargo at dated Brent plus 90 cents CIF Rotterdam, up from a trade at dated Brent plus 65 cents on February 11.
