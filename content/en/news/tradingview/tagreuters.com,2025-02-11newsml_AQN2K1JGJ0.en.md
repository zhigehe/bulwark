Feb 11 (Reuters) - The value of Canadian building permits grew by 11.0% in December from November, Statistics Canada said on Tuesday. The residential sector saw higher intentions for both the single-family and multi-family components, while only the industrial component was up in the non-residential sector. The value of building permits were up in five provinces, with the largest increase recorded in Ontario.

Month on month (%) Dec Nov(rev) Nov(prev) Total +11.0 -5.6 -5.9 Residential +21.2 -6.4 -7.5 Nonresidential -5.9 -4.4 -3.2

NOTE: All figures are seasonally adjusted. Analysts surveyed by Reuters forecast an increase of 1.7% in the value of building permits in December.
