Life360's Chief Financial Officer, Russell John Burke, sold 3,104 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $44.94 per share, totaling $139,493. Following the transaction, Burke directly owns 145,650 shares of Life360. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on September 6, 2024.
