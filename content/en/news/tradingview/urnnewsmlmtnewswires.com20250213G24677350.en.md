Bohai Leasing subsidiary Avolon logged 2024 net profit of $608 million, up 79% from $339 million the previous year.

Avolon's revenue rose 14% year over year to $2.98 billion from $2.61 billion, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of Bohai Leasing rose 2% in recent trade.
