By Najat Kantouar

Shares in Spanish travel technology HBX Group fell slightly on the company's first day of trading on the Madrid stock exchange.

The stock is currently trading around 11.30 euros compared with its initial public offering price of 11.50 euros announced on Feb. 10, implying a market capitalization of 2.79 billion euros ($2.90 billion).

The group--which houses Hotelbeds, Bedsonline and Roiback--has raised 725 million euros of new money by issuing 63.04 million shares as part of its IPO.

It plans to use the money to settle the deferred sale consideration due in respect of the selldown of shares of different members of the group.
