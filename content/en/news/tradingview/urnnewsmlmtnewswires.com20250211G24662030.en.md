Entain on Tuesday said Chief Executive Officer Gavin Isaacs stepped down, effective immediately.

The sports betting and gambling company named nonexecutive Chair Stella David as the interim CEO until a permanent successor is appointed. David previously acted as Entain's interim CEO from December 2023 to September 2024.

Pierre Bouchut will serve as the nonexecutive chair on an interim basis.
