Luoniushan logged lower sales of pigs in January due to fewer sales days during the Chinese New Year holiday.

The animal husbandry company logged sales revenue of 82.5 million yuan on sales of 39,200 pigs, marking respective declines of 29% and 50% year over year, according to a Thursday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.
