Finnish equity research company Inderes concluded a share buyback program after repurchasing shares with a total value of 299,786.60 euros.

The company repurchased 15,000 of its shares between Nov. 25, 2024, and Feb. 7, 2025, at an average price of 19.98577 euros, according to a Tuesday release.

The shares will be used for potential acquisitions or other business arrangements, to finance investments or as part of the company's incentive scheme, or be transferred or canceled.
