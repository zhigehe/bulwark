In Paris, the CAC 40 Index is picking up 61 points or 0.76 percent on Thursday.

Gains are led by Legrand (7.63%), Michelin (5.30%) and Stellantis NV (3.15%).

Biggest losers are ENGIE (-0.82%), Safran (-0.79%) and Unibail Rodamco (-0.75%).
