ABA Chemicals plans to invest in Gansu Haotian Technology by acquiring existing shares and possibly subscribing to newly registered capital, according to a Monday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

The move aims to enhance ABA Chemicals's pharmaceutical business by leveraging Haotian's CDMO and CRO expertise.

While discussions are ongoing, no formal agreement has been signed, and the investment structure is yet to be finalized. Further disclosures will be made as the deal progresses.
