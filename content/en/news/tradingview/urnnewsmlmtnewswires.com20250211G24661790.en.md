Tesla Chief Executive Officer Elon Musk said he is not interested in buying Chinese short video platform TikTok, Reuters reported Saturday.

Musk's remarks, made in late January, were released online on Saturday by Axel Springer, which hosted a summit where Musk spoke via video conference, the report said.

Musk said he has not put in a bid for the app, according to the report.

"I'm not chomping at the bit to acquire TikTok," the newswire quoted Musk as saying, adding he would not know what to do with the app.
