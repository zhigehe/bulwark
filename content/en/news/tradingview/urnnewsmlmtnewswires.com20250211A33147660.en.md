Health care stocks eased Tuesday afternoon with the NYSE Health Care Index down 0.2% and the Health Care Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLV) shedding 0.3%.

The iShares Biotechnology ETF (IBB) fell 1.4%.

In corporate news, Novartis agreed to buy biopharmaceutical firm Anthos Therapeutics for up to $3.1 billion as the Swiss pharmaceutical giant expands its cardiovascular portfolio. Novartis shares added 0.7%.

Lipella Pharmaceuticals shares jumped 39% after it reported positive topline results from a phase 2a trial of LP-310, a liposomal-tacrolimus oral rinse for oral lichen planus.

T2 Biosystems shares dropped 24% after it said Tuesday the Nasdaq Hearings Panel has determined to delist its shares.
