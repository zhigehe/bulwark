Sarytogan Graphite is set to mobilize its exploration team to the field in March for soil sampling and begin planning an aeromagnetic survey at the Kopa copper exploration project in Kazakhstan, according to a Tuesday Australian bourse filing.

The Kopa exploration license was recently granted for a period of six years with options for extensions, the filing said.

It is also planning further soil sampling and drilling at the Baynazar copper exploration project.

The firm's shares rose 4% in recent trading on Tuesday.
