Marble Design (SASE:9575) made changes to its leadership for a term of four years starting from Feb. 6, 2025, and ending on Feb. 5, 2029, according to a Sunday release.

The Saudi Arabia-based marble projects company appointed Mahmoud Madani as chief executive officer succeeding Nawaf Mohammed Abdullah Al-Kharashi, who transitioned to the role of managing director.

Additionally, Ahmed Abdullah Abdulrahman Al-Kharashi was positioned as chairman and Mohammed Abdulrahman Rashid Al-Rumaizan as vice chairman.
