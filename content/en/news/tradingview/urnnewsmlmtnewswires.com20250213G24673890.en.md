Al Sagr Cooperative Insurance (SASE:8180) secured a one-year renewal of its health insurance qualification from Saudi's Insurance Authority.

The new license will be valid from Feb. 16, 2025, until Feb. 15, 2026, according to a Thursday release from the Saudi Arabian insurer.
