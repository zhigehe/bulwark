\*\* Shares of fitness equipment retailer Fitell Corp fall 57% to $2.70

\*\* FTEL says it is raising $10 million by selling about 2 million shares and warrants to an institutional investor in a registered direct offering

\*\* The offering price is $5.02/shr; represents a 20% discount to the FTEL's last close

\*\* Company says the net proceeds will be used for the development and commercial launch of smart fitness equipment and general corporate purposes

\*\* Rodman & Renshaw LLC is acting as the exclusive placement agent for the offering

\*\* FTEL has 20.1 million outstanding shares, with a market capital of $126.4 million, according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* FTEL gained 432% in 2024
