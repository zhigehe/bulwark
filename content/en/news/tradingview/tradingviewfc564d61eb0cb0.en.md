Zhida Hong, CEO and Director of ADDENTAX GROUP CORP., has purchased 50,000 shares of the company's common stock on February 5, 2025. The transactions were made at varying prices, with a weighted average price of approximately $0.7694 per share, totaling $38,378. Following these transactions, Hong directly owns 201,758 shares of ADDENTAX GROUP CORP.
