(Updates to add HSBC's statement)

HSBC Holdings is expected to announce $1.5 billion in planned annual savings after one-time costs following its broad reorganization, London's Financial Times reported on Friday, citing two people familiar with the matter.

Chief Executive Officer Georges Elhedery commenced the overhaul in October 2024 soon after taking charge and is expected to announce the savings figures for the first time with HSBC's annual results on Feb. 19.

The British bank declined to comment when reached by MT Newswires.
