Founder Technology Group said shareholders New Founder Holding Development is planning to trim its shareholding in the company by up to 41.7 million shares, or less than 1% stake, citing funding needs..

This will be undertaken via centralized bidding from March 10 to June 9, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of the computer peripherals and printed circuit board producer fell 7% in recent trade.
