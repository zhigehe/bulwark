\*\* Shares of technology co Audinate fall 3.7% to A$7.07, tracking losses in Australian tech stocks

\*\* Audinate is the second-biggest loser on the sub-index, which is down 1.3%

\*\* The sub-index's performance is in tandem with Wall Street losses after U.S. President Donald Trump said he plans to announce reciprocal tariffs

\*\* Macquarie lowers price target to A$7.70 from A$10.2, retains 'neutral'

\*\* Says the cut reflects lowered earnings outlook for the next three years, accounting for lower sales and negative operating leverage

\*\* Two analysts rate Audinate "buy", 5 "hold"; their median PT is A$9.23, according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Stock fell 54.2% in 2024
