TSH Resources bought back 420,000 shares for 514,403 ringgit on Monday, according to a same-day disclosure on the Singapore bourse.

The shares were repurchased at between 1.21 ringgit and 1.22 ringgit apiece, the dual-listed oil palm cultivation and wood products company said.
