Laura Miele, President of EA Entertainment, sold 2,500 shares of Electronic Arts Common Stock on February 6, 2025, under a 10b5-1 trading plan established on August 2, 2024. The transactions included sales at prices ranging from $129 to $131.96, with weighted average prices noted for some sales. The total sale amount was $327,078. Following these transactions, Miele directly owns 47,514 shares of Electronic Arts.
