Kelly Partners Group Holdings reported run rate revenue guidance of AU$135.2 million for the fiscal year ending June 30, up 25.1% from revenue of AU$108.1 million a year earlier, according to a Wednesday filing with the Australian bourse.

The company also entered agreements to partner with two accounting businesses in the Hunter Region of New South Wales, each generating approximately AU$500,000 in annual revenue, the filing said.

The deal is not expected to have a material impact on the company, the filing said.
