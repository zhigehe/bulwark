Rex International reported January production of 11,730 barrels of oil equivalent per day (BOEPD) from Norway and Oman, according to a Monday filing with the Singapore Exchange.

The company's subsidiary, Lime Petroleum, contributed 9,397 BOEPD, while Masira Oil, another company unit, contributed 2,333 stock tank barrels per day, the filing said.
