STOCKS:

The benchmark BSE Sensex fell 0.7% to 77,311.80, while the broader NSE Nifty 50 index declined 0.76% to 23,381.60, as U.S. President Donald Trump's tariff threats unnerved investors.

RUPEE:

The Indian rupee dropped 0.06% versus the U.S. dollar to 87.48, hurt by mounting tariff worries and persistent investor outflows.

GOVERNMENT BONDS:

The benchmark 10-year bond (IN067934G=CC) was quoted at 100.55 rupees, with the yield up about 1 bp at 6.71%, as investors grapple with policy setback.

OVERNIGHT INDEX SWAPS:

The one-year overnight index swap rate (INR1YMIBROIS=CC) was little changed at 6.35%, while the five-year swap rate (INR5YMIBROIS=CC) rose 2 bps to 6.1375%.

CALL MONEY:

India's overnight call money rate (INROND=) was at 6% and the overnight TREPS rate (INTREPTOT=TCCL) was at 6.18%.
