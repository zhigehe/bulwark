\*\* Shares of Cheviot Co set for worst week since mid-March 2024

\*\* Stock has lost 9% so far this week, last down 7% on the day

\*\* CHVI fell as much as 11% earlier in the session to 1049.60 rupees, lowest since April 2023

\*\* Co, which makes and exports jute products, posted a 81% slump in Q3 profit

\*\* Total income in the reporting qtr fell 9%

\*\* Trading vols at 6,540 shares, over 2x the 30-day avg

\*\* Stock has fallen ~15% YTD; in 2024 it shed ~11%

($1 = 86.8825 Indian rupees)
