Tan Lay Koon, a Director at FLEX LTD., sold 50,000 Ordinary Shares on February 10, 2025, at a price of $43.25 per share, totaling $2,162,500. Following this transaction, Tan Lay Koon directly owns 202,850 Ordinary Shares of FLEX LTD., including 6,889 unvested restricted share units set to vest immediately prior to the company's 2025 annual general meeting.
