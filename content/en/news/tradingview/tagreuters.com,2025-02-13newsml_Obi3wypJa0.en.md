Finanstilsynet has reviewed certain accounting matters related to thefinancialreporting of Belships ASA ("The entity"). Finanstilsynet has pointed outmaterial errors and omissions related to the following:i. Belships has entered into many lease agreements for ships that have not yetcommenced, but to which the company is bound, where IFRS 16 Leases requiresthatdetailed qualitative and quantitative disclosures have to be provided.Including, disclosures about future cash outflows that are not yet reflectedinthe lease liability recognized in the balance sheet.ii. Belships has employees within "ship management" who receive significantremuneration in the form of distributions to silent partners in internalpartnerships, as well as from performance-based bonus agreements. ProvisionsinIAS 19 Employee Benefits and IAS 1 Presentation of Financial Statementsrequire,among other things, that the financial reporting shall include informationaboutthe period's payroll expenses related to these schemes.iii. Finanstilsynet pointed out several other matters regarding Belships'annualfinancial statements for 2023, related to items presented in an aggregatedmanner in the balance sheet and disclosures that were either omitted orinadequate, compared to requirements in IAS 1 or specific accountingstandards.The entity has either already implemented corrective measures as of itsinterimfinancial statements for the third quarter of 2024 or confirmed that it willimplement corrective measures in its annual financial statements for 2024.Finanstilsynet has taken note of the company's measures to correct the errors.If you have any questions, please contact:Lars Jacob Braarud, Head of section, +47 22 93 97 53 / +47 91 34 52 73(mobile)
