USPACE Technology Group entered into an MoU to cooperate with the Kingdom of Bahrain National Space Science Agency, a Wednesday filing with the Hong Kong bourse stated.

The cooperation will be in areas including satellite manufacturing and telemetry, tracking and controlling, and data application activities.
