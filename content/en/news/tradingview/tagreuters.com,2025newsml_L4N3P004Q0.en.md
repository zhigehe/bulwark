\*\* Shares of Star Entertainment Group rise 11.4% to A$0.12, their highest level since

\*\* SGR top gainer in the ASX200 benchmark index , which is trading 0.6% lower as of 2329 GMT

\*\* SGR says received non-binding proposals from each of Hong Kong investors Chow Tai Fook Enterprises (CTFE) and Far East Consortium International (FEC)

\*\* Offer to buy Star's 50% stake in Destination Brisbane Joint Venture (DBC), which owns the Queen's Wharf Brisbane development

\*\* SGR says none of the proposals "provided sufficient value"

\*\* Continues to engage with CTFE and FEC; says continues to explore possible liquidity solutions

\*\* SGR down 39.45% this year, including the day's moves
