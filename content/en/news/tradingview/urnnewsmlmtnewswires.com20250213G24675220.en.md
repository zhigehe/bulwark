Household bank lending in South Korea fell by 500 billion won in January, extending December's decline of 400 billion won, the Bank of Korea said Wednesday.

Meanwhile, corporate lending rebounded by 7.8 trillion won after a decline of 11.5 trillion won in the previous month, driven by seasonal factors, the release said.
