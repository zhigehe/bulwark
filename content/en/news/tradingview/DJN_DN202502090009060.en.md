By Annie Linskey

Donald Trump isn't the only political figure attending the 59th Super Bowl: His predecessor's wife, former first lady Jill Biden, is also in the arena. A Pennsylvania native, Biden arrived wearing a green Eagles scarf. She brought her grandson, Hunter Biden, who is Beau Biden's son, and donned an Eagles jersey featuring the team's quarterback, Jalen Hurts.
