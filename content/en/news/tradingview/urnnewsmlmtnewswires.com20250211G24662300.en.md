AMTD Idea Group's magazine, L'Officiel, which comes under the group's subsidiary, World Media and Entertainment Group, will launch in Australia, Canada, Mexico and Taiwan this year.

The magazine will be launched in both digital and print versions, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Monday.
