Japanese stocks are lower as uncertainty about higher borrowing costs continues. Also weighing on the market are concerns about trade friction between the U.S. and its trading partners. Trading-house stocks are leading the declines. Mitsubishi Corp. is down 2.1% and Itochu is 2.2% lower. USD/JPY is at 151.72, compared with 151.62 as of Friday's Tokyo stock market close. Investors are closely watching developments over U.S. tariffs and domestic corporate earnings. The Nikkei Stock Average is down 0.4% at 38647.60. (kosaku.narioka@wsj.com; @kosakunarioka)
