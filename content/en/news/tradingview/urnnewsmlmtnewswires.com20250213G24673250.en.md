Puncak Niaga unit Danum Sinar secured a court injunction on Feb. 7, to remove a blockade set up by the Penan Community in November 2024, according to a Wednesday Malaysian bourse filing.

The blockade had prevented Danum Sinar from harvesting activities and supplying of crops to Sarawak Oil Palm since December 2024.
