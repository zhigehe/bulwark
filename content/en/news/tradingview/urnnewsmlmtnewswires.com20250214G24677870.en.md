Shenyang Jinbei Automotive appointed Feng Shengliang as chair to succeed Xu Xiaomin, who resigned.

Feng serves as deputy general manager of Shenyang Automobile and general manager of Brilliance Automotive Group, according to a Friday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

Shares of the automotive and auto part manufacturer rose 2% in recent trade.
