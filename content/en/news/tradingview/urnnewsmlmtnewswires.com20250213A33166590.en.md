Honda Motor on Thursday reported earnings for the nine months ended Dec. 31 of 169.69 Japanese yen ($1.10) per diluted share, down from 176.78 yen a year earlier.

Sales for the nine months ended Dec. 31 were 16.3 trillion yen, compared with 15 trillion yen in the same period the year earlier, the company said.

Analyst estimates were not available.

The company expects fiscal 2025 sales of 21.6 trillion yen. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect 21.4 trillion.
