King's Town Bank unit KTB International Leasing plans to sell its entire stake in Cathay No.1 Real Estate Investment Trust for NT$350.4 million by Dec. 31.

The stake, comprising 24,164,400 shares, will be sold at a minimum price of NT$14.5, according to a Monday Taiwan Exchange filing.

The maximum estimated loss from the sale is NT$77.6 million based on acquisition costs.

The disposal aims to adjust the company's working capital, it said.
