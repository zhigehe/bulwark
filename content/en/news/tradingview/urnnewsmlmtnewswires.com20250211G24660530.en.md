Singapore's Housing Development Board launched 10,622 flats for sale for February, according to a media release on Monday.

Of the total, 5,032 flats are build-to-order (BTO) and 5,590 are sale of balance flats during the month.

The BTO flats on offer are spread across five projects, with eight out of 10 flats having a waiting time of four years or less.
