Doximity shares surged 35% amid heavy trading after analysts raised their price targets following improved fiscal Q3 results reported Thursday that beat analyst expectations.

Intraday trading volume catapulted to over 7.4 million from the shares' daily average of about 2.13 million.

Cloudflare reported overnight higher Q4 non-GAAP earnings and revenue, and Truist Securities raised its price target to $175 from $140 while maintaining a buy rating on the stock.

Shares of Cloudflare rose 21% as intraday trading volume surged to over 10 million from a daily average of about 3.13 million.

Sionna Therapeutics said late Thursday that it priced an initial public offering of about 10.6 million shares at $18 per share for expected gross proceeds of $191 million.

The cystic fibrosis-focused pharmaceutical firm began trading on Nasdaq Friday, with shares up 23% amid an intraday trading volume of more than 547,000.
