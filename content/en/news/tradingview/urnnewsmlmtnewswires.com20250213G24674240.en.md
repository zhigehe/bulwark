National Central Cooling (DFM:TABREED), d/b/a Tabreed, will temporarily pause trading on the Dubai Financial Market from 11 am local time on Thursday due to a board meeting.

The Emirati district cooling system company will resume trading once the meeting results are disclosed, according to a same-day release.
