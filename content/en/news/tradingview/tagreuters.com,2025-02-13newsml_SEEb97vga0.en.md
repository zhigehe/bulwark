ZAGREB (Croatia), February 13 (SeeNews) - A1 Telekom Austria Group said its Croatian unit recorded earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortisation (EBITDA) of 224 million euro ($234 million) last year, up from 189 million euro in 2023.

Revenue increased by 8.6% year-on-year, reaching 570 million euro, as service revenue rose 9.5% to 466 million euro, A1 Telekom Austria Group said on Tuesday

EBITDA margin of the group's Croatian unit came in at 39.3% last year, up from 36% in 2023.

($ = 0.959 euro)
