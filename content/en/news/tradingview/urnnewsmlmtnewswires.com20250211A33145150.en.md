HomeTrust Bancshares said Tuesday it will transfer the listing of its common shares to the New York Stock Exchange from Nasdaq.

The company expects its shares to start trading on the NYSE on Feb. 24 under the new "HTB" ticker, it said, adding that it will continue to trade on Nasdaq until the market close on Feb. 21.
