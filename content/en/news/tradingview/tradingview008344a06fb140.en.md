Netflix Co-CEO and Director, Gregory K Peters, has sold a total of 14,586 shares of Netflix Common Stock on February 6 and 7, 2025, with the transactions totaling $14,881,825. The sales were executed at prices ranging from $1,015 to $1,025 per share, leading to a weighted average price within this range. Following these sales, Peters directly owns 12,950 shares of Netflix Common Stock. These transactions were conducted pursuant to a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on October 30, 2024.

Netflix Director Strive Masiyiwa sold all his holdings of 2,813 shares of Common Stock on February 6, 2025, at a price of $1,007.87 per share, totaling $2,835,138. After this sale, Masiyiwa no longer directly owns any shares of Netflix. This sale was also made pursuant to a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan, which was adopted on November 7, 2024.
