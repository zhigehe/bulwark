Hazoor Multi Projects has received a letter of award from the National Highways Authority of India for a project worth 79.1 million Indian rupees, according to a Tuesday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The contract entails acting as a user fee collection agency at Bijora toll Plaza in Maharashtra on the hybrid annuity mode and upkeep and maintenance of adjacent toilet blocks.

The contract is scheduled to be executed in three months.
