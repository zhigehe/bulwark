By Colin Kellaher

Public Service Enterprise Group's board has raised the utility holding company's quarterly dividend by 5%, to 63 cents from 60 cents.

The new payout, equal to $2.52 a year, represents an annual yield of about 3.03% based on Tuesday's early trading price of $83.21, up from 2.88%.

The Newark, N.J., company said the increased dividend is payable March 31 to shareholders of record March 10.
