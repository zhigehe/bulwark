Contemporary Amperex Technology , or CATL, filed for an initial public offering on the Hong Kong bourse.

BofA Securities, CICC, China Securities International, JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley and UBS serve as joint sponsors, according to a filing with the Hong Kong bourse.

The battery manufacturer is planning to raise at least $5 billion in the IPO, Reuters reported.
