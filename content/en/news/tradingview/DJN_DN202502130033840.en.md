Chinese shares ended lower, weighed by semiconductor and tech stocks. Investors are reassessing tech-related stocks' valuations after a recent rally triggered by AI startup DeepSeek's breakthrough. Semiconductor Manufacturing International Corp. dropped 3.1% and Hygon Information Technology was down 4.3%. Beijing Kingsoft Office Software fell 1.8% and iFlytek shed 2.4%. Consumption stocks rose broadly with Yonghui Superstores up 0.7% and Yifeng Pharmacy Chain surging 5.7%. The benchmark Shanghai Composite Index ended 0.4% lower at 3332.48, the Shenzhen Composite Index dropped 0.8% and the ChiNext Price Index was off 0.7%. (sherry.qin@wsj.com)
