Copa said Tuesday its January passenger traffic, measured as revenue passenger miles, rose 23.6% from a year earlier.

The preliminary data also showed that capacity, measured as available seat miles, rose 22.1% from a year earlier.

The system load factor was 86.4%, up from 85.3% a year earlier.
