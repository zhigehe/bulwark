Healthcare services provider Shalby said its Shalby Hospital at Ahmedabad in Gujarat, India, has received approval to perform hand transplant, according to a Tuesday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The approval enables Shalby Hospital to perform hand transplants to restore hand functionality to those who have lost hands, the filing said.
