Fitch increased on Thursday the shareholder support rating of Santander UK Group to a- from bbb+, citing the upgrade of parent Banco Santander .

The rating agency said the higher rating reflects the Spanish lender's improved capacity to support its UK operations, as evidenced by the recent upgrade in its long-term issuer default rating to A from A-.
