RailTel Corporation of India has secured an order worth 169.7 million Indian rupees from the State Project Director, Bihar Education Project Council, India, according to a Wednesday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The contract entails the supply of additional student kits for classes six to 12 to be executed by Feb. 28.

Shares of the company gained over 2% in morning trade Thursday.
