Malaysia's current account surplus (MYCURA=ECI) expanded to 11.4 billion ringgit ($2.6 billion) in the fourth quarter of 2024 from 2.2 billion ringgit in the July-to-September period, central bank and government data showed on Friday.

Portfolio investment (MYFLO=ECI) saw a net outflow of 5.8 billion ringgit in the October-to-December period.

($1 = 4.4400 ringgit)
