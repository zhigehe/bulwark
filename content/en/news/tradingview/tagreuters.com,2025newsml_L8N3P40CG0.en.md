Mare Engineering Group SpA :

* SIGNS BINDING AGREEMENT TO ACQUIRE 70.6% SHARE CAPITAL IN LA SIA FROM CSE HOLDING, ASPASIA, GLSR
* CONSIDERATION IS AT 82.3% THROUGH SHARE EXCHANGE, 17.7% CASH
* 2,560,000 SHARES OF MARE GROUP TO BE CORRESPONDED THROUGH CAPITAL INCREASE RESERVED TO LA SIA IN MARE GROUP FOUNDERS
* EUR 2.4 MLN TO BE PAID IN CASH TO LA SIA IN MARE GROUP FOUNDERS
* AFTER CLOSING, REMAINING 29.4% OF LA SIA SHARE CAPITAL TO BE OFFERED AT SAME CONDITIONS
* FOR EACH MINIMUM LOT OF 500 LA SIA SHARES, 320 MARE GROUP SHARES, EUR 310 CASH, INCLUDING 16.7% PREMIUM ON LA SIA CLOSING PRICE OF FEBRUARY 12
