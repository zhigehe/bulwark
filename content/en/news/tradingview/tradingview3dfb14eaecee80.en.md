Jeffrey H Kripitz, a Director at Parke Bancorp, sold a total of 1,148 shares of Common Stock on February 6 and 7, 2025, for a total sale amount of $22,982. The selling prices per share were $20.15 and $20.00, respectively. Following these transactions, Kripitz directly owns 52,874 shares and indirectly owns 175,305 shares of Parke Bancorp through PSP, IRA, Trust, and ITF.
