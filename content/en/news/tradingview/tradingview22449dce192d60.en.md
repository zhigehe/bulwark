Thomas N Cerovski, SVP of Global Sales at Thermon Group Holdings, purchased 500 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, at a price of $27.72 per share, totaling $13,860. Following this transaction, Cerovski directly owns 42,606 shares of Thermon Group Holdings, which includes 7,571 restricted stock units.
