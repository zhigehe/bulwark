Tech-Bank Food earned revenue of 613.5 million yuan from pig sales in January, slipping 4.1% from 639.4 million yuan the prior year, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure on Tuesday.

The Chinese pig breeder sold 437,200 pigs for an average of 16.55 yuan per kilogram during the month, compared to 701,500 pigs for an average of 12.92 yuan per kilogram a year earlier.
