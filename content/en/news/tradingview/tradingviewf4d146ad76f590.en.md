Matthew Blake McRae, CEO and Director of Arlo Technologies, sold 133,022 shares of common stock on February 5th and 6th, 2025, at weighted average prices of $11.4323 and $11.2356 respectively, totaling $1,506,249. Following these transactions, McRae directly owns 3,054,990 shares of the company. The sales were primarily to satisfy estimated tax withholding obligations related to the settlement of Restricted Stock Units.

Brian Busse, General Counsel of Arlo Technologies, sold 17,255 shares on February 5 and 6, 2025, at weighted average prices of $11.4323 and $11.2355 respectively, totaling $196,126. After these sales, Busse directly owns 585,203 shares of Arlo Technologies Common Stock.
