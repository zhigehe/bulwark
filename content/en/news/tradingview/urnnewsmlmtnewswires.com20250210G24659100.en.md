Technology company Eyantra Ventures has received board approval to incorporate a wholly-owned subsidiary in Massachusetts, USA, tenatively named Eyantra Ventures Inc, according to a Monday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The new entity will work in the information technology consultancy space, with an aim to leverage new growth opportunities in the US.

The incorporation of the subsidiary will require approvals from the Reserve Bank of India and other statutory and regulatory authorities in Massachusetts.
