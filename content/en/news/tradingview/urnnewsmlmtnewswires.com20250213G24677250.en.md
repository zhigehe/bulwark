Guangdong Haid Group posted 2024 net profit attributable to shareholders of 4.52 billion yuan, up 65% from 2.74 billion yuan the previous year.

Earnings per share rose 64% year over year to 2.72 yuan from 1.66 yuan, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

The agricultural and animal husbandry company's revenue was down 1.2% to 114.7 billion yuan from 116.1 billion yuan.

Shares of Guangdong Haid Group rose 3% in recent trade.
