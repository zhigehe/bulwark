Peab (PEAB-B.ST) said Monday it secured a turnkey contract worth 113 million Norwegian kroner from the municipality of Lillestrøm in Norway.

The contract covers a 2,000 square-meter expansion of the Skjetten Elementary School, comprising a new two-story building with eight classrooms and two activity rooms. Additionally, the company will conduct minor refurbishing of the school's existing building.

The Swedish construction company will begin work in the spring of 2025, with completion expected in the summer of 2026.
