Solis Minerals appointed Mitch Thomas as Chief Executive Officer, effective in March, according to a Wednesday filing with the Australian bourse.

Thomas was previously Chief Financial Officer of Latin Resources (ASX:LRS) and the CFO of the battery materials business under Rio Tinto , per the filing.

Shares of Solis Minerals surged 38% in recent trade.
