DoubleU Games posted net income attributable to shareholders of 55.6 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, up 53.7% from 36.2 billion won a year earlier.

The game developer's sales rose 1.5% year over year to 155.8 billion won from 153.5 billion won, according to a Wednesday Korea Exchange filing.
