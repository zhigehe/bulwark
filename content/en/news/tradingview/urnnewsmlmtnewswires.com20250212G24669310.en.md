Eni (ENI.MI) and the Italian Agency for Development Cooperation signed a memorandum of understanding to implement the Energize ATS program to improve technical education in Egypt.

Eni and AICS will establish two new applied technology schools in Matrouh and Damietta in Egypt, the Italy-based integrated energy company said Wednesday.
