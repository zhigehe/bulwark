Michael P. McCuen, EVP/Chief Lending Officer of First Commonwealth Financial Corp, purchased 3,020 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, at a price of $16.57 per share, totaling $50,041. Following this transaction, McCuen directly owns 60,643 shares of the company, including 8,000 shares of restricted stock vesting on September 11, 2026, and 45,000 shares of restricted stock vesting in equal installments on July 1 of 2025, 2026, and 2027.
