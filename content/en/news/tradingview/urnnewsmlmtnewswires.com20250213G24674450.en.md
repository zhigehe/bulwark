Konecranes received an order for four all-electric mobile harbor cranes from Belgian logistics company Katoen Natie, according to a Thursday release.

The Finnish crane manufacturer will deliver two ESP.9 Konecranes Gottwald and two ESP.8 Konecranes Gottwald to the client's operations in the Port of Antwerp, Belgium, in the first quarter of 2026. The order also includes a 10-year service level agreement.
