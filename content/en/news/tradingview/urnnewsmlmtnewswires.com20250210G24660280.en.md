Investcorp Capital (ADX:ICAP) appointed Mohamed Aamer as its interim chief executive officer, effective March 1.

Aamer's appointment follows Tim Mattar's retirement, according to a Monday release.

The United Arab Emirates-based alternative investment company plans to initiate a search process to appoint a long-term successor.
