Consumer stocks were mixed late Tuesday afternoon, with the Consumer Staples Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLP) increasing 0.6% and the Consumer Discretionary Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLY) falling 1.3%.

Redbook US same-store sales last week rose 5.3% from a year earlier, slower than the 5.7% increase in the previous week.

In corporate news, General Motors expects to mitigate up to 50% of potential tariffs that President Donald Trump is threatening to levy on imports from Mexico and Canada, Chief Executive Mary Barra said Tuesday during a Wolfe Research investment conference, according to media reports. GM shares rose 0.3%.
