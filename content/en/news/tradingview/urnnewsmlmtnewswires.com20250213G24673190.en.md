Daodaoquan Grain and Oil completed the registration of its wholly-owned subsidiary in Hong Kong with a registered capital of HK$10,000.

Daodaoquan Grain and Oil (Hong Kong) will focus on the production, trade, storage and logistics of grains, edible oils and agricultural products, according to a Thursday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of the cooking oil manufacturer were down 1% Thursday.
