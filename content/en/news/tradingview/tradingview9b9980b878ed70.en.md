Cummins Inc.'s VP - Chief Financial Officer, Smith Mark Andrew, sold 11,430 common shares on February 10, 2025, in three separate transactions. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $374.8504, $375.683, and $373.6361, totaling $4,281,359. Following these transactions, Smith directly owns 26,122 common shares of Cummins.
