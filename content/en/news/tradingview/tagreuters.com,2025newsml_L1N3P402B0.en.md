\*\* Shares of power solutions group Leoch International Technology Ltd jump 26.4% to HK$2.06, their highest since March 2023, and on course for third consecutive session of gains

\*\* Stock on track for the biggest one-day pct rise since July 2021, if gains hold; top gainer on the Hong Kong bourse

\*\* Leoch said Hong Kong Stock Exchange has approved its plan to spin off and seek a separate listing of its power batteries distribution business, Leoch Energy Inc, on a U.S. Exchange

\*\* Hang Seng Commerce & Industry Index (.HSNC) rises 1% and Hang Seng Index adds 0.9%

\*\* Stock rose 26% last year
