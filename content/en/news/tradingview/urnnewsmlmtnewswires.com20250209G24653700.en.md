Poly Developments and Holdings Group generated a contracted amount of 18 billion yuan in January, falling 11% from the prior year, according to a Shanghai Stock Exchange filing on Monday.

The Chinese real estate company's contracted area during the month dropped 35% year on year to 772,000 square meters, the filing said.
