Tines announced a $125 million funding on Tuesday led by Growth Equity at Goldman Sachs Alternatives with participation from new investors SoftBank Vision Fund 2, valuing the workflow management company at $1.13 billion.

The funding round comes after a $50 million raise in May 2024, and brings the total amount raised by Tines to $272 million.

Existing investors include Accel, Felicis, CrowdStrike Falcon Fund, and Addition.
