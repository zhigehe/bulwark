PODGORICA (Montenegro), February 14 (SeeNews) - Montenegro's customs revenue rose 18% year-on-year to 87 million euro ($90 million) in the first month of 2025, the customs agency said.

Revenue from customs duty increased by an annual 19% to 3.75 million euro in January, while revenue from value added tax rose 11% to 52.6 million euro in the period under review, the customs agency said in a statement on Thursday.

Excise duty revenue grew by 31% on the year to 30.7 million euro in January.

In 2024, Montenegro's customs revenue rose 10% to 1.29 billion euro, with all segments registering annual growth.

($ = 0.962 euro)
