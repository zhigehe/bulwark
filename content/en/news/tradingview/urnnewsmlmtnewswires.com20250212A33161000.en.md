Q2 Holdings reported Q4 breakeven late Wednesday, compared with a loss of $0.31 per diluted share a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected GAAP EPS loss of $0.10.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $183 million, up from $162.1 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $179.9 million.

The company expects total revenue of $184 million to $188 million in Q1 and $772 million to $779 million in full-year 2025. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $184.6 million in Q1 and $772.1 million in 2025.

Shares were up 2% in after-hours activity.
