**Business Highlights**

**Business Overview**

United Express, Inc. is an emerging growth company focused on providing comprehensive management services for long and short-distance logistics across the United States. The company operates in the transportation and logistics sector, delivering merchandise and other items for companies and individuals.

**Operational Challenges**

The company has faced challenges due to fluctuations in oil prices, which have increased costs in the freight and logistics industry. This has necessitated adjustments in pricing to maintain profitability.

**Acquisition of Fighting Leagues**

United Express acquired assets from Jebour Two Limited, including a Nevada State Athletic Commission Professional Promoter license, media rights for 40 shows, and production and staging equipment. This acquisition aims to explore the viability of promoting combat sports events in Nevada.

**Operational Focus**

The company is assessing the profitability and resources required to hold combat sports events, given the significant planning and capital needed. Currently, United Express lacks the capital resources to execute these events.

**Future Outlook**

The company is focused on evaluating the potential of its newly acquired assets to expand into the combat sports promotion industry. However, the realization of earnings from such events may take 6-9 months, and the company is exploring ways to secure the necessary capital.
