Germany's wholesale selling prices rose 0.9% year over year in January, following a 0.1% increase in the previous month, the country's Federal Statistical Office said Friday.

On a monthly basis, wholesale prices were 0.9% higher, against the prior 0.1% gain and the consensus estimate of a 0.2% rise.
