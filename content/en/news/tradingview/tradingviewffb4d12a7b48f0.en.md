Jack Sinclair, Chief Executive Officer and Director of Sprouts Farmers Market, sold a total of 5,830 shares of common stock on February 5 and 6, 2025, for a total sale amount of $979,669. The sales were executed at weighted average prices of $167.5402 and $168.5387 per share, respectively, under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan. Following these transactions, Sinclair directly owns 140,754 shares of the company, including 83,748 shares of common stock and 57,006 restricted stock units.
