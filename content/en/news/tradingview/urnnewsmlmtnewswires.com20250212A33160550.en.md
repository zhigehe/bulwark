Tyler Technologies reported Q4 non-GAAP earnings late Wednesday of $2.43 per diluted share, up from $1.89 a year ago.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $2.43.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $541.1 million, up from $480.9 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $540.7million.

For 2025, the company expects non-GAAP EPS of $10.90 to $11.15 on revenue of $2.30 billion to $2.34 billion. Analysts expect $10.80 on sales of $2.36 billion.
