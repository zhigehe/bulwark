\*\* Shares of NatWest down 2.6% at 425.6p - the top loser on FTSE 100 index CURRENCYCOM:UK100

\*\* Co expects to achieve a return on tangible equity of between 15%-16% in 2025 and over 15% by 2027

\*\* "Guidance lacks a punch," says Matt Britzman, senior equity analyst at Hargreaves Lansdown, adding that the 2025 guidance is in line with expectations, which may cause some weakness in early trading

\*\* Posts FY pretax operating profit of 6.2 billion pounds ($7.80 billion), slightly above analysts' forecasts of 6.1 billion pounds, as per a company compiled poll

\*\* Co intends to increase its ordinary dividend payout ratio from about 40% to about 50% from 2025

\*\* NWG rose about 6% YTD

($1 = 0.7950 pounds)
