Jewelry company Renaissance Global has secured an order worth $1.6 million from US-based retailer WAL/MART for the sale of jewelry on an asset basis, according to a Monday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The contract is for the supply of 63,250 pieces of fast-moving lab-grown diamond fashion jewelry. This is to be supplied in four months.

Shares of the company were up nearly 4% in recent trade.
