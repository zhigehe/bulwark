Holtek Semiconductor incurred a loss attributable to owners of NT$149.6 million, or NT$0.66 per share for 2024, according to a Monday Taiwan bourse filing.

The company did not provide comparative year-ago figures.

Operating revenue accumulated stood at NT$2.50 billion, the bottled water manufacturer said.

Total assets at the end of the period were NT$5.62 billion, while total liabilities were NT$1.96 billion, it noted.
