Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing or TSMC's board approved capital appropriations of about $17.1 billion, according to a Wednesday Taiwan Exchange filing.

The funds will be used to upgrade and expand advanced technology, enhance packaging and specialty technology, and build and equip new fabs, the chipmaker said.
