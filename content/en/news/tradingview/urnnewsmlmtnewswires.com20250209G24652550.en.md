Dug Foodtech , a plant-based food company, completed an initial order of 400,000 kronor with French supermarket company Carrefour .

The contract is part of the plant-based food company's listing across 150 Carrefour hypermarkets across Spain, according to a Feb. 7 release.

As Dug Foodtech forays into the Spanish market, it will also launch into the coffee channel, said Chief Executive Officer Helene Nielsen.
