LG Electronics parent LG Group teamed up with a research team at Seoul National University to develop treatments for intractable diseases by merging AI and biotechnology.

The collaboration will focus on decoding complex protein structures to advance diagnosis and therapies for conditions such as dementia and cancer, Pulse News reported Friday.

This research aims to create a "digital cell map" for the better understanding of disease mechanisms and boost drug development, the report said.

Shares of LG Electronics fell nearly 1% at market close on Monday.
