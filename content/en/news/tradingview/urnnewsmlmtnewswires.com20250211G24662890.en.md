Rockfire Resources said Tuesday its ongoing portable X-ray fluorescence soil survey at the Molaoi zinc deposit in Greece detected a new and coherent zinc zone at Gkagkania, with zinc-in-soil levels exceeding 0.15% Zn.

The mineral exploration company added that the survey identified a geochemical anomaly comparable in size to the existing resource, potentially doubling the project's JORC-compliant resources.
