Sanjay Chowbey, President and CEO of Kennametal, purchased 10,000 shares of the company's common stock on February 11, 2025, at a weighted average price of $21.753 per share, totaling $217,530. Following this transaction, Chowbey directly owns 77,595 shares of Kennametal, including 319.48 shares held in the company's 401(k) plan.
