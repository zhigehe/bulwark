By Stephen Nakrosis

Special purpose acquisition company Harvard Ave Acquisition filed for a $250 million initial public offering with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

The blank-check company, which is incorporated in the Cayman Islands, said Tuesday that its plans to identify a prospective target business won't be limited to a particular industry or geographic location.

Harvard Ave Acquisition said Sung Hyuk Lee, its chief executive officer and director, has served as CEO of Plutus Partners, a private-equity and asset-brokerage firm, since 2021. In addition, Harvard's Chief Financial Officer and Director Hoon Ji Choi has served as managing director of Plutus Partners since 2021.

The company said Lee and Choi are both located in South Korea, and its sponsor's sole member and sole director are located in China.

Harvard Ave Acquisition said its has applied to list its units on the Nasdaq Global Market under the symbol HAVAU.
