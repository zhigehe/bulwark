Daiseki secured an 'A' score from global environmental nonprofit CDP for its climate change transparency, marking its second consecutive year on the organization's A List, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

CDP assessed about 24,800 companies in 2024, including over 2,100 listed on Japan's Prime Market.

Firms were rated from A to D- based on disclosure quality, environmental risk management, and emissions targets, with non-disclosing firms receiving an F.
