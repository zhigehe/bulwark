Leidos Holdings reported fiscal Q4 non-GAAP earnings Tuesday of $2.37 per diluted share, up from $1.99 per share a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $2.27.

Revenue for the quarter ended Jan. 3 was $4.37 billion, up from $3.98 billion a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $4.13 billion.

The company said it expects fiscal 2025 non-GAAP earnings of $10.35 to $10.75 per share. Analysts polled by FactSet expect $10.55.

Revenue for the current fiscal year is projected to be $16.9 billion to $17.3 billion. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $17 billion.

The company's shares were up 2.5% in recent premarket activity on Tuesday.
