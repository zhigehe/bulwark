JX Advanced Metals (JXAM), a wholly owned metals unit of Japan's Eneos Holdings , won approval to list its shares on the Tokyo Stock Exchange on March 19, a regulatory filing showed on Friday.

Eneos, Japan's largest oil refiner, will raise as much as 461 billion yen ($3.01 billion) by listing JXAM, according to Reuters' calculations.
