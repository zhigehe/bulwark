Luxury company Richemont on Friday announced changes to its senior management, with the bosses of jewellery companies Cartier and Van Cleef joining its executive board.

Catherine Renier, CEO of Van Cleef & Arpels, and Louis Ferla, CEO of Cartier have been appointed, the Swiss company said.
