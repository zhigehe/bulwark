A three-member International Monetary Fund (IMF) mission is visiting Pakistan to conduct a Governance and Corruption Diagnostic Assessment under the country's 2024 Extended Fund Facility program, the finance ministry said on Sunday.

The ministry added that the report will identify key governance vulnerabilities and recommend structural reforms.
