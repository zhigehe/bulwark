Hyatt Hotels shares tumbled more than 13% in Thursday trading after the company reported Q4 adjusted earnings and revenue that declined from the year-ago period and missed analyst estimates.

The company reported Q4 adjusted earnings of $0.42 per diluted share, down from $0.70 a year earlier. Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.75.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $1.60 billion, down from $1.66 billion a year earlier. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $1.66 billion.
