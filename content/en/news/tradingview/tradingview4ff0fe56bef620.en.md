Adedoyin Niyi, SVP & CIO of GATX Corp, sold 5,867 shares of common stock on February 5 and 6, 2025, with sales totaling $978,561. The transactions included sales at weighted average prices of $166.1119, $166.5599, and $167.4174. Following these transactions, Niyi directly owns 3,859 shares and indirectly owns 3,766 shares through a 401(k).
