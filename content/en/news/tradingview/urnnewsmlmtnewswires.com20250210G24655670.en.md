Johnson Matthey said Monday it appointed Richard Pike as chief financial officer-designate, effective immediately.

Pike will join the British sustainable technologies company's board on April 1, succeeding Stephen Oxley, who will step down as CFO on March 31.

Prior to the appointment, Pike served as group finance director at packaging company DS Smith (SMDS.L).
