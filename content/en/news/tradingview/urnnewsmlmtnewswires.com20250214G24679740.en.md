Jiangsu Libert's unit, Shanghai Libert Engineering, won the bid for the installation of large-scale industrial modules for 226.2 million yuan, according to a Shanghai Stock Exchange filing on Friday.

The Chinese industrial module manufacturer will construct and install 5BDA and 7BUG modules in a diesel engine plant within 1,480 calendar days, the filing said.

China General Nuclear Power Engineering is the tenderer of the project.
