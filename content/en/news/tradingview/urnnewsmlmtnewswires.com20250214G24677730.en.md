Baltic Horizon Fund's (NHCBHFFT.TL, ) alternative investment fund manager initiated the process of delisting the fund's Swedish depositary receipts from Nasdaq Stockholm, according to a Thursday news release.

Northern Horizon Capital cited the decline in investors and trading for the Swedish depositary receipts for the decision. The cancelation of trading is expected to take effect in August at the earliest.

The fund will continue to trade on Nasdaq Tallinn amid the plan.
