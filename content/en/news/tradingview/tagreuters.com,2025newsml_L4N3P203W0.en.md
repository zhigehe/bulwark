\*\* Shares of Propel Funeral Partners <PFP.AX> fall 1% to A$5.9

\*\* Macquarie flags lower-than-expected deaths reported in birth, death, and marriage (BDM) records for the half-year

\*\* However, brokerage hikes price target on stock to A$7.15 from A$7.00, saying it expects co to ramp up acquisitions in CY25

\*\* Also believes co has sufficient capacity to fund acquisitions; retains 'outperform' rating

\*\* Four analysts rate the stock "buy", 2 "hold"; their median PT is A$6.30, according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Stock rose 13.8% in 2024
