HealthStream said Thursday its CredentialStream application and CredentialStream Hub and Network have received HITRUST r2 certification, which it said is the highest level of health care data security.

Hospitals and health systems use the software as a service-based CredentialStream application for credentialing, privileging and enrollment, the company said.
