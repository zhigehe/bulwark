Following are the results of the Philippine Bureau of the Treasury's (BTr) auction of T-bills on Monday:

\*BTr fully awards 22 billion pesos ($379 million)

\* Tenders total 50.1 billion pesos

\* BTr awards 7 billion pesos of 91-day T-bills at avg rate of 5.128% versus previous auction avg of 5.101%

\* BTr awards 7 billion pesos of 182-day T-bills at avg rate of 5.562% versus previous auction avg of 5.477%

\* BTr awards 8 billion pesos of 364-day T-bills at avg rate of 5.726% versus previous auction avg of 5.671%

\* Details are on the BTr's website www.treasury.gov.ph

($1 = 58.10 Philippine pesos)
