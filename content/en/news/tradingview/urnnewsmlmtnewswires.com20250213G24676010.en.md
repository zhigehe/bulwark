Relx on Thursday launched a share buyback program worth 450 million pounds sterling, set to run until April 23.

The repurchases are part of the UK-based information analytics company's wider 1.5 billion-pound repurchase plan for 2025, according to a same-day release.

The repurchased shares will be held in treasury.
