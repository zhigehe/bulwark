Ningbo Zhongda Leader Intelligent Transmission plans to invest 200 million yuan for the construction of a precision core component production base in Cixi Intelligent Home Appliances High-tech Industrial Park in China, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange filing on Tuesday.

Factories and other ancillary facilities will be built within 24 months, the Chinese gearbox manufacturer said.

The company's shares soared more than 7% in recent trade.
