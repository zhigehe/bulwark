In Tokyo, the Nikkei 225 Index decreased 311 points or 0.79 percent on Friday.

Losses were driven by Otsuka Holdings (-8.98%), Sumitomo Heavy Inds. (-5.91%) and Ricoh (-5.89%).

Biggest rises came from Toppan (14.86%), Sony (8.53%) and Trend Micro (7.73%).
