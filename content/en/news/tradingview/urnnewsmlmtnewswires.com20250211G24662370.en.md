Ericsson (ERIC-B.ST, ERIC-A.ST) said Tuesday that the Aduna venture will enter into a partnership with Sinch to boost the ecosystem for network application programming interfaces.

The collaboration between the venture including the Swedish company and other global telecoms companies and the Swedish cloud communications provider aims to help expand the global implementation and access of network APIs, spurring innovation in digital services.

Aduna was launched in September 2024 to streamline and simplify global access to network APIs.
