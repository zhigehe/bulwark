Latvia’s current account surplus narrowed to EUR 64 million in December 2024, down from EUR 73 million in the same month of the previous year.

The goods account deficit widened to EUR 398 million from EUR 319 million a year earlier, while the primary income surplus decreased to EUR 60 million from EUR 147 million.

On the other hand, the services account surplus increased to EUR 240 million from EUR 177 million, as well as the secondary income surplus to EUR 162 million from EUR 67 million.
