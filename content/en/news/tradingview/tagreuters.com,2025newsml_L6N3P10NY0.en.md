\*\* U.S.-listed shares of Israeli software co Monday.com up 31.5% to $339.63 on Mon after qtrly results, outlook please

\*\* MNDY shares set for biggest daily pct ever, and hit highest levels since Dec 2021

\*\* Work-management firm early Mon posted Q4 rev of $268 mln, up 32% y/r, and adj EPS of $1.08 vs 65 cents/sh profit a yr ago. Analysts on avg had called for rev of $261.4 mln and adj EPS of 79 cents, per LSEG data

\*\* For current qtr, it expects rev of $274-$276 mln, up 26%-27% y/y, and for 2025 anticipates rev to grow 24%-26% to $1.208-$1.221 bln. That compares to consensus views of $274.3 mln and $1.21 bln, respectively

\*\* It also announced its platform called AI Vision and full release of its enterprise service management AI product offering

\*\* Oppenheimer, which rates MNDY 'outperform', raises its PT from $325 to $380

\*\* Co delivered strong Q4 results and encouraging view into 2025, Oppenheimer says in note, adding that AI is also an upside driver not included in guidance

\*\* Of 25 analysts covering MNDY, recommendation breakdown is 21 "strong buy" or "buy", rest "hold" and median PT is $310 - LSEG

\*\* With move on the session, stock up ~44% YTD after 25% gain in 2024
