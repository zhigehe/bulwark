Hangzhou Binjiang Real Estate Group acquired new land reserves in Jinhua, China, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

The company secured four plots for residential and commercial use, with Binjiang holding a 51% equity stake each.

The acquisition strengthens the company's land reserves and supports its sustainable development strategy.
