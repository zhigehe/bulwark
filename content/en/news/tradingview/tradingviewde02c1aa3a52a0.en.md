Nader Elias, CFO and SVP of Finance at QUICKLOGIC Corp, sold 2,843 shares of Common Stock on February 12, 2025, at a weighted average price of $6.7502 per share, totaling $19,190. The sale was conducted to cover taxes related to vested restricted stock units. Following the transaction, Elias directly owns 62,785 shares of QUICKLOGIC Corp.
