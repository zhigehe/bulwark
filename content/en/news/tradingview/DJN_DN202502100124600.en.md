Hong Kong benchmark Hang Seng Index is 0.1% higher at 21538.55, tracking Wall Street's gains overnight. The main theme overnight was risk-on, Commerzbank Research analysts say in a note. Investors have looked past U.S. President Trump's tariff threats on steel and aluminum, they write. BYD rises 3.8% after the company introduced a newdriving-assistance system for low-price mass-market vehicles on Monday. Other advancers include Sunny Optical Technology, which is up 6.2%, and Zijin Mining Group, which climbs 2.7%. Meanwhile, Geely Automobile loses 5.0% and Li Auto is down 3.3%.(amanda.lee@wsj.com)
