Hong Kong shares extend gains at midday thanks to positive sentiment across several sectors on the adoption of DeepSeek's AI model in their services. Tech and healthcare stocks are leading the gains with Alibaba Health Information Technology up 16%, Wuxi Biologics adding 8.15% and WuXi AppTec rising 5.8%. JD.com and Kuaishou Technology are up 4.95% and 4.7%, respectively. Among the few decliners, SMIC drops 2.9% and WH Group falls 2.3%. The benchmark Hang Seng Index is up 2.2% at 22303.80 and the Hang Seng Tech Index gains 3.1%. (jiahui.huang@wsj.com; @ivy\_jiahuihuang)
