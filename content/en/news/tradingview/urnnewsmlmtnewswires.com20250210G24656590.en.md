Polish Development Fund, or PFR, plans to make a takeover offer for Spanish train manufacturer Talgo .

The state-owned financial group said the launch of a public acquisition offer will be followed by a bid for a 40% Talgo stake held by Pegaso Transportation, according to a Feb. 8 filing published on Monday.

In a same-day release, PFR said the proposal to Pegaso was not submitted yet and is subject to approval from the former's governing bodies.
