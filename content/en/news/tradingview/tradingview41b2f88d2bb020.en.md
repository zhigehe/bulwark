Paul W. Breaux, serving as Vice President, General Counsel, Secretary, and Chief Compliance Officer of Carvana Co., has sold a total of 40,000 shares of Class A Common Stock across transactions on February 10 and 11, 2025, under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan. The sales were executed at weighted average prices of $270.08, $270.34, and $271.15, resulting in a total sale amount of $10,821,442. Following these transactions, Breaux directly owns 153,240 shares of Carvana Co.
