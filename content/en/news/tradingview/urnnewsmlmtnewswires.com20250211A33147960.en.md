Microsoft and Anduril Industries said Tuesday they have reached a new partnership through which Anduril will assume oversight of production, future development of software and hardware and delivery timelines for the US Army's Integrated Visual Augmentation System, or IVAS, program.

Microsoft Azure becomes Anduril's preferred hyperscale cloud platform for all workloads related to IVAS and Anduril AI technologies under the agreement.
