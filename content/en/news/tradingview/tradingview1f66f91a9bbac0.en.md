Robert V. Vitale, a Director at Bellring Brands, sold 5,100 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, for a total sale amount of $384,030. The sale was conducted at a weighted average price of $75.3 per share, with prices ranging from $75.13 to $75.55. This transaction was to cover tax obligations related to the vesting of restricted stock units. Following the sale, Vitale directly owns 1,166,691 shares and indirectly owns 298,504 shares through family trusts.
