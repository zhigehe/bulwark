Al Khabeer Diversified Income Traded Fund (SASE:4700) said Tuesday that it will distribute a cash dividend for the second half of 2024.

Unitholders of the fund as of Feb. 18 will receive 0.23 Saudi riyal per unit within 10 business days. It paid 0.29 riyal per unit a year ago.
