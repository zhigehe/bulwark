Bull-Dog Sauce acquired 38,300 shares for 67.8 million yen in January as part of its ongoing share buyback program, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The company set a buyback ceiling of 600,000 shares or 1 billion yen. The repurchase program was set from Oct. 28, 2024, to Oct. 27, 2025.
