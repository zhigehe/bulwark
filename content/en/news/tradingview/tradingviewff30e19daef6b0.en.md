Joan Bottarini, serving as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer at Hyatt Hotels Corp, sold 2,641 shares of Class A Common Stock on February 6, 2025, at a price of $164.0 per share, totaling $433,124. Following this transaction, Bottarini directly owns 7,404 shares of the company. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on September 16, 2024.
