Malaysia's foreign direct investment (FDI) increased to MYR 18.38 billion in Q4 of 2024 from MYR 14.52 billion in Q3, marking the largest amount since Q4 of 2023.

Most of the FDI flowed into the services sector, predominantly in the information & communication sub-sector and Financial activities.

This was followed by manufacturing, mining & quarrying, and agriculture sectors.

The main sources of FDI were the United States of America, Singapore, and Hong Kong.
