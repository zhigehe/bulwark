Physiomics raised 430,000 pounds sterling by placing 86,000,000 new ordinary shares at 0.005 pounds apiece, representing a 37.5% discount to its last closing price.

The British data science group also launched a retail offer via the Winterflood Retail Access Platform to raise up to 70,000 pounds by issuing 14,000,000 new ordinary shares at a similar price, according to a Thursday filing.

The company is also planning a new share placing to raise 500,000 pounds through a bookbuild process at the same per-share price.
