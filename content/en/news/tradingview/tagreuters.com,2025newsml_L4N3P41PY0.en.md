Canadian carrier WestJet and Germany's Lufthansa Technik have signed a multi-billion dollar agreement for engine maintenance services, the companies said on Thursday.

Lufthansa Technik will service CFM International's LEAP-1B engines on WestJet's Boeing 737 fleet, the companies said, adding that operations are set to begin in 2027.
