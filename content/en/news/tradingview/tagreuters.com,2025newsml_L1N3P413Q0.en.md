\*\* Shares of HubSpot rise up 5.6% at $827.21, hitting a record high on Thurs, following the marketing software firm's quarterly results that beat analyst expectations

\*\* HUBS pared gains after hitting intra-day high of $881.13. It is also on track for biggest daily pct gain since Nov

\*\* After the closing bell on Wed, HUBS reported EPS of $2.32 vs 2.19 estimated by Wall Street analysts; rev rose ~21% to $703.17 mln vs street estimate of $673.32 mln, per LSEG data

\*\* Multiple analysts, including from Oppenheimer, JPMorgan, Barclays, Wells Fargo, Stifel, Morgan Stanley, raised their PT for HUBS following the result

\*\* Among the 30 analysts cover HUBS, their avg rating is "BUY" and median PT is $900, up from $750 a month ago

\*\* HUBS is up ~12.7% YTD with today's move vs ~3% gain in the Nasdaq in the same period; Co gained 20% in 2024
