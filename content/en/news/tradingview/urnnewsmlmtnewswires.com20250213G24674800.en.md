The euro area's industrial production fell 1.1% month over month in December 2024, after a revised 0.4% rise in the previous month, Eurostat said Thursday.

The consensus estimate for the month was a decline of 0.6%.

On a yearly basis, industrial production was down 2%, compared with the revised 1.8% decrease in November 2024 and the expected 3.1% drop.
