Alexander H. Rogers, President of QTL & Global Affairs at QUALCOMM, sold 19,287 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, for a total of $3,275,552. The transactions were executed at prices ranging from $169.1 to $170.82, with specific sales at $169.719 and $170.2829, indicating a weighted average price. These sales were conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on February 7, 2024. Following these transactions, Rogers directly owns 20,762 shares of QUALCOMM common stock, which includes 94 shares acquired under the company's Employee Stock Purchase Plan on January 31, 2025.
