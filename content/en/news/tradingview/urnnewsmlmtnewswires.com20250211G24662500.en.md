Shandong Minhe Animal Husbandry earned 62.7 million yuan in chick sales revenue in January, up 19% from the prior year, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure on Tuesday.

The Chinese chicken breeder sold 25.8 million chicks during the month, jumping 47% from a year earlier, the disclosure said.

The company's shares fell more than 2% at the close.
