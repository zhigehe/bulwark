Intercontinental Exchange said Monday it has launched a new service offering intraday price updates and bond analytics data for Canadian fixed income securities.

The offering is through an agreement with CanDeal DNA, a provider of Canadian fixed income and OTC derivatives pricing and analytics data, it said.

The company said it can now provide pricing, spreads and standard analytics fields updating at hourly intervals.
