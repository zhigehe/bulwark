Andreas P. Wagner, Chief Human Resources Officer at Alignment Healthcare, sold 14,878 shares of Common Stock on February 12, 2025, at a price of $14.38 per share, totaling $213,945. This sale was to cover tax withholding obligations related to the vesting of restricted stock units. Following this transaction, Wagner directly owns 200,885 shares of Alignment Healthcare.
