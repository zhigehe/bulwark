ChampionX expects new U.S. import tariffs on steel and aluminium to drive up equipment costs for oilfield service firms, a company official told the India Energy Week conference on Tuesday.

The company is still committed to closing a merger deal with top oilfield services company SLB in the first quarter, Steve Davoren, regional business development director, said.
