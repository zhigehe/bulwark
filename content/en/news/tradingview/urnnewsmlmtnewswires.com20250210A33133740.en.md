MediPharm Labs on Monday said it has terminated its agreement to sell its Napanee facility in Ontario to Kensana Health because the buyer did not meet the required conditions.

MediPharm, which will retain certain deposits received from Kensana Health, said operations at the Napanee Facility, are expected to continue without disruption.

"Growing our profitable international revenue was a critical element of our success in 2024. We will continue to use the Napanee Facility to capitalize on international opportunities in 2025," said David Pidduck, CEO of MediPharm Labs.
