Central China Management contracted sales for projects under the group's management fell 19.2% year over year to 919 million yuan in January.

Contracted gross floor area declined 14.6% to 157,227 square meters, according to a Wednesday filing with the Hong Kong Exchange.

The average selling price for the month was 5,843 yuan per square meter, the filing stated.
