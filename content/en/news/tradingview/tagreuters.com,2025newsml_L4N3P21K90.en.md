\*\* Shares of air-taxi maker Archer Aviation slide as much as 10.3%; last down 7.3% at $9.46

\*\* ACHR says it has raised $301.8 million in financing from institutional investors including BlackRock , the world's largest asset manager

\*\* Co expects the financing to bolster the development of its hybrid aircraft platform and fortify its balance sheet

\*\* Capital raise brings ACHR's total liquidity to ~$1 billion

\*\* Investors have agreed to purchase 35.5 million ACHR shares at $8.50/shr in a registered direct stock offering

\*\* That represents a 16.7% discount to ACHR's last close

\*\* Co has 489.2 million outstanding shares for $4.99 billion market capitalization as of last close, according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* ACHR stock jumped 58.8% in 2024
