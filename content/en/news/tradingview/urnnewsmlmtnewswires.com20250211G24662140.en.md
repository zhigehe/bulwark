Medical imaging company Sectra (SECT-B.ST) received a contract from Sweden's Region Västra Götaland for its digital pathology product.

The healthcare region comprises 17 hospitals and pathology labs in three locations, according to a Tuesday release. Sectra said the pathology contract was signed in January and is an extension of a previous 20-year medical imaging contract.
