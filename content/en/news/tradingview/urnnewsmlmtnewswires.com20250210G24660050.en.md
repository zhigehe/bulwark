Euronext (, ENXP.LS) said Monday that cash trading volumes for January increased year over year.

The bourse operator's cash trading volumes were 253.83 billion euros, compared with 206.22 billion euros, previously.

On a daily average, cash trading volumes in the reporting month rose to 11.54 billion euros from 9.37 billion euros a year ago.
