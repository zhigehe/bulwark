Hong Kong shares closed higher, supported by tech and property stocks. Market sentiment was supported by positive news on Alibaba and China Vanke. Alibaba Group gained 8.5% after reports of its partnership with Apple on providing AI services to its Chinese iPhone users. China Vanke rose 17% after a media report saying that China is mulling a plan of funding $6.8 billion to help the developer repay debt. Chinese property stocks broadly rose after the news, with Longfor Group jumping 8.0% and China Overseas Land & Investment gaining 5.8%. Among the few decliners, Meituan dropped 4.1% and JD.com lost 1.5%. The benchmark Hang Seng Index closed 2.6% higher at 21857.92 and the Hang Seng Tech Index gained 2.7%.
