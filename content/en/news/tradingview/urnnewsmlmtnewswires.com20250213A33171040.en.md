Health care stocks were mixed Thursday afternoon, with the NYSE Health Care Index easing 0.1% and the Health Care Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLV) increasing 0.1%.

The iShares Biotechnology ETF (IBB) rose 0.5%.

In sector news, the US Senate confirmed Robert F. Kennedy Jr. as the next head of the US Department of Health and Human Services, Bloomberg reported.

In corporate news, Pliant Therapeutics' shares surged 23% after the company said it has begun assembling an external expert panel to evaluate unblinded data from the ongoing BEACON/IPF Phase 2b trial of bexotegrast in people with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis.
