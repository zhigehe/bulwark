Verint said Wednesday it is expanding its operations in Bangalore, India, after significant progress in customer experience automation and delivering measurable artificial intelligence business outcomes.

The company said it plans to increase its Bangalore workforce to about 1,000 employees by the end of 2026.

The expansion will focus on recruiting top talent in AI, data science, research, and related fields, it added.
