Align Technology said Monday its Invisalign Palatal Expander System is now commercially available in Turkey.

The company said the Turkish Medicines and Medical Device Agency approved the system for broad patient applicability, including children, teens and adults.

Align described the system as a 3D printed alternative to traditional palatal expanders.

The company's shares were up 2.5% in recent trading.
