BELGRADE (Serbia), February 13 (SeeNews) - A1 Telekom Austria Group said its Serbian unit's earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortisation (EBITDA) rose to 146.2 million euro ($152.3 million) in 2024 from 126.9 million euro in the prior year.

In 2024, total revenue of A1 Srbija increased to 397.8 million euro from 382.5 million euro in 2023, as service revenues rose to 309.7 million euro from 279.7 million euro, A1 Telekom Austria said in an annual financial report published on Tuesday.

A1 Srbija's EBITDA margin stood at 36.8% at the end of 2024, up from 33.2% in the year before.

At the end of 2024, A1 Srbija had a total of 2.368 million mobile subscribers, down from 2.438 million at the end of 2023.

A1 Srbija, previously known as Vip Mobile, was the third largest mobile services provider in Serbia at the end of the third quarter of 2024, with a 23.74% market share, according to the most recent data published by the country's telecommunications agency.
