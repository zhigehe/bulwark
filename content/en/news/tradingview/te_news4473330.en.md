Mexico’s auto exports slipped by 13.7% from a year earlier to 219,414 units in January 2025, following a 5.8% decline in December and marking the lowest level for the month since 2022.

Among major export brands, shipments plunged for General Motors (-23.8% to 48,778), Nissan (-22.9% to 33,807), Stellantis (-57.6% to 15,035) and Volkswagen (-31.7% to 11,950).

The United States was the main destination for light vehicle exports, accounting for 83.6% of total shipments, followed by Canada (9.9%).
