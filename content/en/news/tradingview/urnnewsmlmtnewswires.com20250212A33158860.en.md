Tech stocks were mixed late Wednesday afternoon, with the Technology Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLK) decreasing 0.2% and the SPDR S&P Semiconductor ETF (XSD) fractionally higher.

The Philadelphia Semiconductor index added 0.2%.

In corporate news, Apple's TV+ streaming video service is set to be made available to Android phone users, Bloomberg reported. Apple shares rose 1.7%.
