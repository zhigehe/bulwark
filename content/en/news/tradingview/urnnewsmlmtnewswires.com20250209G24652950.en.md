WiseTech Global said it received two confidential complaints, one from an employee and one from a supplier, concerning Founder and Founding Chief Executive Richard White, according to a Monday filing with the Australian bourse.

The complaints are being reviewed as part of an ongoing board evaluation, the filing said.

Both WiseTech and White continue to operate under the announced terms, the company said.
