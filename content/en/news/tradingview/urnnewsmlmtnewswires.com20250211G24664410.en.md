Osaka Soda reported a profit attributable to owners of parent of 7.94 billion yen for the nine months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, up 49.9% from a year earlier, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Earnings per share were 62.59 yen, compared with 41.63 yen in the prior year.

Sales climbed 6.3% to 74.65 billion yen.

For the full year ending March 31, the company expects attributable profit to rise 50.3% to 11.50 billion yen, with sales increasing 8.9% to 103 billion yen. EPS is forecast at 90.65 yen.

Osaka Soda plans to pay a year-end dividend of 10 yen per share, reflecting a stock split in October 2024. The total dividend for the year is equivalent to 95 yen per share on a pre-split basis.

Osaka Soda shares were down over 9% in Wednesday's morning trade.
