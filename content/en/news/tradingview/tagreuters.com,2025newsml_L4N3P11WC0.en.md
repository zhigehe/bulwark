\*\* Shares of Australian home appliances maker Breville Group down 0.8% at A$37.42

\*\* Stock hits lowest level since January 15

\*\* BRG's forecast for FY25 EBIT growth of 5% to 10% may be slightly underwhelming (or conservative), given Visible Alpha consensus implies 12% growth taking into account the slightly stronger first half, Citi says

\*\* Breville's H1 FY25 net profit after tax of A$97.5 million ($61.23 million) is 1% above Visible Alpha consensus of A$96.6 million - Citi

\*\* Brokerage says interim dividend of 18 Australian cents per share in line with the consensus

\*\* Six of 14 analysts rate the stock "buy", six "hold" and two "sell"; median PT is A$33.09 - data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Shares up 6.6% this year, as of last close

($1 = 1.5924 Australian dollars)
