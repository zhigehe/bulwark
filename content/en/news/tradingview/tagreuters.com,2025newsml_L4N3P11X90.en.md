\*\* Shares of Australian diversified investor SGH rise as much as 9.5% to a record high of A$53.250

\*\* Stock last up 7.8% at A$52.480, among top 3 gainers in the ASX200 benchmark index

\*\* Co says 1H25 net profit attributable more than doubled to A$523.5 million ($328.23 million), comfortably beating Visible Alpha consensus of A$482.7 million

\*\* Maintains FY25 outlook of high single-digit EBIT growth

\*\* Says Boral EBIT A$259 million, up 29%; Boral is a company owned by SGH

\*\* Stock up 13.1% this year, including current session's moves

($1 = 1.5949 Australian dollars)
