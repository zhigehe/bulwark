Dynatrace Director, Stephen J Lifshatz, sold 2,500 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on March 1, 2024. The shares were sold at a weighted average price of $60.8827, totaling $152,206. Following the transaction, Lifshatz directly owns 36,471 shares of Dynatrace.
