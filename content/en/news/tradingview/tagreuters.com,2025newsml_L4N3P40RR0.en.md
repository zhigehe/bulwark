\*\* Shares of Bharat Dynamics climb 3.4% to 1,175.50 rupees

\*\* Stock set to snap five-session losing streak

\*\* India expects to sell its short-range missiles to Philippines this year in a deal worth over $200 million, Reuters reports citing sources

\*\* BARA, the manufacturer of the missiles, was one of the exhibitors at last year's Asian Defense and Security Exhibition in Manila

\*\* Co and India's defence ministry did not immediately respond to Reuters' requests for comment

\*\* Philippine defence spokesperson declined to comment on deal's specifics or procurement plans but said the country's armed forces had "manifested it requires these capabilities"
