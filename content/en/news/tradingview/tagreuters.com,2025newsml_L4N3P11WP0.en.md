\*\* Shares of Ramsay Health Care rise 4.6% to A$35.81, their highest level since December 18 last year

\*\* RHC says it expects 1H25 underlying net profit after tax (NPAT) attributable between A$150 million ($94.16 million) and A$160 million

\*\* Company's expected NPAT comes ahead of Visible Alpha consensus of A$142.6 million and last year's A$140.4 million

\*\* Company flags goodwill and other asset impairment of A$305 million for its UK segment in 1H25 accounts

\*\* Says Ramsay UK Hospitals valuation has increased

\*\* RHC stock largely flat so far this year, including the day's moves

($1 = 1.5931 Australian dollars)
