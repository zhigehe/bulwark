Michael J. Hartnett, President and CEO of RBC Bearings, sold 3,000 shares of common stock on February 6 and 7, 2025, for a total sale amount of $1,103,375. The selling prices per share were $367.95 and $367.00, respectively. Following these transactions, Hartnett directly owns 319,588 shares of RBC Bearings, including 48,297 shares of restricted stock subject to vesting schedules.
