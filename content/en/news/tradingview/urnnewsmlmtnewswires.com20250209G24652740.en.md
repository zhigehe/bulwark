ID Holdings will merge four wholly owned subsidiaries on April 1 to streamline operations and strengthen client engagement, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Information Development will absorb ID Data Center Management, DX Consulting, and ID AI Factory. The move aims to integrate services and enhance synergies in high-margin businesses, the company said.

ID will continue under Takuya Miyazawa, focusing on system management, cybersecurity, and consulting.
