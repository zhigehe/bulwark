Casella Waste Systems reported Q4 adjusted diluted earnings Wednesday of $0.41 per share, compared to $0.29 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.16.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $427.5 million, up from $359.6 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $418.9 million.

The company said it expects full-year 2025 net income of $10 million to $25 million on revenue of between $1.78 billion and $1.81 billion.

Analysts polled by FactSet expect $53.6 million on revenue of $1.74 billion.
