Beijing Dabeinong Technology generated 1.18 billion yuan in revenues from pig sales in January, higher than 924 million yuan earned a year earlier, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure on Monday.

The Chinese agricultural biotechnology products developer sold 694,900 pigs for an average of 15.34 yuan per kilogram during the month, compared to 635,000 pigs sold for 13.66 yuan per kilogram in the previous year, the disclosure said.

The company's shares fell less than 2% in recent trade.
