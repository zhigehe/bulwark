HealthEquity Director, Robert W. Selander, sold a total of 5,750 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, for a total sale amount of $639,926. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $110.9826 and $111.872, with prices ranging for the first sale from $110.62 to $111.60, and for the second sale from $111.62 to $112.50. Following these transactions, Selander directly owns 78,219 shares of HealthEquity common stock. These transactions were executed pursuant to a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on July 09, 2024.
