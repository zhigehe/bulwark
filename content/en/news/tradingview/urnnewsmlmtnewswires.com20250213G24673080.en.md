The UK's quarterly gross domestic product grew 0.1% in the fourth quarter of 2024, after flatlining in the prior three-month period, preliminary data from the Office for National Statistics showed Thursday.

Analysts expected a 0.1% fall for the quarter.

On a yearly basis, the British economy grew 1.4%, faster than the revised 1% increase earlier and the consensus estimate of a 1.1% jump.
