The medical device manufacturer's earnings per share increased to 66.53 yen from 53.66 yen a year ago, according to a Thursday filing.

Revenue grew 13.1% to 772.2 billion yen from nearly 683 billion yen a year earlier.

Terumo forecasted attributable profit to increase 17.0% to 124.5 billion yen, or 84.10 yen per share, and net sales to rise 9.6% to 1.01 trillion yen for the fiscal year ending March 31.

It declared a year-end dividend of 13 yen per share, bringing the total annual dividend to 26 yen per share, adjusted for a two-for-one stock split.

Shares of the company declined over 1% in recent trade.
