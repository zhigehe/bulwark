Cordlife's group chief executive officer, Yiu Pang Fai, will step down from the role on Feb. 28, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Wednesday.

Yiu is leaving the role to pursue other opportunities but will remain available to ensure a "smooth handover of his remaining responsibilities," the filing added.
