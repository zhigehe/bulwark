Yara International's Yara Clean Ammonia unit signed a time-charter contract with Japanese shipping company Nippon Yusen Kabushiki Kaisha for an ammonia-fueled medium gas carrier.

The vessel will be delivered in November 2026, helping the Norwegian crop nutrition group's ammonia distributor cut greenhouse gas emissions and transport the chemical in a more environment-friendly way, according to a Monday release.
