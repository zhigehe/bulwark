South Korean retailer E-Mart posted a net loss attributable to shareholders of 591.7 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, widening from 53.7 billion won a year earlier.

Sales fell 1.4% year over year to 7.25 trillion won from 7.356 trillion won, according to a Tuesday Korea Exchange filing.

Shares of E-Mart rose nearly 4% in recent trade on Thursday.
