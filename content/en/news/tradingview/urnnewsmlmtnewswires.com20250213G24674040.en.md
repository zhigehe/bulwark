Commerzbank expects its net interest income in 2025 to range from 7.7 billion euros to 7.9 billion euros, subject to the interest rate development and the related fair value movement.

The German bank on Thursday reported a net interest income of 8.33 billion euros for 2024.

Meanwhile, Commerzbank projects a net result of 2.8 billion euros before restructuring charges for its transformation and 2.4 billion euros after charges. In 2024, net result attributable to shareholders and investors in additional equity components was 2.68 billion euros.
