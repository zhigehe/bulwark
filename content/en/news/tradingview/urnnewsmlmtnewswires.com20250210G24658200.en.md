Mobvoi integrated the latest reasoning model developed by DeepSeek, the DeepSeek-R1, into the full range of its artificially intelligent products, a Monday bourse filing said.

The company will be using Huawei Cloud's Ascend Cloud Service along with the DeepSeek-R1 model to renew the user experience of its various domestic and overseas products.
