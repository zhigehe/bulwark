Kunshan Asia Aroma plans to sell its 95% stake in subsidiary Kunshan Haolirui Environmental Technology to Kunshan Chengdong Chemical for 61 million yuan, according to a Monday filing with the Shenzhen Bourse.

The transaction aligns with the company's strategic goals and will optimize resource allocation. Following completion, Asia Aroma will no longer hold equity in Haolirui. The deal is not classified as a major asset restructuring.
