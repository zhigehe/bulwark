Ellipsiz's profit attributable to owners of the company surged 19% in fiscal H1 to SG$1.9 million from SG$1.6 million a year earlier, according to a Monday filing with the Singapore Exchange.

Earnings per share rase to SG$0.0112 from SG$0.0093 in the comparable period.

Revenue was up 17% year over year to SG$27.9 million from SG$23.8 million, mainly due to higher sales of equipment, chemicals, spare parts and consumables.

Shares of the company surged nearly 39% in recent trading.
