Radware reported Q4 adjusted earnings Wednesday of $0.27 per diluted share, up from $0.13 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.24.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $73.0 million, up from $65.0 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $71.4 million.
