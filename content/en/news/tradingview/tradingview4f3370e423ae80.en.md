Robert Mehrabian, Executive Chairman and Director at Teledyne Technologies, sold 31,537 shares of common stock across five transactions on February 6 and 7, 2025, totaling $16,153,640. The sales were executed at weighted average prices ranging from $511.0225 to $514.695. Following these transactions, Mehrabian directly owns 144,553 shares of Teledyne Technologies. The ownership includes both direct and indirect holdings, with 4,355 shares held directly and 140,198 shares held indirectly through The Mehrabian Living Trust.
