Julius Baer Group said Tuesday it placed a $400 million perpetual non-cumulative additional tier 1 security for general corporate purposes.

The 7.500% securities have a first reset date of Feb. 19, 2031, and the offering was made to private banks and institutional investors in Europe and Asia.
