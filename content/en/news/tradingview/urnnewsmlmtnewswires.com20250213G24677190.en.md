Besra Gold said its Chair Dato' Lim Khong Soon has agreed to step aside from his position, according to a Friday Australian bourse filing.

The company's board has appointed Jon Morda as non-executive chair on an interim basis, the filing added.

The company shares were down 6% in recent trade.
