Edil San Felice (ESF.MI) plans to offer a non-convertible, sustainability-linked bond of up to 4 million euros by no later than March 31.

The issue will comprise 40 bonds with a nominal value of 100,000 euros each and a maturity date of five years, according to a Monday release.

Proceeds will be used to support the Italian infrastructure maintenance operator's investment plan for 2024 to 2027, including projects related to the low-carbon economy shift.
