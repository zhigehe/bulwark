\*\* Shares of automotive parts maker Amotiv Ltd fall 9.2% to A$9.630, their lowest since July 25, 2023

\*\* Stock posts biggest intraday drop since February 14, 2024

\*\* AOV biggest loser in the ASX200 benchmark index

\*\* Posts 1H25 underlying NPAT of A$58.9 million ($37.07 million), down from A$51.4 million last year

\*\* That missed Visible Alpha consensus of A$61.3 million

\*\* Says growth in FY25 group revenue, underlying EBITA expected to be driven by slightly stronger skew in 2H25

\*\* More than 390,000 shares change hands, 2 times the 30-day average of 192,473 shares

\*\* Stock down more than 9% YTD, including session's moves
