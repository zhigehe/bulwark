Liberty Latin America said late Monday its Cable & Wireless credit silo has priced $755 million worth of 9% senior notes due 2033.

Net proceeds will be used to redeem Cable & Wireless' $735 million senior notes due 2027, the company said.

The issuance is expected to close on Tuesday.
