Auric Mining said the due diligence timeframe for its exclusive right to buy the Burbanks Plant at Coolgardie in Western Australia has been extended to Feb. 28 from Feb. 10, according to a Monday filing with the Australian bourse.

The transaction has a purchase price of AU$4.4 million.

The company's shares were down almost 1% in recent Tuesday trade.
