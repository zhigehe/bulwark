Chinese steel manufacturer Anshan Iron and Steel and CRRC Dalian completed the delivery of the country's first batch of hybrid diesel locomotives, Xinhua News Agency reported Saturday.

The locomotives are able to switch power modes depending on different scenarios using its dual power systems and centralized control units, the report said.

The two companies jointly developed the locomotives.
