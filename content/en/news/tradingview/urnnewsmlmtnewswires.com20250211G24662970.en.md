Italian gas distributor Italgas (IG.MI) signed an agreement with the University of Pavia for research projects that aim to improve the security and resilience of gas distribution networks.

The collaboration will focus on gas distribution networks and strategies to help reduce the impact of natural hazards and integrated monitoring and early warning systems for geohazards, among others, according to a Tuesday release.
