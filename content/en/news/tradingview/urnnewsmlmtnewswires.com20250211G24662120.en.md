Shandong Oriental Ocean Sci-Tech said its subsidiaries, Mass Spectrometry Biotechnology and Avic Bio, received medical device registration certificates from the Shandong Drug Administration, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

Approved products include catecholamine quality control, calibration solutions, and a fecal occult blood/transferrin test kit.
