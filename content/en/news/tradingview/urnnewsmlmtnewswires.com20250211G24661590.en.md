Beijing Easpring Material Technology signed a strategic cooperation framework agreement with Zhongwei New Materials on Monday, according to a same-day filing with the Shenzhen Bourse.

The partnership focuses on resource development, battery material R&D, and supply chain collaboration.

Both parties aim to enhance industrial synergy and market competitiveness.

The agreement is preliminary and non-binding, with future contracts needed for specific cooperation details.
