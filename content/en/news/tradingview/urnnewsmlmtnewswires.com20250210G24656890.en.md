Seacon Shipping Group unit Golden Dahlia agreed to sell a 24,576 gross tonnage chemical/oil carrier to Union Maritime for nearly $32.9 million, a Feb. 7 bourse filing said.

The vessel, which is currently chartered to the seller by Bright Rose Shipping, will first be acquired from the latter through the exercise of a purchase option for a maximum of around $21 million.

The exercise of the purchase option and the disposal come about in order for the shipping company to optimize its vessel fleet.
