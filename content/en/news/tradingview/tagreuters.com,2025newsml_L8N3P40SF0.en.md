\*\* Shares in Lectra jump 7.5% after the French IT services and consulting company posted FY results on Wednesday, including "excellent" free cash flow (FCF) and "attractive" outlook

\*\* Reported an almost 70% jump of its FCF to 22.1 million euros at the end of Q4

\*\* Q4 sales of 132.5 million and EBITDA of 22.6 million were broadly in line, says Midcap Partners

\*\* Lectra sees 2025 revenues between 550 and 600 million euros, with an EBITDA margin close to 20%

\*\* "The positive surprise came from excellent cash generation," says the brokerage

\*\* "A solid publication with an outlook (in terms of growth, operating leverage and cash generation) which remains attractive," it adds

\*\* Stock tracks its best day since end-October, if gains hold
