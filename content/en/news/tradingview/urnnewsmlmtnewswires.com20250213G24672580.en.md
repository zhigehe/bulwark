Siemens said Thursday that its net income and revenue for the fiscal first quarter of 2025 rose year over year.

Net income attributable to shareholders for the three months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 3.71 billion euros, compared with 2.39 billion euros a year ago. EPS moved to 4.66 euros from 2.99 euros.

The German technology group's revenue was 18.35 billion euros, compared with 17.75 billion euros earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet were expecting 18.08 billion euros in sales, 3.54 billion euros in net income, and 3.23 euros in EPS.

Looking ahead, Siemens reiterated its outlook for fiscal 2025 of comparable revenue growth between 3% and 7% and basic EPS between 10.40 euros and 11 euros.
