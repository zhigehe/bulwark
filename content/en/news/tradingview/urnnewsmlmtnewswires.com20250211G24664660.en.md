Predictive Discovery said that the placement of AU$69.2 million to the Lundin family and Zijin Mining Group has been completed, according to a Wednesday Australian bourse filing.

Proceeds will be used to advance the Bankan gold project, the company said in a filing in early February.

Company shares were down past 1% in recent Wednesday trade.
