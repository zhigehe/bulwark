ParaZero Technologies said Thursday it closed a $3.1 million registered direct offering of shares and pre-funded warrants.

The company said it sold about 2.5 million ordinary shares at $1.10 apiece and 300,000 pre-funded warrants at $1.09999 each.

Net proceeds from the offering will be used for general corporate purposes and working capital, ParaZero added.

Shares of the company were up 3.4% in recent trading.
