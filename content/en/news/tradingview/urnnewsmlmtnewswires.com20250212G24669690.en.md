Banco Bilbao Vizcaya Argentaria's BBVA Corporate & Investment Banking is restructuring its business to strengthen its position in advanced financial services.

The Spanish financial services company said Wednesday it will combine the unit's startups and venture capital investors-focused financial services and debt business, Spark, with the group's Digital Assets & Blockchain strategy.
