Naver posted net income attributable to shareholders of 508.6 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, up 62.1% from 313.8 billion won a year earlier.

The internet giant's sales rose 13.7% year over year to 2.886 trillion won from 2.537 trillion won, according to a Friday filing with KIND.

Visible Alpha estimated Q4 sales of 2.885 trillion won.

Shares of Naver rose 2% in recent trade.
