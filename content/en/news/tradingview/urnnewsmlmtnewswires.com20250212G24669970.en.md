Appier Group will acquire AdCreative.ai, a French AI-powered ad creative platform, making it a wholly owned subsidiary, according to a Wednesday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The move aims to accelerate generative AI innovation, strengthen data capabilities, and expand market reach in the US and Europe.

The acquisition, valued at $28.2 million, includes 14,328 shares and an earn-out clause. AdCreative.ai specializes in AI-driven advertising optimization, with a strong client base across retail, e-commerce, and enterprise sectors.

The share transfer is set for March 4.
