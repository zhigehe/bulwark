Anticipated adjustment due to extraordinary dividend in Orkla (44/25)

The following information is based on the press release from Orkla ASA (Orkla)published on February 13, 2025 and may be subject to change.

The Board of Directors of Orkla has announced an intention to propose that theAnnual General Meeting (AGM) scheduled for April 24, 2025, approves anextraordinary dividend of NOK 6.00 per share in addition to an ordinarydividend of NOK 4.00 per share. The Ex-date is yet to be determined. Providedthat the AGM approves the proposal, NASDAQ Derivatives Markets will carry out are-calculation of options, regular and gross return forwards/futures in Orkla(ORKN).
