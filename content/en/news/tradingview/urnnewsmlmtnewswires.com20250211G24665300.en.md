Trial Holdings reported a 3.6% year-over-year increase in January sales, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Customer traffic rose 2.5%, and average spending grew 1.1%.

Fresh foods and daily items spearheaded the New Year sales season, resulting in a strong start with a 12.2% sales increase at comparable stores and a 22.3% rise at all stores in the first five days of the year.

The company opened three Super Centers in Iizuka Shonai (Fukuoka Pref.) and Mashikidai (Kumamoto Pref.) during the month under review.

The store count stood at 342 at the end of January.
