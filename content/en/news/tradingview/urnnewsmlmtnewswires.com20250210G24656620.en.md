Cloetta (CLA-B.ST) canceled the planned investment in a greenfield plant project in the Netherlands.

The Swedish confectionary company said Monday the move is due to increased risk related to energy supply and the ongoing permitting process. Instead, the company intends to optimize its existing manufacturing network.
