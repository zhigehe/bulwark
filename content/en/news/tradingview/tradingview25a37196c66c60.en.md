Joan Lordi Amble, a Director at Booz Allen Hamilton Holding Corp, purchased 930 shares of Class A Common Stock on February 5, 2025, at a price of $130.9 per share, totaling $121,737. Following this transaction, Amble directly owns 56,860 shares of the company's Class A Common Stock, which includes shares of Class A restricted common stock.
