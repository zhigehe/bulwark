CRISIL posted an increase in its consolidated net profit to 2.25 billion Indian rupees in the fiscal third quarter ended Dec. 31, 2024, against 2.10 billion rupees a year ago.

Earnings per share came in at 30.72 rupees compared with the year-ago figure of 28.73 rupees, according to a Monday filing to the Indian stock exchanges by the analytics and ratings company.

Revenue from operations in fiscal Q3, however, slipped to 9.13 billion rupees from 9.18 billion rupees a year earlier.

The company's board has also recommended final dividend of 26 rupees per equity share of face value of 1 rupee each, for the financial year ended Dec. 31, 2024.
