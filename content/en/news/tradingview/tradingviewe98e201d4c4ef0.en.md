Alset's Chief Executive Officer and 10% Owner, Ambrose Chan, has recently purchased a total of 291,060 shares of common stock. These transactions occurred over two days, with 141,060 shares bought at a weighted average price of $0.99 on February 6, 2025, and 150,000 shares at $1.03 on February 7, 2025, totaling a purchase amount of $294,149. Following these transactions, Chan directly owns 6,329,498 shares, with an additional 319,000 shares held indirectly through HFE Holdings Limited, summing up to a beneficial ownership of 6,648,498 shares.
