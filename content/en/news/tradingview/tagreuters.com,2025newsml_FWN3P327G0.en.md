NCAB Group AB (publ) :

* PROPOSES A DIVIDEND OF SEK 1.10 (1.10) PER SHARE TO BE PAID IN MAY.
* PROFITABILITY IN Q4 WAS NEGATIVELY AFFECTED BY LOW NET SALES.
* WE CAN OBSERVE A GRADUAL IMPROVEMENT IN MARKET CONDITIONS IN SOME COUNTRIES, IN LINE WITH MACROECONOMIC SITUATION
* ECONOMY REMAINS WEAK IN MAJOR EUROPEAN COUNTRIES, WHICH MEANS WE EXPECT A WEAK START TO YEAR IN THOSE MARKETS
* WE CONTINUE TO INVEST IN OUR LONG-TERM GROWTH AND TO DEVELOP LEADERSHIP IN TECHNOLOGY AND SUSTAINABILITY
* WE CONTINUE TO ENGAGE IN POSITIVE ACQUISITION DISCUSSIONS.
