Gerresheimer's executive board confirmed early-stage discussions regarding the company's potential sale to private equity investors.

According to a Feb. 7 filing, the investors informally expressed an intent to launch a public takeover offer for the German drug packaging and delivery systems company.

The board cautioned that the talks are still at a preliminary stage and there is no guarantee that a transaction will be carried out.
