Dunelm Group said Tuesday profit and revenue for the fiscal first half rose year over year.

Profit for the 26 weeks ended Dec. 28, 2024, was 91.6 million pounds sterling, compared with 90.7 million pounds for the 26 weeks ended Dec. 30, 2023. EPS moved to 0.450 pound from 0.446 pound.

The home furnishings retailer's revenue was 893.7 million pounds, compared with 872.5 million pounds a year ago.

Meanwhile, the board declared an interim dividend of 0.165 pound per share and a special dividend of 0.35 pound per share for the period, with both payable April 8 to shareholders on record March 14. It paid an interim dividend of 0.16 pound per share and a special dividend of 0.35 pound per share a year ago.

The group continues to expect pretax profit for the full fiscal year to be in line with consensus expectations of 204 million pounds to 214 million pounds.
