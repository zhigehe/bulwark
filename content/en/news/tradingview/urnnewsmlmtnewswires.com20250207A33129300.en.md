Cornish Metals Friday said its closed the initial tranche of a private placement of shares, raising 10.7-million British pounds.

The company said it placed 133.82-million shares in the tranche.

Proceeds will be used to ensure to complete the shaft refurbishment and de-watering process, early project works, and long lead items ahead of a formal final investment decision at its South Crofty tin mine.

The company's shares were last seen up $0.005 to $0.15 on the TSX Venture Exchange.
