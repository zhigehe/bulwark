Real estate developer Kesar India has incorporated a step-down subsidiary named Propsnap Realty to diversify its business in construction and real estate, according to a Monday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The new entity is completely owned by YK Infracon, a wholly-owned subsidiary of the company.

It will deal in the business of estate agents, assessors, project managers, and monitors, of building construction works, property sales, and business transfer agents, among other works.
