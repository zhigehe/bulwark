Richard Todd Schwartz, the Chief Executive Officer and Director of Rush Street Interactive, sold 193,905 shares of Class A Common Stock across two transactions on February 10 and 11, 2025, for a total sale amount of $2,778,242. The sales were executed at weighted average prices of $14.2237 and $14.5603 per share, respectively. Following these transactions, Schwartz directly owns 2,191,943 shares of the company's Class A Common Stock. The sales were conducted under a 10b5-1 Plan established on August 16, 2024.
