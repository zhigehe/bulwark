\*\* Shares of Orora Ltd fall 6.2% to A$2.195, their lowest level since August 12

\*\* ORA fell as much as 11.11% to A$2.080 earlier in the day; marks its worst intraday drop since April 2 last year

\*\* ORA among top losers in the ASX200 benchmark index

\*\* 1H25 underlying operating earnings A$120.8 million ($75.83 million) misses consensus of A$128 million

\*\* 1H25 revenue of A$1.03 billion also misses consensus

\*\* Citi calls it a challenging result, says it will "take some time to digest near term"

\*\* Stock down 9.6% this year, including the day's move

($1 = 1.5931 Australian dollars)
