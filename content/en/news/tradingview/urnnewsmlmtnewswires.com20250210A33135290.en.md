Global Industrial said Monday that Anesa Chaibi has been named chief executive officer, effective Feb. 17.

Chaibi most recently served as chief transformation officer at Coalesce Capital Management, a private equity firm, the company said.

Richard Leeds, who has served as interim CEO, will step down from the role but remain the board's executive chairman.
