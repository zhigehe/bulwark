Total orders received by Swedish industrial grew by 5.8% year-on-year in December 2024, accelerating from a downwardly revised 5% gain in the previous month.

Orders surged from the foreign market (16.5% vs 7% in November), while it fell sharply from domestic demand (-10.8% vs 2.1%).

On a seasonally adjusted monthly basis, new orders advanced 2% in December 2024, slowing from a downwardly revised 2.7% growth in the previous month.

For the entire year of 2024, total orders received by Swedish industry were 2.9% lower compared to the previous year, with the domestic market decreasing by 5.5% and the foreign market declining by 1.3%.
