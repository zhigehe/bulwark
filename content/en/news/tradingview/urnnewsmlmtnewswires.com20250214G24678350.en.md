Zhuhai Port issued ultra-short-term bonds worth 500 million yuan, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

The bonds have a 1.97% issuance rate and a term of 36 days.

China Merchants Bank serves as the bookkeeper and leader underwriter of the bond, the filing said.
