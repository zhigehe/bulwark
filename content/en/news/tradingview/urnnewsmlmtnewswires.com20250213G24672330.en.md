Bharat Forge posted a decline in its consolidated net profit to 2.13 billion Indian rupees in the fiscal third quarter from 2.54 billion rupees a year ago, according to a Wednesday filing by the manufacturing firm.

Earnings per share during the quarter ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, dropped to 4.54 rupees from 5.68 rupees a year earlier. Analysts polled by Visible Alpha expected an EPS of 6.92 rupees.

Revenue from operations in fiscal Q3 also fell to 34.8 billion rupees from 38.7 billion rupees a year earlier. Analysts polled by Visible Alpha estimated a revenue of 39.2 billion rupees.

Additionally, the company's board declared an interim dividend of 1.50 rupees per share for the financial year ending March 31, payable by March 12 to shareholders of record on Feb. 18.
