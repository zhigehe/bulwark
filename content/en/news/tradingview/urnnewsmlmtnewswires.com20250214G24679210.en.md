Coca-Cola Europacific Partners plans to commence a share buyback program totaling 1 billion euros.

The bottling company said Friday it will commence the program on Feb. 18 and has enlisted Goldman Sachs for the initial phase.

The program is expected to be completed by the end of February 2026.
