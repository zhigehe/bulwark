Sichuan Expressway terminated its plan to acquire an 85% stake in Hubei Jingyi Expressway.

The highway operator said the move followed the discovery of unresolved legal disputes involving minority equity in the target company during due diligence, according to a Thursday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

Shares of the company were down 1% in recent trade.
