Michael B. Lucareli, EVP and CFO of Modine Manufacturing Co, sold 17,541 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, at a price of $100.0 per share, totaling $1,754,100. Following the transaction, Lucareli directly owns 132,531 shares of Modine Manufacturing Co, including 971 units held in his 401(k) Retirement Plan. The sale was executed in accordance with a Rule 10b5-1 plan adopted on November 7, 2024.
