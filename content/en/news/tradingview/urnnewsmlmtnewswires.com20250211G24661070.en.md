Shenzhen Everbest Machinery Industry plans to acquire a 9% stake in software developer Shenzhen Diaotuo Technology by raising the latter's capital by 9 million yuan, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure on Tuesday.

The funds injected into the capital will be included in Diaotuo Technology's registered capital.

After the transaction, Diaotuo Tech's capital will increase to 2.5 million yuan from 2.3 million yuan, the Chinese measuring instrument manufacturer said.
