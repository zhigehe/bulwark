Gain Plus Holdings Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Tsang Chiu Kwan resigned effective Thursday, according to a same-day filing with the Hong Kong Exchange.

Tsang will stay on as director during the transition.

The construction services company named Wong Howard to succeed Tsang as chairman and CEO, the filing said.

Shares rose 2% during Friday's afternoon trading.
