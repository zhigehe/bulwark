\*\* Shares in semiconductor equipment maker Onto Innovation Inc down ~10% on Friday, hitting lowest point since January 28, after late Thursday release of quarterly financial forecast range with midpoint below consensus view

\*\* ONTO, so far biggest pct decliner in Philadelphia chip index , reported Q4 EPS of $1.51, beating Street est of $1.41, and rev of $263.94 mln, which topped consensus view of $260.51 mln, as per LSEG

\*\* ONTO forecast Q1 adj EPS of $1.40-$1.54, rev of $260 mln-$274 mln with midpoints below Street view of $1.49 EPS, rev of $268.16 mln, as per latest LSEG data

\*\* Stifel cut PT to $250 from $270 after report

\*\* Stock last traded at $189.44 vs median PT of $250, according to LSEG, which shows 10 analyst ratings: 3 "strong buy", 7 "buy"

\*\* YTD, shares still up 13.7% after rising 9% in 2024 vs SOX ~1% gain so far this year after rising 19.3% in 2024
