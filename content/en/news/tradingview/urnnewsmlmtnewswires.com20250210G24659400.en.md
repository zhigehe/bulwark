Koonenberry Gold kickstarted its inaugural drilling program at the Enmore gold project in New South Wales, according to a Tuesday filing with the Australian bourse.

The program will target broad zones of previously intersected gold mineralization. Additional exploration activities will be conducted in conjunction with the drilling campaign.

The mineral explorer's shares jumped 7% in recent trade.
