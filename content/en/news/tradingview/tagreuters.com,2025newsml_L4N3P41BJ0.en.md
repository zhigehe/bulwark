\*\* Defense conglomerate General Dynamics' shares fall 0.7% to $249.60 premarket

\*\* BTIG downgrades rating to "neutral" from "buy", reiterates PT of $345

\*\* Says regulatory hurdles and supply chain woes unlikely to recede, and further weigh on aerospace segment numbers

\*\* "The weaker margin outlook coupled with greater working capital demands trickles down to weaker free cash flow performance," BTIG adds

\*\* 13 brokerages rate the stock "buy" or higher, 11 "hold" and 1 "sell"; their median PT is $285 - LSEG compiled data

\*\* In 2024, stock had risen 1.5%
