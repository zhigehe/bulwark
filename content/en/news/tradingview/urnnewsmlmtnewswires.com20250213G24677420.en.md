South Korea will invest 28.4 billion won this year to help local medium-sized enterprises expand globally, a 78% increase from 2024, according to the Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy.

Since 2011, South Korea has invested 870.9 billion won into research and development, outbound shipments, and financing to develop major companies that can compete at a global scale, Yonhap News said in a Thursday report.
