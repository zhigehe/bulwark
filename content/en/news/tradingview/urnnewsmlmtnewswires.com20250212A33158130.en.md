TruBridge said Wednesday Cibolo Health has selected the firm as a preferred partner to deliver revenue cycle management technology and services to members of the rural clinically integrated networks served by Cibolo.

The company said its RCM solutions include billing, claims and contract management, denial management and medical claims.
