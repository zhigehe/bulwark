The most-talked-about stocks in the Reddit subforum Wallstreetbets were mostly lower hours before Tuesday's opening bell.

Super Micro Computer declined by 4.1% premarket, after closing Monday with a 17.6% rise.

BigBear.ai Holdings fell by 2.1% in pre-bell hours, swinging from a 1.5% increase at Monday's close.

Nvidia was down 1.1% in premarket activity, following a 2.9% increase from the previous session.

Advanced Micro Devices was 0.6% lower pre-bell, swinging from a 2.7% increase at Monday's close.

Tesla declined by 0.6% premarket, after closing Monday with a 3% fall.

Reddit was down 0.4% in pre-bell activity, following a 0.1% fall from the previous session.

Palantir Technologies fell by 0.1% hours before market open, swinging from a 5.2% increase at Monday's close.

Bucking the trend, AST SpaceMobile advanced by 0.9% premarket, following a 17.5% increase from the previous session.
