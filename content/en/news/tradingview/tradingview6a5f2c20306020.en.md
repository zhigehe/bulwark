Tal Cohen, President of Market Platforms at NASDAQ, sold 8,955 shares of Common Stock on February 6, 2025, at a weighted average price of $83.75 per share, totaling $749,981. Following the transaction, Cohen directly owns 127,940 shares of NASDAQ, INC. This ownership includes 50,917 shares underlying vested PSUs, 75,718 shares or units of restricted stock with 28,061 vested, and 1,305 shares purchased under the Employee Stock Purchase Plan.
