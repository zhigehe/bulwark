Yibin Tianyuan Group's controlling shareholder, Yibin Development Holding Group, proposed to increase its holdings in the company by between 150 million yuan and 300 million yuan, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange filing on Monday.

Yibin Development currently holds 314.7 million shares or 24.17% of the chemical product manufacturer, the filing said.

Yibin Tianyuan's shares rose more than 1% in recent trade.
