CTBC Financial's unit, CTBC Bank, will issue unsecured two-month USD knock-out fixed coupon basket equity-linked structured notes worth $780,000.

The debt securities have a face value of $10,000 each and carry an annual fixed coupon rate of 24.54%, according to a Monday Taiwan Exchange filing.

Proceeds from the issuance will support the Hong Kong branch's business needs and enhance funding liquidity.
