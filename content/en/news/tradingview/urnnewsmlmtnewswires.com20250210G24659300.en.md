The paper products manufacturer said earnings per share rose to 51.19 yen from 42.69 yen a year ago, according to a Monday filing.

Net sales increased 7.1% to 1.384 trillion yen from 1.292 trillion yen a year earlier.

Oji Holdings forecasted attributable profit to grow 12.2% to 57 billion yen, or 57.89 yen per share, and net sales to gain 10.7% to 1.877 trillion yen for the fiscal year ending March 31.

The company expects to pay a final dividend of 12 yen per share for the current fiscal year.
