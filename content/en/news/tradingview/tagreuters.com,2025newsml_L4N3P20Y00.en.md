\*\* Shares of Froot Loops maker WK Kellogg up ~4% at $17 premarket

\*\* Co beats quarterly profit owing to higher product prices

\*\* Logs quarterly adj. profit of 42 cents per share, above analysts' estimate of 26 cents per share - data compiled by LSEG

\*\* KLG's quarterly net income rose 26.7% to $19 mln from a year ago

\*\* Expects fiscal 2025 adj. EBITDA growth in the range of 4% to 6% vs 3% growth reported in fiscal 2024

\*\* KLG says its fiscal 2025 forecast does not include any impact from potential tariffs with Mexico and Canada

\*\* Co posts fourth-quarter revenue of $640 million vs estimate of $641.7 million

\*\* KLG rose about 37% in 2024
