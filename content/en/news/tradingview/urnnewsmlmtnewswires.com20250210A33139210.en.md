Simpson Manufacturing reported Q4 earnings late Monday of $1.31 per diluted share, up from $1.28 a year earlier.

Three analysts polled by FactSet expected $1.27.

Net sales for the quarter ended Dec. 31 were $517.4 million, up from $501.7 million a year ago.

Three analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $496 million.
