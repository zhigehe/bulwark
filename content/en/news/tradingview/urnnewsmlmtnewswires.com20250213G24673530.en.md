Rawasi Albina Investment (SASE:9547) plans to launch a sukuk program, with the first series offered publicly and targeting 50 million Saudi riyals.

The sukuk has a par value and offer price of 1,000 riyals each, a five-year maturity, and a semi-annual return of 10.25% per annum, according to a Thursday release from the Saudi Arabian infrastructure networks company.

The offering is scheduled to start on Feb. 16 and close on Feb. 27.
