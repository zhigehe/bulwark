Capacit'e Infraprojects has received a letter of award for an order worth 13.2 billion Indian rupees from NBCC (India) , according to a Friday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The contract is for the development of Leisure Valley Phase-II Residential Apartments in Greater Noida, India, on a design, engineering, procurement, and construction basis, with a comprehensive operation and maintenance period of two years.

Shares of Capacit'e Infraprojects were down over 12% in recent trade.
