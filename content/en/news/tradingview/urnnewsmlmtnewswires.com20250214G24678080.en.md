Eneco Energy's loss attributable to owners in the second half of 2024, narrowed to SG$309,000 from SG$426,000 a year earlier, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Thursday.

Loss per share stood at SG$0.0001 compared with SG$0.0002 in the corresponding period last year.

Revenue was up 7% year over year to SG$16.1 million, mainly due to higher business volume across major business segments.

Shares of the company surged over 10% in recent trading.
