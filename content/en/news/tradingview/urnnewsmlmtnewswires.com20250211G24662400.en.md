Ovzon appointed André Löfgren as its new chief financial officer, effective May 1, according to a Tuesday release.

Löfgren will succeed interim CFO Viktor Bremer and join the the Swedish mobile satellite communications services company's management team, while Bremer will continue as group finance manager.
