Pro Medicus reported Thursday that its earnings per diluted share in the fiscal first half rose to AU$0.4944 from AU$0.3461 per diluted share a year earlier.

Analysts polled by Visible Alpha were expecting EPS of AU$0.4957.

Revenue from contracts with customers for the six months to Dec. 31,Â 2024, was AU$97.2 million, up from AU$74.1 million in the same period a year earlier. Total revenue rose to AU$100.8 million from AU$76.3 million. Analysts surveyed by Visible Alpha expected total revenue of AU$99.7 million.

The board declared an interim dividend of AU$0.25 per share, up from the AU$0.18 per share in the previous reported period, payable on March 21 to shareholders on record as of Feb. 28.
