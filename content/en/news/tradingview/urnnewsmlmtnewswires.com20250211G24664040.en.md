Nordic home textile retailer Kid ASA said Tuesday it appointed Marianne Fulford as its new chief executive officer starting May 1.

Fulford will succeed outgoing CEO Anders Fjeld, who will step down to pursue a chief executive role at Sport Holding, as announced in November 2024.
