\*\* Shares of Hyatt Hotels drop ~10% to $146.34 on Thurs after the hotel chain's quarterly results fell below analyst estimates

\*\* H shares are on track for biggest daily pct loss since Mar 2020 as well as lowest close since Nov last year

\*\* Co reported on Thurs that rev fell 3.5% to $1.6 bln, missing estimates of $1.66 bln; adj EPS of 42 cents, underperformed expectations of 78 cents, per LSEG data

\*\* Among 22 analysts covering H, their avg rating is "HOLD" and median PT is $161, unchanged from Nov

\*\* H is down ~7% YTD, compared with a ~20% gain in 2024
