West Pharmaceutical Services shares fell past 19% after the company reported a decline in Q4 adjusted earnings and issued guidance for 2025 adjusted diluted earnings per share and revenue below analysts' expectations.

GE Healthcare Technologies shares were up more than 4% after the company reported higher Q4 adjusted earnings and revenue.
