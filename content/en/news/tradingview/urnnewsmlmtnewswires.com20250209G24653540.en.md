SK Hynix obtained TISAX certification for its sites in Icheon, Bundang, and Cheongju, the South Korean chipmaker said Thursday.

The TISAX is a global automobile industry information security certification.

This milestone supports the company's push to develop high-performance memory solutions for AI-driven future automotive technologies, the company said.

Shares of SK Hynix fell nearly 1% in recent trade on Monday.
