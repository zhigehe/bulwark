Compagnie de Saint-Gobain finalized its acquisition of British construction chemicals company Fosroc.

The acquired company, which will boost its Middle Eastern and Indian footprint, will be integrated into the French building materials company's High Performance Solutions unit, according to a Monday release.

The deal was disclosed in June 2024.
