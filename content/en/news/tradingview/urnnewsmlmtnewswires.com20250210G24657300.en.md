Investec (INL.JO, ) unit Investec Bank on Monday announced a tender offer for up to 600 million euros of its outstanding callable fixed-rate resettable senior notes to help manage and optimize its debt profile.

The offer involves the 300 million-euro 1.25% notes due 2026 at 99.300% of the nominal price, and the 300 million-euro 0.500% notes due 2027 at a yet-to-be-determined fee. The bank will pay all accrued interest for the notes.

The offer period starts Monday and expires Feb. 17, with settlement of the transactions expected on Feb. 20.

Additionally, the South African financial services company plans to issue euro-denominated callable fixed-rate resettable senior notes due 2031. Proceeds from the new issue are earmarked for Investec's general financial purposes.
