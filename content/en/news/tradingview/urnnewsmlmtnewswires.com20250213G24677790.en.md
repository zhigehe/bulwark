BMG Resources said it is advancing plans for expanded phase two drilling at its Abercromby gold project in Western Australia, with the assay results from phase one drilling expected shortly, according to a Friday Australian bourse filing.

Three diamond drill holes were completed during the phase one program in December 2024 for a total depth of 1,076 meters.
