Beiqi Foton Motor sold 23,242 new energy vehicles in January, dropping 9.8% from 25,772 units a year earlier, according to a Shanghai Stock Exchange disclosure on Sunday.

The Chinese automobile maker produced 23,101 NEVs, slipping 12% from 26,115 NEVs in the previous year, the disclosure said.
