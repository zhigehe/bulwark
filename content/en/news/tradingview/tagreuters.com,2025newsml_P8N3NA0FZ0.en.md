Aluminium stocks at three major Japanese ports (AL-STK-JPPRT) rose to 324,700 metric tons by the end of January, up about 0.3% from the previous month, Marubeni Corp said on Thursday.

Marubeni collects data from the ports of Yokohama, Nagoya and Osaka.
