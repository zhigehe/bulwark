ZAGREB (Croatia), February 10 (SeeNews) - Passenger numbers at Croatia's airports increased to 13.096 million last year from 11.272 million in 2023, the statistics office said on Monday.

The total number of landings and take-offs at Croatian airports rose 11.7% on the year to 138,595 in 2024, while cargo volumes increased 19% to 10,980 tonnes, the statistical office said in a monthly report.

Zagreb airport handled the most passengers last year, 4.3 million, marking a 16.2% annual increase, followed by Split Airport with almost 3.6 million passengers and Dubrovnik Airport with 2.9 million passengers.

Alone in December, the overall number of passengers went up 15.9% on the year to 393,000.

In 2023, Croatia's airports reported a 15% annual growth in number of passengers, the same office has said earlier.
