Solarvest completed the acquisition of a 30% equity in Kee Ming Electrical, according to a Monday Malaysian bourse filing.

According to earlier filings, the stake, comprising 225,000 shares, was purchased from Liew Kar Hoe, and Liew Kar Wai for for 15.3 million ringgit in cash.
