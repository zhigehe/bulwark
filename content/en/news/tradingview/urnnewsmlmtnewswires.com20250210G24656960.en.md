Ormonde Mining (ORM.AQ) said Monday it secured a three-year extension for its Antofagasta and Cueva Negra gold exploration licenses in Zamora, Spain.

Additionally, the natural resources company has a deal to buy Shearwater Group's 51.3% interest in the licenses for 50,000 euros, boosting its ownership to 100%. The transaction will be settled by issuing 5 million new shares to Shearwater.
