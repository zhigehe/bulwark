CleanTech Lithium conditionally raised 2.4 million pounds sterling via a share placement of 15 million shares at 0.16 pound a piece.

The exploration and development company earmarked proceeds for its capital programs, Laguna Verde pre-feasibility study, its listing on the Australian Securities Exchange, its DLE pilot plant conversion, as well as general working capital.

Admission of the shares on the AIM segment of the London bourse is expected on Friday.
