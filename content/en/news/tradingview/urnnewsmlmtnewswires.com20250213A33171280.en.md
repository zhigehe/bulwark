An investment fund backed by a member of Qatar's royal family is mulling a takeover of Papa John's International , Semafor reported Thursday, citing people familiar with the matter.

Irth Capital Management is discussing a take-private bid for $1.4 billion Papa Johns with its advisers, the publication said.

Papa John's did not immediately reply to a request for comment from MT Newswires.
