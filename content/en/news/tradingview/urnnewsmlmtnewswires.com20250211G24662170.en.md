Yueyang Forest & Paper secured two forestry carbon sink development projects in China, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

The Xing'an county project in Guangxi covers 2.7 million mu of forest land, with a 20-year cooperation period and a 20-year carbon reduction credit period.

The Qilin district project in Yunnan covers 851,400 mu of forest land, with a 20-year cooperation period.
