Siyaram Recycling Industries has bagged a new order worth $600,000 from Hong Kong-based Greenland Trading, according to a Monday filing to the BSE.

The contract is for the supply of 100 tons of brass billets for export to China within 45 days.

Shares of the company were down 2% in recent trade.
