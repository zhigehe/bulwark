Magna International said Tuesday it has expanded its partnership with Mercedes-Benz to provide electric drive systems for Mercedes off-roader vehicles.

Financial terms were not provided.

The company said providing its eDS Duo e-motor drive is part of Magna and Mercedes' ongoing partnership since 1979.
