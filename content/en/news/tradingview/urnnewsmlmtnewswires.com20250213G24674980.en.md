Fastighets AB Balder (BALD-B.ST) intends to keep its nearly 40% stake in Entra after Castellum unveiled plans to launch a mandatory takeover offer for the Norwegian real estate company.

The Swedish property group added that it does not plan to make a counteroffer, according to a Thursday release.

In a separate same-day release, Castellum said a recent transaction increased its holding in Entra to 60,710,724 shares, or a 33.33% stake, and it now intends to make a mandatory offer to acquire the remaining shares at 110.40 Norwegian kroner each.
