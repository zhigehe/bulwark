In London, the FTSE 100 Index is losing 49 points.

Losses were led by British American Tobacco (-7.87%), Unilever (-6.29%) and Barclays (-5.21%).

Offsetting the fall, top gainers were Barratt Redrow (5.98%), Coca-Cola (5.52%) and JD Sports Fashion (2.57%).
