Leidos Holdings, Inc. Declares Regular Quarterly Cash Dividend

PR Newswire

RESTON, Va., Feb. 7, 2025

RESTON, Va., Feb. 7, 2025 /PRNewswire/ — Leidos Holdings, Inc. announced today that its board of directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.40 per outstanding share of the company's common stock. The cash dividend is payable on March 28, 2025, to stockholders of record as of the close of business on March 14, 2025.

About Leidos

Leidos is an industry and technology leader serving government and commercial customers with smarter, more efficient digital and mission innovations. Headquartered in Reston, Virginia, with 48,000 global employees, Leidos reported annual revenues of approximately $15.4 billion for the fiscal year ended December 29, 2023. For more information, visit www.leidos.com.
