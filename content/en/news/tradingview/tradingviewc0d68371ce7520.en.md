Oliver George, Chairman & CEO of Johnson Controls International plc, sold 410,335 Ordinary Shares across four transactions on February 10 and 11, 2025, for a total of $36,561,545. The sales were made at weighted average prices ranging from $88.81 to $89.83. Following these transactions, George directly owns 1,203,892 Ordinary Shares and indirectly owns 178,349 shares through GRO Sr. 5 Year GRAT and Oliver Family Trust (SLAT).
