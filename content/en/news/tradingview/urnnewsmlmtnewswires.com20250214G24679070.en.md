Sudarshan Chemical Industries has incoporated a step-down subsidiary in Dubai, UAE, named Sudarshan Middle East General Trading L.L.C, according to a Thursday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

Sudarshan Middle East is wholly owned by Sudarshan Europe B.V., which is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Indian company.

The newly incorporated company will mainly be in same line of business of pigments manufacturing and supply as that of the parent company.
