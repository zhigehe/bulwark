Powerchip Semiconductor Manufacturing logged a net loss of NT$1.50 billion in the fourth quarter of 2024, according to a Thursday disclosure to the Taiwan Stock Exchange.

Loss per share during the quarter was NT$0.36, while consolidated revenue was NT$11.1 billion, the filing said.
