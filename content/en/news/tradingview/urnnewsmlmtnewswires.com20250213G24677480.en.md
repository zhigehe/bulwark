Ganfeng Lithium Group officially started production at its Mariana lithium project in Argentina.

The first phase of the project has an annual planned production capacity of 20,000 tons of lithium chloride, according to a Friday filing on the Shenzhen bourse.

Mariana has an estimated resource of about 8.1 million tons of lithium carbonate equivalent.

Shares of the miner rose 1% in recent trade.
