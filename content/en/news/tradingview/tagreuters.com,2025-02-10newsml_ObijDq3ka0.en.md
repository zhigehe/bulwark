NRC Group ASA ("NRC" or the "Company") refers to its previous announcementsregarding the dispute with Bane NOR related to the Electrification of Trønderand Meråkerbanen (ETM).

The mediation, which resumed in February 2025, has ended without the partiesbeing able to reach an agreement.

The Company will initiate legal proceedings, as an acceptable agreement couldnot be reached. The Company maintains that the claims are well-founded andwillcontinue to safeguard shareholders' interests.

NRC expects full year EBIT for 2024 to remain in the lower end of thepreviouslycommunicated guidance. Further details will be disclosed in connection withtherelease of the financial results on Wednesday, 12 February, at 07:00 am (CET).

For 2025, the Company still aims for a revenue of approximately NOK 7.0billionand an EBIT margin above 2%.
