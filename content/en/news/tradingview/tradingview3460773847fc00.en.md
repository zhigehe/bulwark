Christopher R. Gruseke, CEO of Bankwell Financial Group, sold 1,908 shares of common stock on February 7, 2025, at a price range of $31.53 - $32.87, with a total sale amount of $61,284. Following the transaction, Gruseke directly owns 181,883 shares and indirectly owns 12,500 shares through an IRA. The shares sold were part of a company-sponsored cashless stock exercise program to cover tax liabilities.
