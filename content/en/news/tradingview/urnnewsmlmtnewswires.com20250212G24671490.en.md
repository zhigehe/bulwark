EssilorLuxottica reported Wednesday that its net profit and revenue for full-year 2024 increased year over year.

Net profit attributable to owners of the parent for the 12 months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 2.36 billion euros, compared with 2.29 billion euros earlier. EPS moved to 5.13 euros from 5.08 euros.

The European eyewear manufacturer's revenue was 26.51 billion euros, compared with 25.40 billion euros a year ago.

Analysts polled by FactSet were expecting 26.37 billion euros in sales, 2.83 billion euros in net income, and 6.87 euros in EPS.

Meanwhile, the board intends to recommend an unchanged dividend of 3.95 euros per share for the period, payable from June 5.

Looking ahead, the company affirmed its long-term outlook for mid-single-digit annual growth in revenue at constant exchange rates, with revenue by 2026 targeted in the range of 27 billion euros to 28 billion euros.
