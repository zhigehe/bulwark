Unicharm's profit attributable to owners fell 4.9% to 81.8 billion yen in the year ended Dec. 31, 2024, from 86.1 billion yen a year ago​.

The wellness care products company's earnings per share declined to 46.41 yen from 48.47 yen a year ago, according to a filing​.

Sales revenue grew 5.0% to nearly 989 billion yen from 941.8 billion yen a year earlier​.

The company declared a year-end dividend of 22 yen per share, payable Feb. 27.

Unicharm forecasts attributable profit to increase 5.6% to 86.40 billion yen, or 49.12 yen per share, and net sales to grow 3.6% to 1.03 trillion yen for 2025.

The company expects to pay interim and year-end dividends of 9 yen per share each, bringing the total annual dividend to 18 yen per share for the current fiscal year.
