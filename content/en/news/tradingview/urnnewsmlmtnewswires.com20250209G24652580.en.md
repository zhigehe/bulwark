Mynaric obtained new loans and agreed to a financial restructuring by proceedings under the German Corporate Stabilization and Restructuring Act, or StaRUG, according to a Feb. 7 release.

The German laser communication equipment manufacturer secured a fourth bridge loan of $28 million at a fixed annual rate equal to 4.5% until Feb. 7, 2031. A new restructuring loan of $25 million will be secured at an 8% interest until Dec. 31, 2028.

Additionally, the maturity of three existing bridge loans worth $21.5 million was extended to June 30.
