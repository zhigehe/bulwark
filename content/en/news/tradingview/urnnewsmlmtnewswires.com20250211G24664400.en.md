Wacoal Holdings posted a profit attributable to owners of parent of 9.09 billion yen for the nine months ended Dec. 31, reversing a loss of 3.90 billion yen a year earlier, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Earnings per share came in at 166.23 yen, compared with a loss per share of 67.98 yen in the prior year.

Revenue declined 5.6% to 133.53 billion yen from 141.41 billion yen.

For the full year ending March 31, the company expects an attributable profit of 4.50 billion yen, with revenue falling 3.3% to 181 billion yen. EPS is forecast at 82.65 yen.

The company plans to pay a year-end dividend of 50 yen per share, bringing the full-year payout to 100 yen.
