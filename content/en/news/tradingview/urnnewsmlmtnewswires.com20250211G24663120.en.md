Saab (SAAB-B.ST) secured an order worth 700 million kronor for its sight and fire control system from BAE Systems (BA.L) unit BAE Systems Hägglunds.

The Swedish defense and security company's Universal Tank and Anti-Aircraft System will be used in CV90 armored combat vehicles produced by the British aerospace and defense company's subsidiary, according to a Tuesday release.
