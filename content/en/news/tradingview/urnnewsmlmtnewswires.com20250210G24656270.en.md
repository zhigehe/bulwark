Neinor Homes and Banco Santander's Santander Alternative Investments unit agreed to form a real estate flex living joint venture in Spain.

The Spanish residential developer said Monday that the venture will invest 60 million euros for the building of 160 housing units.

The company noted that it will hold a 10% stake in the new entity, with Santander Alternative Investments owning 90%. Additionally, it will act as the delivery partner manager, project design, license, and construction supervisor of the project.
