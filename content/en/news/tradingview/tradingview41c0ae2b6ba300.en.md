Mizuho Securities USA LLC, a 10% owner in Aimei Health Technology Co., Ltd., reported selling 150,000 shares of common stock on February 11, 2025, for a total sale amount of $1,629,000. The selling price per share was $10.86. Following this transaction, Mizuho Securities USA LLC directly owns 550,387 shares of Aimei Health Technology Co., Ltd. The transaction was reported as a direct ownership.
