European chemicals maker DSM-Firmenich said on Thursday it expects 2025 adjusted core profits of at least 2.4 billion euro ($2.50 billion) after it reported annual results that were slightly better than expectations.

It announced it intends to launch a share buyback, which will start in the second quarter of 2025 for an initial 500 million euros and will be increased to up to 1 billion euros.

The group's adjusted core profits was 2.12 billion euros in 2024, beating analysts' forecast of 2.11 billion euros in a consensus compiled by Vara Research.
