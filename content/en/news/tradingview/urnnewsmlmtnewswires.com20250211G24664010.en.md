Fintech Asia said Tuesday it received shareholder approval at its extraordinary general meeting for its acquisition of ICFG.

The transaction is expected to be completed on Wednesday, with the London-listed investment company set to change its name to ICFG as part of the deal.

The company will begin trading under its new ICFG ticker symbol on Thursday.
