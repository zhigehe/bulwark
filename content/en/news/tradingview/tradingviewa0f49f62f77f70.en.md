Robert B. Ford, Chairman and CEO of Abbott Laboratories, sold 285,388 common shares without par value on February 5, 2025, at a weighted average price of $131.173 per share, totaling $37,435,200. Following the transaction, Ford directly owns 220,059 shares and indirectly owns 197,403 shares through the Ford Family Trust. The sales were part of multiple transactions with prices ranging from $130.68 to $131.38.
