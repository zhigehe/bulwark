ASE Technology Holding reported Q4 earnings Thursday of 2.07 New Taiwan dollars ($0.06) per diluted share, down from NT$2.13 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected NT$2.04.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was NT$162.26 billion, compared with NT$160.58 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected NT$158.74 billion.
