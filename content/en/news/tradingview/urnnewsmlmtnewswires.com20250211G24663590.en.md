Swedish construction company NCC (NCC-B.ST, NCC-A.ST) secured a turnkey contract worth 150 million kronor from the City of Ackas in Finland to build a new primary school.

The project includes the construction of a three-story building with a total area of 4,000 square meters, according to a Tuesday release. Construction will start in March 2025 and is expected to end in autumn 2026.
