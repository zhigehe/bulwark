Renishaw said Thursday its profit and revenue for the first half of fiscal 2025 rose year over year.

Profit attributable to shareholders of the parent company for the six months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 45.9 million pounds sterling, compared with 45.2 million pounds earlier. EPS moved to 0.632 pound from 0.621 pound.

The engineering company's revenue was 341.4 million pounds, compared with 330.5 million pounds a year ago.

Meanwhile, the board declared an unchanged interim dividend of 0.168 pound per share for the period, payable April 8 to shareholders on record March 7.

For the full fiscal 2025, Renishaw expects revenue in the range of 695 million pounds to 735 million pounds and an adjusted pretax profit of 105 million pounds to 135 million pounds.
