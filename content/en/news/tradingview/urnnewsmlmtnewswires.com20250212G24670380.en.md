Toray Industries' profit attributable to owners of the parent surges nearly 65% to 75.2 billion yen in the nine months ended Dec. 31, 2024, from 45.7 billion yen a year ago.

The company, which specializes in industrial products centered on technologies in organic synthetic chemistry, said earnings per share rose to 46.92 yen from 28.51 yen a year ago.

Revenue increased 5.2% to 1.924 trillion yen from 1.829 trillion yen a year earlier, according to a Tokyo Stock Exchange filing on Wednesday.

Toray Industries forecasted attributable profit to grow 301.9% to 88 billion yen or 55.14 yen per share and net sales to gain 5.1% to 2.59 trillion yen for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2025.

The company expects to pay a year-end dividend of 9.00 yen per share, bringing the total to 18.00 yen per share for the fiscal year.

Shares of the company surged 9% in morning trade Thursday.
