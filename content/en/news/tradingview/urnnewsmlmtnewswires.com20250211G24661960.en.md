Investment management firm Nisus Finance Services has exited its investment in a self-redevelopment housing project in Mumbai, India with an internal rate of return of approximately 21%, according to a Monday filing to the BSE.

In 2022, Nisus Finance had invested 220 million Indian rupees in the self-redevelopment project, while Trilogy Developers acted as the development manager for the project.

Shares of the company were down 5% in recent trade.
