Samsung Electronics officially unveiled the Galaxy S25 series, available globally from Feb. 7, Friday, the electronics products maker said in a same-day release.

The lineup integrates AI-driven features powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite platform, including Gemini multimodal agents for seamless interactions across apps and new tools like Brief and Now Bar, the release said.

The Galaxy S25 Ultra comes equipped with a 50MP ultrawide sensor, Virtual Aperture, and Samsung Log for pro-grade content creation.

The device also supports C2PA Content Credentials, enhancing transparency for AI-generated content. Buyers will receive six months of Gemini Advanced and 2TB cloud storage at no extra cost, it said.
