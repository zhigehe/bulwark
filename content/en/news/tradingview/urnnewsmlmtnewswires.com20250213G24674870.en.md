UGRO Capital raised 2.60 billion Indian rupees through non-convertible debentures subscribed by Dutch entrepreneurial development bank FMO, according to a Thursday filing with the Indian bourses.

The funds will support women empowerment, reduce territorial inequalities, and foster youth development, UGRO Capital said.
