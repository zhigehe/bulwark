Nitta Corp. will appoint Seiichi Kitamura as president on April 1, succeeding Yasunori Ishikiriyama, who will become chairman, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The changes support the next phase of its "SHIFT 2030" plan. Toshihiko Morishita is set to join as full-time Audit & Supervisory board member, pending shareholder approval in June.

New executive officers Fumi Kizuka and Atsuhito Wake will oversee corporate center functions.
