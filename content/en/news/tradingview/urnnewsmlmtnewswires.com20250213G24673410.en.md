Coca-Cola HBC said Thursday its profit and net sales revenue for 2024 grew year over year.

Profit attributable to owners of the parent for the 12 months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 820.6 million euros, compared with 636.5 million euros earlier. EPS moved to 2.25 euros from 1.73 euros.

The bottling company's net sales revenue was 10.75 billion euros, compared with 10.18 billion euros a year ago.

Meanwhile, the board will propose a dividend of 1.03 euros per share for 2024, payable in 2025. It paid 0.93 euro per share a year ago.

Looking ahead, the company anticipates its organic revenue to grow between 6% and 8% in 2025.
