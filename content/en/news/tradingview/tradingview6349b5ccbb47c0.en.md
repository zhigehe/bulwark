Control Empresarial De Capitales S.A. De C.V., a 10% owner of PBF Energy, purchased 150,000 Class A Common Shares on February 6, 2025, for a total of $4,305,060. The shares were bought at a weighted average price of $28.7004, with prices ranging from $28.075 to $29.40. Following this transaction, Control Empresarial De Capitales S.A. De C.V. directly owns 29,505,998 Class A Common Shares of PBF Energy.
