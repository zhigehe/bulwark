Vermilion Energy on Tuesday said it closed its US$400 million private offering of eight-year senior unsecured notes.

The notes mature on Feb. 15, 2033 and have a fixed coupon of 7.250% per annum.

The company's shares were last seen up $0.39 to $13.97 on the Toronto Stock Exchange.
