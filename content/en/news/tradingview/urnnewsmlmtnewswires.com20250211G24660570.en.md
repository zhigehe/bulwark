Mexican telco, Total Play Telecomunicaciones, filed for the listing of about $820.7 million worth of 11.125% senior secured bonds due 2032, on the Singapore bourse.

The bonds will be listed and quoted in the Bonds Market on Feb. 12, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Tuesday.
