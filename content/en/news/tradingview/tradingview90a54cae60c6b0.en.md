Cadence Design Systems Senior Vice President, Teng Chin-Chi, sold 12,760 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, with transactions ranging in price from $301.42 to $305.09, resulting in a total sale amount of $3,878,256. These sales were conducted under a weighted average price system. Following these transactions, Teng Chin-Chi directly owns 93,387 shares of Cadence Design Systems.
