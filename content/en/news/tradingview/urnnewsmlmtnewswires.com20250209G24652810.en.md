MegaChips will sell part of its stake in SiTime Corp, booking an extraordinary gain of about 3.5 billion yen in the fiscal year ending March 2025, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The Osaka-based firm said the sale aims to strengthen its financial position and support long-term growth in amusement, ASIC, and telecommunications businesses.
