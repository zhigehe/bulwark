Anne T Madden, Senior Vice President and General Counsel at Honeywell International, sold 28,885 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $207.89 per share, totaling $6,004,902. Following the transaction, Madden directly owns 41,580 shares and indirectly owns 928 shares through a 401(k).
