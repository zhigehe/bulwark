Ningbo Deye Technology Group said its actual controller and chair, Zhang Hejun, raised his stake in the company by 120,000 shares.

This increased Zhang and his affiliates' stake in the solar inverter manufacturer to 60.82% from 60.81%, according to a Tuesday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

Shares of the company rose 1% in recent trade.
