Saudi Industrial Investment Group's (SASE:2250) lodged a request with the Saudi Capital Market Authority to approve its proposed capital decrease.

The petrochemical company will write off 75,480,000 shares, valued at 754.8 million Saudi riyals, reducing its capital to 6.79 billion riyals from 7.55 billion riyals, according to a Thursday release.

The capital reduction is subject to regulatory and extraordinary general assembly approvals.
