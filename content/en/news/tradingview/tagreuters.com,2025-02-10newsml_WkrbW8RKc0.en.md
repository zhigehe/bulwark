BioStock

Saniona made the headlines late last year when the company entered a partnership with Acadia Pharmaceuticals, a deal worth up to 610 million USD. But what does this mean for the company's future, and what is the vision and strategy for 2025? These are some of the topics that will be discussed when CEO Thomas Feldthus and board member John Haurum comes to BioStock's Studio for a live interview on February 11 at 11:00 CET. Submit your questions and have them answered live!

The live interview with **Saniona** will be broadcast tomorrow, Tuesday, February 11, at 11:00 CET on BioStock's YouTube channel. Direct link: https://www.youtube.com/live/Df\_VE2nly5Q?si=5sLAE-aj3yKIv9Mj

During the company presentation and interview, CEO **Thomas Feldthus** and board member **John Haurum**, who has been proposed as the board's new chairman, will present Saniona's strategy and vision for the future.

**Submit Your Questions!**

You are invited to submit your questions to the company. Questions can be submitted in two ways:

* Directly in the live stream's comment section on YouTube during the broadcast.
* By emailing questions to redaktion@biostock.se no later than 10:00 CET on February 11.

Don't miss this opportunity to get your questions answered by Saniona's management!
