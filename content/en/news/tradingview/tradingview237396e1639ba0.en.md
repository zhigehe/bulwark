Kimberly K. Nelson, EVP & CFO of SPS Commerce, sold 9,290 shares of Common Stock on February 5, 2025, under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices ranging from $180.3309 to $182.7358, totaling $1,682,317. Following these transactions, Nelson directly owns 147,002 shares and indirectly owns 435 shares through a 401(k) Plan.
