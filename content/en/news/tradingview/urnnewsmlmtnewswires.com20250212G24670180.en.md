Spackman Entertainment appointed Anthony Wei Kit Wong as interim chief executive officer and executive director, effective Feb. 12, according to a Wednesday filing on the Singapore Exchange.

Wong previously served as the company's non-executive, non-independent chairman and was a member of its audit, risk management, and remuneration committees. With his new role, he steps down from these positions.

A chartered accountant based in Canada, Wong has held CFO roles at Canada Rare Earth Corp. and China Public Procurement. He will oversee the company's business and operational decisions as interim CEO.
