Jiangsu Huahong Technology subsidiary Xintai Technology signed an oxide procurement cooperation framework agreement with China Rare Earth International Trade, Ningbo Funeng, and Chenguang Rare Earth.

The agreements with three three firms secure the supply of rare earth oxides for Xintai Technology from February 2025 to January 2026, according to a Saturday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Pricing will be based on monthly market prices, with monthly order volume to range between 42.5 tons and 64 tons.
