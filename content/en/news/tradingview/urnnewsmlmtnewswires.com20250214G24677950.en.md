Kingsmen Creatives' US-based entertainment subsidiary, Kingsmen Xperience, entered into a strategic deal memorandum with Black Spade Capital subsidiary S Experience to develop location-based branded attractions in Asia Pacific.

Initially, the attractions will focus on Macau, the Philippines, Vietnam and Thailand, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Thursday.

Shares of the creative entertainment company rose nearly 4% in recent trading.
