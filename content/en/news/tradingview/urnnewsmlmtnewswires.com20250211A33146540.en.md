Gray Media and Atlanta Dream said Tuesday they have renewed their partnership for the 2025 WNBA season.

Gray will be the Official Broadcast Partner of the Dream, Peachtree TV and Peachtree Sports Network to air Dream games and additional content across the region, according to the release.
