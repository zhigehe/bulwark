The Carlyle Group reported Q4 after-tax distributable earnings Tuesday of $0.92 per share, up from $0.86 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.95.

Total revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $1.03 billion, up from $926.2 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $961.6 million.

The private equity firm maintained its quarterly dividend at $0.35 per share, payable Feb. 28 to shareholders of record on Feb. 21.

Shares were down nearly 4% in premarket activity.
