Knife River reported Q4 net income Thursday of $0.41 per diluted share, compared with $0.36 a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $0.34.

Revenue for the quarter was $657.2 million, compared with $646.9 million a year earlier. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $651.7 million.

The construction materials company said it expects 2025 revenue of $3.0 billion to $3.2 billion. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $3.06 billion.

Shares were down 2% in recent premarket activity.
