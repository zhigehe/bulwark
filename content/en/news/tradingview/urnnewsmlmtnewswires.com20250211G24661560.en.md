Saudi Enaya Cooperative Insurance Co.'s (SASE:8311) planned extraordinary general assembly meeting on Monday did not meet the required legal quorum.

The insurer said in a Tuesday filing that the attendance rate for the first meeting was 13.86% and the rate for the second meeting was 14.32%.

The company plans to set a date for a third meeting after receiving regulatory approval. It noted that the third meeting will be held regardless of representation from the number of shares with voting rights.
