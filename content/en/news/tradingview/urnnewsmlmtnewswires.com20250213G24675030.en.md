Casino Guichard-Perrachon , d/b/a Casino Group, renewed its retail partnership with fuel distributor Avia Thévenin Ducrot covering service stations in eastern France for another three years.

The French retailer will continue providing a comprehensive range of products and services to the stores at 46 motorway service stations and 41 urban or suburban stations, according to a Thursday release.

The partnership started in 2018 and was previously renewed twice.
