Prudential enlisted Citigroup to help with the initial public offering of ICICI Prudential Asset Management, Bloomberg News reported, citing people familiar with the matter.

ICICI Prudential is seen to raise $1 billion from the IPO, the report said.

The listing will entail Pru's partial divestment in its joint venture with ICICI Bank . The Indian bank will retain its shareholding.

Prudential, ICICI, and Citigroup did not respond immediately to requests for comment from MT Newswires.
