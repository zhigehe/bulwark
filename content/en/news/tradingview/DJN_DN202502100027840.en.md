India's benchmark Sensex extended early declines to end 0.7% lower at 77311.80. Investors are keeping an eye on tariff developments and waiting for U.S. inflation data due later this week for clues on the timing of interest-rate cuts. Among major stocks, Tata Motors dropped 1.5% and Tata Steel was 3.1% lower. Power Grid Corp. of India declined 3.45%. Banks and technology companies were among the few gainers. Kotak Mahindra Bank was 1.2% higher and ICICI Bank added 0.5%. HCL Technologies gained 0.4%. (tracy.qu@wsj.com)
