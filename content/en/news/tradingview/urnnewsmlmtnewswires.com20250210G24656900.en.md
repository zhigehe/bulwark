Jingrui Holdings recorded contracted sales of around 60 million yuan in January, with a contracted gross floor area (GFA) of 4,841 square meters, a Monday bourse filing said.

The real estate firm's contracted average selling price for the month was approximately 12,394 yuan per square meter.
