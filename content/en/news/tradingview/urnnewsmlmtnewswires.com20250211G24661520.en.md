HSBC Holdings bought back 2,660,800 shares in Hong Kong on Monday at a weighted average price of HK$82.37 per share, according to Tuesday bourse filing.

Since the current buyback program has started, the British lender has repurchased 312,709,939 shares for about $2.96 billion.
