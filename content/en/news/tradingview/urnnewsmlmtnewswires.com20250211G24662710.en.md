Phase Holographic Imaging (PHI.ST) appointed Ivan Jurkovic as its acting chief executive officer, according to a Tuesday release.

Jurkovic, the chief financial officer of Phase Holographic Imaging's largest shareholder Altium, succeeds Anders Månsson, who is stepping down as the Swedish medical technology company prioritizes its clinical product development program and seeks a new leader for the initiative.
