The UK's monthly manufacturing production grew 0.7% in December 2024, following a 0.3% decline in the prior month, the Office for National Statistics said Thursday.

Analysts expected a 0.1% fall for the month.

On a yearly basis, British manufacturing output was down 1.4%, compared with the revised 1.1% decrease earlier and the consensus estimate of a 1.9% slump.
