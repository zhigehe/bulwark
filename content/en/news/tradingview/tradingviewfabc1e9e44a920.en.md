Frank M. Svoboda, Co-Chairman & CEO and Director at Globe Life, sold 11,000 shares of Common Stock on February 11, 2025, with sales prices ranging from $120.94 to $122.31 per share, resulting in a total sale amount of $1,335,890. Following these transactions, Svoboda directly owns 18,948 shares and indirectly owns 146,833 shares through a Family Trust and 401(k) Plan.
