Zhejiang Yinlun Machinery subsidiary Yinlun TDI obtained a contract to supply cooling systems for electric vehicles of a North American automotive brand.

The project is set to begin mass production in 2027 and expected to generate revenue of about $61.3 million over its life cycle, according to a Thursday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of the oil cooler manufacturer were down 2% Thursday.
