Telecom services provider Vodafone Idea has appointed Gurucharan Singh Gandhi as chief human resource officer, with effect from April 1, according to a Tuesday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

Gandhi will take over from current chief human resource officer Suvamoy Roy Choudhary who will be retiring from the company on March 31.
