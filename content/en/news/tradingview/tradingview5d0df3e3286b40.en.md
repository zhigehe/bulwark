Heather A. Planishek, Chief Accounting Officer at Palantir Technologies, sold 13,891 shares of Class A Common Stock on February 11, 2025, for a total of $1,610,744. The sales were executed at weighted average prices ranging from $113.0346 to $118.2236. Following these transactions, Planishek directly owns 512,006 shares and indirectly owns 8,130 shares held for a minor child under the Uniform Transfers to Minors Act. The sales were made pursuant to a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan.
