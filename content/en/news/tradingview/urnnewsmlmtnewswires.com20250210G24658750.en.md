Furukawa will repurchase up to 7 million shares, or 19.3% of outstanding stock, for a maximum of 10 billion yen between Feb. 12, 2025, and Feb. 10, 2026, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

As of Dec. 31, 2024, the metals and machinery firm had 36.3 million shares outstanding, excluding 4.2 million in treasury.
