\*\* Shares of animal healthcare company Zoetis fall 10.06% to $156.39; the biggest percentage drop in over two years

\*\* Company forecasts 2025 adj. profit per share to be between $6.00 and $6.10, below analysts' estimates of $6.29 per share, according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Zoetis expects annual revenue to be between $9.23 billion and $9.38 billion, vs est. of $9.57 billion

\*\* Though the miss can be largely attributed to a stronger dollar and the divestiture of Zoetis' medicated feed additive product portfolio, it had not being accurately captured in many analyst models - brokerage William Blair

\*\* Company reports Q4 adj. profit of $1.40 vs est. of $1.34

\*\* Up to last close, stock down about 12% in the last 12 months
