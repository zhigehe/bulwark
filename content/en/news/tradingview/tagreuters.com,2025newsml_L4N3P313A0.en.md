\*\* Shares of utility Dominion Energy fall 1.3% to $55

\*\* Company narrows forecast for 2025 adjusted profit per share to range of $3.28 to $3.52, from $3.25 to $3.54

\*\* Analysts expected Dominion to post a profit of $3.39 per share in 2025, according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Company posts Q4 adj earnings of 58 cents/share vs. est of 56 cents/share

\*\* The broader S&P 500 Utilities Sector was also down, pressured by higher U.S. dollar and Treasury yields

\*\* However, co raises five-year capex plan to $50.1 billion from $43.2 billion

\*\* Including session's moves, Dominion shares were up 2.2% YTD
