Lagercrantz (LAGR-B.ST) said Tuesday its unit Radonova Laboratories completed the purchase of Track Analysis Systems Ltd, or TASL, a specialist in radon measurements and neutron dosimetry based in Bristol, UK.

The completion follows the receipt of all regulatory approvals, with TASL operating as a subsidiary of Radonova Laboratories under Lagercrantz's control division.

The Swedish technology group added that the transaction will impact the group's EPS marginally on an annual basis.
