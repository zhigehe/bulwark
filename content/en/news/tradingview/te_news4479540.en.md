Romania's annual inflation rate edged down to 5% in January 2025 from 5.1% in each of the previous two months, in line with market expectations.

Prices softened for food (4.54% vs 5.09% in December) and services (6.54% vs 7.1%).

Meanwhile, costs for non-food products increased slightly (4.60% vs 4.38%).

On a monthly basis, consumer prices rose by 0.9% in January, following a 0.3% increase in December.
