Transfar Zhilian said its subsidiary, Chuanhua Logistics, will sell a 60% stake in Qingdao Chuanhua Logistics Base to Qingdao SCO Guoda Industrial Holding for 509.5 million yuan, according to Saturday filing with the Shenzhen Bourse.

The board approved the deal and aims to optimize asset allocation. Payment will be made in three installments, with completion expected within six months. The transaction does not involve related parties or major restructuring.
