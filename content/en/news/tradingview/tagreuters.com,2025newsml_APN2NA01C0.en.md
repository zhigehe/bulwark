Greek industrial output rose by 5.8% year-on-year in December, after a downwardly revised 3.3% increase in November, data from statistics service ELSTAT showed on Monday.

A breakdown of index components showed manufacturing production rose 3.6% from the same month in 2023. Electricity output was up by 16.4% year-on-year, and mining production was down by 4.3%.

Compared to the same month a year earlier, in December the greatest percentage increase was recorded in computers, electronic and optical products division, while the highest percentage decrease was recorded in leather and related products division, ELSTAT said.
