\*\* Indian benchmarks NSE Nifty 50 and BSE Sensex drop 1% and 0.8%, respectively

\*\* Concerns over implications of President Donald Trump's plans to impose reciprocal U.S. tariffs on a country-by-country basis weighing on Indian markets, say analysts

\*\* India's tariff differential with U.S. higher than most other Asian countries, making its exports especially vulnerable to reciprocal tariffs, they say

\*\* Drug makers , which have significant U.S. exposure, shed 3.2%

\*\* Telecom operator Vodafone Idea loses 6% after reports say India's top court rejects request by telecom cos to recalculate dues they owe the government

\*\* Smallcaps and midcaps lose 4.2% and 3.2%, hurt by slowing earnings momentum and high valuations
