Directa Plus said Monday its Setcar environmental unit secured new and renewed contract deals.

The new contract with Midia International involves tank cleaning and waste disposal services for an offshore drilling campaign in the Black Sea set for completion in the second half. Setcar's contract covers both onshore and offshore mobile drilling unit.

The graphene-based products supply company subsidiary also extended its waste management deal with Ford Motor's Romania-based business Ford Otosan. It includes waste disposal, transportation, treatment, recycling, equipment and personnel.
