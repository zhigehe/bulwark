The US Treasury posted a $128.64 billion budget deficit in January compared with the $94.8 billion deficit expected in a survey compiled by Bloomberg and much larger than the $21.93 billion budget deficit a year earlier.

The monthly deficit was $86.73 billion in December.

Through the first four months of the fiscal year, the deficit is $839.59 billion, larger than the $531.86 billion gap in the same period a year earlier.
