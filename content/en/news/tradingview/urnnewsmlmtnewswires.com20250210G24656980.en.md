African Pioneer raised 420,000 pounds sterling by issuing 42 million new ordinary shares at 0.01 pound apiece to existing and new investors.

The exploration and resource development company also issued 1,207,039 shares to settle accrued consultancy fees of 17,246 pounds, according to a Monday release. Trading in the shares is anticipated to commence on Thursday.

African Pioneer will use the net proceeds primarily to advance the Ongombo copper gold project in Namibia, and others in Zambia and Botswana.
