Taiwan Business Bank was fined NT$2 million by the Financial Supervisory Commission (FSC) over fund misappropriation by former employees at the lender's World Trade Center and Xi Zhi branches.

The bank said it has enhanced internal controls and strengthened management regulations to prevent future violations, according to a Wednesday Taiwan Exchange filing.
