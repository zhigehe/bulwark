LG Energy Solution was selected to manage the bidding operations for South Korea's largest offshore wind power facility, the Jeju Hanlim Offshore Wind Farm, Pulse News reported Monday.

The 100-megawatt site, spanning 5.5 square kilometers off Jeju Island, houses 18 turbines and produces over 234,000 megawatt-hours of renewable energy on an annual basis. The battery manufacturer will oversee power generation forecasts and participate in Korea Power Exchange's bidding system to facilitate renewable energy distribution nationwide, the report said.

Shares of LG Energy Solution rose nearly 1% in recent trade on Tuesday.
