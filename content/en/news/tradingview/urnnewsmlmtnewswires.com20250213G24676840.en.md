BPM Minerals launched a 6,000-meter air core drilling program at its Claw gold project in Western Australia, according to a Friday filing with the Australian bourse.

The program targets extensions of known mineralization along an 8-kilometer highly prospective corridor, per the filing.

The mineral explorer's shares tumbled nearly 24% in recent Friday trade.
