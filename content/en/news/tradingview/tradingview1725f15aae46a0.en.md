David B Ingram, a Director at Pinnacle Financial Partners, sold 47,071 shares of PNFP Common Stock on February 6, 2025, for a total of $5,954,835. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $126.5 and $126.55, with some shares sold in multiple transactions ranging from $126.50 to $126.72. Following these transactions, Ingram directly owns 40,991 shares of PNFP Common Stock and 120,000 Depositary Shares. Indirectly, through the Ingram Trust and by spouse, he owns an additional 81,728 shares of PNFP Common Stock.
