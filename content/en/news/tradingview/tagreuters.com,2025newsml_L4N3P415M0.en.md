\*\* Shares of real estate investment trust Iron Mountain fall 2% to $100.80 in early trading

\*\* IRM posts fourth quarter revenue of $1.58 billion, compared with estimates of $1.60 billion - LSEG

\*\* Co expects full-year 2025 revenue to be between $6.65 bln and $6.80 bln, midpoint of which is slightly below analysts' average estimate of $6.77 bln

\*\* Co's fourth-quarter adjusted funds from operations (FFO) rise 12% to $1.24 per share, compared with $1.11 a year ago

\*\* In 2024, the shares of the company rose 50.2%
