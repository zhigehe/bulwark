\*\* Shares of ASX rise as much as 2.7% to A$68.140, set for their best week since April 3, 2023, if weekly gain of 7.3% holds

\*\* Co posted a half-year underlying net profit of A$253.7 million on Thursday, a 10.1% increase from a year ago

\*\* Morgan Stanley hikes Price Target to A$56.15 from A$55.05, 'Underweight' on stock

\*\* Brokerage takes into account increased earnings estimate for FY25, reflecting lower operating expenses

\*\* 8 of 12 analysts rate the stock hold, and 4 "sell"; their median PT is A$64.28 – LSEG data

\*\* Stock rose 3.2% in 2024
