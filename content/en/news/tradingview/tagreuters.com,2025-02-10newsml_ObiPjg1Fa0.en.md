Pexip will present its results for the fourth quarter of 2024 on Thursday, 13February, at 08:30 CET. The financial report and presentation material will bemade available at 07:00 CET at investor.pexip.com and newsweb.no(https://newsweb.oslobors.no/) under the company's ticker "PEXIP".

The presentation will be held by Trond K. Johannessen (CEO), Øystein Hem (CFO)and Åsmund Fodstad (CRO). The presentation will be held in English andconductedas a webcast. Questions can be submitted to IR@pexip.com in advance of thepresentation or live during the presentation.

Please join the webcast via the following link:

https://www.pexip.com/events/q4-2024-results-presentation

A recorded version of the presentation will also be available after thewebcastis concluded.
