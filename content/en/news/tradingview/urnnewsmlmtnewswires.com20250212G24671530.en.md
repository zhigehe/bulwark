Himadri Speciality Chemical incorporated a wholly-owned subsidiary in the US named Himadri Speciality, according to a Wednesday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The new entity will focus on expanding the company's carbon black and other speciality carbon products business in the US.

Shares of the company were up nearly 2% in recent trade.
