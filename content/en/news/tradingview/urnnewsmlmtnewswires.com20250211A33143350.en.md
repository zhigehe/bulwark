Arvinas reported a Q4 net loss Tuesday of $0.63 per diluted share, narrower than its loss of $2.53 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected a loss of $0.96.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $59.2 million.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $62 million.

Based on its current operating plan, the company said its cash, cash equivalents, and marketable securities as of Dec. 31 of $1.04 billion are sufficient to cover planned expenses and capital expenditures through 2027.
