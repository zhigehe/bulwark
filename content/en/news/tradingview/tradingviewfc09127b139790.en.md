Elizabeth M O'Callahan, EVP, Chief Legal Officer at NetApp, sold 452 common shares on February 10, 2025, at a price of $120.75 per share, totaling $54,579. Following the transaction, O'Callahan directly owns 11,553 common shares of NetApp. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on December 20, 2023.
