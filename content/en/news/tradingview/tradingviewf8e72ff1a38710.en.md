SelectQuote, Inc., a leading provider of insurance products and services, has released its latest Form 10-Q report, showcasing significant improvements in both financial and operational performance. The report highlights the company's robust growth across its key segments, driven by strategic initiatives and enhanced operational efficiencies.

**Financial Highlights**

**Total Revenue**: $481.1 million, reflecting a significant increase from the previous year's $405.4 million, driven by growth in both the Senior and Healthcare Services segments.

**Income from Operations**: $70.9 million, up from $55.3 million in the prior year, indicating improved operational efficiency.

**Net Income**: $53.2 million, a substantial increase compared to $19.4 million in the previous year, highlighting strong financial performance.

**Basic EPS**: $0.31, compared to $0.12 in the prior year, demonstrating enhanced profitability per share.

**Diluted EPS**: $0.30, up from $0.11 in the previous year, reflecting the company's improved earnings performance.

**Business Highlights**

**Revenue Segments**: The company operates through three main segments: Senior, Healthcare Services, and Life. The Senior segment primarily sells Medicare-related health insurance products. Healthcare Services includes SelectRx, Population Health, and SelectPatient Management, focusing on improving patient health outcomes. The Life segment sells term life and final expense products.

**Intersegment Revenue**: The company reports intersegment revenues, which consist of affiliate marketing fees for services provided by the Senior segment to Healthcare Services and Life segments, as well as services provided by Life to other segments.

**Operational Performance**: The Senior segment reported external revenue of $253.8 million, Healthcare Services reported $183.3 million, and the Life segment reported $39.8 million for the three months ended December 31, 2024.

**Adjusted Segment EBITDA**: For the three months ended December 31, 2024, the Senior segment achieved an Adjusted EBITDA of $100.5 million, Healthcare Services $2.2 million, and Life $7.4 million, indicating strong operational performance across segments.

**Future Outlook**: The company anticipates continued growth in its Healthcare Services segment, particularly through SelectRx and SelectPatient Management, which are expected to drive improvements in patient health outcomes and operational efficiencies.
