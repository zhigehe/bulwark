\*\* Shares of Manhattan Associates drop 8% to $184.36 on Mon after software firm announces retirement of President and CEO Eddie Capel

\*\* MANH on track for lowest close since Oct 2023 and biggest daily pct drop since Jan 29

\*\* Capel retirement is effective Feb. 12. He will continue to serve as executive vice chairman, assisting with CEO transition and special projects, according to a statement on Mon

\*\* Capel will be succeeded by Eric Clark, who has been serving as CEO of NTT Data North America. Clark will also join the MANH's board

\*\* Among the 11 analysts that cover MANH, median PT is $270 and avg rating is "BUY"

\*\* With today's move, MANH is down ~32% YTD after gaining ~26% in 2024
