Denny Laurence, Chief Legal Officer of 8X8, sold 10,000 shares of common stock on February 11, 2025, at a price of $3.0 per share, totaling $30,000. Following this transaction, Laurence directly owns 233,791 shares of the company. The sale includes shares purchased on February 9, 2025, under the company's Employee Stock Purchase Plan.
