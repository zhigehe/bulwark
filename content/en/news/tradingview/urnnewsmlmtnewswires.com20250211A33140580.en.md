Foxx Development Holdings said Monday it has entered into a strategic distribution agreement with FreeCast to make the latter's streaming platform available to users of its mobile device portfolio.

The platform will be installed on smartphones and tablets of the company, with users gaining immediate access to FreeCast's entertainment hub that features over 700 free channels, extensive on-demand content and integration with major streaming services, Foxx said.
