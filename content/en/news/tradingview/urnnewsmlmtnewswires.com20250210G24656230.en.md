Perak signed a collaboration deal with Spectrum sets to jointly develop 54.58 acres of land in Perak, Malaysia.

The project aims to transform the land lots into a mixed-use development, according to a Friday Malaysian bourse filing.

The deal is not subject to shareholder nod, the transportation and logistics firm said.
