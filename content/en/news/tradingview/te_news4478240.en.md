Core producer prices in the United States, which exclude food and energy costs, rose by 0.3% over a month in January 2025, following an upwardly revised 0.4% increase in December, in line with market forecasts.

On a yearly basis, core producer inflation was 3.6% in January, after an upwardly revised 3.7% rise in December and well above market estimates of 3.3%.
