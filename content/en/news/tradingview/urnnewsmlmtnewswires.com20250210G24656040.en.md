Shanghai CDXJ Digital Technology is one of the candidates for China Telecom's Shanghai Lingang Park Data Center project bid, according to a Shanghai Stock Exchange disclosure on Sunday.

The construction will be delivered in two phases, with 40 megawatts of information technology power consumption will be installed in the building, the Chinese engineering and construction company said.

CDXJ's shares closed less than 3% higher, China Telecom's Shanghai shares closed less than 7% higher, and HK shares closed over 4% higher.
