\*\* Shares in Hermes jump around 4% to a fresh record high after the Birkin bag maker posted better-than-expected Q4 sales

\*\* Q4 sales came in at 3.96 billion euros ($4.14 billion), an 18% rise at constant exchange rates, ahead of analyst expectations for a 10% rise, according to a Visible Alpha consensus cited by UBS

\*\* "Execution and delivery remain spotless," J.P.Morgan says, describing the results as "very solid"

\*\* "Following Moncler last night, Hermes further confirms that during Q4, the luxury consumer was there, including in China, for the brands with solid momentum and execution," JPM adds

\*\* The stock sits among the top performers of France's CAC 40 index and hits a new all-time high of 2,957 euros

\*\* Peers LVMH and Gucci owner Kering rise 1.6% and 1%, respectively
