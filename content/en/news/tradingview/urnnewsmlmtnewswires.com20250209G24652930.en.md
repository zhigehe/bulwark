Pilbara Minerals said that its Korean joint venture, POSCO Pilbara Lithium Solution (PPLS), is expected to report a net loss for the fiscal first half, driven by lower lithium market prices and the continued ramp-up of operations, according to a Monday filing with the Australian bourse.

For its Pilgangoora Operation in Western Australia, PLS expects underlying fiscal first-half net loss after tax at Pilgangoora to be in the range of AU$5 million to AU$7 million, the filing said.

Underlying fiscal-first half earnings before interest, tax, depreciation, and amortization at the project is expected to be in the range of AU$71 million to AU$75 million, the filing said.
