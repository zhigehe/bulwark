Porsche plans to axe 1,900 jobs at two German sites by 2029 amid a decrease in demand for electric vehicles, Bloomberg News reported Thursday, citing the company.

The German luxury automaker intends to reduce 15% of its workforce at its plants in Zuffenhausen and Weissach through voluntary measures, including early retirement and severance packages, and implement a "restrictive approach" to new hiring.
