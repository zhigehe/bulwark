Acushnet Holdings will replace Retail Opportunity Investments in the S&P SmallCap 600, effective prior to the opening of trading on Thursday, S&P Global said late Monday.

Blackstone (BX) is acquiring Retail Opportunity Investments in a deal expected to close soon, the indexing and research firm said.
