CBRE Group reported Q4 core earnings Thursday of $2.32 per diluted share, up from $1.38 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $2.23.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $10.4 billion, compared with $8.95 billion a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $10.29 billion.

The company said it expects 2025 core EPS of $5.80 to $6.10. Analysts polled by FactSet expect $6.03.
