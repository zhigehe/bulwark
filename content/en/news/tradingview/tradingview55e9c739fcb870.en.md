Christopher W Silcock, serving as President, Global Brands & Commercial Services at Hilton Worldwide Holdings, sold 2,914 shares of Common Stock on February 7, 2025, at a price of $269.54 per share, totaling $785,439. Following this transaction, Silcock directly owns 70,453 shares of the company. The sale was executed to cover the payment of an option exercise price and tax liability related to an option granted in February 2015.
