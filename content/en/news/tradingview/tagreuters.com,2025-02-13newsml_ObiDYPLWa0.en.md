Lea bank ASA reported a profit before tax of NOK 29.7 million for Q4 2024.Grossloans amounted to NOK 7,656 million, compared to NOK 6,913 million last year.Equity at the end of the quarter was NOK 1,490 million.

This will be the last quarter reporting as a Norwegian bank. According to CEOOddbjørn Berentsen, the team of Lea Bank is exited to build Lea Bank 2.0 as adigital niche bank with headquarters in Sweden. We look forward to explore newstrategic opportunities, says Berentsen.

Highlights of Q4 2024:

Profit before tax NOK 29.7 million (after tax NOK 22.3 million)

Net interest income of NOK 136.8 million

Gross loan portfolio of NOK 7.7 billion

Cost-income ratio (C/I) of 35.3%

Book equity per share of NOK 15.02

Targets going forward for Lea Bank AB:

· Return on equity > 15% · Cost / income < 30% · Attractive dividend payments

Lea Bank will present the results for the 4[th] quarter of 2024 today at 09:30AM.

The presentation will be held through a Webcast with CEO Oddbjørn Berentsen,CFOGard Haugen and Chief of Credit Risk Christian Svendsen. It will be possibletosubmit questions during the presentation.
