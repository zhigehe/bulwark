HBX Group set a final offer price of 11.50 euros per share for its initial public offering on the Madrid, Barcelona, Bilbao, and Valencia stock exchanges, implying a market capitalization of 2.84 billion euros.

The business-to-business travel technology group aims to raise a total of 860 million euros from the offering of 74,799,999 shares, which comprise the original 63,043,478 offer shares, an additional 2 million existing shares, and an over-allotment option of 9,756,521 shares, according to a Tuesday release.

Trading and settlement are slated on Thursday, with the company trading under the ticker symbol 'HBX.'

Following the offering, Cinven and EQT will retain a 63.72% stake in the company.
