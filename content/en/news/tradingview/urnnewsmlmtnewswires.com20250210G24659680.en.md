Swift Networks Group signed a three-year agreement with Shell for their Prelude vessel to install Swift Access, with Swift's Secure Casting and provide ongoing Swift support services, according to a Tuesday Australian bourse filing.

The Prelude vessel is a floating liquefied natural gas platform located offshore Western Australia.

The total contract value is AU$804,191, and the subscription revenue from the Swift Access and Swift support services will start on March 1.
