Binastra unit Binastra Builders secured a deal worth 250.3 million ringgit for the proposed construction and completion of a data center in Selangor, Malaysia.

The deal was awarded by MYT DC3, according to a Wednesday Malaysian bourse filing.

The contract works will begin on Feb. 26 and will shall be completed in sections between June 15 and Dec. 15 this year, the system integration and maintenance services provider said.
