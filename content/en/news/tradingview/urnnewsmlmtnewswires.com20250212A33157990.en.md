Apollo Global Management said Wednesday that funds managed by its affiliates have acquired a majority stake in oil and gas infrastructure resource company Bold Production Services.

Financial terms were not disclosed.

Bold provides contract services in the treating and removal of impurities in natural gas, oil and water, Apollo said.
