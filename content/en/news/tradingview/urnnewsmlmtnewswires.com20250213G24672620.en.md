Digital China Information Service Group subsidiary System Integration Co. was awarded the highest-level CS5 certification by the China Electronics Information Industry Federation.

The certification, which is valid until the end of 2028, recognizes the company's excellence in strategic management, business operations and innovation, according to a Thursday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.
