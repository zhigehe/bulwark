Danaos reported Q4 adjusted earnings late Monday of $6.93 per diluted share, down from $6.99 a year earlier.

Two analysts polled by FactSet expected $6.95.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $258.2 million, up from $249.3 million a year earlier.

Two analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $253.4 million.

The company kept a quarterly dividend of $0.85 per share, payable March 5 to stockholders of record as of Feb. 24.
