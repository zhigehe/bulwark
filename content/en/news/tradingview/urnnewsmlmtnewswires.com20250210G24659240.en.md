South Korean chemicals company OCI Holdings widened its net loss attributable to shareholders of parent company to 66.01 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024 from 3.75 billion won a year earlier.

Sales jumped 32.3% year over year to 853.9 billion won from 645.6 billion won, according to a Monday filing with DART.

Shares of OCI Holdings rose nearly 1% in recent trade on Tuesday.
