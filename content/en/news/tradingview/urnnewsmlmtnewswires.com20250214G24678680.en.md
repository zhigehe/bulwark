Super Telecom appointed Guo Yanqi as chief financial officer to succeed Hu Hongyue.

Guo built an entrepreneurial team and was responsible for team management and operation coordination from September 2023 to December 2024, according to a Wednesday filing with the Shanghai bourse. He joined the telecommunication services provider in January.

Shares of Super Telecom rose 4% in recent trade.
