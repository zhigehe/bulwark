GomSpace A/S

Subject to successful completion of the mission, the initial agreement is expected to be followed by mission operations support services and additional satellite builds.

GomSpace North America today announced an agreement valued at $650,000 (7.1 MSEK) to provide satellite products and services to a U.S.-based firm.

Subject to successful completion of the first mission, the company anticipates follow-on contracts to support mission operations, as well as additional satellite builds for future constellations.

**About GomSpace Group AB**

The company’s business operations are mainly conducted through the wholly-owned Danish subsidiary, GomSpace A/S, with operational office in Aalborg, Denmark. GomSpace is a space company with a mission to be engaged in the global market for space systems and services by introducing new products, i.e. components, platforms and systems based on innovation within professional nanosatellites. The company is listed on the Nasdaq First North Premier exchange under the ticker GOMX. FNCA Sweden AB is the Company’s Certified Adviser. For more information, please visit our website on www.gomspace.com.

**About GomSpace North America**

GomSpace North America serves satellite buyers in the Federal, military, security, university, and commercial arenas throughout the Western Hemisphere from its headquarters in Alexandria, Virginia. Their products include satellite power supplies, communication systems including software-defined radios, command and data handling components, altitude and orbit control systems, satellite buses and structures, as well as products for ground control. GomSpace also provides services including their cloud-based ground control system (HOOP) as well as training and customized component design and fabrication.
