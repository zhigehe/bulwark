Automotive sunroof manufacturer Shanghai Mobitech Technology (SHE:301173) filed a prospectus for an initial public offering of 219.587 million shares on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange's ChiNext board

Expected issue date is Feb. 20, according to a Wednesday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Total share capital after IPO will be 87,834,772 shares.

Guotai Junan Securities serves as lead underwriter.
