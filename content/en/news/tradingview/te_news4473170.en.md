In Mumbai, the SENSEX Index decreased 548 points or 0.70 percent on Monday.

Losses were driven by Power Grid (-3.40%), Tata Steel (-3.09%) and Titan Company (-2.84%).

Biggest rises came from Kotak Mahindra Bank (1.60%), Bharti Airtel (0.97%) and HCL Tech (0.48%).
