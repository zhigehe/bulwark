Tradeweb Markets said Thursday is collaborating with Coremont to "enhance" fixed income trading workflows for buyside professionals.

Tradeweb will integrate its global fixed income execution workflows into Coremont's Clarion platform, the company said. The collaboration will initially focus on global swaps markets, Tradeweb added.
