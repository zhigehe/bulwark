Stavanger, 14 February 2025:

Reference is made to the stock exchange announcements made by Moreld AS("Moreld") earlier today regarding the publishing of the financial results forthe fourth quarter of 2024, the new dividend policy, and an intention toproposea dividend distribution. As set out in the announcement, the board ofdirectorsof Moreld intends to propose to the annual general meeting of Moreld, expectedto be held on 20 May 2025, to resolve an initial dividend distribution in theamount of NOK 0.42 per share for the second quarter of 2025, in total approx.75million.

The dates relevant for the proposed dividend distribution are set out below:

Dividend amount: NOK 0.42 per shareAnnounced currency: NOKLast day including right: 20 May 2025Ex-date: 21 May 2025Record date: 22 May 2025Payment date: On or about 26 May 2025Date of approval: 20 May 2025

The distribution shall constitute a repayment of paid-in capital by theshareholders of Moreld.

This information is published in accordance with the requirements of theEuronext Growth Oslo Rule Book Part II.
