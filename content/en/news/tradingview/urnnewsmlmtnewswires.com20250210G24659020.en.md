South Korea faces increasing economic risks due to an anticipated US-led trade war and a prolonged slump in the construction sector, the Korea Development Institute (KDI) warned, according to a Yonhap News report on Monday.

US President Donald Trump's tariff plans are seen as a considerable threat to the South Korean economy, it said.

While semiconductor and automobile production supported modest growth, weakening retail consumption, declining exports, and delayed construction recovery remained key concerns, the report said, citing KDI.
