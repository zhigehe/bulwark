Godolphin Resources hit high-grade gold mineralization from a recent drill program conducted at the Lewis Ponds gold and silver project in New South Wales, Australia, according to a Monday filing with the Australian bourse.

The assay results yielded 49.6 meters at 3.5 grams per tonne (g/t) grade of gold equivalent from 210 meters, including an internal high-grade core of 28.2 meters at 5.8 g/t gold equivalent from 219.4 meters.
