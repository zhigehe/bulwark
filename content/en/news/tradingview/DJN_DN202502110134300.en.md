Hanwha Aerospace's 2025 defense revenue guidance looks upbeat, Daiwa Capital Markets analysts say in a research report. They cite management's expectations for 20% increase in defense revenue, driven by shipments of over 70 K9 self-propelled howitzers and over 50 Chunmoo multiple rocket launcher systems to Poland. Although a detailed order pipeline is unclear, the brokerage also expects further opportunities for the South Korean company from Asia and the Middle East in 2025-2027. Daiwa lifts its 2025-2026 EPS estimates for Hanwha Aerospace by 52%-64%. It raises the stock's target price to KRW660,000.00 from KRW350,000.00, with an unchanged buy rating. Shares are 3.6% higher at KRW516,000.00. (ronnie.harui@wsj.com)
