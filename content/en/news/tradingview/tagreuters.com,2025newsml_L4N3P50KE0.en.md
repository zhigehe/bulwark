\*\* Shares of Anupam Rasayan India rise 2.6% to 686 rupees, in their busiest session in over six months

\*\* Specialty chemicals maker's Q3 consol profit surges 53% Y/Y, helped by strong demand in pharma and polymer segments; rev up 32%

\*\* Co aims for 30%-35% increase in rev in FY26

\*\* Co also signs a $12.5 mln per annum contract with a US co for supply of a specialty chemical product

\*\* More than 750,000 shares traded, over nine times their 30-day avg

\*\* Avg of rating by analysts covering the stock is "hold," same as that on peer Deepak Nitrite - LSEG

\*\* Their median PT is 737.5 rupees - LSEG

\*\* Stock declined 31% in 2024 vs flat DPNT shares
