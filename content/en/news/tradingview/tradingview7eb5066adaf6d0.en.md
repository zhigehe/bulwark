Robert L. Schwartz, VP of Human Resources at POTLATCHDELTIC CORP, sold 2,456 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, for a total of $109,832, with a weighted average price per share of $44.72. This sale was to satisfy tax withholding obligations related to restricted stock unit awards and performance share awards. Following the transaction, Schwartz directly owns 50,049 shares of the company.

Darin Robert Ball, Vice President of Timberlands at POTLATCHDELTIC CORP, sold 2,236 shares of Common Stock on the same date, at a weighted average price of $44.73 per share, totaling $100,016. This sale was also part of a pre-arranged plan to cover tax obligations related to restricted stock unit awards. After the sale, Ball directly owns 46,531 shares of the company.
