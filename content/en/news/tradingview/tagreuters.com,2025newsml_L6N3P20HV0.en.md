(The Insurer) - Munich Re Specialty has appointed Tyler Coleman as North America head of public entity risk solutions (PERS), succeeding Lauren Tredinnick, who was recently named chief risk officer of Munich Re America Services.

Coleman, who joined Munich Re Specialty in 2020, previously led its E&S healthcare team.

Before that, he spent five years at Swiss Re, serving as senior vice president and head of healthcare for North America.

His earlier career includes roles at Scor Re and CNA Insurance.

"His exceptional leadership, strategic vision, and advanced underwriting and operational capabilities have been instrumental in driving our mission to deliver innovative risk solutions to our clients," the company stated in a LinkedIn post.
