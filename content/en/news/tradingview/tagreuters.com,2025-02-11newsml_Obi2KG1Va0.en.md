Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published on 4 October2024regarding the acquisition of Predicare AB and the announcement published on 18December 2020 regarding the acquisition of Fertsoft AB.

In conjunction with these two acuisitions, in total partly settled in kind,thethe Company's share capital was increased by the issuance of a total of417.188new shares in the company. The share capital increase in Omda AS has beenregistered with the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises: - Sharecapital:NOK 2.132.234,50 (new) - Total number of shares: 21.322.345 (new) - Par valueper share: NOK 0,10.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act and the Euronext Growth Oslo RuleBook Part II.
