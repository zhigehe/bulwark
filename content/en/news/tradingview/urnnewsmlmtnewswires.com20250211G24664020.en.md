Alstom will help digitize the rail network in Germany under a 600 million-euro long-term framework deal with railway company Deutsche Bahn.

The contract includes multiple call-offs between 2025 and 2028, with the first call-off to be executed within the current quarter. The French rolling stock manufacturer will deliver at least 1,890 interlocking units, as well as control and safety technologies, according to a Tuesday release.

Implementation of the individual projects is expected to be completed in 2032.
