U.S. WTI Midland and North Sea Forties crude differentials rose on Monday, while Ekofisk weakened.

PLATTS WINDOW

\* Indications are free on board (FOB) unless marked as cost, insurance and freight (CIF) or delivered at place (DAP).

\* Three cargoes traded, a trade source said.

\* WTI Midland: Mercuria sold to Glencore Feb. 23-27 at dated Brent plus 85 cents CIF Rotterdam.

\* WTI Midland: Trafigura sold to Glencore Feb. 28-March 4 at dated Brent plus 95 cents CIF Rotterdam.

\* WTI Midland: Glencore bid for another cargo at dated plus $1.15.

\* As a result, WTI is worth dated Brent plus 10 cents on a FOB basis according to Reuters calculations, up from Friday.

\* Ekofisk: Glencore bought from Gunvor Feb. 25-27 at dated Brent plus 75 cents, down from Friday.

\* Forties: Bid at dated plus 20 cents by Gunvor and offered by Equinor at dated plus 25 cents, up from Friday.
