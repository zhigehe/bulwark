Mastercraft Boat Holdings' subsidiary, MasterCraft Boat, said Tuesday that Action Water Sports is its newest authorized dealer in the Dallas-Fort Worth area.

As dealer, Action Water Sports will offer MasterCraft's range of boats, along with on-water demonstrations and other services, the company said.

Shares of Mastercraft Boat Holdings fell 2.5% in recent trading activity.
