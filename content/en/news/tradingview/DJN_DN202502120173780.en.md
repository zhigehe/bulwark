India's Sensex is flat at 76168.67 as gains in financial stocks help offset losses in tech shares. Kotak Mahindra Bank is up 2.0% and Bajaj Finserv is 1.5% higher while Tech Mahindra is down 1.9% and HCL Technologies is 0.6% lower. Among individual movers, Natco Pharma is down 15% after third-quarter net profit dropped 37% on year. Investors are focusing on earnings as well as U.S. trade policies. Hindalco Industries is scheduled to report its quarterly results later in the day. (kosaku.narioka@wsj.com; @kosakunarioka)
