Metso agreed to acquire the screening business and key assets of Selm (Beijing) Technology Co, a manufacturer of mining and aggregate screen technologies in Shenyang, China.

The transaction is expected to expand the Finnish industrial machinery group's product offerings in China, according to a Monday release. Closing is anticipated in the second quarter, subject to customary conditions.

No financial terms of the deal were disclosed.
