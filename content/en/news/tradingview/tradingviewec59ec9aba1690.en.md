Several executives at Joby Aviation have recently sold shares of the company, according to SEC Form 4 filings.

Kate DeHoff, General Counsel and Corporate Secretary, sold 3,090 shares on February 11 and 12, 2025, at weighted average prices of $7.50 and $7.25, totaling $22,691. This was part of a 10b5-1 trading plan to cover taxes for RSU settlement. Following these transactions, DeHoff directly owns 190,132 shares.

Joeben Bevirt, CEO and Chief Architect, sold 1,706 shares on February 11, 2025, at a weighted average price of $7.5, totaling $12,795, to cover taxes for RSU settlement. Bevirt directly owns 774,060 shares and indirectly owns 93,582,719 shares through various trusts and his spouse.

Papadopoulos Didier, President of Aircraft OEM, sold 2,867 shares on February 11 and 12, 2025, at weighted average prices of $7.5 and $7.25, totaling $21,143. Following these transactions, Didier directly owns 52,835 shares.
