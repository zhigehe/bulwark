Ferrari Group (FERGR.AS) on Thursday priced its initial public offering on Euronext Amsterdam at 8.60 euros per share to raise 196.3 million euros in gross proceeds.

The UK-based luxury goods shipment company intends to issue 22,825,000 existing shares in a private placement with institutional investors, according to a same-day release. The offer price implies a market capitalization of 785.2 million euros.

Trading on an "as-if-and-when-delivered" basis will begin Thursday, while settlement is scheduled for Feb. 17.
