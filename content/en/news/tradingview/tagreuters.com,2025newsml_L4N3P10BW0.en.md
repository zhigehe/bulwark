Thailand will start the selection process in March for the next governor of its central bank, the finance minister said on Monday.

The current governor's term ends in September.

Finance Minister Pichai Chunhavajira also said the government had nominated for another role, the chair of the Bank of Thailand's board, former senior finance ministry official Somchai Sujjapongse. The board post has no direct impact on monetary policy.
