Dongwon Systems posted net income attributable to shareholders of 16.7 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, up 34.2% from 12.4 billion won a year earlier.

Sales jumped 11.02% year over year to 332.3 billion won from 299.3 billion won, according to a Tuesday Korea Exchange filing.

Shares of Dongwon Systems rose nearly 2% in recent trade on Wednesday.
