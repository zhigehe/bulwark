Revenue increased 14.7% to 205.5 billion yen from 179.2 billion yen a year earlier.

M3 forecasted attributable profit to be in the range of 44.0 billion to 46.0 billion yen or 64.80 yen to 67.75 yen per share, with net sales projected at 268 billion yen to 273 billion yen for the fiscal year ending March 31.
