Highlights 2024:

Obtaining a Swedish banking license for Lea Bank AB.

Successful redomiciliation from Norway to Sweden, and change of listing toNasdaq Stockholm

Profit before tax of NOK 120 milllion

Loan growth of NOK 743 million

Cost to income ratio of 31.5%

As a Norwegian bank we have delivered positive results every quarter since Q12017 and has generated a pre-tax profit in this period of around 870 MNOK,saysCEO Oddbjørn Berentsen. Over the past years, Lea Bank has paid out 186 MNOK individends to shareholders (NOK 1.96 per share) with a dividend yield of 6.7%,9.2% and 7.1% in 2021, 2022 and 2023.
