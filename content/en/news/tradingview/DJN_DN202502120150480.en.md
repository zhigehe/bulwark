Central Securities Corporation Releases Annual Report to Stockholders for the Year Ended December 31, 2024

NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--February 12, 2025--

Central Securities Corporation (NYSE American: CET), a closed-end investment company, today released its Annual Report to Stockholders for the year ended December 31, 2024.

Figures as of December 31, 2024, compared with those of one year ago, are as follows:

```
   
                               December 31,    December 31,   
                                   2024            2023   
                              --------------  --------------   
   
Net assets per common share           $54.26          $46.49   
Net assets                    $1,569,940,654  $1,319,864,836   
Shares outstanding                28,935,676      28,387,828   
 
```

Additional details are available at www.centralsecurities.com.
