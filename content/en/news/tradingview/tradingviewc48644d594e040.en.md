David L Finkelstein, CEO and Co-CIO of Annaly Capital Management, sold 50,000 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $20.6 per share, totaling $1,030,000. Following the transaction, Finkelstein directly owns 677,465 shares of the company. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 plan adopted for tax and estate planning purposes.
