NORMA GROUP SE :

* DIVESTMENT PROCESS FOR THE WATER MANAGEMENT BUSINESS INITIATED
* ACCORDING TO PRELIMINARY, UNAUDITED FIGURES, GROUP SALES IN THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER TO DECEMBER 2024 TOTALED EUR 266.8 MILLION
* Q4 ADJUSTED EBIT AMOUNTED TO EUR 19.5 MILLION IN THE FOURTH QUARTER, 15.1 PERCENT BELOW THE PRIOR-YEAR FIGURE (Q4 2023: EUR 23.0 MILLION)
* Q4 ADJUSTED EBIT MARGIN WAS 7.3 PERCENT (Q4 2023: 8.0 PERCENT)
