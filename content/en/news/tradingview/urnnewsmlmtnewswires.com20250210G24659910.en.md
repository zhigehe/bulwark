Hubei Energy's power generation in January dropped about 17% year on year to 3.37 billion kilowatt hours, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange filing on Monday.

The drop was attributed to the decrease in hydropower and thermal power generation, which fell 61% and 8.4%, respectively.

New energy power rose 74% year on year, the Chinese utility network operator said.
