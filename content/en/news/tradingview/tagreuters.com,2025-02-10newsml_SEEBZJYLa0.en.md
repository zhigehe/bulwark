TIRANA (Albania), February 10 (SeeNews) - The non-performing loan (NPL) ratio of Albania's banking system stood at 4.17% at the end of December, a decrease from the 4.48% registered at the end of November, the central bank said on Monday.

The NPL ratio also decreased on an annual comparison basis, down from 4.74% in December 2023, the central bank said in a data release.

The data refers to the loan portfolios of eleven banks operating in Albania.
