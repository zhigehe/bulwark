ParaZero Technologies said Wednesday it has signed agreements to sell about $3.1 million shares and pre-funded warrants to some institutional investors.

The offering consists of about 2.8 million shares at $1.10 per share, or pre-funded warrants at $1.09999 pre-funded warrant, the company said. It expects to close the offering around Thursday, with Aegis Capital Corp. as placement agent.

The aerospace company plans to use the net proceeds for general corporate purposes and working capital.

Shares of ParaZero were down more than 13% in recent trading.
