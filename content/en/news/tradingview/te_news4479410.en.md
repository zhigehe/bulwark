Finland’s economy expanded 0.3% quarter-on-quarter in the fourth quarter of 2024, up from a 0.1% rise in the previous three-month period but below market forecasts of a 0.4% advance, preliminary estimates showed.

This marked the third consecutive period of economic growth.

On a yearly basis, GDP rose 0.7% in the December quarter, following a 0.5% growth in the prior quarter.
