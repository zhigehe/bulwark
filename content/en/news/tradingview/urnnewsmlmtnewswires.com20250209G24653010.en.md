GQG Partners reported funds under management of $160.4 billion as of Jan. 31, up from AU$153 billion last month, according to a Monday filing with the Australian bourse.

International equity rose to $60.6 billion from $57.2 billion, while global equity grew to $41.3 billion from $38.8 billion.

January net flows were $1.7 billion.

GQG Partners' shares were up nearly 4% in recent Monday trade.
