The actual volume of ready-mixed concrete produced in New Zealand fell 6.2% year on year to 967,964 cubic meters in the December 2024 quarter, following a 5.5% decline in the prior three-month period, Stats NZ data showed Wednesday.

In the year ended December 2024, ready-mixed concrete produced fell 7.7% to 3.82 million cubic meters.

In seasonally adjusted terms, the volume of ready-mixed concrete fell 4.7% in the December 2024 quarter, following a 0.6% rise in the prior three-month period.
