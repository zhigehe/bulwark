Kingland Technology is planning to invest 10 million yuan in establishing a subsidiary in Baiyun district in Guangzhou, China.

The tentatively named Indium Target Technology (Guangzhou) will focus on electronic materials research and development, metal materials sales and recycling technologies, according to a Feb. 8 filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of the ecological environment services provider closed 1% higher Monday.
