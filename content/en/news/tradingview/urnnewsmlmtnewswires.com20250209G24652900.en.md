Templeton Emerging Markets Investment Trust purchased for cancellation 618,331 ordinary shares at a price of 1.71 pounds sterling apiece, according to a Monday filing with the New Zealand bourse.

Following the transaction, the company's share capital consists of 1.04 billion shares in issue and 103.8 million shares in treasury, per the filing.
