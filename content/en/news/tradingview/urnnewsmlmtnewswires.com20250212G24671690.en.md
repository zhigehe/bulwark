Carnaby Resources found copper and gold mineralization at the Greater Duchess copper-gold project in Mt Isa, Queensland, according to a Thursday Australian bourse filing.

The results include 12.2 meters at 6.8% copper and 1.8 grams per tonne of gold from 558 meters at Mount Hope Central, per the filing.

Results from Mount Hope North include 18 meters at 1.3% copper and 0.1g/t gold from 127 meters, the filing added.

Company shares were down 9% in recent trade.
