Mexico's car output rose by 1.7% year-on-year to 351,257 units in January 2025, following a 4.2% increase in the previous month, marking the highest figure for a January since 2020.

Light trucks accounted for 73.8% of the total production, while the remainder was attributed to automobile manufacturing.

Among the major producers, output increased at Nissan (+8% to 61,085), Toyota (+243.1% to 26,509) and KIA (+53.8% to 24,300), but declined for General Motors (-10.7% to 60,113).
