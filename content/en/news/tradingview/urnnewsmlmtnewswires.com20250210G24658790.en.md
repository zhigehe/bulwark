Recruit Holdings repurchased a total of 64.6 million shares for approximately 600.00 billion yen between July 10, 2024, and Feb. 7, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The buyback program, which was approved on July 9, 2024, was completed after the company bought back 1,066,500 shares for 11.5 billion yen from Feb. 1 to Feb. 7 on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.
