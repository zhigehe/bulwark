United Fidelity Insurance (ADX:FIDELITYUNITED) shares will pause trading on the Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange from 1 pm local time on Thursday due to a board meeting.

The United Arab Emirates-based insurance company will resume trading after the Abu Dhabi bourse receives the meeting's results, according to a Monday release.
