\*\* Shares of Hess Midstream down 2.1% at $39.98 in extended trading on news of secondary offering

\*\* Houston-based subsidiary of Hess Corp commences 10 mln share offering by affiliate of Global Infrastructure Partners (GIP)

\*\* Offering to cut BlackRock-owned GIP's stake from 14.3% to 9.6%, or ~20.8 mln shares, per the SEC filing

\*\* Goldman Sachs sole bookrunner

\*\* HESM shares on Mon finished up 0.9% at $40.85. Stock up 10% YTD and up about 27% over the past 12 months
