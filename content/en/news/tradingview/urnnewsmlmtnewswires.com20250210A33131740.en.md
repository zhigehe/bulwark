Cognizant Technology Solutions said Monday it has extended its IT consulting agreement with bank-insurer KBC Group until 2027

Financial terms were not disclosed.

Under the agreement, Cognizant will support KBC Group's IT and business operations, including application development, data services, infrastructure and engineering, the company said.
