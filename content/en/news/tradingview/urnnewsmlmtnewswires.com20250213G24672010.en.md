As expected, Germany's annual inflation rate fell to 2.3% in January from 2.6% in the prior month, final data from the country's Federal Statistical Office showed Thursday.

On a monthly basis, consumer prices were 0.2% lower, consistent with the preliminary reading.

The harmonized inflation rate held steady at 2.8% on an annual basis, in line with the flash estimate. Month over month, harmonized consumer prices declined 0.2%, matching the initial data.
