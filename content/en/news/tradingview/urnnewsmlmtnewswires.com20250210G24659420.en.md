Radius Residential Care repurchased 10,000 ordinary shares at an average price of NZ$0.1930 apiece in an on-market buyback on Monday, according to a Tuesday New Zealand bourse filing.

Following the transaction, the company has nearly 284.7 million ordinary shares on issue, the filing said.

The acquired shares will be cancelled.
