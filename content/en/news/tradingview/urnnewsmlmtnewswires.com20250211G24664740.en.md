Maruichi Steel Tube's profit attributable to owners of the parent plunged 44.7% to 11.31 billion yen in the nine months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, from 20.4 billion yen a year ago.

The steel manufacturer's earnings per share declined to 140.73 yen from 256.55 yen a year ago, according to a Tokyo Stock Exchange filing on Monday.

Net sales fell 2.3% to 198.8 billion yen from 203.6 billion yen a year earlier.

Maruichi Steel forecasted attributable profit to grow 1.9% to 26.6 billion yen or 332.86 yen per share and net sales to decline 4.9% to 258 billion yen for the fiscal year ending March 31.

It forecasted year-end dividend of 76 yen per share, bringing the expected total annual dividend to 131 yen per share.

Shares of the company gained nearly 2% in recent trade.
