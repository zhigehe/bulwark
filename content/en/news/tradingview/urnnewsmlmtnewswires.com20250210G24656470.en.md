Left Field Printing's unit Union Offset has extended the lease term for a premise in Australia leased from landlord 16 Nyrang Street Fyshwick, to eight years, from April 1, 2022 till March 31, 2030, a Feb. 7 bourse filing said.

The lease was previously effective for three years, from April 1, 2022 to March 31, 2025.

The printing company currently uses the premises for factory and office use for its operation in Canberra, Australia.
