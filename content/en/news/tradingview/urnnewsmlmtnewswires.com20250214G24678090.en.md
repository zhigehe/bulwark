BII Railway Transportation Technology will sell its 49% stake in Metro Technology through public tender on the China Beijing Equity Exchange, according to a Thursday filing with the Hong Kong Exchange.

Metro Technology, a public transport networking and controlling systems company, is a joint venture that is 49% held by BII Railway Transportation Technology and 51% held by Beijing Subway.

The minimum consideration is slated to be 68.3 million yuan, the filing said.

BII Railway will use the proceeds from the divestment to repay its loans, and fund working capital and future investments.

Shares rose 2% during Friday's afternoon trading.
