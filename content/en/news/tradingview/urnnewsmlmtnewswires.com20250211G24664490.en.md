Asante reported January net sales of 815 million yen, down 3.5% from a year earlier, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The pest control and home maintenance firm recorded a year-to-date sales increase of 2.3%, with total revenue reaching 11.8 billion yen. December 2024 sales had risen 16.1%, offsetting weaker months in November and January.
