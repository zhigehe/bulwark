Joseph J Wolk, serving as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer at Johnson & Johnson, sold 13,015 shares of common stock on February 7, 2025, at a weighted average price of $153.89 per share, totaling $2,002,878. Following this transaction, Wolk no longer directly owns any common stock in the company but retains indirect ownership of 70,948 shares through a spousal lifetime access trust and a 401k plan.
