Hunan Kylinsec Technology has been removed from the military procurement blacklist, effective immediately, according to a Friday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

This reinstatement allows the company to once again participate in military material and engineering service procurement activities.

Due to a regulatory violation, the company was previously restricted from bidding for one year, from Jan. 29, 2024, to Jan. 28, 2025.

Qilin Xinan commits to strengthening corporate governance and internal controls to enhance competitiveness.
