Hanesbrands said Thursday that Chief Executive Steve Bratspies will depart at the end of 2025, or after the appointment of a successor.

The company said it has retained executive search firm Spencer Stuart to find a successor.

Bratspies will also step down from the board at end of his tenure but remain in an advisory role once a new chief executive is named, the company said.
