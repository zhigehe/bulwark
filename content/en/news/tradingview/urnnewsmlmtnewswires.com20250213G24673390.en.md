Barclays said Thursday that its profit and total income for the full-year 2024 increased year over year.

Profit attributable to equity holders of the parent for the 12 months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 5.32 billion pounds sterling, compared with 4.27 billion pounds a year ago. EPS moved to 0.348 pound from 0.269 pound.

The British lender's total income was 26.79 billion pounds, compared with 25.38 billion pounds earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet were expecting 26.32 billion pounds in sales, 5.32 billion pounds in net income, and 0.35 pound in EPS.

Meanwhile, a full-year dividend of 0.055 pound per share will be paid on April 4 to shareholders on record Feb. 28. It paid 0.053 pound per share a year ago.

Barclays plans to achieve group net interest income, excluding Barclays Investment Bank and Head Office, of 12.2 billion pounds in 2025 and a total income of 30 billion pounds in 2026.
