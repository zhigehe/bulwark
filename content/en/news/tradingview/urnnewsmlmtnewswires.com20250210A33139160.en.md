Brixmor Property Group reported Q4 funds from operations of $0.53 per diluted share late Monday, up from $0.51 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.54.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $328.4 million, up from $316.5 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $327.7 million.

The company expects 2025 FFO of $2.19 to $2.24 per diluted share. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $2.22.
