Canada's main stock index opened higher on Monday, lifted by metal mining shares, while the country's second-biggest lender Toronto-Dominion Bank rose 1.7% on plans to divest its stake worth $15.4 billion in Charles Schwab .

At 9:31 a.m. ET (1431 GMT), the Toronto Stock Exchange's S&P/TSX composite index was up 0.98% at 25691.57.
