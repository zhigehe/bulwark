Waja (SASE:9560) renewed its sharia-compliant credit facility with Arab National Bank (SASE:1080), according to a Sunday release.

The 6 million-Saudi-riyal facility is backed by a promissory note and will be used by the construction, media, and information technology company for short-term Tawarruq financing.

The facility offers a period of one year.
