Ordinary revenue rose 12.6% to 175.9 billion yen from 156.1 billion yen a year earlier.

The lender forecasted attributable profit to grow 7.8% to 40 billion yen or 84.39 yen per share and net sales to gain 60.4% to 56.5 billion yen for the fiscal year ending March 31.
