Wens Foodstuff Group reported mixed revenue on the sales of chickens and pigs in January.

The company posted revenue on chicken sales of 2.37 billion yuan, down from 2.79 billion yuan the previous year, due to fewer chicken sold and lower prices.

Revenue from pig sales rose year over year to 5.35 billion yuan from 4.24 billion yuan amid increases in both the volume and prices sold.

Shares of the chicken and pig product producer were down 1% in recent trade.
