Lander Sports Development officially changed its name to Chengdu New Tianfu Culture Tourism Development, effective Friday, according to a same-dated Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure.

The company completed the filing procedures for the new name.

The Chinese sports and leisure facilities developer's abbreviation is changed to Tianfu Culture Tourism, but its stock code will remain unchanged.

The company's shares closed 1% lower.
