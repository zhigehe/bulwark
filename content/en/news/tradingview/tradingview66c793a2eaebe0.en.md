F&M BANK CORP Director, Christopher S. Runion, has reported purchasing a total of 825 shares of Common Stock across six transactions, with prices per share ranging from $17.14 to $20.99. The total purchase amount reported is $15,848. Following these transactions, Runion directly owns 7,553 shares and indirectly owns 771 shares of F&M BANK CORP Common Stock through the Heifer Investment Corporate Account.
