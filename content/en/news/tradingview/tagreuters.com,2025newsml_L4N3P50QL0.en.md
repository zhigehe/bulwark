\*\* India's Hexaware Technologies 87.50 billion rupees ($1.01 bln) IPO fully subscribed on day 3 of bidding, exchange data shows

\*\* Carlyle Group-backed IT firm's IPO receives bids worth over 90.31 billion rupees at the upper end of the 674-708 rupees price band

\*\* Qualified institutional buyers bid for nearly five times the shares reserved - exchange data

\*\* Non-institutional investors and retail subscriptions muted at hardly a tenth of shares on offer - exchange data

\*\* HEXW, earlier this month, cut its IPO size to 87.5 billion rupees from 99.5 billion rupees

\*\* No new shares will be issued in the IPO, while CA Magnum Holdings - affiliate of Carlyle Group - will sell part of its stake, prospectus shows

($1 = 86.8450 Indian rupees)
