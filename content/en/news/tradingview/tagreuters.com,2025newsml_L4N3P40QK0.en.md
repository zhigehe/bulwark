\*\* Coca Cola HBC AG jumps 6.5% to record high of 3,162p; stock last up 5.5%, top mover on FTSE 100 CURRENCYCOM:UK100

\*\* Switzerland-based bottler posts FY adjusted EBIT of EUR 1.19 billion ($1.24 billion), beating co-compiled analyst consensus of EUR 1.18 billion

\*\* Co estimates 7%-11% FY25 organic EBIT growth, 6-8% organic revenue growth

\*\* London-listed company expects macroeconomic and geopolitical environment to remain challenging this year

\*\* "We see the business better able to absorb volatility vs history, shares look inexpensive for the quality," Jefferies analysts say

\*\* In 2024, the stock rose by 18.52%
