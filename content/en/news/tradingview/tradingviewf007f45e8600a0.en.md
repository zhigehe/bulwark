The Bank of Nova Scotia, a 10% owner and considered a director-by-deputization of KeyCorp, purchased 50,000 common shares on February 5, 2025, at a price of $17.85 per share, totaling $892,500. Following this transaction, the Bank of Nova Scotia directly owns 162,983,726 common shares of KeyCorp.
