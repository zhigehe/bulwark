In connection with the release of the 4th quarter 2024 results, Bonheur ASAwillpublish its 4th quarter report on Thursday 27th February at 09:00, by way of awebcast.

To watch presentation in a webcast, use this link:https://edge.media-server.com/mmc/p/mvug5tes

To participate in the webcast and ask questions during the Q&A session pleaseuse this link to register:https://register-conf.media-server.com/register/BIbf0140bb22a9427b9165e062a6e09477

Step 1: Click on the call link and complete the online registration form.Step 2: Upon registering you will receive the dial-in info and a unique PIN tojoin the call as well as an email confirmation with the details.Step 3: Select a method for joining the call: a) Dial-In: A dial in number and unique PIN are displayed to connect directlyfrom your phone. b) Call Me: Enter your phone number and click "Call Me" for an immediatecallback from the system. The call will come from a US number.

The quarterly report and the presentation file will be published on ourhomepage(www.Bonheur.no) and Oslo Stock Exchange (www.newsweb.no) prior to theconference.
