Hanwha posted net loss attributable to shareholders of 4.5 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, narrowing from 118.4 billion won a year earlier.

The South Korean conglomerate's sales jumped 24.3% year over year to 18 trillion won from 14.5 trillion won, according to a Wednesday filing with DART.

Shares of Hanwha jumped nearly 14% in recent trade on Thursday.
