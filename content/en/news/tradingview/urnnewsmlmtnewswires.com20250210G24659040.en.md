FMG Pilbara's off-market bid for Red Hawk Mining was declared unconditional after the condition of the offer was fulfilled, according to a Tuesday Australian bourse filing.

FMG Pilbara is a unit of Fortescue Ltd , and it holds an 87.25% stake in Red Hawk Mining as of Tuesday.

The firm will send a payment to Red Hawk shareholders who accepted the offer, which is scheduled to close on March 3.
