Kimbal Musk, serving as a Director at Tesla, sold a total of 75,000 shares of Tesla's Common Stock on February 6, 2025. The transactions were executed at weighted average prices of $367.868 and $368.43, with the total sale amounting to $27,590,618. Following these transactions, Kimbal Musk directly owns 1,463,220 shares of Tesla.
