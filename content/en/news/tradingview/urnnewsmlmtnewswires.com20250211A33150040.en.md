Invesco reported late Tuesday preliminary assets under management of $1.903 trillion at the end of January, up 3.1% from $1.846 trillion at December end.

"Favorable market returns" had a $37 billion effect on January AUM, with a $1.2 billion gain from foreign exchange fluctuations, the company said.
