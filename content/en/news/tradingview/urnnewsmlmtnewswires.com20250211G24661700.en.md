EssilorLuxottica purchased Cellview Imaging, a manufacturer of ophthalmic imaging devices based in Toronto, Canada.

The acquisition will support the eyewear giant's development of a wider ophthalmic instruments portfolio for early diagnosis of retinal pathologies, according to a Tuesday release.
