An application by Poly Property's unit Poly Property Group for the proposed issue of up to 7 billion yuan of bonds has been approved by China Securities Regulatory Commission, a Thursday Hong Kong bourse filing said.

The domestic corporate bonds are intended to be issued to professional investors in multiple tranches within 24 months of approval.
