Tegna's KFAA unit and The Dallas Wings said Thursday that they entered into a broadcast rights deal that makes the station the exclusive local television partner of the women's basketball team.

KFAA channel 29 will air all of the Dallas Wings games in 2025 that are not designated for national television, while KFAA and Tegna will manage local advertising and sponsorships, Tegna and Dallas Wings said.

Financial terms of the broadcast rights deal were not disclosed.
