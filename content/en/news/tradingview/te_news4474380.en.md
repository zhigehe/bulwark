The annual inflation rate in Moldova climbed to 9.1% in January 2025 from 7% in December 2024.

This marked the highest inflation rate since August 2023, as prices increased at a faster pace across all sub-indices: food products (7.43% vs. 7.17% in December), non-food products (4.69% vs. 4.55%), and services (18.04% vs. 10.53%).

On a monthly basis, consumer prices rose to 2.8% in January 2025 from 1% in December 2024, marking its highest level since April 2022.
