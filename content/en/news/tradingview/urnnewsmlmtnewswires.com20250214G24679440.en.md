Royal KPN and Dutch pension fund ABP's tower joint venture Althio began operations on Friday, according to a same-day release.

With its 51% stake, the Dutch telecommunications company fully consolidates Althio from the transaction's closing, leading the company to raise its 2025 adjusted EBITDA AL guidance to more than 2.6 billion euros. KPN also reaffirms its outlook for 3% group service revenue growth.

The two parties signed a deal to create the company in June 2024.
