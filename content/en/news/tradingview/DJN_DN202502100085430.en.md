By Janet H. Cho

President Donald Trump is expected to direct the Justice Department to pause enforcing the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act, which prohibits U.S. companies from bribing officials of foreign governments.

He is expected to sign an executive order outlining the change in U.S. policy on Monday afternoon, according to reports by Bloomberg and CNBC.

The White House could not be immediately reached for confirmation.
