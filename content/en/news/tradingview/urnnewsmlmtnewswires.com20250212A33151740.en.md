Generac Holdings reported Q4 adjusted net income Wednesday of $2.80 per diluted share, up from $2.07 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $2.53.

Net sales for the quarter ended Dec. 31 were $1.23 billion, up from $1.06 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $1.25 billion.

Generac said it expects 2025 sales to grow between 3% to 7%.

The company's shares were up 2% in recent premarket activity Wednesday.
