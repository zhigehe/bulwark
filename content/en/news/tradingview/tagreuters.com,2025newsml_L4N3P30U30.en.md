State-owned explorer Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) said on Wednesday that its joint venture with utility firm NTPC Green Energy will acquire Ayana Renewable Power Pvt Ltd.

Ayana is valued at $2.3 billion including debt, ONGC said in a statement.
