(Corrects the Q4 revenue figure in the third paragraph.)

Curbline Properties reported Q4 funds from operations Tuesday of $0.22 per diluted share, up from $0.16 a year earlier.

That result matched the average estimate of analysts polled by FactSet.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $34.9 million, up from $25.5 million a year earlier.

The company said it expects 2025 Operating FFO of $0.97 to $1.01. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $0.87 per share.
