The Oregon Community Foundation, a 10% owner in Jewett Cameron Trading Co Ltd, reported selling a total of 11,562 shares of common stock on February 12 and 13, 2025. The sales were conducted at prices of $4.6357 and $4.5243 per share, respectively, totaling $52,533. Following these transactions, the Oregon Community Foundation directly owns 995,400 shares of Jewett Cameron Trading Co Ltd.
