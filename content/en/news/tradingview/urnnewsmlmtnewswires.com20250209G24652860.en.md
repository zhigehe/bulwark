Sega Sammy Holdings postponed the expected closing of its acquisition of Nasdaq-listed GAN Ltd. to the first quarter of fiscal 2026, citing pending regulatory approvals in certain jurisdictions, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The Japanese gaming and entertainment group said the delay, agreed upon with GAN, does not affect other terms of the deal.
