The following are the top stories on the New York Times business pages. Reuters has not verified these stories and does not vouch for their accuracy.

- U.S. oil and gas company Chevron said that it will lay off about 20% or up to 9,000 positions in its workforce, as part of a cost-cutting effort.

- Federal Communications Commission Chair Brendan Carr has ordered an investigation into the diversity, equity and inclusion programs at Comcast , the parent company of NBC News and Universal Studios.

- OpenAI's board of directors in a court filing questioned the rationale of a $97.4 billion bid from Elon Musk and others to gain control of the artificial intelligence company.

- The arts and crafts retailer Joann said it is preparing to close 500 of its 800 remaining stores after its latest bankruptcy filing.
