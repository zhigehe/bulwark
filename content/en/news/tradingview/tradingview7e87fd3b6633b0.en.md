Edward K. Christian Trust, a 10% owner in Saga Communications, sold 1,750 shares of Class A Common Stock on February 7, 2025. The shares were sold at a weighted average price of $12.7458, totaling $22,305. Following the transaction, the Edward K. Christian Trust directly owns 943,250 shares of Saga Communications.
