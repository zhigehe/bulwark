Reference is made to the stock exchange notice from 3 February 2025 announcingthe continuation of the share buyback program for Otello Corporation ASA (theCompany). From 3 February 2025 through 7 February 2025, Pareto Securities AShasbought 303,000 shares on behalf of the Company at an average price of NOK 7.60and a total value of NOK 2,302,790. Since the initiation of this continuationofthe share buyback program a total of 303,000 shares at an average price of NOK7.60 and a total value of NOK 2,302,790 have been acquired. The maximumconsideration to be paid for shares acquired under this buyback program is NOK15 per share and the maximum number of shares that can be purchased under thiscontinuation of the buyback program is 866,690 shares (being the maximumremaining number of outstanding shares that can be purchased under theexistingauthorization). At present date, Otello owns 8,546,260 treasury shares in theCompany (including those acquired under the previous buyback program). Theshares are purchased in accordance with the authorization granted at thecompany's annual general meeting on 3 June 2024.
