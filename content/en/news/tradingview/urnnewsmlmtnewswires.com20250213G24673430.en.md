The UK's construction output decreased 0.2% month over month in December 2024, following a revised 0.6% increase in the previous month, the Office for National Statistics said Thursday.

On a yearly basis, the construction output in Britain was up 1.5%, against the revised 1% incline earlier and the consensus forecast of a 1.1% gain.
