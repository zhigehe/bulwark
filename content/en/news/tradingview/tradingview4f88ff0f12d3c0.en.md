Glenn Darrel Sanford, CEO and Chairman of the Board of EXP World Holdings, and a 10% owner, sold 25,000 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025. The shares were sold at a weighted average price of $10.9631, totaling $274,077. Following the transaction, Sanford directly owns 39,968,400 shares of the company.
