Beijing Capital International Airport expects a loss of between 1.35 billion yuan and 1.65 billion yuan for the year 2024, down 2.9% to 21% from 1.7 billion yuan a year prior, a Friday Hong Kong bourse filing said.

The airport owner and operator attributed the anticipated loss mainly to a reversal of certain deferred tax assets, leading to an increase in income tax expenses.
