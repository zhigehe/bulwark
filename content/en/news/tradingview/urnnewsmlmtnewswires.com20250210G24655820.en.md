Kingland Technology is planning to invest 10 million yuan in establishing a subsidiary in Shenzhen's Nanshan district.

The tentatively named Indium Target Technology (Shenzhen) will focus on electronic materials research and development, metal sales and resource recycling technologies, according to a Feb. 8 filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of the ecological environment services provider rose 1% in recent trade.
