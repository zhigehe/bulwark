South Korea's export price index, measured in Korean won, rose 8.5% year on year in January, while the import price index increased 6.6%, the Bank of Korea reported Friday.

On an on-month basis, the export price index and the import price index increased 1.2% and 2.3%, respectively, the South Korean central bank said.

The net barter terms of trade index rose 3.1% on-year during the month.
