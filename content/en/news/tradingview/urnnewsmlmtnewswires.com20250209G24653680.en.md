South Korean apparel maker F&F posted net income attributable to shareholders of 107.2 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, up 5.5% from 101.6 billion won a year earlier.

Sales declined 6.2% year over year to 546.5 billion won from 582.8 billion won, according to a Friday filing with KIND.

Shares of F&F rose more than 3% in recent trade on Monday.
