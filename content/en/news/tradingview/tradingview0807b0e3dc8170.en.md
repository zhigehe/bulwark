Christopher J. Nassetta, serving in a role denoted as 'See Remarks', and identified as a Director at Hilton Worldwide Holdings, sold 40,653 shares of Common Stock on February 7, 2025. The shares were sold at a weighted average price of $268.83, totaling $10,928,745. Following this transaction, Nassetta directly owns 77,383 shares and indirectly owns 3,416,805 shares of Hilton Worldwide Holdings Common Stock. The indirect ownership includes shares held in a revocable trust and by Harwood Road LLC, with Nassetta disclaiming beneficial ownership except to the extent of his pecuniary interest.
