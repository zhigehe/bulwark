Steel Hawk (KLSE:SKHAWK) secured a deal for the supply of manpower for bagging operations at the solid product warehouse, Pengerang Integrated Complex in Johor, Malaysia.

The deal was awarded by Petroliam Nasional's (PETRONAS) unit, PRPC Utilities and Facilities, according to a Wednesday Malaysian bourse filing.

The contract will run from Feb. 1, 2025, until Jan. 31, 2028, the oil and gas services and equipment provider said.

The deal will not have materially affect the share capital and shareholding structure of the company.
