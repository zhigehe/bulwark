Orion Minerals appointed Kevin Moxham as chief operating officer, effective Monday, according to a Tuesday Australian bourse filing.

Moxham is a registered professional engineer with 40 years of experience in mining, engineering, and project management, the filing added.

Company shares were down past 6% in recent Wednesday trade.
