\*\* Shares of state-run telecom company Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Ltd (MTNL) jump 6.4% to 56 rupees

\*\* Indian government approves 60 bln rupees ($683 mln) spending to accelerate 4G network expansion of MTNL and Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd (BSNL), broadcaster CNBC TV18 reports

\*\* MTNL did not immediately respond to a request for comment

\*\* Trading vols 11 mln shares vs 30-day avg of ~7 mln shares

\*\* Day's move take YTD gains to ~11%
