Several directors at Lifevantage Corp have recently sold shares of the company's common stock to cover tax obligations related to the vesting of restricted stock awards.

Michael A. Beindorff, a Director at Lifevantage Corp, sold 5,850 shares on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $19.54, totaling $114,309. Following the sale, Beindorff directly owns 44,168 shares and indirectly owns 80,054 shares through trusts and family arrangements. The sales were executed in multiple transactions with prices ranging from $19.39 to $19.63.

Cynthia Latham, another Director at Lifevantage Corp, sold 4,450 shares on the same date at a price of $20.02 per share, totaling $89,089. After the transaction, Latham directly owns 51,348 shares of the company.

Raymond B Greer, also a Director at Lifevantage Corp, sold 8,000 shares on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $20.1527, with the total sale amounting to $161,221. Prices for Greer's sale ranged from $20.02 to $20.30. Following this transaction, Greer directly owns 99,288 shares, with a portion held in a living trust where he is the sole trustee and lifetime beneficiary.
