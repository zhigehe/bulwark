The UK Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors house price balance decreased to 22% in January, from the revised 26% in the previous month, according to data released Thursday.

Analysts expected a 27% reading for the month.

While house prices continued to increase across the UK, the momentum was the strongest in Northern Ireland and the North West of England. Meanwhile, price growth moderated across Yorkshire and the Humber and the South East.
