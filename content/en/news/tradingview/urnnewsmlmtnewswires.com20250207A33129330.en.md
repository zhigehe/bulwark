President Donald Trump plans to disclose "reciprocal" tariffs next week and will hold a press conference on the matter, he told reporters at the Oval Office, multiple media outlets reported Friday.

The move will reportedly be a major escalation of his trade war with US partners, Bloomberg reported.

Trump also said that tariffs targeting automobiles were under consideration, according to Bloomberg.
