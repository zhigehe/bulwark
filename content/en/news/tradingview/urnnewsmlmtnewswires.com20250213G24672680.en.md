Hillgrove Resources identified exploration targets with potential for down-dip extensions to known mineralization at the Kanmantoo copper project in South Australia, according to a Thursday Australian bourse filing.

The firm is planning to conduct over 19,000 meters of diamond drilling after identifying the targets.

The firm's shares rose over 9% on market close on Thursday.
