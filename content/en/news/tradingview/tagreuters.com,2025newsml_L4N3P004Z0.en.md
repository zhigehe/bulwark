\*\* Shares of electronics retailer JB Hi-Fi rise as much as 5.5% to their all-time high of A$108.08

\*\* Co posts 8% rise in HY NPAT to A$285.4 million ($178.49 million), beating Visible Alpha consensus estimate of A$278.3 million

\*\* Declares interim dividend of 170 Australia cents per share, higher than Jefferies estimate of 166 cents

\*\* Says January comparable sales growth for JB Hi-Fi Australia was 7.1% vs consensus estimate of 4.3% growth

\*\* As of last close, stock up 10.5% YTD
