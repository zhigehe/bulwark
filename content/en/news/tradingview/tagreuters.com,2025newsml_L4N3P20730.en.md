THDC India plans to raise 7 billion rupees ($80.04 million), including a greenshoe option of 5 billion rupees, selling bonds maturing in 10 years, three bankers said on Tuesday.

The company has invited coupon and commitment bids from bankers and investors on Friday, day, they said.

The company did not immediately reply to a Reuters email seeking comment.
