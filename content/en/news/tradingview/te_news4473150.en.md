Industrial production in Greece increased by 5.8% year-on-year in December 2024, accelerating from a downwardly revised 3.3% rise in the previous month.

Output grew for manufacturing (3.6%), electricity supply (16.4%), and water supply (5.7%).

Meanwhile, production for mining & quarrying declined (-4.3%).

On a seasonally adjusted monthly basis, industrial output rose by 2.4% in December, following an upwardly revised 1.7% gain in November.

In 2024, industrial production increased by 5.3%.
