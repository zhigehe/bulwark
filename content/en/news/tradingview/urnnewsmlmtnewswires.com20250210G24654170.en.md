National Co. for Learning & Education (SASE:4291), or NCLE, bought a plot of land in Jeddah's North Obhur district in Saudi Arabia for 27.9 million Saudi riyals.

The educational institution company said Sunday it intends to use the 10,631 square-meter land purchased from Abdullah bin Omar bin Qasim Al-Esayi to build and operate an educational complex to expand its operations and increase the number of students served.

The transaction was financed through internal resources and bank facilities.
