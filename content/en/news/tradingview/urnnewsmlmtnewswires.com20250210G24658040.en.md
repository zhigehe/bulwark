Swedish real estate company Logistea (LOGI-A.ST, LOGI-B.ST) said Monday it agreed to purchase warehouse property Automobilen 1 in Nyköping, Sweden, through its acquisition of Nyköping Logistics PropCo.

The property has an underlying value of 450 million kronor, with an estimated lettable area of 44,900 square meters. The entire warehouse is being rented by the Nyköping municipality, with an average remaining lease term of six years and an annual rental income of 43.9 million kronor.

Closing of the transaction is expected on March 10.
