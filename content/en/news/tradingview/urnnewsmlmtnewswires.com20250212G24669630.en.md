Tribe Technology said a majority of shareholders approved its proposed delisting from the AIM segment of the London bourse and its re-registration as a private company at the general meeting held Wednesday.

As such, the mining equipment manufacturer's shares are expected to be canceled from the London bourse on Feb. 21. Tribe Technology will also re-register its business under the new name Tribe Technology Holdings on March 14.
