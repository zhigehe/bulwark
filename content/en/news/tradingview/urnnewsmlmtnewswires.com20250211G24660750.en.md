Philippines ​foreign direct investment (FDI) registered net inflows of $901 million in November 2024, slumping 19.8% from the $1.1 billion net inflows recorded a year earlier, Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas said Monday.

From January 2024 to November 2024, the cumulative FDI level rose 4.4% annually to $8.6 billion from $8.2 billion, the central bank said.
