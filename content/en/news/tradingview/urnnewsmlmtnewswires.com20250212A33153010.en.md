Exelon reported Q4 adjusted operating earnings Wednesday of $0.64 per diluted share, up from $0.60 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.59.

Operating revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $5.47 billion, up from $5.37 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $4.51 billion.

The energy company expects 2025 adjusted operating earnings of $2.64 to $2.74 per share. Analysts polled by FactSet expect $2.63.

Exelon increased its regular quarterly dividend to $0.40 per share from $0.38, payable March 14 to shareholders of record as of Feb. 24.
