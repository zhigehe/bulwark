Apollo Hospitals Enterprise said its consolidated profit attributable to the owners climbed to 3.72 billion Indian rupees in the fiscal third quarter from 2.45 billion rupees a year ago, according to an Indian bourse filing on Monday by the hospital chain.

Revenue from operations increased year on year to 55.3 billion rupees from 48.5 billion rupees. Analysts polled by Visible Alpha expected revenue of 55.8 billion rupees.

The board of Apollo Hospitals Enterprise declared an interim dividend of 9 Indian rupees each for the fiscal year ending March 31, according to the filing.
