Beijing Centergate Technologies Holding's unit, Shandong Huasu Health Care Products, obtained a utility model patent certificate from the China National Intellectual Property Administration, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure on Thursday.

The patent is for the Chinese biomedicine and healthcare company's pH tester for antibacterial mouthwash, the disclosure said.
