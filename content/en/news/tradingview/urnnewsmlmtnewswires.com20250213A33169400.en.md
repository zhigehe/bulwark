Morgan Stanley is looking to boost the size of its latest X Holdings debt offering, Bloomberg reported Thursday, citing people with knowledge of the matter.

Morgan Stanley has been marketing $3 billion in debt that may price with no discount, and demand has been so strong that the bank has told some investors that the size of the offering probably will be increased, the report said.

Morgan Stanley didn't immediately reply to a request for comment from MT Newswires.
