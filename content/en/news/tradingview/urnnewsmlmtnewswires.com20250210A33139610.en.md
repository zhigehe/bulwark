Unitil reported full-year 2024 adjusted earnings late Monday of $2.97 per share, up from $2.82 a year earlier.

Two analysts polled by FactSet expected $2.91.

Operating revenue for the year ended Dec. 31 was $494.8 million, down from $557.1 million a year earlier.

Two analysts polled by FactSet expected $551.9 million.
