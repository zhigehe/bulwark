Neste on Thursday said it expects to lay off 600 employees globally as it moves to streamline its operating model to boost profitability and reduce external costs.

Consequently, the renewable energy group commenced change negotiations for the planned job cuts, of which 450 are in Finland. The positions set to be affected are within Neste's oil products and renewable products business segments.

The company aims to deliver annual cost savings of 65 million euros from the performance improvement program.
