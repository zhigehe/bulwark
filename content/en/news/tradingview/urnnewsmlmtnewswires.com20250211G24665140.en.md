South Korea is ramping up efforts to commercialize quantum technology through collaboration between businesses, research institutes, and academics, Yonhap News reported Tuesday, citing the Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy.

The industry ministry pledged continued discussions and infrastructure support to advance the sector, it said.
