BOX's Chief Financial Officer, Dylan C Smith, sold 13,000 shares of Class A Common Stock on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $34.317 per share, totaling $446,121. Following the transaction, Smith directly owns 1,453,684 shares of BOX. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on April 2, 2024.
