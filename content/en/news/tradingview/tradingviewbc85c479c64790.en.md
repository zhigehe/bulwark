David I. Goldberg, SVP and Chief Financial Officer of Beazer Homes USA, has acquired 443 shares of the company's common stock on February 7, 2025, at a price of $22.8 per share, amounting to a total of $10,100. Following this transaction, Goldberg's direct ownership in the company amounts to 137,094 shares, with an additional 1,185 shares held indirectly through a joint account. This purchase is aligned with the company's 2018 Employee Stock Option Grant Program, which provides matching options that vest over a two-year period, conditional upon continued employment and retention of the shares.

Michael Anthony Dunn, serving as SVP, GC, and Corporate Secretary for Beazer Homes USA, also participated in the stock purchase, acquiring a total of 449 shares on February 7, 2025, at weighted average prices of $22.3272 and $22.28, leading to a total expenditure of $10,003. Post-transaction, Dunn directly owns 11,624 shares in the company. Similar to Goldberg's acquisition, Dunn's purchase falls under the 2018 Employee Stock Option Grant Program, with matching options that vest on the second anniversary, subject to Dunn's ongoing employment and share ownership.
