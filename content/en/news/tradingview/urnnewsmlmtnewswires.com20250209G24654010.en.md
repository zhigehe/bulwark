Lana Co. (SASE:9587) secured an award worth 4 million Saudi riyals from the Saudi Ministry of Health, according to a Sunday release.

The medical device company will provide plastic consumables and supplies for hazardous medical waste to Prince Mohammed bin Abdulaziz Hospital in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

The deal is anticipated to be signed on April 1.
