Japan Eyewear Holdings received approval from the Tokyo Stock Exchange to transfer its listing to the bourse's Prime Market from the Standard Market, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The transfer is set to take effect between Feb. 27 and March 4, depending on the delivery date of a secondary share offering.
