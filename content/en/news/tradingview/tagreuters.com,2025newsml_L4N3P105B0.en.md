\*\* Shares of Australia's Alterity Therapeutics fall as much as 8.3% to A$0.011, on track for a third consecutive session of decline

\*\* Stock hits its lowest level since Jan. 31

\*\* Biotechnology co raises A$40.0 mln ($25.02 mln) in a placement at an issue price of A$0.011 per share

\*\* Issue price represents a discount of 8.3% to stock's last close

\*\* Co says funds will be used primarily to advance development of ATH434, a small molecule drug candidate for Parkinsonian disorders

\*\* Stock up 43.8% this year, as of 0159 GMT

($1 = 1.5990 Australian dollars)
