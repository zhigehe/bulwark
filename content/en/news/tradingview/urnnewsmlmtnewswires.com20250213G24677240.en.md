Wynn Macau adjusted property pre-tax earnings decreased to $108.2 million in the fourth quarter of 2024 from $125.8 million in the year-ago period, according to a Friday filing with the Hong Kong Exchange.

Operating revenue fell to $363.7 million from $386.2 million in the year-ago period, the filing said.
