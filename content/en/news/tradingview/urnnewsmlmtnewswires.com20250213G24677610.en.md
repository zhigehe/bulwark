Technip Energies said Thursday it opened a new office and a research and innovation center in India as part of its strategic expansion in the country.

The office is located in the Gujarat International Finance Tec-City and marks the French engineering and technology company's fourth operating center in India.

Meanwhile, the research center located in the Indian Institute of Technology Madras Research Park in the city of Chennai will focus on critical areas in line with the group's key markets, namely low carbon energy derivatives, industrial decarbonization and circularity.
