\*\* Shares of banking and payments processing conglomerate Fidelity National Information Services slip 8.5% to $75.60 in premarket

\*\* FIS expects Q1 adjusted EPS between $1.17 and $1.22, compared with analysts' expectations of $1.28, according to estimates compiled by LSEG

\*\* That overshadowed FIS's strong annual forecast and Q4 results

\*\* Co. expects annual adjusted EPS between $5.70 and $5.80, largely above Street expectations of $5.72

\*\* Meanwhile, Q4 adjusted EPS of $1.40 beat expectations of $1.36

\*\* FIS stock jumped 34.5% in 2024
