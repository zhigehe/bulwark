Qualisys Holding plans to list on the Nasdaq First North Premier Growth Market in February, according to a Tuesday release.

The Swedish motion capture technology company will offer 340 million kronor worth of existing shares including those held by principal shareholder Vätterledens Invest. The offer also has an overallotment option of up to 15%. The shares will be sold at 76 kronor per share, valuing the Swedish company at 760 million kronor.

Vätterledens Invest intends to retain a 30% stake in the company and 151 million kronor of the offering is secured commitments from Ramhill, Andra AP-fonden, Edastra, Handelsbanken Fonder, Prior & Nilsson Fond- och Kapitalförvaltning, Investment AB Spiltan and Swedbank Robur Fonder.

Qualisys engaged Avanza Bank as the sole bookrunner of the offer.
