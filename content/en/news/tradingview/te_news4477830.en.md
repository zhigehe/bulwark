The Swiss Franc firmed to around 0.90 per USD, its highest in a week, amid a weakening dollar following news that the US aims to begin talks with Russia to end the war in Ukraine.

Meanwhile, Switzerland's consumer price inflation eased for the second straight month in January to the lowest level in nearly four years at 0.6%.
