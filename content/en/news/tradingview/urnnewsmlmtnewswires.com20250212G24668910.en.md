SAL Saudi Logistics Services (SASE:4263) entered a cooperation deal with the Madinah Region Development Authority to bolster Prince Mohammed bin Abdulaziz International Airport's logistics services.

Under the agreement, the two entities will evaluate and deploy smart facility systems at the airport, which could be applied to the logistics company's facilities, according to a Wednesday release.

Additionally, the deal covers the development and implementation of environmental sustainability goals, as well as the establishment of a task force for workshops and events.
