Indian shares are lower as concerns persist about trade friction between U.S. and its trading partners. Industrial stocks are leading the declines. Tata Steel is down 3.0% and Reliance Industries is 1.1% lower. Among individual movers, Oil India is down 4.2% after third-quarter net profit dropped 43% on year. Earnings are in focus, with FSN E-Commerce Ventures and Varun Beverages set to announce their results later in the day. The benchmark Sensex is down 0.4% at 77521.55.
