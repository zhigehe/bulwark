Advance Residence Investment secured 1 billion yen in loans to repay maturing debt, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

MUFG Bank will provide 500 million yen on Feb. 14 at a floating rate of the JBA 1-month yen TIBOR plus 0.160%, due in February 2032.

The Bank of Fukuoka will extend another 500 million yen on Feb. 28 at TIBOR plus 0.180%, maturing in January 2034.

Both loans are unsecured and repayable in full at maturity.
