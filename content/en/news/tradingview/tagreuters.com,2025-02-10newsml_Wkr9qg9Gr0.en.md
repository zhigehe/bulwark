Advenica

Advenica has won an order from a Swedish authority for continued development. The order is worth 8 MSEK and delivery will take place continuously until 2028.

Advenica delivers cybersecurity solutions within encryption and network segmentation with the highest level of approval from the EU and Sweden. Customers are found in sectors such as defence, government, infrastructure and industry.

To meet the cyber threats of the future, Advenica continuously develops new products. The company has now won an order from a Swedish authority regarding continued research and development within encryption and IT security. Delivery of the order will take place from now until 2028.

Together with our customers, we continue to develop solutions at the highest level of security - solutions that protect the most valuable information.                                                              Marie Bengtsson, CEO Advenica

Marie Bengtsson, CEO Advenica AB, +46 (0) 703 860 032 marie.bengtsson@advenica.com

The information was submitted for publication, through the agency of the contact person set out above, at 15.15 CET on February 10th, 2025.

**About Advenica**

Advenica provides cybersecurity solutions within encryption and network segmentation with the highest level of EU-and national approvals. We were founded in 1993 and are based in Malmö, Sweden, where most of our products are designed, developed, and manufactured. Advenica specialises in the sectors of defence, authorities, infrastructure, and industry. With decades of experience working with Sweden's national security, Advenica is known for delivering cybersecurity with exceptional service. Read more at *advenica.com*

Advenica AB is a publicly traded company listed on Nasdaq First North Premier Growth with the name ADVE. Certified Adviser is Redeye AB,  www.redeye.se
