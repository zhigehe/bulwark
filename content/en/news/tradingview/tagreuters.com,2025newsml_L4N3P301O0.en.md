\*\* Shares of Vitura Health Ltd rise as much as 7.6% to A$0.085, their highest since January 22

\*\* Healthcare software co says it will acquire cannabis clinic business Candor Medical for A$5.9 million ($3.71 million)

\*\* Co says it will fund the acquisition via share placement at A$0.06912 apiece

\*\* Co anticipates increase in earnings per share from acquisition

\*\* About 579,000 shares change hands, vs the 30-day average of around 257,000 shares

\*\* Stock down 1.2% YTD, including session's moves

($1 = 1.5891 Australian dollars)
