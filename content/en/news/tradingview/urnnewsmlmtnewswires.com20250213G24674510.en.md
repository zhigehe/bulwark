The Saudi Exchange set the fluctuation limits for Dar Almarkabah For Renting Cars (SASE:9577) after shareholders approval for its forward shares split, according to a Thursday release.

The limits will be based on the vehicle rental company's share price of 4 Saudi riyals, with outstanding orders canceled. Saudi Arabia's Edaa will add the shares to the portfolios of investors by Feb. 17.
