London-listed Creo Medical Group completed a 51% stake sale in its subsidiary Creo Medical into escrow with 30 million euros of net cash receipts pending.

Under the deal Micro-Tech (NL) International, a unit of China's Micro-Tech (Nanjing) has acquired the majority stake in the company at an equivalent equity value of 72 million euros, the medical device company said Wednesday. The proceeds will be used to strengthen the company's balance sheet.

The deal secured regulatory approvals in China, Spain, France, Belgium, and Germany.
