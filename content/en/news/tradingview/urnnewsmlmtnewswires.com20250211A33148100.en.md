Health care stocks were mixed late Tuesday afternoon with the NYSE Health Care Index fractionally higher and the Health Care Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLV) shedding 0.1%.

The iShares Biotechnology ETF (IBB) fell 1.4%.

In corporate news, Travere Therapeutics shares rose past 13% after the company said it completed a meeting with the US Food and Drug Administration and plans to submit a supplemental new drug application for approval of Filspari as a potential treatment for focal segmental glomerulosclerosis, a rare kidney disorder.
