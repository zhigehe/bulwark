Firetail Resources said that an airborne electromagnetic survey at its Skyline copper project in Canada identified new target areas, according to a Monday Australian bourse filing.

Petrophysics testing based on a known high-grade diamond drill core aligns with the electromagnetic survey results.

The firm plans to conduct further exploration using airborne electromagnetic surveys in the second quarter, the filing said.
