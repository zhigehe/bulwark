Pinming Technology said its board of directors has appointed Mo Xujun as Honorary Chairman, according to a Friday filing with the Shanghai Bourse.

Mo was the company's founder, and while the honorary position does not confer board or executive responsibilities, he will continue advising on strategic development, industry trends, and major projects.
