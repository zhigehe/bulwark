Compass Chairman and CEO, Robert L. Reffkin, sold 224,510 shares of Class A Common Stock on February 11, 2025, at a weighted average price of $7.9028 per share, totaling $1,774,257. Following the transaction, Reffkin directly owns 1,139,136 shares and indirectly owns 7,828,116 shares through various trusts and corporations. The sale was executed under a pre-arranged Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on May 10, 2024.
