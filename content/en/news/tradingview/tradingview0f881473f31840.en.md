Haris Clinton M., SVP & President of the APS Division at Entegris, sold 12,652 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $106.92 per share, totaling $1,352,751. Following the transaction, Clinton directly owns 34,819 shares of Entegris. The sale price range was $106.88 to $107.05.
