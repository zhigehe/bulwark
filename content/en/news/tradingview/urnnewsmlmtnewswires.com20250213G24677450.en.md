SKY Network Television expects to complete its satellite migration in April, one month ahead of its recent target, according to a Friday filing with the Australian and New Zealand bourses.

The company started testing to ensure signal delivery on the satellite on Wednesday and will continue to do so in the coming weeks, per the filing.

The telecommunications services provider's shares were up by nearly 2% in recent Friday trade in Australia.
