NASDAQ NordicFlexible Derivatives Products

Anticipated adjustment due to split in Atrium Ljungberg (02/25)

The following information is based on a press release from Atrium Ljungberg AB(Atrium Ljungberg) published on February 13, 2025, and may be subject tochange.

The Board of Atrium Ljungberg has proposed that the Annual General Meeting(AGM) scheduled for March 20, 2025, approves a stock split whereby every one(1) share held will be replaced by five (5) new shares (5:1) (irrespective ofclass). The Ex-date is yet to be determined. Provided that the AGM approves theproposal, NASDAQ Derivatives Markets will carry out a re-calculation ofoptions, regular and gross return forwards/futures in Atrium Ljungberg(ATRLJB).
