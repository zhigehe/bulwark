Metaplanet posted a profit attributable to owners of parent of 6.40 billion yen for the nine months ended Dec. 31, reversing a loss of 683 million yen a year earlier, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Basic earnings per share came in at 326.60 yen, compared with a loss per share of 62.93 yen in the prior year.

Revenue surged over fourfold to 1.06 billion yen from 261 million yen, the filing showed.

For the full year ending Dec. 31, the company expects revenue to rise 220% to 3.40 billion yen, with operating profit projected at 2.50 billion yen.
