Switzerland's monthly producer and import price index edged up 0.1% in January, following zero growth in the prior month, data from the country's Federal Statistical Office showed Friday.

Analysts expected a 0.1% increase for the month.

On a yearly basis, Swiss producer and import prices were 0.3% lower, compared with a 0.9% decline in December 2024.
