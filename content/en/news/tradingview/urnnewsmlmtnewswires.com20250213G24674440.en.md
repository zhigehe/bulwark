Tele2 (TEL2-B.ST, TEL2-A.ST) and Delivery Hero's foodora will begin food and grocery deliveries by drone in Värmdö and Gustavsberg in Sweden.

The first delivery is scheduled for Friday, with the primary delivery area covering up to 10 kilometers in diameter, with potential for expansion up to 15 kilometers, according to a Thursday release. A phased expansion of delivery areas is planned.

The Swedish telecom group supplied 5G connectivity for the drones developed by Norwegian drone technology company Aviant.
