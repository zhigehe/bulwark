Middlefield Canadian Income and its Middlefield Canadian Income - GBP PC fund received a request to convene a general meeting from a nominee account representing one of its shareholders, Saba Capital Management.

The shareholder is proposing a new scheme or process that could involve rolling into an existing or newly established UK-listed open-ended investment company.

Middlefield's board asked shareholders to refrain from taking any action at the moment and said it would provide further updates on the meeting request.
