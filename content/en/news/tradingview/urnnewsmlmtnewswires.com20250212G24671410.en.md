Hindusthan Urban Infrastructure has appointed Shailendra Jhalani, senior vice president Commercial, as chief financial officer of the company, with effect from Wednesday, according to a same-day disclosure to the BSE.

Jhalani takes over from Vishal Pachisia, who resigned from the position of CFO, effective Wednesday.
