William S. Demchak, CEO and Director of PNC Financial Services Group, sold 1,242 shares of the company's $5 Par Common Stock on February 7, 2025, at a price of $202.61 per share, totaling $251,641. Following the transaction, Demchak directly owns 521,345 shares and indirectly owns 2,702 shares through a 401(k) Plan. The sale was executed under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on March 15, 2024.
