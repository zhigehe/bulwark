STOCKS:

The benchmark BSE Sensex was down 1.32% to 76293.60, while the broader NSE Nifty 50 index declined 1.32% to 23071.80, as fears over the U.S. imposing reciprocal trade tariffs forced investors to offload riskier assets.

RUPEE:

The Indian rupee rose 0.7% versus the U.S. dollar to 86.8275, helped by strong intervention by the Reserve Bank of India.

GOVERNMENT BONDS:

The benchmark 10-year bond (IN067934G=CC) was quoted at 100.665 rupees, with the yield down nearly 2 bps to 6.6937%, as markets cheered a debt purchase surprise.

OVERNIGHT INDEX SWAPS:

The one-year overnight index swap rate (INR1YMIBROIS=CC) was unchanged at 6.355%, while the five-year swap rate (INR5YMIBROIS=CC) was little changed at 6.14%.

CALL MONEY:

India's overnight call money rate (INROND=) was at 5.95% and the overnight TREPS rate (INTREPTOT=TCCL) was at 5.80%.
