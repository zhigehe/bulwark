Auto parts manufacturer Hefei Conver Holding (SHA:603409) on Thursday filed for an initial public offering of 31,507,704 shares on the Shanghai Stock Exchange.

Proceeds will go towards building new factories, purchasing advanced production equipment, research and development investment and technological innovation efforts, according to a Thursday filing on the bourse.

Bank of China International Securities serves as lead underwriter.

Expected date of issuance is Feb. 21.
