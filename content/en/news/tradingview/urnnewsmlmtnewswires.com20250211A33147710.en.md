Economic Investment Trust on Tuesday declared a cash dividend of $4.57 per share payable March 31, to shareholders of record on March 14.

The $4.57 cash dividend includes a $0.30 quarterly dividend, and an additional $4.27 per share, representing a distribution of the balance of net investment income for 2024.

The previous declared cash dividend payable Dec. 31, 2024 to shareholders of record on Dec. 16, 2024 was $0.30 per common share.
