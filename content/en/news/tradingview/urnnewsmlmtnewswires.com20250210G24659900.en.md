Bank of Suzhou recorded a net profit attributable to shareholders of 5.07 billion yuan in 2024, up 10% from 4.60 billion yuan in the preceding year, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange filing on Monday.

Earnings per share rose 7.4% year on year to 1.31 yuan from 1.22 yuan.

The Chinese lender generated an operating income worth 12.2 billion yuan, 3.1% higher than 11.9 billion yuan a year earlier, the filing said.

The company's shares rose less than 2% in recent trade.
