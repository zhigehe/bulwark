Cohen & Steers reported preliminary assets under management Monday of $86.41 billion as of Jan. 31, up from $85.81 billion a month earlier.

The investment firm attributed the gain to $960 million in market appreciation, which was partially offset by $149 million in distributions and $213 million in net outflows.
