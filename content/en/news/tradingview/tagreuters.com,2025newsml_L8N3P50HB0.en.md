\*\* Shares in Tietoevry fall around 9% after the Finnish IT services firm reported adjusted EBITA of EUR 89.3 million in Q4, missing Vara consensus by around 6%

\*\* OP analyst Kimmo Stenvall notes that revenues came down in every business area, which the company explains by weak demand

\*\* Stenvall also says the outlook for -3% to +1% organic growth and 12-13% operating margin in 2025 is "soft", given the continued market challenges

\*\* Tietoevry shares are at the bottom of STOXX 600 index; if losses hold they will see their worst day since April 2010
