Polish footwear and fashion retailer CCC reported on Monday a 122% year-on-year jump in its preliminary fourth quarter core profit.

Preliminary earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortisation (EBITDA) came in at 525 million zlotys ($129.57 million) compared with 442 million zlotys expected by analysts polled by Reuters.

($1 = 4.0520 zlotys)
