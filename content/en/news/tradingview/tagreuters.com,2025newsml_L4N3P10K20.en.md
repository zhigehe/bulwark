\*\* India's Kewal Kiran Clothing slips as much as 5.7% to 530 rupees

\*\* Company posts 26% y/y drop in Q3 profit at 246 million rupees ($2.81 million) on jump in raw material prices

\*\* Lifestyle apparel company, which operates Killer and other brands, sees EBITDA margin contract to 18.4% from 19.4% a year ago

\*\* KKCL down 9.2% so far in 2025; fell 23.8% in 2024

\*\* WhiteOak Capital AMC, Union AMC and Bank of India Investment Managers pare stake in company in last six months, while ICICI Prudential AMC and Bandhan AMC increase holdings - LSEG data

($1 = 87.5810 Indian rupees)
