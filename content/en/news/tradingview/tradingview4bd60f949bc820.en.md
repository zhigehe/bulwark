James W. Ayers, a 10% owner of FB Financial Corp, has purchased a total of 4,000 shares of common stock on February 7 and February 10, 2025. The purchases were made at weighted average prices of $52.9 and $52.58 respectively, amounting to a total purchase amount of $210,960. Following these transactions, Ayers directly owns 10,925,841 shares of FB Financial Corp's common stock.
