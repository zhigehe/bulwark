Vornado Realty Trust reported Q4 non-GAAP funds from operations late Monday of $0.61 per diluted share, down from $0.63 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.51.

Revenue for the three months ended Dec. 31 rose to $457.8 million from $441.9 million a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $448.9 million.
