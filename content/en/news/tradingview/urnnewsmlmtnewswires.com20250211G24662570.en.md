Hefei Fengle Seed has appointed Dai Dengan as its chairman, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure on Tuesday.

Dai will step down as general manager to take over the chairman post from Feng Yue, who resigned due to work adjustments, the Chinese agricultural chemical manufacturer said.

The company's shares closed 1% lower.
