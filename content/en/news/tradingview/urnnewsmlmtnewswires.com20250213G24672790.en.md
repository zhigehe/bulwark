Orange said Thursday net income and revenue for 2024 slipped year over year.

Net income attributable to owners of the parent company for the 12 months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was 2.35 billion euros, compared with 2.44 billion euros earlier. EPS moved to 0.82 euro from 0.85 euro.

The telecom operator's revenue was 40.26 billion euros, compared with 39.68 billion euros a year ago.

Analysts polled by FactSet were expecting 40.30 billion euros in sales, 2.65 billion euros in net income, and 1.01 euros in EPS.

Meanwhile, the board plans to propose a dividend of 0.75 euro per share for the period. It paid a total dividend of 0.72 euro per share a year ago.
