Shaanxi Construction Engineering Group won three major construction projects with contract values exceeding 500 million yuan in January.

The largest of these is the Shenlufang urban village renovation residential resettlement project for 1.93 billion yuan, according to a Thursday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

The other two were the phase three supporting tank area project for 750 million yuan. and the Handan city central heating project for 510 million yuan.

Shares of the construction and engineering company rose nearly 1% in recent trade.
