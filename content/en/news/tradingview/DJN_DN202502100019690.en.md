* Europe's biggest vet group IVC Evidensia is considering a listing on London's main market in what would be one of the city's largest amid a listings drought, the Financial Times reports, citing people familiar with the matter.
* The Bristol-based company, which was last valued at 12.3 billion euros ($12.71 billion), is exploring several listing options for next year, the FT reports, citing sources.
* IVC's backers include its biggest shareholder, private equity group EQT, as well as consumer giant Nestle and Silver Lake, a private equity firm focused on technology, according to the FT.
