South Korean video game company NCSoft posted a net loss attributable to shareholders of 7.5 billion won in the fourth quarter of 2024, swinging from a net income attributable to shareholders of 25 billion won a year earlier.

Sales fell 6.5% year over year to 409.4 billion won from 437.7 billion won, according to a Wednesday DART filing.

Shares of NCSoft fell more than 1% in recent trade on Thursday.
