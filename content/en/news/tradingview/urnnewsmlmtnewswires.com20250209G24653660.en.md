Panjit International's board approved signing an MOU with Torex Semiconductor to acquire some or all shares of its unit, Torex Vietnam Semiconductor.

Panjit aims to secure production lines at orex Vietnam Semiconductor, and acquire over 50% of its shares, according to a Friday Taiwan Exchange filing.

Investment details will be finalized after due diligence, the electronic component manufacturer said.
