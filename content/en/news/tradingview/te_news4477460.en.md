Industrial production in Hungary shrank by 5.3% year-on-year in December 2024, confirming initial estimates and compared to a 4.2% fall in the previous month.

This marked the eighth consecutive month of declining industrial activity and the sharpest contraction since September, as production continued to decrease for manufacturing (-7.1% vs -5.1% in November), particularly manufacture of vehicle (-21%), electrical equipment (-25%), and rubber, plastic, and non-metallic mineral products (-2.1%).

Meanwhile, output accelerated sharply for mining and quarrying (81% vs 52%) and electricity, gas, steam supply, air conditioning (24% vs 5.1%).

On a seasonally adjusted monthly basis, industrial output fell 1.8% in December 2024, slipping further from a 1.6% drop in the previous month.

For the entire year of 2024, industrial production was 4% lower than in the previous year.
