Eevia Health (EEVIA.ST) said Tuesday it received 54,000 euros of new orders from European and Asian customers.

The Finland-based plant extracts supplier received orders for Feno-Myrtillus 36 and Fenoprolic Full Spectrum from two German buyers, an order for tart cherry extract from Australia, and a 15,000 euro order from Taiwan.
