Singapore-based Patience Capital Group partnered with Gaw Capital Partners to establish a joint venture to acquire and manage the Tokyu Plaza Ginza shopping mall in Japan, according to a media release on Friday.

Gaw will own a 91% stake in the joint venture while Patience Capital will hold a 9% stake.

The Japanese prime retail asset has an area of 50,093 square meters and is one of the largest and rarest retail facilities in Ginza, the release said.
