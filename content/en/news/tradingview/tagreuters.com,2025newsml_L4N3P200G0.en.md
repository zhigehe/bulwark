\*\* Australian gold sub-index rises as much as 2.6% to a record high of 10,261.60 points

\*\* Gold prices broke through the key $2,900 level for the first time on Monday, driven by safe-haven demand

\*\* Northern Star Resources jumps as much as 3.5% to its all-time high and ASX-listed shares of Newmont rise 3.3%

\*\* Evolution Mining climbs 3.7% to reach its highest level since November 2020

\*\* De Grey Mining climbs up to 4.4% to hit its highest since early-April, 2008

\*\* As of last close, AXGD up 18.8% YTD vs a ~4% gain in benchmark index
