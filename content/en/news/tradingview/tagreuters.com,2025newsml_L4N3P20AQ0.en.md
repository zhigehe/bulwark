Citicorp Finance (India) plans to raise 9 billion rupees ($103.59 million), including a greenshoe option of 7 billion rupees, through the sale of bonds maturing in 2 years and 1 month, three bankers said on Tuesday.

It has invited coupon and commitment bids for the issue on Feb.12, they said.

The company did not immediately reply to a Reuters email seeking comment.

| THDC India | 10 years | To be decided | 2+5 | Feb. 14 | AA(Care) |
