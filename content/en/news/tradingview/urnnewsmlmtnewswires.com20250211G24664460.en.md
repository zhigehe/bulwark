Japan Real Estate Investment secured a place on CDP's Climate Change "A List," receiving the highest rating in the non-profit's 2024 environmental assessment, according to a Friday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

CDP evaluates companies on governance, risk management, business strategy, and climate-related performance, scoring them from Disclosure (D/D-) to Leadership (A/A-).
