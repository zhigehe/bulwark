Macquarie Group Macquarie Asset Management reported assets under management of AU$942.7 billion in the fiscal third quarter, up 3% from the September 2024 quarter, according to a Tuesday filing with the Australian bourse.

The company's banking and financial services businesses logged deposits of AU$163.8 billion as of Dec. 31, 2024, up 7% from the previous quarter.

The net profit after tax for the nine months ended Dec. 31, 2024, was in line with a year earlier, per the filing.
