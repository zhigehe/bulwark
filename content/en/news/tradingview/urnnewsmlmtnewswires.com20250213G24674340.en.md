Peab (PEAB-B.ST) secured a turnkey contract worth 275 million kronor from Gothenburg's City Property Administration to redevelop the Lunden School in Sweden.

The construction company will construct a new building as well as modify and expand existing ones, along with maintenance, according to a Thursday release. The school will continue operations during the construction period.

Project planning and development will commence immediately, with anticipated completion in the spring of 2027.
