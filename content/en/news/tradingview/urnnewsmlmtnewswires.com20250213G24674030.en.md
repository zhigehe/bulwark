Nissan Motor , Honda Motor , and Mitsubishi Motors have scrapped their memorandum of understanding (MOU) on a three-way collaboration, the companies said in a joint statement on Thursday.

This follows the termination of a separate MOU on business integration between Nissan and Honda signed on Dec. 23 last year. The automakers said they will instead advance cooperation through a strategic partnership focused on intelligent and electric vehicles.
