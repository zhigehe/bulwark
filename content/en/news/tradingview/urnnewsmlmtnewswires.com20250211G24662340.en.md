Swedish Logistic Property (SLP-B.ST), or SLP, set new sustainability targets due to meeting its previous targets in 2024, according to a Tuesday release.

The logistics real estate property company's new goals include being a net-zero company in terms of its scope 3 emissions by 2040, as well as an up to 5% decrease to its energy class F and G assets' lettable areas by 2027-end.
