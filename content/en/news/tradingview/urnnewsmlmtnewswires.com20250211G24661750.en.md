Kumpulan Kitacon unit Kitacon secured a deal worth 104.3 million ringgit from Sime Darby Property (BBR3 Asset 1), according to a Monday Malaysian bourse filing.

The deal pertains to the proposed construction and completion of factories units in Selangor, Malaysia.

The works will begin on Feb. 24, and will be completed within 18 months, it noted.
