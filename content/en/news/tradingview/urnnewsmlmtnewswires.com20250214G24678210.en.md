Graubündner Kantonalbank , d/b/a GKB, said Friday profit for 2024 slipped while operating income rose year over year.

Group profit for the 12 months ended Dec. 31, 2024, was 229.5 million francs, compared with 230.6 million francs earlier. EPS moved to 96.4 francs from 97.6 francs.

The Swiss cantonal bank's operating income was 528.4 million francs, compared with 525.9 million francs a year ago.

Meanwhile, the board declared an unchanged dividend of 47.50 francs per participation certificate for the period.

For 2025, GKB expects consolidated profit in the range of 210 million francs and 230 million francs.
