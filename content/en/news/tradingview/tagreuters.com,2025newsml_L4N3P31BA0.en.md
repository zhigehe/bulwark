\*\* Shares of restaurant chain operator Denny's fall as much as 22.3% to a near five-year low of $5.2 in early trade

\*\* Company posts Q4 revenue of $114.7 million vs. estimates of $115.7 million, hurt by decline in the number of equivalent units and franchise occupancy revenue

\*\* Company also reports Q4 adjusted EPS of 14 cents vs. estimates of 15 cents - data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Forecasts Denny's segment domestic system-wide same-restaurant sales to be between a 2% decline and 1% growth, compared to a 0.2% fall in 2024

\*\* DENN fell 44.4% in 2024
