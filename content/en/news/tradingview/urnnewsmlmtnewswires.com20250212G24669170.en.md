Nibe , has signed licensing agreements with Research & Development Establishment (Engrs), Pune, and Pune Research & Development Organisation (DRDO), Ministry of Defense.

The agreements grant Nibe the technology rights to manufacture and sell the 24m Mountain Foot Bridge for all-terrain vehicle (ATV), including its launching systems, according to a Tuesday filing with the Indian bourses.
