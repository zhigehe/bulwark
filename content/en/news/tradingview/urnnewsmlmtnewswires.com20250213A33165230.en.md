American Electric Power reported Q4 non-GAAP earnings Thursday of $1.24 per share, up from $1.23 a year earlier.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $1.25.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $4.70 billion, compared with $4.58 billion a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $4.91 billion.

The company reaffirmed its 2025 non-GAAP earnings per share guidance range of $5.75 to $5.95. Analysts surveyed by FactSet expect $5.88.
