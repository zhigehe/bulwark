Japan's benchmark Nikkei Stock Average closed higher, led by export-dependent companies that could benefit from a falling yen. Electronic and automobile stocks led the session's gain. The index rose 1.3% to close at 39461.47. In the electronics segment, Sony added 1.1% while Canon and Nikon added 3.1% and 0.3%, respectively. Toyota Motor and Honda Motor were 0.9% and 2.1% higher, respectively. USD/JPY was 0.2% lower at 154.08. (venkat.pr@wsj.com)
