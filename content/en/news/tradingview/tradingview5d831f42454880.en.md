Guidewire Software's Chief Executive Officer, Michael George Rosenbaum, sold 1,400 shares of Common Stock on February 10, 2025, at a price of $214.68 per share, totaling $300,552. Following the transaction, Rosenbaum directly owns 274,730 shares of the company. The sale was executed under an automatic trading plan adopted on October 15, 2024.
