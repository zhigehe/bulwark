Afry secured a framework agreement with renewable energy company Statkraft to provide technical consultancy services for hydropower and electric power in Norway.

The deal runs for three years with the option for two one-year extensions, the Swedish engineering services group said Thursday.

Afry has already delivered technical advisory services to the Norwegian state-owned company in the Swedish market.
