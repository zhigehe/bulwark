Dongfeng Automobile expects its indirect controlling shareholder to change as its current indirect controlling shareholder, Dongfeng Motor Group , plans to reorganize with other state-owned central enterprises, according to a Shanghai Stock Exchange disclosure on Monday.

The potential revamp, which is subject to regulatory approvals, will not have a significant impact on the production and operations of the Chinese automobile maker, the disclosure said.

Dongfeng's shares soared 10% in recent trade.
