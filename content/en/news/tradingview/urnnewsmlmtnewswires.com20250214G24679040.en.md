Hermès International said Friday its net income and revenue for the full year 2024 increased year over year.

Net income attributable to owners of the parent for the 12 months ended Dec. 31, 2024, was 4.60 billion euros, compared with 4.31 billion euros earlier. EPS moved to 43.87 euros from 41.12 euros.

The luxury fashion house's revenue was 15.17 billion euros, compared with 13.43 billion euros a year ago.

Analysts polled by FactSet were expecting 14.93 billion euros in sales, 4.49 billion euros in net income, and 42.86 euros in EPS.

Meanwhile, the board intends to propose an ordinary dividend of 16 euros per share and an exceptional dividend of 10 euros per share for the period. It paid an ordinary dividend of 15 euros per share and an exceptional dividend of 10 euros per share a year ago.

Looking ahead, Hermès affirmed its medium-term guidance of revenue growth at constant exchange rates.
