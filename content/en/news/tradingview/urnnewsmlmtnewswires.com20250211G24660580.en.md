Yi Shin Textile Industrial sold 150,000 shares of Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing for NT$145.6 million.

The shares were sold at an average price of NT$970.31 each, from July 22, 2024, to Feb. 10, 2025, according to a Monday Taiwan Exchange filing.

The company generated a gain of NT$2.53 million.

The disposal was part of the company's financial and investment management strategy.
