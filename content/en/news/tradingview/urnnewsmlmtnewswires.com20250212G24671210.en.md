Forrestania Resources kickstarted a follow-up reverse circulation drilling program at the Ada Ann prospect within its Bonnie Vale project in Western Australia, according to a Thursday filing with the Australian bourse.

The company aims to test depth and strike extensions to further confirm and expand the mineralization potential identified in its maiden drilling program.

The mineral explorer's shares surged past 21% in recent Thursday trade.
