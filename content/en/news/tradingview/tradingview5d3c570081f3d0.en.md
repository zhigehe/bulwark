Federated Hermes CEO, Saker Anwar Nusseibeh, sold 5,546 shares of Class B Common Stock on February 7, 2025, for a total of $215,220. The sale was conducted at a weighted average price of $38.8065 per share, with transactions ranging from $38.67 to $39.06. This sale was to satisfy tax obligations from the vesting of restricted stock. Following the transaction, Nusseibeh directly owns 179,096 shares of Federated Hermes.
