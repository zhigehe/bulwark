India's Eicher Motors , which makes the Royal Enfield brand of motorcycles, missed third-quarter profit estimates on Monday, hurt by a mix of high marketing expenses and sales of lower-margin motorcycles outpacing higher-margin models.

The company reported a net profit of 11.71 billion rupees (about $134 million) in the quarter, compared to analysts' expectation of 12.12 billion rupees, as per data compiled by LSEG.

($1 = 87.4380 Indian rupees)
