PureHealth Holding (ADX:PUREHEALTH) reportedly backed out of a potential offer for NMC Healthcare.

The Abu Dhabi-based healthcare services company, which is backed by sovereign fund ADQ, walked away from the deal due to a disagreement over NMC Healthcare's valuation, Bloomberg News reported Monday, citing people familiar with the matter.

NMC Healthcare said it is exploring strategic options but would not comment on specific talks, the news outlet reported, citing a company spokesperson.

PureHealth did not immediately respond to MT Newswires' request for comment.
