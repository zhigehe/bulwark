Energy stocks leaned lower premarket Thursday with the Energy Select Sector SPDR Fund recently declining by 0.2%.

The United States Oil Fund was down 0.8% and the United States Natural Gas Fund was 4.3% higher.

Front-month US West Texas Intermediate crude oil was down 1.2% at $70.52 per barrel at the New York Mercantile Exchange. Global benchmark North Sea Brent crude oil lost 1.2% to $74.27 per barrel, and natural gas futures were 4.3% higher at $3.72 per 1 million British Thermal Units.

Genesis Energy shares were up over 2% even after the company reported a wider Q4 net loss as revenue declined during the period.

DTE Energy shares were down more than 1% after the company reported a decline in Q4 operating earnings.

PBF Energy shares fell by over 4% after the company reported a wider Q4 adjusted loss as revenue fell during the quarter.
