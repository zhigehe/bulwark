Mianyang Fulin Precision is planning to invest 110 million yuan in a new robotic smart joint module research and development and production base in Mianyang in China's Sichuan province.

The project, located in the LianDong U Valley Industrial Park, will include the construction of a production line for smart joint modules, primarily targeting applications in humanoid robots and robotic dogs, according to a Wednesday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of the battery maker were down 1% in recent trade.
