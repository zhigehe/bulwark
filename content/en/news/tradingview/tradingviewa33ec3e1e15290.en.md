Shawna L'Italien, serving as a Director for CONSUMERS BANCORP /OH/, purchased 525 shares of the company's Common Stock on February 7, 2025, at a price of $19.1 per share, totaling $10,027. Following this transaction, L'Italien directly owns 5,421 shares of CONSUMERS BANCORP INC /OH/, which includes shares acquired through the dividend reinvestment plan.
