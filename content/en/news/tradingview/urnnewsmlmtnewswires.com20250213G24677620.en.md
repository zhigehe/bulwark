Arabian Internet and Communications Services (SASE:7202), d/b/a Solutions, signed a revenue-sharing agreement to provide Sports Boulevard Foundation with smart parking services in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

The information and technology services company will design, deploy and operate services such as real-time parking availability tracking and mobile payment options to streamline the parking process at the Sports Boulevard, according to a Thursday release.

The company said the five-year contract could generate over 5% of its 2023 audited annual revenue, though the actual amount will depend on transaction volume.
