Unilever said Thursday that profit for full-year 2024 declined, while turnover climbed year over year.

Profit attributable to shareholders' equity for the 12 months ended Dec. 31, 2024, was 5.74 billion euros, compared with 6.49 billion euros earlier. EPS moved to 2.29 euros from 2.56 euros.

The consumer goods giant's turnover was 60.76 billion euros, compared with 59.60 billion euros a year ago.

Meanwhile, the board declared a 2024 fourth-quarter dividend of 0.4528 euro per share for the period, payable March 28, 2025, to shareholders on record Feb. 28, 2025. It paid 0.4268 euro per share a year ago.

Looking ahead, the company expects its underlying sales to grow between 3% and 5% for 2025.
