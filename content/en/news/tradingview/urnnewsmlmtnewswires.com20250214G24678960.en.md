Digital investment platform operator Derayah Financial said Friday it priced its initial public offering in Saudi Arabia at 30 riyals per share.

The offer price reflects a total volume of 1.5 billion Saudi riyals and implies a market capitalization of 7.5 billion riyals. The institutional bookbuilding garnered total orders of 243 billion riyals from local and international investors.

The subscription period for individual investors runs from Feb. 20 to Feb. 22.
