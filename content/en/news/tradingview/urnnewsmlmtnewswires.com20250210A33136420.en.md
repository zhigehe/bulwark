Atlantic International said Monday that its Lyneer Staffing Solutions unit signed a workforce deal with an unnamed North American distributor serving the aerospace, defense, automotive, and medical sectors.

The agreement is projected to generate up to $10 million, with $5 million in contract business and $5 million in direct hire spending, and allows Lyneer to offer services at over 20 locations across the US, the company said.

Shares of Atlantic International were down more than 2% in recent Monday trading.
