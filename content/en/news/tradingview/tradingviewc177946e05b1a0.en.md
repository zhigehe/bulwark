Akash J. Palkhiwala, CFO & COO of QUALCOMM, sold 3,000 shares of common stock on February 12, 2025, under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan. The transactions occurred at weighted average prices of $169.0251, $170.0528, and $170.6891, totaling $509,396. Following these sales, Palkhiwala directly owns 63,115 shares of QUALCOMM. The sales prices ranged within specific intervals for each transaction, with the company agreeing to provide detailed pricing information upon request.
