Chahua Modern Housewares approved a 45 million yuan capital increase for its wholly-owned subsidiary, Shenzhen Damai Technology Intelligent, according to a Thursday filing with the Shanghai bourse.

The move, approved by the board without requiring shareholder approval, aims to enhance operational capacity and accelerate the company's transition toward product-driven solutions.

Post-investment, Damai Intelligent's registered capital will rise from 5 million yuan to 50 million yuan.
