The Kenyan shilling strengthened a little against the U.S. dollar on Thursday, bolstered by foreign exchange inflows from the hospitality sector, traders said.

At 0838 GMT the shilling was quoted at 128.90/129.40 per dollar, compared to 129.00/129.50 at the close of Wednesday's session.
