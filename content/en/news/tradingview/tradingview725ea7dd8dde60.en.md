Tonit M Calaway, EVP, CAO, General Counsel & Secretary of BorgWarner, sold 13,500 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, at a weighted average price of $30.1555 per share, totaling $407,099. Following the transaction, Calaway directly owns 259,310 shares of BorgWarner. The sale prices ranged from $30.0650 to $30.3200.
