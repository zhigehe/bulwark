The Polish economy expanded by 3.2 percent year-on-year in the fourth quarter of 2024, accelerating from a 2.7 percent growth in the previous three-month period and matching market expectations, preliminary estimates showed.

This marked the sixth consecutive period of annual GDP growth.

On a seasonally adjusted quarterly basis, the economy climbed 1.3% in the fourth quarter of 2024, rebounding from a 0.1% fall in the previous three-month period.
