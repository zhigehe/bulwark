Novonesis (NSIS-B.CO) agreed to buy out Dutch nutrition, health and beauty company DSM-Firmenich from their joint feed enzymes business Feed Enzyme Alliance for 1.5 billion euros in cash.

The acquisition is in line with the Danish biotechnology company's growth strategy and is accretive to both its revenue and adjusted EPS, according to a Tuesday release.
