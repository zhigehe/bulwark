Ador Multiproducts said its board approved the disinvestment of its equity stake in Hemp Horizon on Feb. 7, citing no operations and management disputes, according to a Monday filing with the BSE.

The company received 5,000 Indian rupees against its original investment of 5,00,015 rupees, according to the filing.
