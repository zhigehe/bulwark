ZTE further subscribed for wealth management products for 9.7 million yuan, according to a Hong Kong Stock Exchange disclosure on Monday.

The Chinese telecommunication company bought eight of the products from Bank of Hangzhou from Oct. 8, 2024, to Feb. 10, 2025.

The products are expected to give an annualized yield rate of between 0.8% and 2.7%, the disclosure said.

ZTE's Hong Kong and Shenzhen shares both fell less than 2% in recent trade.
