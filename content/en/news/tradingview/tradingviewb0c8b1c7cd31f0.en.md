Several top executives at Roblox Corp have recently engaged in transactions involving the sale of Class A Common Stock as part of 'sell-to-cover' activities to satisfy tax withholding obligations related to the vesting of performance stock units.

Manuel Bronstein, the Chief Product Officer, sold 26,149 shares at an average price of $65.9401 per share, totaling $1,724,267. Following this transaction, Bronstein directly owns 659,163 shares of the company.

Mark Reinstra, the Chief Legal Officer & Corporate Secretary, disposed of 19,525 shares at an average price of $65.944 per share, with the total sale amounting to $1,287,556. Post-sale, Reinstra's direct ownership stands at 339,206 shares, with an additional 223,154 shares held indirectly through various trusts.

Michael Guthrie, the Chief Financial Officer, sold 26,154 shares at an average price of $65.943, totaling $1,724,673. Guthrie now directly owns 417,079 shares and indirectly owns 61,422 shares through the Guthrie Family Irrevocable GST Exempt Trust.
