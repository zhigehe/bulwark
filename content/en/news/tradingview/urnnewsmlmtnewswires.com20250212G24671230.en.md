China's tax cuts in support of scientific innovation and manufacturing development reached 2.63 trillion yuan in 2024, Xinhua News Agency reported Wednesday, citing the State Taxation Administration.

Sales from China's high-tech sectors increased 9.6 percentage points faster than the country's overall growth rate, the report said.

Sales from manufacturing companies in the country jumped 2.2 percentage points faster than the overall rate, according to the report.
