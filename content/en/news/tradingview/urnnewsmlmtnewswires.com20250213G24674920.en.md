SIG Group officially opened a production facility for aseptic carton packs in India, its first in the Asian country.

The Swiss packaging company invested 90 million euros to complete the plant in Ahmedabad, according to a Thursday release. The facility has an initial production capacity of up to 4 billion aseptic carton packs per year.

The plant will supply SIG's filler base, which serves dairy and non-carbonated soft drink companies in India.
