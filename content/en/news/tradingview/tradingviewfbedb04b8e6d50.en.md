Michael L. Baur, CEO & Chairman of the Board of ScanSource, sold 35,000 shares of common stock over transactions dated February 6, 7, and 10, 2025, for a total sale amount of $1,348,683. The sales were conducted at weighted average prices of $38.91, $38.17, and $39.12, with prices ranging across different transactions. Following these transactions, Baur directly owns 142,760 shares of ScanSource.
