\*\* Beverage shop chain Dutch Bros' shares up 24.56% at $80.6 premarket

\*\* Company forecasts annual total revenues to a range of $1.55 billion and $1.57 billion, the midpoint of which is above estimates of $1.56 billion, as per data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Co's fourth quarter net revenues rise 34.9% to $342.8 million beating analysts' expectations of $317.8 million

\*\* In the quarter, excluding items, the co earned 7 cents per share as compared to analyst expectations of 2 cents

\*\* As we survey the industry landscape, we believe Dutch Bros is uniquely positioned and on-trend with an emphasis on iced beverages, personalization, and speed - CEO Christine Barone

\*\* BROS stock rose 65% in 2024
