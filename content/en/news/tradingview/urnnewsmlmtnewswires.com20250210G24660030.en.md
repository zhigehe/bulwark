GF Securities has issued 40 billion yuan in short-term corporate bonds under an October 2023 approval of a public issuance, with 20 billion yuan repaid and 20 billion yuan outstanding.

The company's current issuance is the 10th tranche, with a maximum size of 4 billion yuan, according to a filing with the Shenzhen bourse.
