Yaoko Co. recorded a profit attributable to owners of the parent of 23.0 billion yen for the nine months ended Dec. 31, 2024, 4% higher than 22.1 billion yen logged for the same period a year ago.

The company's basic earnings per share for the reporting period decreased to 561.35 yen from 568.50 yen per share a year ago, according to a Monday filing with the Tokyo Exchange.

Total revenue of the supermarket company came in at 557.6 billion yen, 18.7% higher than 469.6 billion yen a year earlier.

It forecasted an attributable profit of 18.7 billion yen, a basic EPS of 476.04 yen, a total revenue of 707 billion yen, and a final dividend of 55 yen per share for the fiscal year ending March. 31.
