Prudential bought back 633,451 shares from the London Stock Exchange on Monday for an average price of 6.7282 pounds sterling apiece.

The insurer plans to cancel the repurchased shares, a Tuesday filing with the Hong Kong bourse said.

Following the buyback, the company will have 2,638,917,123 shares in issue.
