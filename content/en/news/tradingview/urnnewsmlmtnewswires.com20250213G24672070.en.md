The UK's gross domestic product rose 0.4% month over month in December 2024, following a 0.1% increase in the previous month, the Office for National Statistics said Thursday.

Analysts expected a 0.1% gain for the month.

On a yearly basis, Britain's GDP was 1.5% higher, against the revised 1.1% growth earlier and the expected 1% increase.
