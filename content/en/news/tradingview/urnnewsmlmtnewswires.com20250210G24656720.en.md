Helios Underwriting said Monday that Executive Chairman Michael Wade will step down at the end of February to focus on his non-executive roles.

Wade will be succeeded by the current nonexecutive director John Chambers who will assume the charge as interim executive chairman and the company's strategic priorities will remain unchanged, the UK-based investment company said.
