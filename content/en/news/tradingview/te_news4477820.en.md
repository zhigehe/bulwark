The annual inflation rate in Palestine slowed for the second consecutive month to 22.77% in January 2025 from 87.99% in December 2024.

This marked the lowest inflation rate since January 2024, mainly influenced by the announcement of a ceasefire in the Gaza Strip.

On a monthly basis, consumer prices decreased 53.27% in January 2025, reversing from a 2.97% increase in December.
