Kim Joo Mi, Chief Financial Officer of QUALYS, sold 4,487 shares of common stock on February 10, 2025, at a price of $140.0 per share, totaling $628,180. Following the transaction, Kim Joo Mi directly owns 112,757 shares of QUALYS. The sale was conducted under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on August 21, 2024.
