ASX Limited reported Thursday that its statutory earnings in the fiscal first half rose to AU$1.256 from AU$1.19 per diluted share a year earlier.

Analysts polled by Visible Alpha were expecting EPS of AU$1.268.

Underlying EPS was AU$1.309, compared with AU$1.19 a year earlier. Analysts polled by Visible Alpha expected AU$1.27.

Revenue for the six months to Dec. 31,Â 2024, was AU$855.9 million, up from AU$771.7 million in the same period a year earlier. Operating revenue rose 5.9% to AU$541.9 million from AU$511.7 million. Analysts surveyed by Visible Alpha expected AU$546 million in operating revenue.

The board declared an interim dividend of AU$1.112 per share, up from the AU$1.01 in the previous reported period, payable on March 21 to shareholders on record as of Feb. 24.
