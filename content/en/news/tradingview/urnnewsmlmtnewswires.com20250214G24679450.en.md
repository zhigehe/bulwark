CSG Holding plans to apply for a three-year loan of up to 436.5 million yuan to repurchase domestic and foreign-listed shares, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange filing on Friday.

The amount of domestic or A shares to be bought back will be between 243 million yuan and 485 million yuan and the amount of H shares to be repurchased will be between HK$50 million and HK$100 million.

The Chinese high-end energy-saving glass product manufacturer will buy back the shares within 12 months after the approval of shareholders.

The shares will be used for equity incentives, the filing said.

The company's B shares closed 8% higher.
