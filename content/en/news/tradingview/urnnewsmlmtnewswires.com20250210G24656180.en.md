Zecon secured approval from the Sarawak State Government for an electricity generation license, according to a Friday Malaysian bourse filing.

The license, effective Feb. 5, allows the company to design, construct, and operate a 300 megawatts solar power plant across 3,000 acres in Kuching, Sarawak, supplying electricity to non-domestic consumers.

The license is valid for 30 years with an annual fee of 300,000 ringgit.
