\*\* Shares of U.S. oil and gas companies fall, tracking declining oil prices

\*\* Brent Crude futures down 1.25% to $76.04/barrel and US West Texas down 1.05% to $72.27/barrel, after data showed U.S. crude stockpiles rose by more than expected last week and hawkish remarks from the Federal Reserve chair

\*\* Energy major Exxon Mobil down 1.8%

\*\* Oilfield services firms Halliburton and SLB down 0.7% each; Baker Hughes down 1.16%

\*\* Refiners Phillips 66 down 1.2% and Valero Energy down 1.5%

\*\* Reuters reports Chevron to lay off 15% to 20% of its global workforce, shares down 0.8% at $156.10
