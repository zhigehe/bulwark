By Dominic Chopping

STOCKHOLM--Telia Company appointed Andre Visse as head of Telia Estonia, effective March 1.

The Swedish telecommunications operator said Thursday that Visse is currently acting head of the unit and Telia Estonia's chief technology officer. He took on the acting head role on Feb. 1, after Holger Haljand moved to become head of Telia Finland.
