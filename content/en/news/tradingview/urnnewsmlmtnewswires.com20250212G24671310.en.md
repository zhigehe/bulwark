Silitech Technology announced a public tender offer to acquire a 45% stake, comprising 15.5 million shares, of Japan's FDK at 435 yen per share.

Fujitsu, which owns 58.82% of FDK, will tender all its shares, but Silitech will acquire only up to the planned quantity, according to a Wednesday Taiwan Exchange filing.

The deal aims to expand Silitech's footprint in the energy and power sector, it noted.

Shares of the company jumped about 10% recently.
