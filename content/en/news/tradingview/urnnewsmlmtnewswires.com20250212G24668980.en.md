Hunan Xiangjia Animal Husbandry's live poultry sales revenue increased 6.1% year over year to 81.8 million yuan in January, according to a Wednesday filing with the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

Poultry sales volume increased 8.7% to about 4 million heads, the filing said.
