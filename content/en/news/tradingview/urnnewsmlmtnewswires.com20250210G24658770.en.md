Furukawa will sell shares in three listed firms by March 31 as part of efforts to enhance capital efficiency, according to a Monday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The metals and machinery company expects to book a gain of about 4 billion yen as extraordinary income in the fiscal fourth quarter ending March 31, with the impact already reflected in its full-year forecast.
