Apollo Commercial Real Estate Finance reported Q4 Non-GAAP earnings late Monday of $0.32 per diluted share, down from $0.36 a year earlier.

Analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $0.23.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $70.5 million, down from $84.2 million a year earlier.

Three analysts polled by FactSet expected $70.8 million.

Company shares were up 2.5% in recent after-hours activity.
