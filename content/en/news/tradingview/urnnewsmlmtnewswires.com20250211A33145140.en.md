Accenture said Tuesday that as part of its collaboration with Alphabet's Google Cloud, the companies will launch their joint Generative AI Center of Excellence in Saudi Arabia.

Accenture said the project is expected to provide businesses with AI-driven tools and cloud services while supporting compliance with local data regulations.

Accenture said the partnership includes training programs and hands-on labs to develop local expertise in AI and cloud technologies.
