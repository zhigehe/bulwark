\*\* Hong Kong shares of Hua Hong Semiconductor Ltd fall 5.9% to HK$24.75, on course for their biggest one-day pct drop since Jan. 27

\*\* Shenzhen stock falls 3.6%

\*\* Co posts $25.2 mln loss in 4Q 2024, compared to a net profit of $35.4 mln in 4Q 2023 and $44.8 mln in 3Q 2024, amid foreign exchange losses

\*\* Revenue $539.2 mln and gross margin 11.4%

\*\* Co expects revenue to be about $530 mln-$550 mln in first quarter of 2025 and gross margin in the range of 9%-11%

\*\* The Hang Seng Commerce & Industry Index (.HSNC) rises 1.5%, the Hang Seng Index gains 1.2%

\*\* Co's Hong Kong stock rose 14.7% last year, Shenzhen shares climbed 8.7%
