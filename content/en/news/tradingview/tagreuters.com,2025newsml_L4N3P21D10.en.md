\*\* Shares of CoverGirl parent Coty fall as much as 7.2% to over two-year low of $6.28

\*\* Coty cut its annual profit forecast and posted a surprise drop in quarterly revenue, as it faces slowing demand for cosmetics

\*\* Company now expects annual adjusted EPS to be between 50 cents and 52 cents, compared with its prior forecast of between 54 cents and 57 cents

\*\* Coty's Q2 net revenue falls 3.3% to $1.67 billion, its first drop in eight quarters, missing estimates of $1.72 billion, according to data compiled by LSEG

\*\* Company also seeing a hit from the closure of some major drug stores as well as from retailers cutting back on their beauty inventory to limit a hit from slowing demand for cosmetics

\*\* "We are not surprised by the weaker Q2 result and guidance reduction given ongoing beauty category weakness across categories and geographies" - Stifel analysts

\*\* Stifel cuts PT on Coty to $8 from $8.5

\*\* In 2024, stock had fallen nearly 44%
