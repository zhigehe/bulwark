Konecranes secured an order from Terminales Marítimas de Vigo, or Termavi, to supply two hybrid rubber-tired gantry cranes to the Port of Vigo in Spain.

The Finnish crane manufacturer will deliver the cranes equipped with its Konecranes smart features in the fourth quarter, according to a Monday release.

The contract is part of Konecranes' Ecolifting vision to minimize the carbon footprint of customers through its products and services.
