Adani Harbour Services, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone , has incorporated a joint venture company in Qatar with Sea Horizon Offshore Marine Services and Jamal A. Rab A M Al Yafei, according to a Wednesday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The joint venture company, named Al Annabi Marine Services,

will carry out the business of operations and management of

ships.

Adani Harbour holds a 49% stake in Al Annabi with the investment in 49 ordinary shares of 1,000 Qatari riyals each.
