MTI will transfer its fortune-telling service business to Concourse, a wholly owned unit of Zappallas Inc., through a simplified absorption-type company split, according to a Wednesday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

The transaction, valued at 45 million yen, aligns with MTI's strategy to focus on healthcare and school digital transformation businesses. The transfer is set to take effect on April 1.
