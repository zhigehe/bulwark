EMTEK (Shenzhen) plans to jointly establish a robotics subsidiary with Wali Technology and the company's actual controller and chair, Lv Jiezhong.

The tentatively named Shenzhen Xincheng Standard Robotics Technology has a registered capital of 30 million yuan, with the company planning to invest 18 million yuan for a 60% stake, according to a Feb. 8 filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Wali Technology and Lv will each contribute 6 million yuan each.
