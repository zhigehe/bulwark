Alphatec Holdings CEO, Miles Patrick, sold 43,459 shares of common stock on February 5, 2025, for a total of $519,335. The sale was conducted at a weighted average price of $11.95 per share, with prices ranging from $11.81 to $12.14. This transaction was executed under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on June 13, 2024, to satisfy tax obligations from vested restricted stock units. Following the sale, Patrick directly owns 6,065,772 shares and indirectly owns 260,900 shares of Alphatec Holdings.
