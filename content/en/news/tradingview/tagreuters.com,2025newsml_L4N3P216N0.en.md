\*\* Medtech firm Firefly Neuroscience's shares rise 50% to $4.58

\*\* Company [says](https://newsfile.refinitiv.com/getnewsfile/v1/story?guid=urn:newsml:reuters.com:20250211:nGNX7PZd1g&default-theme=true) it has been accepted into the Nvidia Connect program

\*\* The program provides member companies support, such as software development resources and technical training

\*\* AIFF expects to use the new tools and resources to build a human brain foundation model software using its FDA-cleared Brain Network Analytics (BNA) technology platform

\*\* BNA was developed using artificial intelligence and machine learning to provide doctors insights into brain function of patients with neurological and mental disorders, and improve accuracy of their diagnoses

\*\* In the last 12 months, stock has fallen 51.25%
