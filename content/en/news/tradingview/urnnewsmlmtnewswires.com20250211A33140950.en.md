Century Aluminum and Cleveland-Cliffs shares led a pre-bell rise in aluminum and steel stocks Tuesday a day after US President Donald Trump announced new 25% tariffs on metal imports.

Shares of Century Aluminum were up 3.5% in premarket activity, while Cleveland-Cliffs rose 3%.

Commercial Metals advanced 2%, Nucor was up 1.5%, U.S. Steel edged up 1% ahead of Alcoa , which was up almost 1%.

Shares of ArcelorMittal , based in Luxembourg, were down nearly 2%.
