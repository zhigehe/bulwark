Alan Hoops, a Director at Corvel Corp, sold a total of 4,800 shares of Common Stock on February 7, 2025, for a total sale amount of $582,037. The sales were conducted at prices of $121.289 and $121.1019 per share, reflecting a weighted average price for the transactions. Following these transactions, Hoops directly owns 19,688 shares and indirectly owns 128,413 shares through the Hoops Irrevocable Trust.
