Toho Co.'s movie theater chain business division posted 5.67 billion yen in box office revenue in January, marking a 6% increase from the same month last year, according to a Wednesday filing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Theater receipts were supported by titles including Grand Maison Paris, Mobile Suit Gundam GQuuuuuuX -Beginning-, and Doctor-X The Movie. Hollywood releases such as Moana 2, Mufasa: The Lion King, and Sonic the Hedgehog 3 also contributed to ticket sales.

Toho Cinemas generated 5.35 billion yen in January 2024 and reported 60.94 billion yen in full-year revenue.
