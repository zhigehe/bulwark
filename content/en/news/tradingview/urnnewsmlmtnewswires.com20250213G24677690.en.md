Power supplier CGN Power secured approval from the Shenzhen Stock Exchange for the issue of A-share convertible bonds to unspecified entities.

The issuance is still subject to approval and registration by the China Securities Regulatory Commission, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.
