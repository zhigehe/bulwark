Power and Water Utility Co. for Jubail and Yanbu (SASE:2083), d/b/a Marafiq, attained financial closure for its Al-Haer independent sewage treatment plant project worth 1.5 billion Saudi riyals.

The closure follows the completion of the project's requirements with Banque Saudi Fransi (SASE:1050), Arab Petroleum Investments Corp., and Riyad Bank (SASE:1010), as well as the receipt of Saudi Water Partnership certificates, according to a Sunday release.

The project was done by a consortium that included the utility company, Miahona (SASE:2084), and NV Besix.
