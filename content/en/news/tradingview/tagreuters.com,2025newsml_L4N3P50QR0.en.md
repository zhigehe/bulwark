\*\* Shares of UK's XP Pensions jump 10.63% to 386p, their highest since November 28

\*\* Stock is a top mover on London's mid-cap index (.FTMC)

\*\* The pensions consulting and administration firm expects 15-16% 2025 revenue growth

\*\* Co expects operational gearing to have improved and sees full-year results materially ahead of previously upgraded expectations

\*\* XPS says strong performance driven by ‍continued regulatory change, new clients, inflation-linkage of contracts

\*\* Co says ‍workplace pensions remain high on agenda, with further regulatory changes expected later this year

\*\* In 2024, stocks rose 9.68%
