Jiangsu Changyou Environmental Protection Technology (SHE:301557) filed for an initial public offering of 11,080,000 shares at 28.88 yuan each on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange.

Proceeds of nearly 320 million yuan will go towards expanding composite parts production, research and development, supplementing working capital and repaying bank loans, according to a Friday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares will be issued Feb. 18.

Haitong Securities serves as lead sponsor.
