Hefei Department Store Group's unit, Anhui Taikelong Chain Supermarket, plans to publicly list and sell some of its idle real estate through the Anhui Changjiang Property Rights Exchange, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange disclosure on Friday.

The assets in Xuancheng Economic and Technological Development Zone, Xuancheng City, Anhui Province, China will include factory buildings, office buildings, and industrial land. The industrial park covers an area of ​​32,369.1 square meters.

The property has a market value of 28.4 million yuan based on April 3, 2024, the Chinese department store operator said.

The company's shares closed 2% lower.
