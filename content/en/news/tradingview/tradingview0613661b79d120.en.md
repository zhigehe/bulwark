James Jeffrey Peoples, Chairman, President & CEO of APC at Southern Co, sold 14,540 shares of Southern Company Common Stock on February 11, 2025, at a price of $86.0 per share, totaling $1,250,440. Following the transaction, Peoples directly owns 13,236 shares and indirectly owns 8,469 shares through a 401(k).
