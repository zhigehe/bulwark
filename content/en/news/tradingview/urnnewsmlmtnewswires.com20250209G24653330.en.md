HSBC Holdings bought back 2,034,800 shares in Hong Kong on Friday at a weighted average price of HK$81.72 per share, according to Monday bourse filing.

Since the current buyback program has started, the British lender has repurchased 310,049,139 shares for about $2.93 billion.
