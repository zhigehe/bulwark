Sarah A. Halliday, EVP/President of Commercial Banking at NBT Bancorp, sold 5,000 shares of the company's common stock on February 10, 2025, at a price of $48.45 per share, totaling $242,250. Following the transaction, Halliday directly owns 25,564 shares and indirectly owns 7,075 shares through a 401k.
