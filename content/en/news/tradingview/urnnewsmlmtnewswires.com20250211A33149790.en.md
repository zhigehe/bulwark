Pitney Bowes reported Q4 adjusted earnings late Tuesday of $0.32 per share, up from $0.20 a year earlier.

Two analysts polled by FactSet expected $0.14.

Revenue for the quarter that ended Dec. 31 was $516.1 million, down from $526.4 million a year earlier.

One analyst surveyed by FactSet expected $509.7 million.

The shipping and mailing company said it expects 2025 revenue growth to range from $1.95 billion and $2 billion. One analyst expects $1.97 billion.

Shares of Pitney Bowes were up nearly 8% in after-hours activity.
