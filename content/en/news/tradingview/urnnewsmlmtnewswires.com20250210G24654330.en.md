Oil India recorded a decline in its consolidated net profit to 13.4 billion Indian rupees in the fiscal third quarter ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, from 23.5 billion rupees a year ago.

Earnings per share contracted to 8.23 rupees compared with the year-ago figure of 14.43 rupees, according to a Friday filing to the Indian stock exchanges by the state-owned oil and gas explorer.

Revenue from operations in fiscal Q3 also fell to 90.9 billion rupees from 109.1 billion rupees a year earlier.

The company's board also declared a second interim dividend of 7 rupees per share for the financial year 2024-25 to be paid by March 8.
