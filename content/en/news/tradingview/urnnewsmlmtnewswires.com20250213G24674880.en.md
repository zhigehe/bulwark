The court sanction hearing for Leopard UK Bidco's takeover of Learning Technologies Group , or LTG, will be held on March 24, according to a Thursday release.

General Atlantic via Leopard UK Bidco's acquisition of LTG will take effect on March 31 if the court approves and the remaining conditions in the scheme of arrangement document are met.

The London-based workplace digital learning company said eligible shareholders must make their choices regarding the alternative offers by March 17.
