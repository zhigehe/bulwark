In London, the FTSE 100 Index went up by 73 points or 0.84 percent on Monday.

Top gainers were BP (7.26%), Vistry Group (5.61%) and Fresnillo (5.16%).

Biggest losses came from International Airlines Group (-3.28%), SSE (-1.52%) and Coca-Cola (-1.35%).
