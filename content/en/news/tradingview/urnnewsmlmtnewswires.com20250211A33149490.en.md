Brighthouse Financial reported Q4 adjusted earnings late Tuesday of $5.07 per diluted share, up from $2.73 a year ago.

Analysts polled by FactSet expected $4.34.

Revenue in the three months ended Dec. 31 fell to $1.21 billion from $1.4 billion a year earlier.

Three analysts surveyed by FactSet expected $2.32 billion.
