Prospect Capital reported a fiscal Q2 loss late Monday of $0.07 per diluted share, narrower than a loss of $0.13 a year earlier.

A single analyst polled by FactSet expected earnings of $0.14 per share.

Total investment income for the quarter ended Dec. 31 was $185.5 million, down from $210.9 million a year earlier.

A single analyst polled by FactSet expected $189.7 million.
