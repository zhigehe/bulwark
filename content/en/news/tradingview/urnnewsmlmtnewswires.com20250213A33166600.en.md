Dynacor Group said Thursday that it achieved its second-highest monthly gold sales in January.

The company, which processes ore purchased from artisanal and small-scale miners, posted gold sales of US$28.7 million in January 2025, rising from US$26.9 million in January 2024.

Dynacor attributed the increase to a 28% jump in sales prices, offset by a 21% decline in volume.

The company said the selling price of gold averaged US$2,737 per ounce in January, a 34.8% increase from US$2,030 per ounce in January 2024.

Dynacor will release its 2025 financial guidance at the end of February and its 2024 annual financial results at the end of March.
