Goa Carbon has resumed operation of its manufacturing unit at Bilaspur in Chhattisgarh, India with effect from Sunday, according to a same-day disclosure to the Indian stock exchanges.

The company said the kiln has been lit up and the production has been normalized from Sunday.

In mid-January, the company temporarily shut down its Bilaspur manufacturing unit for "urgent maintenance work."
