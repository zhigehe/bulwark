Deveer R. Kipp, Co-President and Director of Ares Management Corp, sold 78,933 shares of Class A Common Stock on February 6, 2025, under a 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on September 6, 2024. The transactions were executed at weighted average prices ranging from $187.08 to $191.58, totaling $14,998,894 in sales. Following these transactions, Kipp directly owns 1,150,000 shares, represented as restricted units under an equity incentive plan, which vest in installments.
