The cost of insuring Chinese debt against default using credit default swaps falls as market sentiment improves driven by China's AI advancements. News about China's DeepSeek AI advances have boosted risk appetite and driven gains for Chinese stocks, Patrick Munnelly, partner at Tickmill Group says in a note. China's five-year credit default swaps decline 2 basis points to 47bps, a two-year low, S&P Global Market Intelligence data show. (miriam.mukuru@wsj.com)
