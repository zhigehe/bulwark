Alcidion Group signed a AU$5.5 million annual recurring subscription license contract with the Hywel Dda University Health Board in Wales for the use of its Miya Precision platform, according to a Tuesday Australian bourse filing.

The university health board will use Miya Precision for patient flow, observations and assessments, as well as a Smartpage for clinical communications and tasking.

The contract will last over five years, with an option to further extend for seven years for a total contract value of AU$7.7 million.

The company's shares rose past 5% in recent trading on Tuesday.
