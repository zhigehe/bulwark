Jiangsu Chuanzhiboke Education Technology completed its acquisition of a 51% stake in YouYou HuiLian for 106.4 million yuan.

The move supports the online training course provider's goal of expanding its vocational education offerings, particularly in e-commerce and cross-border trade disciplines, for higher education institutions, according to a Thursday filing with the Shenzhen bourse.

Shares of Jiangsu Chuanzhiboke Education Technology closed less than 1% higher Thursday.
