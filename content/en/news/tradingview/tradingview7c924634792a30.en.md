Oliver George, Chairman & CEO of Johnson Controls International plc, sold 410,336 Ordinary Shares across four transactions on February 6 and 7, 2025, for a total of $35,741,858. The sales were made at weighted average prices ranging from $85.99 to $88.57 per share. Following these transactions, George directly owns 1,260,526 Ordinary Shares and indirectly owns 178,349 shares through GRO Sr. 5 Year GRAT and Oliver Family Trust (SLAT).
