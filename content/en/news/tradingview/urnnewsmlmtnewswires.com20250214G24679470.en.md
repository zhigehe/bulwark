Huhtamäki will split its Fiber Foodservice Europe-Asia-Oceania business into two segments, with the changes to effect by April 1.

The business will be divided into Fiber Packaging and Foodservice Europe-Asia-Oceania segments, according to a Friday release.

The Finnish packaging company said the separation is subject to finalizing potential statutory consultation requirements.
