Scientex made first issuance of 260 million ringgit in aggregate nominal value of unrated Sukuk Wakalah under the Sukuk Wakalah Program.

The Sukuk Wakalah was issued for terms of three years, five years, and seven years, according to a Monday Malaysian bourse filing.

Proceeds will be used to refinance the existing Shariah-compliant financing of SQSB, it said.
