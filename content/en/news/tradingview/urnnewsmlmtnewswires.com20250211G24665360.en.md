HSBC Holdings bought back 3,673,200 shares in Hong Kong on Tuesday at a weighted average price of HK$83.54 per share, according to a Wednesday bourse filing.

Since the commencement of the buyback, the British lender repurchased 185,761,539 ordinary shares on the UK venues for cancellation at a volume-weighted average price of 7.49 pounds sterling per ordinary share. In Hong Kong, the bank bought back 130,621,600 ordinary shares on the stock exchange at a volume-weighted average price of HK$74.45 per share.

The total share buyback, which cost the company about $3 billion, has now concluded.
