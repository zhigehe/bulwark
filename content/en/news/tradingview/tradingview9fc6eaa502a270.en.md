Glenn Pushis, Senior Vice President of Steel Dynamics, sold 23,649 shares of Common Stock on February 11, 2025, at a weighted average price of $134.67 per share, totaling $3,184,810. Following the transaction, Pushis directly owns 122,140 shares of the company. The sale prices ranged from $134.39 to $134.83.
