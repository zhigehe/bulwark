Vection Technologies said it has achieved the Dell Technologies Titanium Partner status, according to a Monday filing with the Australian bourse.

The status allows Vection to access co-marketing opportunities, exclusive product roadmaps, and direct collaboration with Dell specialists to refine solutions and accelerate go-to-market strategies, the filing said.

The recognition also gives Vection priority in co-marketing and ambassador-level support, the filing added.
