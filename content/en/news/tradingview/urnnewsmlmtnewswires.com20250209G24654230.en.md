Facilities maintenance provider ISOTeam secured contracts worth around SG$36.4 million, according to a filing with the Singapore Exchange on Friday.

The contracts include addition & alteration projects, repair & redecoration projects, interior design projects, coating & painting projects, and landscaping projects.

Following the award of the new contracts, the group's current order book now stands at around SG$188.7 million.

Shares of the company were up nearly 2% in recent trading.
