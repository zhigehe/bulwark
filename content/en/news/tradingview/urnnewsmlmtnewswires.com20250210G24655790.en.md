Sterling Holiday Resorts, a subsidiary of Thomas Cook (India) , has opened a new jungle retreat resort named Sterling Ratan Villas Jawai, in Rajasthan, India, according to a Monday filing to the Indian stock exchanges.

The property is Sterling's eighth resort in Rajasthan, and it has plans to open more in the state.

Shares of the company were down nearly 3% in recent trade.
