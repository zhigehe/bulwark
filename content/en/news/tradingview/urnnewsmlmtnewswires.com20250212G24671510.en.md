Shenzhen Lifotronic Technology's units received medical device registration certificates for two devices, according to a Shanghai Stock Exchange disclosure on Thursday.

The certificates are for spectrum therapy device and quality control material for blood cell analyzer (optical method), valid until Jan. 25, 2030 and Feb. 7, 2030, respectively.

The certificates are from the Food and Drug Administrations of China's Chongqing municipality and Guangdong Province, the Chinese medical device manufacturer said.
