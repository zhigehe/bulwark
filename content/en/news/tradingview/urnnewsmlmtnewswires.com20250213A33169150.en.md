US natural gas stocks fell by 100 billion cubic feet in the week ended Feb. 7, a larger drop than the 92 billion decline expected in a survey compiled by Bloomberg as of 7:30 am ET and following a decrease of 174 billion cubic feet in the previous week.

Stocks at 2.297 trillion cubic feet are 9.7% lower than in the comparable week a year ago and 2.8% below their 5-year average.
