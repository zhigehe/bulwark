Joseph D Mansueto, Executive Chairman and 10% Owner of Morningstar, sold 9,341 shares of Common Stock over two days, with transactions dated February 6 and 7, 2025. The sales were executed at weighted average prices ranging from $325.7168 to $327.6778, totaling $3,050,875. Following these transactions, Mansueto directly owns 10,133,353 shares and indirectly owns 4,986,106 shares through trusts. The sales were made under a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan adopted on November 17, 2023.
