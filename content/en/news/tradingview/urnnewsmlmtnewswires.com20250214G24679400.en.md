Shenzhen Soling Industrial , through a unit Wuxi Soling Technology, completed the establishment of a 20 million yuan technical service subsidiary, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange filing on Friday.

The Chinese car informatic devices manufacturer received the business registration procedures for the new subsidiary, Chengdu Soling Autolink Technology, the filing said.
