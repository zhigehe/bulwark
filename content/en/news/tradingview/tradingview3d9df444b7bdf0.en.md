Robert M Averick, a 10% Owner and Director of AMTECH SYSTEMS, purchased 54,789 shares of Common Stock on February 7, 2025, at a weighted average price of $5.02 per share, totaling $275,040. Following this transaction, Averick directly owns 560,789 shares and indirectly owns 2,261,500 shares of AMTECH SYSTEMS. The indirect ownership includes shares beneficially owned by the Kokino Family Clients, held in a custodial account for the benefit of an immediate family member, and held by Averick's spouse.
