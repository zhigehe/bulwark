Nordic Aqua Partners said Thursday it restarted Atlantic salmon sales at its Ningbo facility in China after addressing operational challenges.

The land-based salmon farming company has taken measures to counteract geosmin and implemented enhanced operational protocols, according to a release.

Nordic Aqua added that construction for the second-stage expansion of the facility is also underway, with a first harvest expected in the third quarter of 2026.
