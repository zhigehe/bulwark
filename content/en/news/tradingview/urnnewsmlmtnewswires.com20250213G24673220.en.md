Business investment in the UK dropped 3.2% on a quarterly basis in the three months to December 2024, compared with the 1.9% rise in the prior quarter, preliminary data from the Office for National Statistics showed Thursday.

The flash figure missed the consensus estimate of a 1.1% growth.

Annually, fourth-quarter 2024 business investment was 0.7% lower, against the revised 4.4% increase earlier.
