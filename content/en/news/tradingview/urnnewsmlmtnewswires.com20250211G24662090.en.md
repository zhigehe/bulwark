HgCapital Trust's manager Hg agreed to partially sell its stake in software as a service and operating data platform provider Trackunit to Goldman Sachs Alternatives' Private Equity business.

The deal values the private equity company's investment in Trackunit at 53.4 million pounds sterling, according to a Tuesday release.

The transaction, the terms of which were not disclosed, is subject to customary regulatory approvals and expected to close in the early summer of 2025.
