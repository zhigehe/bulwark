Great Portland Estates leased 6,700 square feet of refurbished fully managed office space at its Piccadilly Estate in London, securing 1.6 million pounds sterling in annual rent.

The four tenants are family offices or from the financial services sector, occupying their new space by the end of February, according to a Thursday filing by the real estate company.

The new leases bring the total flex space at the 201,000-square-foot property to 29,000 square feet, representing 60% of the let office space.
