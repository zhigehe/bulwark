Lier, 13 February 2025: Kid Group, a leading home textile retailer in theNordics, announces its financial results for the fourth quarter of 2024.

HIGHLIGHTS FOR THE QUARTER INCLUDE:

· Revenues for the Group amounted to MNOK 1,403.7, an increase of 12.0%,driven by strong growth in both Kid Interior and Hemtex · Gross margin decreased 2.2 percentage points to 61.2%. Strong gross marginfor the full-year in a historical perspective · EBITDA increased by MNOK 44.6 to MNOK 464.5 · EPS of NOK 7.11 up from NOK 5.74 last year, positively impacted by thesaleof the warehouse in Sweden · The Board will propose to the Annual General Meeting a dividend payment ofNOK 5.00 payable in May 2025. Together with the prepayment of NOK 3.00 fromNovember 2025, total dividend of NOK 8.00 represents 82% of the net profit for2024

"We are proud to present a strong final quarter for Kid Group in 2024,reachingall-time high EBITDA of MNOK 464.5. The growth is attributed to successfulassortment and category development initiatives, effective marketing, high-quality store operations, and a dedicated team", says Anders Fjeld, CEO ofKidASA.

The results will be presented at 08:30 CET by CEO Anders Fjeld and CFO MadsKigen as a live webcast hosted by DNB Markets. A recorded version of thewebcastwill be made available at http://investor.kid.no.
