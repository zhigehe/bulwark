* Freeport McMoRan is expected to resume shipments of copper concentrate from Indonesia this month, Reuters is reporting, citing two sources.

* Freeport McMoRan would resume the shipments under a new export license, after the previous one expired in December, Reuters reports.

* The company will start loading cargoes destined for China on Friday, and a shipment of copper concentrate from the company's mine at Grasberg is expected to depart by late February, Reuters cited.
