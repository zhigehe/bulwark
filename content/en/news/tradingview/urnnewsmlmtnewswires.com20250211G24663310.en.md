Ashtead Group said Tuesday that Chief Financial Officer Michael Pratt will step down on Feb. 28, ahead of his retirement in September.

CFO Designate Alex Pease, who joined in October 2024, will become the British industrial equipment rental company's CFO on March 1, 2025.

The change was first announced Sept. 3, 2024.
