NSL Ltd. said its public float stood at 7.9% as of Feb. 5, with 29.3 million shares held by 2,469 public shareholders, according to a Friday filing on the Singapore Exchange.

The Singapore Exchange granted the company an extension until June 6 to restore its free float while allowing shares to continue trading. NSL said the current distribution supports orderly trading.
