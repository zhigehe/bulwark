Unilever said Thursday that the planned separation of the Ice Cream business remains on track for completion by the end of 2025.

The consumer goods giant plans to demerge the business and list it in Amsterdam, Netherlands, London, and New York. The process would conclude the Unilever board's review of separation options for the business launched in March 2024.

Unilever named Jean-Francois van Boxmeer as the chair designate for the separated business. The appointee serves as chair of Vodafone Group and chief executive officer of Heineken .
