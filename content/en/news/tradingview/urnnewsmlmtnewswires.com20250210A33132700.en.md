The most-talked-about stocks in the Reddit subforum Wallstreetbets were mostly higher hours before Monday's opening bell.

Kingsoft Cloud Holdings rose by 6.6% in premarket hours, reversing a 2.6% decline from the previous session.

BigBear.ai Holdings advanced by 5.9% in pre-bell activity, after closing Friday with a 26.9% rise.

Super Micro Computer was up 3.2% premarket, following a 7.2% increase from the previous session.

Reddit was 1.4% higher pre-bell, following a 4% rise at Friday's close.

Palantir Technologies advanced by 1.4% hours before market open, reversing a 0.4% decline from the previous session.

Advanced Micro Devices was up 0.6% pre-bell, swinging from a 2.4% fall at Friday's close.

Tesla declined by 1.1% premarket, following a 3.4% fall at Friday's close.

Nvidia was 0.3% lower pre-bell, swinging from a 0.9% increase at Friday's close.
