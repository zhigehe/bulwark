ICE canola futures edged higher Tuesday on general vegoil strength.

• March canola (RSH5) settled up $2.80 at $664.60 per metric ton, the fourth day of gains at the highest prices since mid-November.

• Traders said the U.S. Department of Agriculture supply and demand reports released Tuesday did not undermine the bullish sentiment around vegoils.

• Chicago Board of Trade soybean futures fell 0.57% on the USDA confirmation of a 5-year high in stocks. Soyoil futures (BOv1) rose 0.85% on tight North American vegoil stocks.

• Euronext rapeseed futures (COMc1) rose 1.26% following Monday strength in Malaysian palm oil futures .

• The Canadian dollar was rangebound.
