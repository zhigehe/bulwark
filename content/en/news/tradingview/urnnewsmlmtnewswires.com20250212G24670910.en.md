Medallion Metals appointed Richard Hill as Chief Financial Officer, effective immediately, according to a Thursday filing with the Australian bourse.

Hill was most recently in the same role at Calidus Resources (ASX:CAI), the filing said.

Medallion Metals' share price climbed 7% in recent Thursday trade to reach its highest since late 2022, per the filing.
