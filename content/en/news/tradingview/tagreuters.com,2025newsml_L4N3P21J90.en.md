\*\* Shares of term life sales agency SelectQuote jump as much as 56.6%, hitting their highest since three years

\*\* Stock last up 27.7% at $5.59

\*\* SLQT announced $350 million investment from funds managed by Bain Capital, Morgan Stanley Private Credit and Newlight Partners late on Monday

\*\* Deal allows SLQT to bolster its healthcare service business and fortify its balance sheet

\*\* Jefferies and Wachtell, Lipton, Rosen & Katz advised SelectQuote in the transaction

\*\* Shares of SLQT more than doubled in 2024
