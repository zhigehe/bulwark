By Stephen Nakrosis

Ambitions Enterprise, which provides tour operator, travel agency, and event planning and management services, filed for an initial public offering on Friday.

The is a Cayman Islands holding company with operations conducted by subsidiaries. Those subsidiaries provide travel services in the UAE, Jordan, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia and Oman, the company said.

For the six months ended June 30, 2024, the company reported revenue of $8.5 million up from $5.2 million for the six months ended June 30, 2023.

The company said it applied to list on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbol AHMA.

Ambitions Enterprise didn't offer a planned size for its IPO, but said the offering is expected to price between $4 and $5 per share.
