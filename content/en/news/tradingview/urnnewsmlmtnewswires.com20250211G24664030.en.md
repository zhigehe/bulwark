The Saudi Central Bank approved four new financial technology startups to test their platforms in its regulatory sandbox as part of efforts to develop Saudi Arabia's fintech sector.

SpireTech received permission for open banking, The Lending Hub and Soar for peer-to-peer lending, and Ldun for providing factoring services to micro, small, and medium enterprises, the central bank said Tuesday.
