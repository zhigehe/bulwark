International Game Technology said Thursday that its IGT Global Solutions unit has signed a nine-year contract extension with the Tennessee Education Lottery.

The extension will start in June 2027 and run through June 2036, according to the company.

Under the agreement, IGT will among others deploy its Aurora central system and provide upgraded Wizard back-office business applications, the company said.

The shares were up about 2% in recent trading.
