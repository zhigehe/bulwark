Adnoc Drilling (ADX:ADNOCDRILL) said Thursday that its profit and revenue for full-year 2024 rose year over year.

Profit attributable to shareholders for the 12 months ended Dec. 31,Â 2024, was $1.30 billion, compared with $1.03 billion a year ago. EPS moved to $0.081 from $0.065.

The Abu Dhabi-listed offshore drilling company's revenue was $4.03 billion, compared with $3.06 billion earlier.

Meanwhile, the board proposed a final cash dividend of 0.0905 dirham per share for the period, taking the total dividend for 2024 to 0.181 dirham per share. The dividend will be paid in the first half of April. It paid a final cash dividend of 0.0822 dirham per share and a total dividend of 0.1645 dirham per share in 2023.

Adnoc Drilling provided its guidance for revenue in 2025 to be between $4.60 billion and $4.80 billion and net profit between $1.35 billion and $1.45 billion. The company also expects $5 billion in revenue in 2026.
