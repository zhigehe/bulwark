Ferde AS, rated AA+ (Stable) by Scope Ratings, has successfully issued a 5yearbond amounting to NOK 1 500 million. The bond carries a coupon of 3m Nibor +0.49 % p.a.

The net proceeds will be used for financing and refinancing of investments inthe Rogfast project.

The new bond will have guarantee (selvskyldnergaranti) from Rogaland CountyMunicipality, rated AAA (Stable) by Scope Ratings. The existing Ferde bondswithsole guarantee from Rogaland County Municipality are rated AAA by ScopeRatings.The new bond is also expected to receive a bond rating of AAA (Stable) byScopeRatings.

DNB Markets and Handelsbanken acted as Joint Lead Managers.
