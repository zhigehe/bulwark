Strip Tinning received an order valued at $780,000 for its C samples from an unnamed German automotive motion technology supplier for a US-based autonomous vehicle program.

The British automotive component company said Tuesday the order covers the supply of the cell contact system for the vehicle's battery pack modules, supporting an undisclosed US-based vehicle manufacturer's preparations for production in 2026.
