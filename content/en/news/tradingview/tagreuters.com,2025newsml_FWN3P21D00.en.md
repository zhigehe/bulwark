Europe's largest sugar producer Suedzucker raised its 2024/2025 outlook on Tuesday, forecasting core earnings and an operating result in the upper range of its expectations.

The German firm said it expects earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization (EBITDA) in the upper area of 550-650 million euros ($569-672 million).

Operating profit is expected in the upper part of Suedzucker's 175-275 million euro range.

($1 = 0.9670 euros)
