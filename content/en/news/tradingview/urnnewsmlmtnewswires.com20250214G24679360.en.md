Anupam Rasayan India said it has signed a letter of intent with a US-based multinational company for the supply of a specialty chemical used in polymer applications, according to an Indian bourse filing on Thursday.

The agreement is expected to generate cumulative sales of $195 million over 10 years, with production to take place the company's existing facilities, according to the filing.
