U.S. sanctions on Russian oil are not expected to affect Moscow's trade with India, Pavel Sorokin, Russia's first deputy energy minister, said at on Tuesday.

"Our relationship with India is based on economic pragmatism," he told the India Energy Week conference.

"We believe energy trade shouldn't be hindered by any politics."
