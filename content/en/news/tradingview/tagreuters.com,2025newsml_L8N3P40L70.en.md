\*\* Shares in the Belgian bank KBC rise edge up 2.7% to a three-year high after strong FY results

\*\* "Strong finish to 2024," J.P.Morgan says, pointing to KBC's Q4 PBT of 1.02 bln euros ($1.06 bln), 7% above company-compiled consensus, and total income of 2.89 bln which is 4% above consensus

\*\* The broker also highlights KBC's better outlook for 2025-27

\*\* The results show "strength across the board in Q4", while guidance implies upside to consensus, RBC says

\*\* RBC adds that all revenue lines performed well, cost-control remains disciplined

\*\* JPM also anticipates low single digit upgrades to 2025-27E consensus

($1 = 0.9597 euros)
