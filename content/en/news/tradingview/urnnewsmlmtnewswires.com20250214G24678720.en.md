Shenzhen Sea Star Technology's unit, Wuxi Yiming Optoelectronics, received a high-tech enterprise certificate from the local government of China's Jiangsu Province, according to a Shenzhen Stock Exchange filing on Friday.

Companies with the certificate will be allowed to pay corporate income tax at a rate of 15%, the Chinese manufacturer of consumer electronics and LED products said.

The company's shares dropped more than 2% in recent trade.
