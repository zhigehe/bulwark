Charter Hall Retail REIT said Charter Hall Wholesale Management, acting as trustee for various investment trusts, further raised its stake in Hotel Property Investments to 85.43% from 79.25%, according to a Wednesday filing with the Australian bourse.

The real estate investment trust is acquiring Hotel Property investment via an off-market takeover for AU$3.752 per security.
