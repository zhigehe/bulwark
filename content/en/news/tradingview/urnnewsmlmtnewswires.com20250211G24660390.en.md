Shanghai is planning to issue 500 million yuan in consumption vouchers for the service sector, Xinhua News reported Monday

The allocations will be 360 million yuan for catering, 90 million yuan for tourism, 30 million yuan for cinemas and 20 million yuan for sports.

The vouchers will be valid for redemption starting March 1, with full distribution targeted by the end of June.
