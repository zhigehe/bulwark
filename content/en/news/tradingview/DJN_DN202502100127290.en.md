Hong Kong shares are lower at midday on some profit taking. Semiconductor and auto stocks are leading the losses. Auto-related names have been on a solid run after the Lunar New Year holidays. They are likely experiencing a short-term correction with not many positive changes from the auto market, says Nomura auto analyst Joel Ying. XPeng is down 7.2% and Li Auto is off 5.5%. Chinese chip foundries, Semiconductor Manufacturing International Corp. and Hua Hong Semiconductor are due to report 4Q results after market close. The stocks are down 2.8% and 3.7%, respectively. The benchmark Hang Seng Index is 0.6% lower at 21395.73, the Hang Seng Tech Index is off 1.55%. (sherry.qin@wsj.com)
