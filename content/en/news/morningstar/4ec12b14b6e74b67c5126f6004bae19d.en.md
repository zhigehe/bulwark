GE HealthCare Technologies said Thursday its fourth-quarter adjusted earnings rose to $1.45 a share from $1.18 a share, in the year-ago period.

The company topped the FactSet consensus estimate of $1.26 a share.

GE Healthcare's (GEHC) fourth-quarter revenue grew by 2% to $5.32 billion, compared to the analyst estimate of $5.33 billion.

For full-year 2025, GE HealthCare Technologies expects adjusted earnings of $4.61 a share to $4.75 a share, compared to the analyst estimate of $4.66 a share.

GE HealthCare Technologies stock was down 1.3% on Wednesday.
