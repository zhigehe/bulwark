Crocs Inc.'s stock was up nearly 16% in premarket trading on Thursday after the footwear maker's outlook for adjusted 2025 profit came in ahead of Wall Street analyst estimates.

Crocs (CROX) said it expects full-year profit of $12.70 a share to $13.15 a share, ahead of the FactSet consensus estimate of $12.58 a share.

For the first quarter, Crocs expects adjusted earnings of $2.38 a share to $2.52 a share, below the analyst estimate of $2.65 a share.
