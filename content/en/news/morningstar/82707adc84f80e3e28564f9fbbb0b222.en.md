--Commerzbank plans to cut thousands of jobs and set new targets in a bid to convince investors it can remain independent as Italy's UniCredit shows interest in a potential tie-up, Reuters reports, citing unnamed sources.

--The German bank--due to outline a new strategy on Thursday--could cut between 3,000 and 4,000 jobs out of a workforce of some 42,000, Reuters reports, citing one source.

--Commerzbank declined to comment, according to Reuters.
