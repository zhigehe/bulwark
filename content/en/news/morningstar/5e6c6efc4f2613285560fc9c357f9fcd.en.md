--Yield is up for three consecutive trading days

--Yield is up 0.068 percentage point over the last three trading days

--Largest three-day yield gain since Thursday, Jan. 9, 2025

--Today's yield is the fourth lowest this year

--Yield is off 0.277 percentage point from its 52-week high of 4.986% hit Monday, Jan. 13, 2025

--Yield is up 0.774 percentage point from its 52-week low of 3.936% hit Monday, Sept. 16, 2024

--Yield is up 0.340 percentage point from 52 weeks ago

--Yield is up 0.068 percentage point from its 2025 closing low of 4.642% hit Wednesday, Feb. 5, 2025

--Year-to-date the yield is down 0.076 percentage point
