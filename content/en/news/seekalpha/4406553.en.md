* Eddy Smart Home Solutions (TSXV:[EDY:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/EDY:CA "Eddy Smart Home Solutions Ltd.")) said on Tuesday that it has [entered](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999209-eddy-smart-home-solutions-ltd-secures-1-million-working-capital-loan-facility) into a loan agreement with a non-arm's length private lender for a $1M revolving credit facility.
* The facility bears interest at 12% per annum and will mature in two years. There were no fees paid in connection with the facility.
* The company intends to use the net proceeds from the financing largely for inventory purchases, to satisfy its contracted revenue backlog.
* This facility can be increased at the request of the company by an additional $500,000, for an aggregate amount of $1.5M based on growth in receivables and increased inventory.
