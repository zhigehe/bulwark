* Robert F. Kennedy Jr., President Trump's choice to head HHS, is a step closer to being confirmed after the Senate voted to advance his nomination.
* The vote was 53-47 along party lines.
* Kennedy Jr. still has to get through a formal confirmation vote, and there is a possibility that some Republicans who voted to advance his nomination may decide against him in the final vote. They include Sens. Bill Cassidy (La.), who is a medical doctor; Mitch McConnell (Ky.); Susan Collins (Maine); and Lisa Murkowski (Alaska).
* Vaccine makers: GSK (NYSE:[GSK](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GSK "GSK plc")), Sanofi (NASDAQ:[SNY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SNY "Sanofi")), Pfizer (NYSE:[PFE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PFE "Pfizer Inc."))/BioNTech ([BNTX](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BNTX "BioNTech SE")), Moderna (NASDAQ:[MRNA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MRNA "Moderna, Inc.")), and Novavax ([NVAX](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NVAX "Novavax, Inc.")).
* Weight loss drugmakers: Novo Nordisk ([NVO](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NVO "Novo Nordisk A/S")) and Eli Lilly ([LLY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LLY "Eli Lilly and Company")).
