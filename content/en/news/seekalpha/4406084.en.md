* ParaZero Technologies (NASDAQ:[PRZO](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PRZO "ParaZero Technologies Ltd.")) on Tuesday said its SafeAir systems, integrated with the DJI Matrice 350, DJI Mavic 3T and DJI Mavic 3E, have successfully achieved CE Class C5 compliance.
* The CE Class C5 certification is crucial for compliance with the European Union Aviation Safety Agency regulations, especially for operators navigating the complex Specific Operations Risk Assessment process.
