DMC Global (NASDAQ:[BOOM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BOOM "DMC Global Inc.")) advanced 6% in after hours trading after Steel Connect offered $10.18 a share in a revised bid.

On Thursday, Steel Connect, which has a 9.9% stake in DMC Global, delivered the non-binding proposal to DMC Global, according to [a 13D/A filing o](https://seekingalpha.com/filing/9527095 "a 13D/A filing o")n Friday. The $10.18 offer represents a 29% premium to DMC Global's (NASDAQ:[BOOM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BOOM "DMC Global Inc.")) closing price on Friday.

The latest offer comes after Steel Connect late last month [reiterated its interest in a potential acquisition](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4398867-steel-connect-reiterates-1650share-buyout-offer-for-dmc-global " reiterated its interest in a potential acquisition ") of DMC and called on the company to engage in comprehensive due diligence. Steel Connect [first offered $16.50 a share i](https://tinyurl.com/yps7ebd4 "first offered $16.50 a share i")n September.
