Sera Prognostics (NASDAQ:[SERA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SERA "Sera Prognostics, Inc.")) [priced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19997487-sera-prognostics-announces-pricing-of-50-million-public-offering "priced") its underwritten public offering of 1.25M shares at a public offering price of $4.00 per share, pre-funded warrants to purchase 11.25M shares at $3.9999 per pre-funded warrant.

The gross proceeds are expected to be approximately $50M.

Underwriters have a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional ~1.88M shares.

The offering is expected to close on February 12, 2025.

Net proceeds will be deployed to expand its commercial infrastructure and capabilities in the U.S., accelerate preparations for expansion in the EU, fund additional studies designed to increase adoption of the PreTRM test and for general corporate purposes.

The stock price dropped 5.2% on Monday during after-market hours of trading.
