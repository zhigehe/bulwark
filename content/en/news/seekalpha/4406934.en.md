* Diana Shipping (NYSE:[DSX](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/DSX "Diana Shipping Inc.")) has agreed to sell its 2010-built Post-Panamax vessel "Alcmene" for ~$11.9M, with delivery by March 7, 2025.
* The company has also signed a time charter with SwissMarine Pte., Singapore, for its Newcastlemax vessel, m/v San Francisco, at $26,000/day, minus a 5% commission.
* The charter will run from February 27, 2025, to October 25, 2026 (with an option to extend until December 25, 2026), generating ~$15.55M in revenue for the minimum period.
* After the sale, Diana Shippingâ€™s fleet will consist of 37 vessels, including 4 Newcastlemax, 8 Capesize, 4 Post-Panamax, 6 Kamsarmax, 6 Panamax, and 9 Ultramax.
* The company also expects to take delivery of two methanol dual fuel new-building Kamsarmax dry bulk vessels by the second half of 2027 and the first half of 2028, respectively.
* DSX shares rise over 2% on Wednesday.
