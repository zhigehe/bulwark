* Sceptre Ventures (SVP.H:CA) [announced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999179-sceptre-ventures-announces-non-brokered-private-placement) on Tuesday a non-brokered private placement financing of up to $5M.
* The private placement will consist of the issuance of up to 50M units of the company, at a price of $0.01 per unit.
* Each unit comprises one common share in the capital of the Issuer and one transferable common share purchase warrant.
* Each warrant will entitle the holder thereof to acquire one additional share at a price of $0.05 per warrant share for a period of two years following the closing of the private placement.
* Proceeds of the private placement will be used for identifying and evaluating a proposed qualifying transaction and for general working capital purposes.
