* Rua Gold (TSXV:[RUA:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/RUA:CA "Rua Gold Inc.")) [said](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20000823-rua-gold-announces-c-5-million-brokered-offering-of-common-shares) on Wednesday that it has entered into an agreement with Cormark Securities in connection with the public offering of 8.33M common shares in the capital of the company at a price of C$0.60 per common share for gross proceeds of C$5M.
* The offering is expected to close on or about February 20, 2025.
* The company has granted to the agent an option to sell, at the offering price, up to 1.25M additional common shares for market stabilization purposes and to cover over-allotments, if any.
* The company intends to use the net proceeds from the offering for continuing the exploration program on its Reefton Project, and for general working capital and general corporate purposes.
