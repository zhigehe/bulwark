* British American Tobacco [press release](https://www.bat.com/content/dam/batcom/global/main-nav/investors-and-reporting/results-centre/pdf/FY_2024_Announcement.pdf) (NYSE:[BTI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BTI "British American Tobacco p.l.c.")): FY GAAP EPS of 136.0 p
* Revenue of £25.87B (-5.2% Y/Y).
* Adj. gross margin of 67.2% up 60bps
* Total adjusted organic gross profit at constant rates, grew by £396 million, an increase of 2.2% compared to 2023
* 2025 Outlook – Global tobacco industry volume expected to be down c.2%. – c.1% revenue growth (at constant rates), as we navigate increased excise and VAT in Bangladesh and new tobacco regulations in Australia. – 1.5-2.5% adjusted profit from operations growth (adjusted for Canada, at constant rates)\*, including an expected c.1.5% transactional FX headwind. – Performance expected to be second half weighted as we deploy our innovations throughout the year. – Net finance costs\* expected to be around £1.8 billion (adjusted for Canada).
