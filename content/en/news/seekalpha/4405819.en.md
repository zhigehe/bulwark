Apollo Commercial Real Estate Finance (NYSE:[ARI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ARI "Apollo Commercial Real Estate Finance, Inc.")) stock gained 2.5% in Monday after-hours trading after its Q4 earnings beat the Wall Street consensus.

[Q4 distributable EPS of $0.32](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19997304-apollo-commercial-real-estate-finance-inc-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2024-results "Q4 distributable EPS of $0.32"), topping the average analyst estimate of $0.23, were unchanged from Q3 and dropped from $0.36 in Q4 2023.

Q4 net interest income of $43.5M, missing the Visible Alpha consensus of $43.9M, declined from $47.0M in the prior quarter and $58.0M a year ago.

Book value per share of $12.77, beating the $12.27 Visible Alpha estimate, rose from $12.73 in Q3.

Apollo Commercial Real Estate's (NYSE:[ARI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ARI "Apollo Commercial Real Estate Finance, Inc.")) total assets of $8.41B vs. $9.10B at Sept. 30, and $9.30B at Dec. 31, 2023.

Total operating expenses of $38.5M fell from $38.6M in Q3 and increased from $37.8M in Q4 2023.
