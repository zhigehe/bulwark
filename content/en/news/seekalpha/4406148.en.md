* Glass House Brands ([OTC:GLASF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GLASF "Glass House Brands Inc.")) on Tuesday said the company expects Q4 total revenue to be between $52 and $54 million, compared to [consensus revenue estimates](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GLASF/earnings/estimates?period=quarterly) of $48.10 million.
* The company anticipates Q4 2024 cost of production will be below $115 per pound, well below previous guidance of $125 per pound.
* Glass House Brands anticipates that fourth quarter adjusted EBITDA will be $7 to $9 million, versus previous guidance of $3 million to $5 million.
* The company expects that FY revenue between $200 million and $202 million, compared to [consensus revenue estimates](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GLASF/earnings/estimates?period=annual) of $195.75 million.
* The company anticipates that full year 2024 consolidated gross margin will be in the high 40% range, compared to 50.3% in 2023, and full year 2024 adjusted EBITDA will be $38 to $40 million.
