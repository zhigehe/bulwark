* ConnectM Technology Solutions (NASDAQ:[CNTM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CNTM "ConnectM Technology Solutions, Inc.")) says it now anticipates Q4 2024 revenue of approximately $9M, a 102% increase compared to $4.5M revenue in Q4 2023.
* The revised Q4 ’24 guidance elevates ConnectM’s full-year 2024 revenue projection to $26.3M, reflecting 33% year-over-year growth compared to full-year 2023.
