On Monday, Cathie Wood and Ark Invest sold close to 500,000 shares of UiPath (NYSE:[PATH](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PATH "UiPath Inc.")), after the stock climbed nearly 2% on the day.

Yesterday, Wood’s flagship ARK Innovation ETF (NYSEARCA:[ARKK](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ARKK "ARK Innovation ETF")) dumped 459,205 shares of PATH while the ARK Next Generation Internet ETF (NYSEARCA:[ARKW](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ARKW "ARK Next Generation Internet ETF")) sold a total of 33,061 shares of PATH. In total, Wood and her team offloaded 492,266 shares of UiPath.

As for ARKK and ARKW’s portfolio positions on PATH, ARKK lists the company as its 11th largest holding with an allocation of 2.17% and a market value of $144.95M. At the same time, ARKW classifies the stock as its 23rd largest holding with an allocation of 1.36% which equates to a market value of $26.52M.

UiPath (NYSE:[PATH](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PATH "UiPath Inc.")) has delivered performance over the past six trading months, jumping 27.8% during the period. However, the global software firm has faced significant challenges over the past year, with the stock down 47.1% over a 1-year time frame.

Moreover, UiPath is also held by shares of ARK Autonomous Technology & Robotics ETF ([ARKQ](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ARKQ "ARK Autonomous Technology & Robotics ETF")) and ARK Space Exploration & Innovation ETF ([ARKX](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ARKX "ARK Space Exploration & Innovation ETF")) but according to Wood’s trade records, which she makes public each day did not sell shares of PATH.

Year to date price action for the above-mentioned instruments:

* PATH + 11.9% YTD.
* ARKK +10.5% YTD.
* ARKW +13.1% YTD.
* ARKQ +7.1% YTD.
* ARKX +8.7% YTD.
