* Cryptocurrency mining and specialty finance company LM Funding America (NASDAQ:[LMFA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LMFA "LM Funding America, Inc.")) estimated bitcoin holdings worth ~$16.1M as of January 31.
* LMFA shares were trading +2.13% pre-market at $1.92.
* The company had bitcoin holdings of 158.2 at the end of the month, compared to 150.2 in the prior month.
* The estimated value of ~$16.1M is based on a bitcoin price of about $102K as of January 31.
* January-end hashrate was flat month-over-month at 0.63 EH/s.
