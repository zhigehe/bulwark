* Iveco Group [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19995287-iveco-group-2024-full-year-and-fourth-quarter-results) ([OTCPK:IVCGF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/IVCGF "Iveco Group N.V.")): FY Consolidated revenues amounted to €15,289 million compared to €15,978 million in 2023.
* Net revenues of Industrial Activities were €14,948 million compared to €15,640 million in 2023, with positive price realization partially offsetting lower volumes in Truck and Powertrain.
* Adjusted diluted earnings per share was €2.09, up €0.74 compared to 2023.
