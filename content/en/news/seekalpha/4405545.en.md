* Lichen China (NASDAQ:[LICN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LICN "Lichen China Limited")) Monday said that it had completed the acquisition of the remaining 40% equity interest in Bondly Enterprises, a domestic consulting firm specializing in business process management.
* The acquisition, valued at $5,330,000, underscores Lichen China's (NASDAQ:[LICN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LICN "Lichen China Limited")) efforts to strengthen its foothold in the AI and technology sectors.
* This deal follows Lichen China's initial purchase of 60% equity in Bondly in 2024 and marks a significant step in its strategy to fully integrate Bondly's advanced technologies and AI capabilities into its operations.
