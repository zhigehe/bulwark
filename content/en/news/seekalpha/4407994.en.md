Banco Santander (NYSE:[SAN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SAN "Banco Santander, S.A.")) had held talks about a possible sale of its UK retail business to NatWest ([NWG](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NWG "NatWest Group plc")).

Early-stage discussions occurred last year, though both firms remain interested in a deal, according to a [Financial Times report](https://www.ft.com/content/5868abdb-0f46-4c56-a342-49763a55cdb8 " Financial Times report ") on Thursday, which cited people familiar with the matter. 

Santander (NYSE:[SAN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SAN "Banco Santander, S.A.")) has said its UK retail business is "not for sale" after the Financial Times reported last month that the bank was [exploring a possible exit o](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4396393-santander-considering-leaving-the-uk-report "exploring a possible exit o")f the UK business.

NatWest told the FT that it doesn't comment on speculation.
