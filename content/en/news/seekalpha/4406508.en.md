* Greenfire Resources (NYSE:[GFR](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GFR "Greenfire Resources Ltd.")) [announced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999159-greenfire-resources-announces-new-president-and-departure-of-senior-executives) on Tuesday the appointment of Colin Germaniuk as President and Adam Waterous as Executive Chairman of the company.
* As part of this leadership transition, Robert Logan, President and Chief Executive Officer, along with three Senior Vice Presidents - Kevin Millar (SVP, Operations & Steam Chief), Albert Ma (SVP, Engineering), and Crystal Park (SVP, Commercial) - will be departing the company.
* Tony Kraljic and Jonathan Kanderka will continue in their roles as Chief Financial Officer and Chief Operating Officer of the company, respectively, and will report to Colin Germaniuk.
* Prior to joining Greenfire, Germaniuk spent eight years at Serafina Energy, a private thermal oil company, where he was an early employee and a member of the executive management team.
