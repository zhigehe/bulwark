XPeng (NYSE:[XPEV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XPEV "XPeng Inc.")) announced its official entry to the UK market on Wednesday and initiated the pre-sale of its first right-hand drive model.

The UK is seen as a crucial step in XPeng's (NYSE:[XPEV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XPEV "XPeng Inc.")) internationalization strategy as the largest electric vehicle market in Europe in 2024.

XPeng (NYSE:[XPEV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XPEV "XPeng Inc.")) and IML plan to open 20 dealerships across the UK in 2025 to ensure a strong local presence and a more widespread customer support network. As part of its UK debut, XPeng ([XPEV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XPEV "XPeng Inc.")) has opened pre-orders for its highly anticipated G6 Ultra Smart Coupe SUV, with launch prices starting at Â£39,990 and deliveries to begin in March 2025.

"Our launch in the UK, in partnership with International Motors Ltd, marks a significant step in XPeng's European and global expansion," noted Vice Chairman said Dr. Brian Gu. The company plans to help usher in a new era of smarter, more sustainable mobility to the UK market and globally.

As for the European continent, XPeng ([XPEV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XPEV "XPeng Inc.")) is now operating in the UK, Norway, Denmark, Sweden, Finland, Iceland, the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany, France, Spain, Portugal, and Ireland.

Shares of XPeng ([XPEV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XPEV "XPeng Inc.")) jumped 3.65% in premarket trading on Wednesday.
