* Tractor Supply Company (NASDAQ:[TSCO](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TSCO "Tractor Supply Company")) has increased its dividend by $0.04, or 4.5% year-over-year, to $0.92 a share on an annualized basis for fiscal year 2025.
* In accordance with this increase, the board of directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.23 a share of the xompany’s common stock.
* Payable March 11; for shareholders of record Feb. 26; ex-div Feb. 26.
* In addition, Tractor Supply announced a $1B increase to its existing share repurchase program, bringing the total amount authorized to date under the program to $7.5B.
* As of December 28, 2024, the company had repurchased 357.4M shares of its common stock (adjusted to reflect the effect of stock splits) for approximately $6.03B since the inception of its share repurchase program in 2007.
