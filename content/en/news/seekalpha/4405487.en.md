Sun Country Airlines (NASDAQ:[SNCY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SNCY "Sun Country Airlines Holdings, Inc.")) [announced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996287-sun-country-airlines-holdings-inc-announces-launch-of-secondary-public-offering-of-common "announced") the commencement of a proposed secondary public offering of ~6.35M shares by an affiliate of certain investment funds managed by affiliates of Apollo Global Management, which represent all the remaining shares owned by the selling stockholder.

In addition, Sun Country Airlines has authorized the purchase from the underwriters of approximately $10M shares at a price per share equal to the price at which the underwriters will purchase the shares from the selling stockholder in the proposed offering.

Sun Country Airlines plans to fund the concurrent share repurchase from existing cash on hand.

The stock price declined 3.33% on Monday during pre-market hours of trading.
