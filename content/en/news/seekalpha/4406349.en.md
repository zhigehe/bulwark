Parkland ([OTCPK:PKIUF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PKIUF "Parkland Corporation")) +15.4% in Tuesday's trading after Ontario's provincial court found that Simpson Oil, the company's largest investor, is [no longer bound](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19997470-parkland-provides-update-on-governance-agreement-court-decision "no longer bound") by voting and standstill restrictions in the governance agreement.

The two parties have been involved in a legal dispute over their governance agreement since August 2024, when Simpson Oil, which owns 19.8% of Parkland ([OTCPK:PKIUF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PKIUF "Parkland Corporation")), began an application with the Ontario Superior Court of Justice seeking a declaration that the standstill and voting restrictions no longer applied.

Simpson Oil also has pushed for a strategic review which could include a potential sale of the business; Parkland ([OTCPK:PKIUF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PKIUF "Parkland Corporation")) said it remains open to representation from Simpson Oil on its board.
