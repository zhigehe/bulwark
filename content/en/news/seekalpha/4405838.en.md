* CEMATRIX (TSX:[CEMX:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CEMX:CA "CEMATRIX Corporation")) said on Monday that it has won $5.8 million in new contract awards, split between executed contracts and contracts in process.
* These include a contract for a large heavy tunnel grouting application, a contract for an increase in scope of a large lightweight fill application on a bridge abutment project and a change order for additional lightweight fill applications on a light rail transit extension project.
