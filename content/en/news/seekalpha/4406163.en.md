* Shares of Oramed Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ:[ORMP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ORMP "Oramed Pharmaceuticals Inc.")) traded higher on Tuesday after the oral drug developer announced agreements with Chinese biopharma Hefei Tianhui Biotech to spin off its Protein Oral Delivery technology into a new joint venture.
* Named OraTech Pharmaceuticals, the new entity is also expected to house Oramed’s (ORMD) oral insulin candidate. HITT has agreed to provide supply arrangements for the treatment while the company seeks its approval in the U.S. and elsewhere.
* With a majority equity interest from Oramed (NASDAQ:[ORMP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ORMP "Oramed Pharmaceuticals Inc.")), OraTech is expected to go public on Nasdaq and receive initial capital investments worth $60M from HITT and $15M from the company.
* "OraTech will be singularly focused on bringing oral insulin to market and unlocking the broader potential of oral drug delivery for additional therapeutic targets,” said Oramed (NASDAQ:[ORMP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ORMP "Oramed Pharmaceuticals Inc.")) CEO Nadav Kidron, highlighting the company’s plans to resume a pivotal Phase 3 trial [in the U.S. in Q1.](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19998458-oramed-announces-transformative-joint-venture-to-accelerate-development-and-commercialization)
