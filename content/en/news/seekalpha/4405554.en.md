* Automotive Properties Real Estate Investment Trust (TSX:[APR.UN:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/APR.UN:CA "Automotive Properties Real Estate Investment Trust")) on Monday said it has entered into an agreement to acquire a Tesla-tenanted collision center property in Dublin, Ohio, from a third party for a purchase price of about $17.8 million.
* The property is tenanted by Tesla under a mid-term net lease.
* The REIT expects to close the property acquisition in the first quarter of 2025.
* The REIT intends to fund the purchase price of the acquisition primarily by drawing on its revolving credit facilities.
