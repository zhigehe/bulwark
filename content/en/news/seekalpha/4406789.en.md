January Consumer Price Index: **+0.5%** vs. +0.3% consensus and +0.4% prior, according to data released by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics on Wednesday.

**+3.0%** Y/Y vs. +2.9% consensus and +2.9% prior.

Core CPI, which excludes food and energy**: +0.4%** vs. +0.3% consensus and +0.2% prior.

**+3.3%** Y/Y vs. +3.1% consensus and +3.2% in December.
