* Hexcel Corporation (NYSE:[HXL](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HXL "Hexcel Corporation")) on Tuesday [priced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999215-hexcel-prices-300-million-of-5_875-percent-senior-notes-due-2035) an offering of $300M of unsecured 5.875% Senior Notes due 2035. The notes will be sold at a price of 99.985% of their face value.
* The net proceeds from this offering are estimated to be approximately $298M.
* Hexcel intends to use the net proceeds from the offering to fund the redemption of its unsecured 4.700% Senior Notes due 2025, of which $300 million was outstanding as of the date hereof.
