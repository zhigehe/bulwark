A weekly summary of insider trading highlighting a few notable buying and selling by investors, directors and executives. The trades have taken place between February 3, 2025 and February 7, 2025.

• Exxon Mobil’s ([XOM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XOM "Exxon Mobil Corporation")) VP - Corp Strategic Planning, Darrin L. Talley, [sold](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/34088/000112760225003114/xslF345X05/form4.xml) about 2,100 shares of common stock at a price of $110 per share for a total of $231,000. Talley now owns about 18,087 shares of the company.

• American Express’ ([AXP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/AXP "American Express Company")), Chief Information Officer, Radhakrishnan Ravikumar, [sold](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/4962/000112760225003373/xslF345X05/form4.xml) 9,485 shares of common stock at a price of $319.17 per share, totaling $3M. Ravikumar now holds about 13,500 shares of the company.

• Mark Blinn, Director at Texas Instruments ([TXN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TXN "Texas Instruments Incorporated")) [disclosed](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/97476/000112760225003198/xslF345X05/form4.xml) through a filing that he sold 590 shares of the company at a price of $180.71 per share for total worth $106,617. Blinn now holds 11,717 shares of the company.

• Gerald Johnson, Director at Caterpillar ([CAT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CAT "Caterpillar Inc.")) [disclosed](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/18230/000110465925009475/xslF345X05/tm255527-1_4seq1.xml) a transaction this week. Johnson purchased 100 shares at a price of $365.57 per share for a total value of $ 36,557. Johnson now holds 3,053 shares of the company.

• Berkshire Hathaway, [disclosed](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/315090/000095017025012600/xslF345X05/ownership.xml) transactions at Sirius XM Holdings ([SIRI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SIRI "Sirius XM Holdings Inc.")) this week. It purchased 716,978 shares at a price of $23.71 per share, 840,000 shares at a price of $23.62 per share and 751,141 shares at a price of $22.79 per share. Berkshire Hathaway now owns about 120M shares of the company.
