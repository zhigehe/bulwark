* Lifeway Foods (NASDAQ:[LWAY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LWAY "Lifeway Foods, Inc.")) expects to realize ~$5M of net profit as a result of the recently announced sale of Simple Foods to Flowers Foods.
* Lifeway was an early investor in Simple Foods in 2015 and since that time, the company has already realized a $1.5M gain in 2019, bringing the total expected net proceeds to Lifeway of ~$6.5M.
* The acquisition of Simple Mills is projected to close during Q1 2025.
