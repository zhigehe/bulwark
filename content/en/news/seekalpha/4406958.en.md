* Micron Technology (NASDAQ:[MU](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MU "Micron Technology, Inc.")) offered up an update on its third-quarter at the Wolfe Research investment conference on Wednesday, telling onlookers that volumes have picked up.
* Shares fell 3.3% in late morning trading.
* The Boise, Idaho-based company is seeing stronger volumes than it had anticipated in a number of consumer markets and now projects low to mid-single digit growth in areas like PCs and smartphones, company executives said at the event.
* It also expects bit shipments to be higher in the coming period, while inventory levels have continued to improve.
* Separately on Wednesday, it was [reported](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4406770-micron-said-to-look-for-partner-for-phase-2-of-chip-plant-in-india-report "reported") that Micron is looking for a partner of the phase 2 portion of its fab in India at Sanand, Gujara.
