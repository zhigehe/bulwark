* Tanger (NYSE:[SKT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SKT "Tanger Inc.")) on Thursday said it has acquired Pinecrest, a 640,000-square-foot open-air, grocery-anchored, mixed-use center in Ohio.
* Tanger acquired Pinecrest for about $167 million, using cash on hand and available liquidity.
* Management estimates the center to deliver an eight percent return during the first year, with potential for additional growth over time, the company said.
