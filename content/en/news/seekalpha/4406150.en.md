* Euroseas (NASDAQ:[ESEA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ESEA "Euroseas Ltd.")) on Tuesday announced a new time charter contract for its 1,740 teu feeder containership, M/V EM Hydra for a minimum period of 24 to a maximum period of 26 months, at the option of the charterer, at a gross daily rate of $19,000, in direct continuation of its current charter.
* The new charter period is expected to commence on May 1, 2025.
* ESEA +2.4% premarket to $34.4813.
