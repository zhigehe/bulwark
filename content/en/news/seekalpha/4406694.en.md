* NiSource [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999455-nisource-announces-full-year-2024-results) (NYSE:[NI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NI "NiSource Inc.")): Q4 Non-GAAP EPS of $0.49  misses by $0.01.
* NiSource is raising 2025 non-GAAP adjusted EPS guidance to $1.85-$1.89 **(prior $1.84-1.88) vs. consensus of $1.86**. The base capital expenditure plan is increased to $19.4 billion, a $100 million increase versus the prior $19.3 billion. This is expected to result in 8%-10% rate base growth and non-GAAP adjusted EPS growth of 6%-8% annually for the 2025-2029 period.
* Reaffirming expected annual 6%-8% 2025-2029 non-GAAP adjusted EPS growth.
