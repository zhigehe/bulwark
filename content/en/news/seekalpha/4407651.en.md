* LightPath Technologies (NASDAQ:[LPTH](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LPTH "LightPath Technologies, Inc.")) on Thursday announced the accretive acquisition of G5 Infrared, an infrared camera systems manufacturer, for a combination of cash and stock.
* The company said the aggregate consideration payable to the G5 members is $27 million, consisting of $20.25 million in cash and $6.75 million in common shares.
* "This accretive acquisition strengthens our financial profile, creating a combined company that we believe will achieve revenue of at least $55 million in the twelve months following the acquisition and increased EBITDA margins," it added.
* LightPath Technologies has alos entered into definitive agreements to finance the cash consideration payable to the G5 members through private placements of $25.5 million of a newly-created series of 6.5% fixed price convertible preferred equity and related warrants, common shares totaling $1.5 million, and senior secured promissory notes totaling $5.2 million.
* LPTH -2.56% premarket to $3.42.
