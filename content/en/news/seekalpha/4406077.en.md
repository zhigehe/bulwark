* AGCO (NYSE:[AGCO](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/AGCO "AGCO Corporation")) and SDF, a manufacturer of tractors, harvesters, electric self-driving tractors and diesel engines, have signed a supply agreement that will offer farmers a streamlined low-mid range horsepower tractor portfolio for AGCO's Massey Ferguson brand.
* Beginning mid-year 2025, SDF will produce proprietary tractors with up to 85 horsepower for most global markets.
