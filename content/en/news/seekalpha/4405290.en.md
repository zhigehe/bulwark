Conduent Inc. (NASDAQ:[CNDT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CNDT "Conduent Incorporated")) soared 15% on a report that the company is exploring a sale after getting takeover interest.

The firm is working with an adviser on discussions with possible buyers, including private equity firms, according to a Reuters report on Friday, which cited people familiar with the matter. If talks are successful, an agreement could come in weeks. 

Conduent (NASDAQ:[CNDT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CNDT "Conduent Incorporated")) didn't immediately respond to Reuters requests for comment. Conduent has a market cap of $630 million.
