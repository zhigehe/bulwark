* NG Energy International (TSXV:[GASX:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GASX:CA "NG Energy International Corp.")) ([OTCQX:GASXF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GASXF "NG Energy International Corp.")) on Monday said it has entered into an asset purchase agreement with Etablissements Maurel & Prom for the sale of a 40% operating working interest in the Sinu-9 Block for total cash consideration of $150 million.
* The transfer of the 40% operating working interest in Sinu-9 to M&P is effective as of February 1, 2025.
* Prior to the transaction, the company held a 72% operating working interest in Sinu-9. Following the transaction and the sale of the 40% effective operating working interest in Sinu-9 to M&P, the company holds a 32% effective operating working interest in Sinu-9.
* In consideration for the 40% operating working interest in Sinu-9, the company will receive total cash consideration of $150M payable as $20M to be paid as an initial payment, and $130M upon completion.
* The company has agreed that $10M from the initial payment will be used exclusively in respect of the operations at Sinu-9.
