* District Metals (TSXV:[DMX:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/DMX:CA "District Metals Corp.")) and Boliden Minerals AB ([OTCPK:BDNNY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BDNNY "Boliden AB (publ)")) have approved an increased exploration budget of C$3.0M on the Tomtebo and Stollberg properties for 2025, the companies said Monday.
* This will be a 50% increase compared to the 2024 budget and will be equally split between both the properties.
* District Metals (TSXV:[DMX:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/DMX:CA "District Metals Corp.")) is the operator of the Tomtebo-Stollberg collaboration, and the company has commenced core drilling at the high-grade base metal polymetallic Tomtebo Property located in the Bergslagen Mining District in south-central Sweden.
