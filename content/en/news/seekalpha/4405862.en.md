* Tantech (NASDAQ:[TANH](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TANH "Tantech Holdings Ltd")) announced that the company's board approved on January 21, 2025, to effect a share consolidation of the company's common shares at the ratio of one-for-forty with the marketplace effective date of February 13, 2025.
* The objective of the share consolidation is to enable the company to regain compliance with Nasdaq and maintain its listing on Nasdaq.
* Beginning with the opening of trading on February 13, 2025, the company's common shares will trade on Nasdaq on a split-adjusted basis.
* The share consolidation will reduce the number of common shares issued and outstanding from 47.56M to ~1.19M.
* TANH shares fall over 19% post-market on Monday.
