Orion Group Holdings (NYSE:[ORN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ORN "Orion Group Holdings, Inc.")) +15.8% in Monday's trading to its highest since July after saying it received new Marine and Concrete awards totaling $211.7M.

In the biggest award, Orion (NYSE:[ORN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ORN "Orion Group Holdings, Inc.")) said the Texas Department of Transportation approved a contract of $113.7M to replace the State Highway 6 bridge over Lake Waco in central Texas.

The projects are expected to begin in 2025 with varying completion dates extending through 2026.

Not included in the $211.7M total is Orion's ([ORN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ORN "Orion Group Holdings, Inc.")) portion of the Deschutes Estuary Restoration project in Washington state, which the client currently estimates a project value of $350M while actively pursuing full funding, and a 1M sq. ft. data center in Iowa, which would bring Orion's portfolio of data center projects to 33 total projects, including both active and completed projects.
