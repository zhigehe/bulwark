BP (NYSE:[BP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BP "BP p.l.c.")) will [cut bonuses](https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/bp-cuts-senior-leadership-bonuses-45-after-missing-2024-financial-targets-2025-02-11/ "cut bonuses") for its senior leaders to 45% of target, Reuters reported Tuesday, citing an internal memo, after the company missed some key financial goals in 2024, including EBITDA of $38B, which came in below its target of $40.9B.

BP (NYSE:[BP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BP "BP p.l.c.")) shares trade flat after [Q4 earnings missed expectations](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4406027-bp-ceo-pledges-fundamental-reset-as-q4-profit-drops-60 "Q4 earnings missed expectations"), dragged down by weaker refining margins that fell to $13.10/bbl from $18.50/bbl in the year-earlier quarter, and underlying replacement cost profit, the company's definition of net income, fell to $1.17B from $2.99B a year ago.

For FY 2024, BP ([BP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BP "BP p.l.c.")) earned $8.9B in underlying profit compared with $13.8B in 2023, the worst annual result since it lost $5.7B in 2020, the year of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Separately, BP ([BP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BP "BP p.l.c.")) [plans to add six new wells](https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/bp-plans-six-new-gas-wells-sustain-output-azeri-caspian-sea-field-2025-02-11/ "plans to add six new wells") to its operation in Azerbaijan's Shah Deniz gas field in the Caspian Sea to ensure stable production in the years ahead, a senior manager at the international energy major told Reuters; the company currently operates 21 gas wells in Shah Deniz, which is developed by a BP-led consortium.

Under a 2022 deal, Azerbaijan is expected to double its exports to the EU to at least 20B cm/year by 2027.
