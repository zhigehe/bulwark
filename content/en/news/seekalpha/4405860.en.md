* Southwest Airlines (NYSE:[LUV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LUV "Southwest Airlines Co.")) [said](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19997371-southwest-airlines-announces-tom-doxey-as-executive-vice-president-and-chief-financial) on Monday that Tom Doxey has been named Executive Vice President & Chief Financial Officer, effective March 10.
* Doxey most recently served as President of Breeze Airways, where he led the airline through a successful transformation from a startup to profitability in under two years, the company said.
* Before Breeze, Doxey held several leadership roles at United Airlines, including Senior Vice President of Technical Operations and CFO of Operations.
* Southwest Airlines ([LUV](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LUV "Southwest Airlines Co.")) shares were up 0.10% in extended trading on Monday.
