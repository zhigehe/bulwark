MDA Space (TSX:[MDA:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MDA:CA "MDA Space Ltd.")) [signed a contract](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996294-mda-space-signs-1_1b-contract-with-globalstar-to-build-next-generation-leo-constellation "signed a contract") with Globalstar (NYSE:[GSAT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GSAT "Globalstar, Inc.")) to be the prime contractor for the satellite operator's next-generation low Earth orbit (LEO) constellation, with a total contract value of ~$1.1B.

As part of the contract for the full LEO constellation, MDA Space (TSX:[MDA:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MDA:CA "MDA Space Ltd.")) will manufacture more than 50 MDA AURORAsoftware-defined digital satellites for Globalstar (NYSE:[GSAT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GSAT "Globalstar, Inc.")).

A contract value of ~$750M will be added to the company's backlog in Q1 fiscal 2025. This amount is in addition to the ATP value of ~$350 million that was previously added to the backlog, for a total value of $1.1B under the definitive contract.
