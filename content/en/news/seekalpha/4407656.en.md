* Sadot Group (NASDAQ:[SDOT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SDOT "Sadot Group Inc.")) on Thursday announced the appointment of Catia Jorge as its new chief executive officer, effective immediately.
* Jorge, who succeeds Michael Roper, most recently served as Brazil Country Head and Vice President/Grains Business Head Latin America for Olam Agri. Prior to Olam, she held leadership roles at Cargill Agricola South America.
* Roper will transition as chief governance and compliance officer, Sadot said, adding that he will also continue to lead the initiative to divest the company's remaining restaurant operations.
* SDOT +4.34% premarket to $2.88.
