* BP p.l.c. (NYSE:[BP](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BP "BP p.l.c.")) declares [$0.48/ADS quarterly dividend](https://www.bp.com/content/dam/bp/business-sites/en/global/corporate/pdfs/investors/bp-fourth-quarter-2024-results.pdf), in line with previous.
* [Forward yield](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BP/dividends/yield?source=news_bullet) 5.58%
* Payable March 28; for shareholders of record Feb. 21; ex-div Feb. 21.
* The board has decided not to offer a scrip dividend alternative in respect of the fourth quarter 2024 dividend.
* Related to the fourth quarter results, bp intends to execute a $1.75 billion share buyback prior to reporting the first quarter results.
* As part of our capital markets update scheduled for 26 February, we intend to review elements of our financial guidance, including our expectations for 2025 share buybacks and capital expenditure.
