* Hyperfine (NASDAQ:[HYPR](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HYPR "Hyperfine, Inc.")) said it is looking to [raise $6M](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19998021-hyperfine-announces-6_0-million-registered-direct-offering "raise $6M") through the purchase and sale of 4.5M shares of its class A common stock and warrants to purchase up to 4.5M shares of class A common stock.
* The firm added each share and accompanying warrant were sold together at a combined offering price of $1.33 in a registered direct offering.
* The warrants have an exercise price of $1.33 apiece.
* The offering is expected to close on or about February 12.
* Shares were down 11.4% in premarket trade.
