* HomeTrust Bancshares (NASDAQ:[HTBI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HTBI "HomeTrust Bancshares, Inc.")), the holding company of HomeTrust Bank, said on Tuesday that the company will [transfer the listing](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19998071-hometrust-bancshares-inc-announces-transfer-of-listing-of-common-stock-to-the-new-york-stock) of its common stock from the NASDAQ Stock Market to the New York Stock Exchange.
* HomeTrust’s common stock is expected to commence trading on the NYSE on Monday, February 24, 2025, under a new ticker symbol, “HTB”.
* The company’s common stock is expected to continue to trade on NASDAQ until the close of the market on Friday, February 21, 2025.
