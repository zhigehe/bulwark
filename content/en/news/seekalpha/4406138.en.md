* Haoxi Health Technology (NASDAQ:[HAO](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HAO "Haoxi Health Technology Limited")) received confirmation from Nasdaq that it has regained compliance with the $1/share minimum bid price requirement.
* This follows its shares closing at $1 or higher for 10 consecutive business days from January 27 to February 7, 2025.
* HAO shares fall over 4% premarket on Tuesday.
