Faraday Future Intelligent Electric (NASDAQ:[FFIE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/FFIE "Faraday Future Intelligent Electric Inc.")) on Monday said that FF China will ship two FX 6 prototype mules to its Los Angeles headquarters later this month.

The shipment of the two test mules, both of which will be branded as “FX 6 Series” models, signifies a further step in the FX brand’s plan to bring affordable mass market AIEV’s to the marketplace, [the company said.](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996188-faraday-future-s-fx-brand-to-ship-first-fx-6-prototype-mules-to-u-s-headquarters-kicking-off "the company said.")

The shipment of the prototype mules also marks the official launch of the development and testing phase for this model in the U.S. and will allow continuous road testing on various systems.

FF plans to unveil further updates on the FX 6 series and the latest FX strategy in March.
