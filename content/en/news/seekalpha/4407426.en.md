* Euronet [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20000928-euronet-reports-record-results-across-all-financial-metrics-for-the-fourth-quarter-and-full) (NASDAQ:[EEFT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/EEFT "Euronet Worldwide, Inc.")): Q4 Non-GAAP EPS of $2.08  beats by $0.02.
* Revenue of $1.05B (+9.6% Y/Y) in-line.
* Adjusted EBITDA of $165.8 million, a 12% increase from $147.6 million (13% increase on a constant currency basis).
* Euronet's cash and cash equivalents were $1,278.8 million and ATM cash was $643.8 million, totaling $1,922.6 million as of December 31, 2024, and availability under its revolving credit facilities was approximately $1,335 million.
* **Outlook:** Taking into consideration recent trends in the business and the global economy, the company anticipates its 2025 adjusted EPS will grow 12% to 16% year-over-year **vs. estimated growth of 12.71% Y/Y**, consistent with its 10 and 20 year compounded annualized growth rates.
