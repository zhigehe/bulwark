* ASE Technology (NYSE:[ASX](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ASX "ASE Technology Holding Co., Ltd.")) [said that](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996196-ase-technology-holding-co-ltd-announces-monthly-net-revenues "said that") its January revenue dropped 1.3% Y/Y to ~$1.51B, down 7.7% Q/Q.
* Revenue in its home currency was up 4.3% Y/Y, and down 6.5% sequentially to NT$ 49.44B.
* Net revenues for ATM assembly, testing, and material business were $857M, up 7% Y/Y, and down 6.9% Q/Q.
