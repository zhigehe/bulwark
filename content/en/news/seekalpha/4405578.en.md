* CompoSecure (NASDAQ:[CMPO](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CMPO "CompoSecure, Inc.")) on Monday approved a plan to spin-off its newly formed subsidiary, Resolute Holdings Management besides increasing the company's current share repurchase authorization from $40 million up to $100 million.
* Shares of Resolute Holdings are expected to trade on Nasdaq under the ticker “RHLD” with “when-issued” trading expected to commence on or about February 20, 2025, which is also set as the record date for CompoSecure shareholders to receive shares of Resolute Holdings.
* The distribution is expected to occur prior to the opening of trading on February 28, 2025.
* All CompoSecure shareholders as of the record date will receive one share of Resolute Holdings for every twelve shares of CompoSecure.
* The company expects to report net sales for the year ended December 31, 2024 of $420.6 million (in line with previous guidance of $418 to $424 million).
* Consensus for FY24 [revenue](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CMPO/earnings/estimates) is $422.23M.
* Net income is expected to be in the range of $(89.2) to $(87.7) million, and adjusted EBITDA is expected to be in the range of $149.4 to $151.4 million (in line with previous guidance of $148 to $151 million).
* The company expects to report its audited 2024 financial results and hold an investor conference call in late February or early March.
