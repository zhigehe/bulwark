WW International (NASDAQ:[WW](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/WW "WW International, Inc.")) fell 8.3% after advisers representing lenders to the firm signed confidentiality agreements to begin restructuring talks with the diet company.

The talks are at an early stage, according to a [WSJ report](https://www.wsj.com/articles/weightwatchers-lender-advisers-sign-confidentiality-agreement-for-restructuring-talks-fa41b91d " WSJ report ")on Thursday, which cited people familiar with the matter. The talks focus on understanding WW's business plan and financial forecasts to see the lenders' options in their discussions with the company.

Shares of WW (NASDAQ:[WW](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/WW "WW International, Inc.")) have plunged 80% over the past year as the entry of GLP-1 drugs such as Ozempic has hurt the company's sales.
