* Precision Drilling [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20000880-precision-drilling-announces-2024-fourth-quarter-and-year-end-unaudited-financial-results) (NYSE:[PDS](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PDS "Precision Drilling Corporation")): Q4 GAAP EPS of C$1.06.
* Revenue of C$468.17M (-7.6% Y/Y).
* Adjusted EBITDA was C$121 million in the quarter and included C$15 million of share-based compensation charges, $4 million for rig reactivation costs and C$4 million of non-recurring charges.
* For 2025, we expect to reduce debt by at least C$100 million in 2025 and have increased our long-term debt reduction target to C$700 million and extended our debt reduction period to 2027. In 2025, we plan to increase direct shareholder returns to 35% to 45% of free cash flow, before debt repayments. To the extent excess cash is generated, these allocations may be increased.
