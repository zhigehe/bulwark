Riot Platforms (NASDAQ:[RIOT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/RIOT "Riot Platforms, Inc.")) rose 2% amid some takeover speculation on the bitcoin miner.

Riot (NASDAQ:[RIOT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/RIOT "Riot Platforms, Inc.")) may have attracted takeover interest amid activist Starboard's reported stake, according to traders, who cited a Betaville "uncooked" alert circulating on Friday.

Privately held CoreWeave is said to be one of the companies circling RIOT, according to the report. 

The WSJ reported in December that activist Starboard [took a stake in RIOT.](https://tinyurl.com/3dx5v6rc " took a stake in RIOT. ")
