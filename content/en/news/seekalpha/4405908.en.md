* Rogers Communications (NYSE:[RCI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/RCI "Rogers Communications Inc.")) has priced a U.S. public offering of two series of U.S. dollar-denominated fixed-to-fixed rate subordinated notes with an aggregate principal amount of $2.1 billion.
* The offering comprises $1.1 billion of 7.0% fixed-to-fixed rate subordinated notes due 2055 and $1.0 billion of 7.125% fixed-to-fixed rate subordinated notes due 2055, [the company said.](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19997472-rogers-communications-inc-announces-pricing-of-public-offering-of-us-2_1-billion-fixed-to "the company said.")
* It also announced a Canadian private placement of $1.0 billion of 5.625% fixed-to-fixed rate subordinated notes due 2055.
