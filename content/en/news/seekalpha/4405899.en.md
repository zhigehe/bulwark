* Danaos [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19997440-danaos-corporation-reports-results-for-the-fourth-quarter-and-year-ended-december-31-2024) (NYSE:[DAC](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/DAC "Danaos Corporation")): Q4 Non-GAAP EPS of $6.93  beats by $0.29.
* Revenue of $258.18M (+3.6% Y/Y).
* Operating revenues of our container vessels segment decreased by 0.6%, or $1.4 million, to $237.5 million in the three months ended December 31, 2024 from $238.9 million in the three months ended December 31, 2023.
* Operating revenues of our drybulk vessels segment added an incremental $10.3 million of revenues in the three months ended December 31, 2024 compared to the three months ended December 31, 2023.
* Adjusted EBITDA increased by 9.9%, or $17.1 million, to $189.7 million in the three months ended December 31, 2024 from $172.6 million in the three months ended December 31, 2023.
* During the three months ended December 31, 2024, Danaos had an average of 72.9 container vessels and 10.0 drybulk vessels compared to 68.0 container vessels and 4.5 drybulk vessels during the three months ended December 31, 2023.
* Container vessels utilization for the three months ended December 31, 2024 was 96.4% compared to 97.7% in the three months ended December 31, 2023.
* Drybulk vessels utilization for the three months ended December 31, 2024 was 84.2% compared to 81.8% in the three months ended December 31, 2023.
