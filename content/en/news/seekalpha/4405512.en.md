* MediPharm Labs ([OTCQB:MEDIF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MEDIF "MediPharm Labs Corp.")) has terminated the previously announced sale of its facility in Napanee, Ontario to Kensana Health, it said Monday.
* The deal was to be completed through the sale of the company's wholly-owned subsidiary ABcann Medicinals to Kensana Health under the terms of a share purchase agreement.
* MediPharm Labs ([OTCQB:MEDIF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MEDIF "MediPharm Labs Corp.")) said Kensana Health did not meet certain agreed upon terms and conditions within the timelines set out.
* MediPharm Labs' current, commercial agreements and operations at the Napanee Facility, are expected to continue without disruption, [the company said.](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996371-medipharm-labs-announces-termination-of-the-proposed-sale-of-napanee-facility-to-kensana " the company said.")
* "Growing our profitable international revenue was a critical element of our success in 2024. We will continue to use the Napanee Facility to capitalize on international opportunities in 2025" said David Pidduck, CEO of MediPharm Labs.
