* Microbot Medical (NASDAQ:[MBOT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MBOT "Microbot Medical Inc.")) on Monday said it has entered into definitive agreements for the purchase and sale of 6.10 millions shares of the company’s common stock at $2.13 per share of common stock in a registered direct offering.
* In addition, in a concurrent private placement, the company will issue unregistered short-term series I preferred investment options to purchase up to 12.20 million shares of common stocks at $2.13 per share.
* The closing of the registered direct offering and the concurrent private placement is expected to occur on or about February 11, 2025.
* The gross proceeds to the company from the offerings are expected to be approximately $13 million.
* The company intends to use the net proceeds from the offerings for the continued development, commercialization and regulatory activities for the company’s LIBERTY Robotic System, expansion and development of additional applications derived from the Company’s existing IP portfolio, and for working capital and other general corporate purposes.
