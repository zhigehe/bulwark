* Kornit Digital [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999592-kornit-digital-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2024-results) (NASDAQ:[KRNT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/KRNT "Kornit Digital Ltd.")): Q4 Non-GAAP EPS of $0.18  misses by $0.02.
* Revenue of $60.7M (+7.2% Y/Y)  beats by $0.31M.
* Adjusted EBITDA for the fourth quarter of 2024 was $8.4 million compared with adjusted EBITDA of $0.2 million for the fourth quarter of 2023. Adjusted EBITDA margin for the fourth quarter of 2024 was 13.8% compared with 0.3% for the fourth quarter of 2023.
* **First Quarter 2025 Guidance**
  
  For the first quarter of 2025, the company expects revenues to be in the range of $45.5 million to $49.5 million **vs. $46.47M consensus** and adjusted EBITDA margin between –9% to –4% of revenue.
