Super Micro Computer (NASDAQ:[SMCI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SMCI "Super Micro Computer, Inc.")) [priced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20000933-supermicro-announces-pricing-of-private-placement-of-700_0-million-of-new-2_25-percent "priced") $700M aggregate principal amount of newly issued 2.25% convertible senior notes due 2028

The company also announced the amended pricing terms of its existing $1.725B aggregate convertible senior notes due 2029.

The amendments are expected to be effective, and the subscription is expected to close on or about February 20, 2025.

The company will receive gross proceeds of ~$700M and intends to use such proceeds for general corporate purposes, including to fund working capital for growth and business expansion.

It also announced the amended convertible notes will bear an annual interest rate of 3.50%, payable semi-annually on each March 1 and September 1, beginning on September 1, 2025.
