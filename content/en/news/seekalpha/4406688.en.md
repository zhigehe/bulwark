* Ares Commercial Real Estate (NYSE:[ACRE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ACRE "Ares Commercial Real Estate Corporation")) declares [$0.15/share quarterly dividend](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999403-ares-commercial-real-estate-corporation-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2024-results), -40% decrease from prior dividend of $0.25.
* [Forward yield](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ACRE/dividends/yield?source=news_bullet) 9.85%
* Payable April 15; for shareholders of record March 31; ex-div March 31.
* “In 2025, we remain squarely focused on further addressing our remaining underperforming loans and REOs and have elected to lower our quarterly dividend to $0.15 per share as our earnings during the year are expected to be impacted by our more flexible balance sheet position,” said Bryan Donohoe, Chief Executive Officer of Ares Commercial Real Estate Corporation.
