* Northern Trust Wealth Management (NASDAQ:[NTRS](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NTRS "Northern Trust Corporation")) has announced two leadership changes: Glenda Pedroso will become Head of Banking and Specialized Services, and Michael Bracci will be named President of the [East Region](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996506-northern-trust-wealth-management-announces-leadership-changes).
* In addition, these changes will create expanded roles for two regional leaders. Alexander Adams, Group Managing Director for South Florida, will add the Mid-Atlantic Region to his responsibilities. Stacey Hallberg, Senior Managing Director for the Boca Raton and Delray Beach offices, will become Group Managing Director for East Florida.
* The changes will be effective March 1, 2025.
