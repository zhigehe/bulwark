* Rua Gold (TSXV:[RUA:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/RUA:CA "Rua Gold Inc.")) has signed an agreement with Cormark Securities to act as the sole agent for a public offering of 8.33M common shares at C$0.60 each, raising C$5M in gross proceeds.
* The offering is expected to close on or about February 20, 2025.
* The company has given the agent a 30-day option to sell up to 1.25M additional shares at the offering price for market stabilization and over-allotments.
* The company intends to use the net proceeds from the offering for continuing the exploration program on its Reefton Project, and for general working capital and general corporate purposes.
