* Strategy (NASDAQ:[MSTR](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MSTR "Strategy")), formerly known as MicroStrategy, disclosed it [acquired](https://seekingalpha.com/filing/9529094 "acquired") ~7,633 Bitcoins ([BTC-USD](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BTC-USD "Bitcoin USD")) for about $742.4M in cash in the week ended February 9.
* The firm acquired the BTC at an average price of ~97.3K apiece, and the purchases were made using proceeds from the issuance and sale of shares under the sales agreement and the STRK offering.
* As of February 9, the company, along with its units, held ~478,740 BTC.
* MSTR shares were up 2% in premarket trade.
