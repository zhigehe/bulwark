* Atai Life Sciences (NASDAQ:[ATAI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ATAI "Atai Life Sciences N.V.")) is down ~18% in after-hours trading Wednesday after announcing a proposed offering of $55M worth of common stock.
* Berenberg, the sole bookrunner, has been given a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional $8.25M worth of shares.
* The developer of psychedelic-based drug treatments for mental illnesses [said](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20000539-atai-life-sciences-announces-proposed-public-offering-of-common-shares "said") that the proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, such as working capital, and to advance candidates in the pipeline.
