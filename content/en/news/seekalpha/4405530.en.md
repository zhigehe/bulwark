ON Semiconductor (NASDAQ:[ON](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ON "ON Semiconductor Corporation")) dropped in premarket trading on Monday as the Arizona-based chipmaker forecasted current quarter guidance below estimates, while also falling short of fourth-quarter expectations.

Global semiconductor sales are [expected to slow](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4397269-semiconductor-revenue-growth-slows-to-single-digits-in-2025-susquehanna "expected to slow") to mid-single digits in 2025, marking a steep fall from the 20% growth seen in 2024.

For the ongoing first quarter, ON Semiconductor's [guided](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996448-onsemi-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2024-results "guided") non-GAAP diluted per-share earnings to be between $0.45 and $0.55, with the midpoint being $0.50, compared to the average Wall Street estimate of $0.90.

Similarly, ON Semiconductor's quarterly first-quarter revenue expectation of $1.35 billion to $1.45 billion was also well below the Wall Street forecast of $1.69 billion.

"While 2025 remains uncertain, we remain committed to our long-term strategy," said President and CEO Hassane El-Khoury.

Shares of ON Semiconductor (NASDAQ:[ON](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ON "ON Semiconductor Corporation")) fell 4.41% to $51.25 by 0814 GMT. Peers Analog Devices ([ADI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ADI "Analog Devices, Inc.")) traded 0.6% lower, while Texas Instruments ([TXN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TXN "Texas Instruments Incorporated")) and NXP Semiconductors ([NXPI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NXPI "NXP Semiconductors N.V.")) were 0.3% and 0.5% higher, respectively.

Last month, Netherlands-based chipmaker STMicroelectronics ([STM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/STM "STMicroelectronics N.V.")) also posted a first-quarter revenue forecast that [was below consensus](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4400531-stmicroelectronics-falls-after-weak-q1-outlook "was below consensus"). Shares of ([STM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/STM "STMicroelectronics N.V.")) remained unmoved on Monday.

For the fourth quarter, ON Semiconductor [reported](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4405513-on-semiconductor-non-gaap-eps-of-0_95-misses-by-0_02-revenue-of-1_73b-misses-by-30m "reported") non-GAAP EPS of $0.95 missing estimates by $0.02. Quarterly revenue fell 14.6% to $1.73 billion, about $30 million lower than consensus.
