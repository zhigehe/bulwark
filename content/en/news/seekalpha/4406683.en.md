Barrick Gold (NYSE:[GOLD](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GOLD "Barrick Gold Corporation")) plans to undertake a new share repurchase program to purchase up to $1.0 billion of its outstanding common shares over the next 12 months.

In connection with the new program, Barrick has terminated the share repurchase plan announced on February 14, 2024.

The company repurchased $498M in common shares under its 2024 share repurchase program.

GOLD +1.35% premarket to $17.3.
