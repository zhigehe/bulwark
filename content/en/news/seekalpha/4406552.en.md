* Lockheed Martin (NYSE:[LMT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LMT "Lockheed Martin Corporation")) has been [awarded](https://www.defense.gov/News/Contracts/Contract/Article/4063099/) a $114.2M modification to a previously awarded U.S. Navy contract to exercise options for the Navy’s procurement of hardware to support sound navigation and ranging systems.
* Work will be performed in Virginia, Florida, and New York and is expected to be completed by May 2031.
* The Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
