* Beyond Medical Technologies ([OTCPK:DOCKF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/DOCKF "Beyond Medical Technologies Inc.")) on Tuesday said it intends to complete a non-brokered private placement of up to 7.5M units at a price of $0.05 per unit for aggregate gross proceeds of up to $375,000.
* Each unit will consist of one common share in the capital of the company and one common share purchase warrant. Each warrant will entitle the holder thereof to purchase one share at a price of $0.075 per share for a period of 24 months from the date of issuance.
* The company intends to use the net proceeds of the offering to settle outstanding debt and for general and administrative expenses.
