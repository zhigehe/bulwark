Dreamworks Animation’s “Dog Man” was the top earner for the second time at the box office this weekend, according to studio estimates released Sunday.

The film, based on the graphic novel series, sold $13.7M in tickets, while Valentine’s Day-themed horror movie "Heart Eyes” came in second with $8.5M. Action movie “Love Hurts” finished third with $5.8M. Disney’s “Mufasa: The Lion King” and “Companion” round out the Top 5 with $3.9M and $3M, respectively. 

The box office should heat up next week as Marvel’s “Captain America: Brave New World” and Sony’s “Paddington in Peru” open in Hollywood’s biggest weekend so far this year.

Estimated ticket sales for Friday through Sunday at U.S. and Canadian theaters, according to Comscore:

1. “Dog Man,” $13.7M

2. “Heart Eyes,” $8.5M

3. “Love Hurts,” $5.8M

4. “Mufasa: The Lion King,” $3.9M

5. “Companion,” $3M

6. “One of Them Days,” $3M

7. “Becoming Led Zeppelin,” $2.6M

8. “Flight Risk,” $2.6M

9. “Sonic the Hedgehog,” $1.8M

10. “Moana 3,” $1.5M
