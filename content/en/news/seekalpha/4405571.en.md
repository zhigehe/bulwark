* Alterity Therapeutics (NASDAQ:[ATHE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ATHE "Alterity Therapeutics Limited")) on Monday said it has received binding commitments to raise A$40 million via a two tranche placement of fully paid ordinary shares to Australian and international institutions and other unrelated sophisticated, professional or exempt investors.
* The company said that the placement was fully subscribed and was conducted at A$0.011 per share. For every three new shares issued, one free attaching option will be issued with an exercise price of A$0.028 and an expiry date on 26 February 2027.
* Tranche one of the placement is to raise about A$12.8 million via the issue of 1.2 billion new shares on or about 17 February 2025.
* Tranche two of the placement is expected to raise around A$27.2 million via the issue of 2.5 billion new shares. Tranche Two of the placement includes an aggregate of A$0.15 million of commitments from related parties
* The company said the use of proceeds from this financing will provide a strong balance sheet to fund ongoing clinical development programs for ATH434, including planned advancements in MSA, continuing discovery and research efforts in neurodegenerative diseases, including Parkinsonâ€™s Disease, and general working capital.
