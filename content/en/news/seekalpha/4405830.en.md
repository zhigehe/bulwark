* Hamilton Lane (NASDAQ:[HLNE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HLNE "Hamilton Lane Incorporated")) has commenced an underwritten public offering of 1.57M shares of Class A common stock, to be sold by Hamilton Lane and a selling stockholder.
* Hamilton Lane intends to use the proceeds from the sale to settle in cash exchanges of membership units in Hamilton Lane Advisors held by certain of its members.
* The company will not receive any proceeds from the sale of shares by the selling stockholder.
* HLNE shares fell 3% post-market on Monday.
