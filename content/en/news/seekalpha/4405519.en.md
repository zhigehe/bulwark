* Hydrofarm Holdings Group (NASDAQ:[HYFM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HYFM "Hydrofarm Holdings Group, Inc.")) on Monday approved a 1-for-10 reverse stock split of its common stock, to comply with the minimum bid price requirement of $1.00 per share of the company’s common stock for continued listing on The Nasdaq Capital Market.
* The reverse stock split will be effective from 5:00 pm eastern time on February 12, 2025.
* The reverse stock split will reduce the number of shares of common stock issued and outstanding from 46,144,512 to approximately 4,614,451. The total authorized number of shares will not be reduced.
* HYFM -9.50% premarket to $0.5645
