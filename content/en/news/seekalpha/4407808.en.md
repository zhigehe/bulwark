* Pernod Ricard [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20001120-pernod-ricard-approval-of-the-half-year-financial-results-and-filing-of-the-half-year) ([OTCPK:PRNDY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PRNDY "Pernod Ricard SA")): 1H Sales totaled €6,176m, an organic decline of -4% and -6% reported.
* Earnings Per Share in decline of -11% at €5.06.
* Free Cash Flow at €440m, +€139m vs H1 FY24.
* Net debt up €1,099m vs. 30 June 2024 to €12,050m.
* **FY25 Outlook and medium-term update**
* Anticipating low single digit decline in Organic Net Sales for FY25.
* From FY27 to FY29, projecting stronger Organic Net Sales growth, aiming for a range, on average of +3% to +6%, accompanied with Organic Operating Margin expansion.
