Kroger (NYSE:[KR](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/KR "The Kroger Co.")) has tapped David Kennerley as its next chief financial officer, effective April 3.

Kennerley, currently senior vice president and CFO for PepsiCo Europe, will join Kroger on March 10 as senior vice president, succeeding Todd Foley who will continue as interim CFO through the end of Kroger's fiscal year 2024 reporting cycle.

Foley joined Kroger in 2001 as an audit manager in the Internal Audit Department. He stepped into the interim CFO role in 2024.
