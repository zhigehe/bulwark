Northpointe Bancshares ([NPB](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NPB "Northpointe Bancshares, Inc.")) a Michigan corporation and the bank holding company for Northpointe Bank, a wholly owned subsidiary and a Michigan non-member bank, [priced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20002655-northpointe-bancshares-inc-northpointe-announced-today-the-pricing-of-its-initial-public "priced") 10.42M shares at a price to the public of $14.50 per share, including 8.62M shares offered by Northpointe and 1.8M shares by certain shareholders of Northpointe.

The underwriters will have a 30-day option to purchase up to 2,574 additional shares of common stock from Northpointe and ~1.56M additional shares of common stock from the selling shareholders, at the initial public offering price.

The shares of Northpointe common stock are expected to begin trading on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol “NPB” on February 14, 2025.

The offering is expected to close on February 18, 2025.

Northpointe will receive net proceeds of approximately $114.4M and intends to use the net proceeds from this offering for general corporate purposes, which may include growing its existing lines of business or using a portion to redeem all, or a portion, of its preferred stock.
