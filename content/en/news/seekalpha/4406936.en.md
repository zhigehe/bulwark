Dentsply SIrona (NASDAQ:[XRAY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XRAY "DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc.")) earlier ticked higher by 2% amid some speculation that it may be the target of an activist investor.

The speculation comes as Dentsply (NASDAQ:[XRAY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XRAY "DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc.")) said Tuesday it started a process to [evaluate strategic alternatives](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4406100-dentsply-sirona-initiates-review-of-strategic-alternatives-for-wellspect-healthcare " evaluate strategic alternatives ") for its Wellspect Healthcare business. Gordon Haskett highlighted in a note on Wednesday that the review announcement comes 10 days before the nomination window for XRAY's board closes.

Gordon Haskett's Don Bilson also pointed out that Dentsply ([XRAY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XRAY "DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc.")) named [two new directors](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19994143-dentsply-sirona-appoints-michael-barber-and-daniel-scavilla-as-new-board-members " two new directors ") last week. A potential activist could target the supplier of dental products, as Dentsply shares dropped 47% last year.

Separately, late last month Dentsply ([XRAY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XRAY "DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc.")) competitor Henry Schein ([HSIC](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HSIC "Henry Schein, Inc.")) ) announced a [strategic investment](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4399885-henry-schein-stock-gains-kkr-builds-stake " strategic investment ") from funds affiliated with private equity firm KKR.

Dentsply ([XRAY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XRAY "DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc.")) is set to [report Q4 results on Feb. 27.](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19982064-dentsply-sirona-to-host-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2024-conference-call-on-february-27th "report Q4 results on Feb. 27. ")
