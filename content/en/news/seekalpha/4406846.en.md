Chimera Investment (NYSE:[CIM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CIM "Chimera Investment Corporation")) stock slid 9.0% in Wednesday morning trading after the mortgage REIT's Q4 earnings fell short of the average analyst estimate.

[Q4 EPS available for distribution](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19999470-correcting-and-replacing-chimera-investment-corporation-reports-4th-quarter-2024-earnings "Q4 EPS available for distribution") of $0.37, missing the $0.38 consensus, rose from $0.36 in Q3 and fell from $0.40 in Q4 2023.

GAAP book value of $19.72 per common share at Dec. 31, 2024, fell from $22.35 at Sept. 30.

Q4 economic net interest income of $69.2M vs. $71.5 in the previous quarter and $68.3M a year ago.

"In 2024, we made progress on our initiative towards building a more durable and diversified portfolio. Chimera's press release and investor presentation didn't break out much in the Q4 results. Here are some of its FY2024 metrics:

FY 2024 net interest income of $264.7M, missing the Visible Alpha consensus of $273.1M, compared with $263.4M in the prior year.

Its increase in provision for credit losses declined to $9.84M in FY2024 from $11.4M in Q3.

Total assets of $13.1B at Dec. 31, 2024, rose from $12.9B at Dec. 31, 2023.

FY2024 economic return grew to 4.40% vs. -0.53% in FY 2023.
