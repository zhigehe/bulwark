Military startup Anduril is in discussions for a new funding round that could double its valuation to $28 billion.

The drone maker may raise as much as $2.5 billion in a Series G founding round, according to a CNBC report on Friday, which cited people familiar with the matter. The round is led by a $1 billion investment from Founders Fund. Anduril's revenue doubled to $1 billion in 2024, according to the report. 

Anduril and Palantir Technologies ([PLTR](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/PLTR "Palantir Technologies Inc.")) in December [formed a consortium](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4395801-ai-military-startup-anduril-set-to-build-1b-factory-in-ohio "formed a consortium") to advance AI capabilities for the U.S. military and security agencies.
