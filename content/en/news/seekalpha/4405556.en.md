* Converge Technology Solutions ([OTCQX:CTSDF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CTSDF "Converge Technology Solutions Corp.")) ([CTS:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CTS:CA "Converge Technology Solutions Corp.")) announced on Monday Q4 revenue of ~$680.8M, an increase of 4.6% year-over-year.
* Revenue for the full-year is estimated to be ~$2.6B, down 4.2% from last year.
* The company expects Q4 adjusted EBITDA to stand at about $47.9M (+3.0% Y/Y) and full-year adjusted EBITDA of roughly $167.3M (-1.7% Y/Y).
