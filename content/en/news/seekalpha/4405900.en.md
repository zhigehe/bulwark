* Odysight.ai ([OTCPK:ODYS](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ODYS "Odysight.ai Inc.")) [priced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19997465-odysight-ai-announces-the-pricing-of-21_5-million-public-offering-and-uplisting-to-the-nasdaq) a public offering of ~3.31M shares at a price to the public of $6.50 per share.
* The sole book-running manager of the offering will have a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 496,153 shares from Odysight.ai at the public offering price, less underwriting discounts and commissions.
* Odysight.ai’s common stock has been approved for listing and is expected to begin trading on the Nasdaq under the symbol “ODYS” on Tuesday, February 11, 2025.
* The offering is expected to close on February 12, 2025.
* The gross proceeds are expected to be ~$21.5M.
* Net proceeds will be used for expanded research and development, increased sales and marketing, working capital and other general corporate purposes.
