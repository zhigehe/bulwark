* Buhler Industries (TSX:[BUI:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BUI:CA "Buhler Industries Inc.")) on Wednesday said it has executed a definitive amalgamation agreement with ASKO Sinai ve Teknoloji Üretim Sanayi Ticaret Anonim Şirketi and a wholly-owned subsidiary of ASKO, Newco, pursuant to which Buhler Industries will be taken private by way of an amalgamation.
* ASKO owns 24.18M Class A common shares, representing about 96.7% of Buhler's 25M issued and outstanding common shares.
* Under the agreement, Buhler and Newco would amalgamate and continue as one company Amalco.
* Pursuant to the amalgamation each common share of Buhler owned by public shareholders other than Newco will be converted into one redeemable preferred share of Amalco and each Amalco redeemable preferred share would then be redeemed by Amalco for CDN$7.30.
