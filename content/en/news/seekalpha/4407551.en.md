* Operating cash flow was $168 million; Adjusted free cash flow was $155 million, representing 128% Adjusted free cash flow conversion.
* **Outlook for 2025:**
  
  + Initiates Q1 2025 adjusted diluted net EPS guidance in the range of $0.71 to $0.74 **vs. $0.74 consensus.**
  + Initiates full year 2025 adjusted diluted net EPS guidance in the range of $3.00 to $3.15 **vs. $3.13 consensus**.
