* The Alkaline Water Company ([OTC:WTER](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/WTER "The Alkaline Water Company Inc.")) announced the signing of a letter of intent with FZA Note Buyers, a Florida limited liability company, for a strategic equipment acquisition proposed to be fulfilled through the issuance of 34M shares of common stock subject to a 12-month lock-up period.
* Following closing, the Company plans a rapid 30-day implementation timeline for equipment integration into its production infrastructure.
