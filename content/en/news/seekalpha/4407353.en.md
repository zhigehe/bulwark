* Corsair Gaming (NASDAQ:[CRSR](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CRSR "Corsair Gaming, Inc.")) announced the planned retirement of Andy Paul, founder and chief executive officer, from the company and its board effective July 1, 2025.
* As part of the planned transition, the board appointed Thi La, currently Corsair’s president and chief operating officer, as chief executive, effective July 1, 2025.
* La oversaw the expansion of the company’s product portfolio from a small range of DIY components, adding many gaming and streaming solutions to 30 different product lines.
* CRSR shares fall 6.8% post-market on Wednesday.
