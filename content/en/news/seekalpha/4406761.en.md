Vertiv Holdings’ (NYSE:[VRT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/VRT "Vertiv Holdings Co")) stock fell 9.3% in premarket trading Wednesday after the provider of power and cooling systems for data centers provided disappointing guidance.

The company estimated earnings adjusted for one-time items will be $0.57 a share to $0.63 a share for the first quarter of 2025, less than the consensus estimate of $0.64 a share.

Management estimated that net sales will be $1.90 billion to $1.95 billion in the quarter, compared with the average estimate of $1.92 billion.

For the full-year ahead, Vertiv (NYSE:[VRT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/VRT "Vertiv Holdings Co")) foresees net sales of $9.125 billion to $9.275 billion, and adjusted earnings of $3.50 to $3.60 share. Analysts estimate net sales of $9.25 billion and adjusted earnings of $3.57 a share.

The company's stock doubled in price in the 12 months through February 11.
