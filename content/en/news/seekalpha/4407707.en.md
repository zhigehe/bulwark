* Reklaim ([OTCPK:MYIDF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MYIDF "Reklaim Ltd.")) on Thursday announced an 18% reduction in overall debt, fully funded by existing cash flow.
* Separate from the debt reduction, Reklaim has completed a non-brokered private placement of convertible debenture units totalling $465,400 to accredited investors, replacing an existing outstanding facility.
* The debentures accrue interest at a rate of 12% per annum, are payable semi-annually, and mature in 2027.
* The company has also agreed to issue 16,380 common shares at a price of C$0.11 per share to certain platform users in Canada who participated in Reklaim's beta testing for a new product feature. The total value of this settlement is C$1,800.
