* SATO Technologies (TSXV:[SATO:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/SATO:CA "SATO Technologies Corp.")) on Monday said that the bitcoin computing organization earned eight bitcoins in January, down from nine in December 2024.
* BTC produced per EH/s stood at 18.53 for the month.
* The company reported January mining revenue of $750,768, down from $861,094 in the prior month.
* A total of 13 bitcoins were sold in January, with the holdings coming to 28.
* Average operating hashrate was 0.41 EH/s, compared to December's 0.47 EH/s.
