* Eutelsat Communications [press release](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20002699-eutelsat-communications-second-quarter-and-first-half-2024minus-25-results) ([OTCPK:EUTLF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/EUTLF "Eutelsat Group")): 1H Revenue of €606.2M (+5.9% Y/Y).
* Operating verticals revenues of €600m up 3.9% like-for-like.
* Adjusted EBITDA margin of 55.2%.
* First Half Video revenues were down by 6.4% to €309.2 million, in line with the broader secular market decline.
* Total Connectivity revenues for the First Half of FY 2024-25 stood at €290.7 million, up by 21.1% on a reported basis and up by 17.8% like-for-like.
* First Half Fixed Connectivity revenues stood at €118.9 million, up 22.2% year-on-year.
* First Half Government Services revenues stood at €96.4 million, up by 21.9% year-on-year.
* First Half Mobile Connectivity revenues stood at €75.3 million, up 7.1% year-on-year.
* The backlog stood at €3.7 billion on 31 December 2024 versus €3.9 billion a year earlier.
* FY 2024–25 objectives confirmed; Gross capital expenditure in FY 2024-25 initially expected in a range of €700-800 million euros is now expected c.€200 lower, in a range of €500-600 million euros.
* Eutelsat also continues to target leverage of c.3x in the medium term.
