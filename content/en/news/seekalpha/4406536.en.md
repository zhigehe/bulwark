* Thomson Reuters (NYSE:[TRI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TRI "Thomson Reuters Corporation")) has launched an exchange offer to swap its outstanding old notes for new notes issued by its subsidiary TR Finance, with identical terms and full guarantees from TRC and its U.S. subsidiaries.
* The exchange offers aim to optimize capital structure and align revenue generation with indebtedness, offering bondholders the same financial terms and similar covenants.
* The exchange period began on February 11, 2025, and will expire on March 17, 2025, with early tenderers receiving $1,000 in new notes plus a $2.50 consent fee per $1,000 of old notes.
* The Holders tendering old notes will receive new notes with the same interest rate, maturity, and redemption terms, fully guaranteed by TRC and subsidiary guarantors.
