David Tepper's Appaloosa LP took a new 1.5M share position in Corning Inc. ([GLW](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/GLW "Corning Incorporated")) during the last quarter of 2024, the hedge fund disclosed in a 13F filing on Monday, while closing out its Adobe ([ADBE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/ADBE "Adobe Inc.")) stake.

The firm further boosted its holdings in several Chinese tech stocks, including Alibaba ([BABA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BABA "Alibaba Group Holding Limited")), to 11.8M shares from 10M shares, JD.com ([JD](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/JD "JD.com, Inc.")), to 10.5M from 7.3M, and KE Holdings ([KE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/KE "Kimball Electronics, Inc.")), to 2.57M from 2.18M. Stakes were also increased in Nvidia ([NVDA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NVDA "NVIDIA Corporation")), to 680K from 625K, Uber Technologies ([UBER](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/UBER "Uber Technologies, Inc.")), to 1.5M from 1.4M, and Advanced Micro Devices ([AMD](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/AMD "Advanced Micro Devices, Inc.")), to 1.2M from 1.1M.

Decreased positions include Meta Platforms ([META](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/META "Meta Platforms, Inc.")), to 490K shares from 625K shares, Lyft ([LYFT](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/LYFT "Lyft, Inc.")), to 13.5M from 15.8M, Intel ([INTC](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/INTC "Intel Corporation")), to 1M from 2.5M, and Amazon ([AMZN](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/AMZN "Amazon.com, Inc.")), to 2.6M from 3.2M, the [filing](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1656456/000165645625000001/xslForm13F_X02/Form13FInfoTable.xml "filing") showed.
