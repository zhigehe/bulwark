Digital Brands Group ([OTC:DBGI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/DBGI "Digital Brands Group, Inc.")) [priced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20002661-dbgi-announces-pricing-of-7_5-million-public-offering-of-units-of-common-stock-and-or-pre "priced") its public offering of an aggregate of 11.36M units, of which each unit includes a share of common stock and/or pre-funded warrant to purchase common stock, plus two common stock purchase warrants, at a public offering price of $0.66 per unit.

The gross proceeds are expected to be $7.5M.

DBG intends to use the net proceeds of the offering for working capital, general corporate purposes, and the repayment of debt.

The offering is expected to close on February 18, 2025.
