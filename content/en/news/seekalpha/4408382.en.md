EzFill Holdings (NASDAQ:[EZFL](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/EZFL "NextNRG Inc.")) has announced the [pricing of a public offering](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20002641-ezfill-holdings-announces-pricing-of-15-million-public-offering-and-closing-of-share-exchange "pricing of a public offering") of 5 million shares of its common stock at $3.00 per share, for gross proceeds of approximately $15 million.

Underwriters' over-allotment is an additional 750K shares of common stock at the public offering price.

EzFill plans to use the net proceeds from the offering to expand its business, repay outstanding indebtedness, and general corporate purposes, including working capital.

The company expects to close the offering on February 18, 2025.

Furthermore, the company has closed its share exchange agreement with NextNRG Holding Corp, changing its name to "NextNRG, Inc." Effective February 14, 2025, the company's common stock will begin trading on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the ticker symbol "NXXT" and new CUSIP number 652941105.

Stock price of EZFL shares were down 2.20% after-hours on Thursday.
