Thomson Reuters (NYSE:[TRI](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TRI "Thomson Reuters Corporation")) will voluntarily transfer its U.S. stock exchange listing to the Nasdaq Global Select Market from the New York Stock Exchange.

The company’s common shares will likely cease trading on the NYSE at market close on or about February 24, 2025. Its common shares will commence trading on the Nasdaq on or about February 25, 2025.

Thomson Reuters joined the New York Stock Exchange on April 17, 2008, under the ticker symbol "TRI" after the merger of Thomson Corporation and Reuters Group.

The listing on the Toronto Stock Exchange will not be impacted.
