* Revenue of $409M (-22.5% Y/Y)  misses by $5.71M.
* **Outlook for the full-year of 2025**:
  
  + Public cloud ARR growth of 14% to 18% year-over-year, in constant currency
  + Total ARR in the range of flat to 2% year-over-year, in constant currency
  + Recurring revenue in the range of -3% to -5% year-over-year, in constant currency
  + Total revenue in the range of -4% to -6% year-over-year, in constant currency
  + GAAP diluted EPS is expected to be in the range of $1.05 to $1.15 per share
  + Non-GAAP diluted EPS is expected to be in the range of $2.15 to $2.25 per share
  + Cash flow from operations of $270 million to $300 million
  + Free cash flow of $250 million to $280 million
  
  **Outlook for the first quarter of 2025:**
  
  + Recurring revenue in the range of -4% to -6% year-over-year, in constant currency
  + Total revenue in the range of -6% to -8% year-over-year, in constant currency
  + GAAP diluted EPS is expected to be in the range of $0.33 to $0.37 per share
  + Non-GAAP diluted EPS is expected to be in the range of $0.55 to $0.59 per share
