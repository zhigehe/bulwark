Trend Micro ([OTCPK:TMICY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TMICY "Trend Micro Incorporated")) ADRs jumped 15% on a report that several private equity firms are interested in a takeover of the company.

Advent International, Bain Capital, and EQT have expressed interest in a possible Trend Micro ([OTCPK:TMICY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TMICY "Trend Micro Incorporated")) deal in recent weeks, according to a R[euters report](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/exclusive-buyout-firms-vie-cybersecurity-003204122.html "euters report ") on Wednesday, which cited people familiar with the mater. KKR ([KKR](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/KKR "KKR & Co. Inc.")) is also said to be interested. 

Trend Micro didn't immediately respond to Reuters requests for comment.

Reuters reported in August that Japanese cybersecurity firm Trend Micro ([OTCPK:TMICY](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TMICY "Trend Micro Incorporated")) was evaluating a sale after [receiving takeover interest.](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4137398-cybersecurity-firm-trend-micro-exploring-sale-amid-takeover-interest-reuters "receiving takeover interest. ")
