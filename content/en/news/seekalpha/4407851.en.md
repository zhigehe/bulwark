* Mynaric (NASDAQ:[MYNA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MYNA "Mynaric AG")) has received a delisting notification dated Feb. 10, 2025 from the Listing Qualifications Department of [The Nasdaq](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20001937-mynaric-announces-receipt-of-delisting-notice-from-nasdaq).
* The Delisting Notice also advises Mynaric of its right to appeal Nasdaq's determination pursuant to procedures set forth in the Nasdaq Listing Rule 5800 Series.
* Mynaric does not intend to pursue an appeal.
* Trading of the ADS will be suspended at the opening of business on Feb. 18, 2025.
* MYNA is -39.41% to $0.38.
