* Manhattan Associates (NASDAQ:[MANH](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MANH "Manhattan Associates, Inc.")) [said](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996810-manhattan-associates-announces-ceo-succession) on Monday that Eddie Capel, Manhattan’s President and CEO, will retire from his position effective February 12, 2025.
* He will continue to serve Manhattan in the role of Executive Vice-Chairman of the board, assisting with CEO transition and special projects.
* Capel will be succeeded by Eric Clark, who has been serving as CEO of NTT Data North America. Clark will also join the Manhattan Board.
* Clark joined NTT Data Services in 2018 and in October 2022, he became CEO of NTT Ltd. Americas. Earlier in his career, Clark held numerous global, senior leadership positions with ServiceNow, Dell, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, Arthur Andersen Business Consulting, Ernst & Young and Bank of America.
