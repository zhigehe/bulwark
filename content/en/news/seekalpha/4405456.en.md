Foxconn Technology's ([OTCPK:FXCOF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/FXCOF "Foxconn Technology Co., Ltd.")) January revenue rose 3.16% year-over-year, and the company expects strong growth in the first quarter, compared to the year-ago period.

**First Quarter 2025 Outlook**: The Apple ([AAPL](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/AAPL "Apple Inc.")) supplier — which is formally known as Hon Hai Precision ([OTCPK:HNHAF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HNHAF "Hon Hai Precision Industry Co., Ltd.")) ([OTCPK:HNHPF](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/HNHPF "Hon Hai Precision Industry Co., Ltd.")) — [said](https://image.honhai.com/upload/financy_by_month/Hon%20Hai%20Monthly%20Revenue%20Report_January_2025_EN_20250210_4441.pdf "said") even though fourth quarter revenue of 2024 reached a record high and despite the onset of the traditional off-season, current visibility for the first quarter is better than the previous month

The Taiwan-based company added that sequential performance of the current quarter is expected to be better than the average level of the past five years; and on a year-on-year basis, growthwill be strong.

January Revenue: Foxconn said revenue grew 3.16% year-over-year to NT$538.7B, reflecting the second-highest figure for the same period on record (January 2023 ranked the highest with NT$660.4B).

However, month-over-month the revenue fell 17.74% as the company had generated NT$654.8B in December 2024.

**Month-over-month**: Foxconn said Cloud and Networking Products segment showed significant growth. With strong demand from AI servers, revenue experienced significant growth month-over-month, according to the company.

Nvidia ([NVDA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NVDA "NVIDIA Corporation")) is among the company's prominent customers.

Foxconn noted that while Components and Other Products, Computing Products and Smart Consumer Electronics declined compared to the previous month.

**Year-over-year**: The company noted that Cloud and Networking Products segment showed strong growth due to strong demand from AI servers. Revenue from Components and Other Products demonstrated strong growth year-over-year because of an increase in shipments of keycomponents and growth in automotive-related business.

Meanwhile, Computing Products was flattish, and Smart Consumer Electronics declinedcompared to the same period a year ago.
