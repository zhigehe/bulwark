* Callan JMB (NASDAQ:[CJMB](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CJMB "Callan JMB Inc.")) has been awarded a second five-year contract by the Oregon Health Authority for medical emergency preparedness and response services.
* The new contract involves Callan JMB’s services for storing and managing a broad inventory of medical countermeasures to meet all new threat assessments and event response missions in the State of Oregon.
* CJMB shares rise nearly 6% premarket on Tuesday.
