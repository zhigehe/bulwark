* North Peak Resources (TSXV:[NPR:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NPR:CA "North Peak Resources Ltd.")) has announced the appointment of [Rupert Williams as CEO](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/19996468-north-peak-announces-ceo-transition) and as a director of the Co.
* Brian Hinchcliffe, who had been acting as the Co.'s CEO, will remain as the Co.'s Executive Chairman and on the Board of Directors of the Co.
* "Following our successful drilling campaign at Prospect Mountain last year, we have exciting plans for 2025 and by welcoming Rupert Williams, with his strong financing experience, management is well positioned to drive the Co. forward," commented, Brian Hinchcliffe, Co. Chairman.
