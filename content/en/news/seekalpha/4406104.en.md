* BIOREM (TSXV:[BRM:CA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/BRM:CA "BioRem Inc.")) on Tuesday said it has received several new orders totalling more than $8 million.
* "A good mix of infrastructure and industrial applications make up the bulk of the new orders," President and Chief Executive Officer Derek S. Webb said.
* The orders will see BIOREM implement its leading-edge air emission abatement technologies for a range of new and repeat clients in various locations across Asia and the Americas, the company added.
* BIOREM's total order backlog stands at $60.5 million, the company said.
