* For January 2025, Copa Holdings (NYSE:[CPA](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CPA "Copa Holdings, S.A.")) capacity (available seat miles) increased by 22.1%, while system-wide passenger traffic (revenue passenger miles) increased by 23.6%, compared to 2024.
* The system load factor for the month was 86.4%, 1.1 percentage points higher than in January 2024.
* These results include the effect of the partial grounding of the Company's Boeing 737 MAX-9 fleet in January 2024.
