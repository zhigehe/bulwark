* TEN Holdings ([XHLD](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/XHLD "TEN Holdings, Inc.")) on Thursday [announced the pricing of its initial public offering](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20000902-ten-holdings-inc-announces-pricing-of-10_0-million-initial-public-offering "announced the pricing of its initial public offering") of an aggregate of 1,667,000 shares of common stock for $6.00 per share.
* The shares are expected to begin trading on NASDAQ under the ticker symbol "XHLD" on February 13.
* The company expects to receive aggregate gross proceeds of approximately $10.0 million from the offering, before deducting underwriting discounts and other related expenses.
* The offering is expected to close on or about February 14, 2025.
