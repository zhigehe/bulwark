[IHH Healthcare’s](https://healthcareasiamagazine.com/news/ihh-seeks-17b-in-compensation-jps-daiichi-sankyo-over-fortis-deal) Parkway Shenton Family Medicine Clinic has reopened in Singapore after renovations.

The upgraded clinic features elderly-friendly amenities, expanded waiting areas, and medical exams and vaccination services.

The clinic was previously known as Ang Mo Kio FMC.
