**Earnings per share is at $0.0112.**

Ellipsiz Ltd recorded a profit after tax of $1.3m in H1 FY25 compared to $500,000 in H1 FY24 from continuing operations.

The group’s revenue for H1 FY25 increased by 17% to $27.9m, due to higher sales of equipment, chemicals, spare parts, and consumables, as well as an increase in demand for [engineering](https://sbr.com.sg/building-engineering) services in the distribution and services solutions (DSS) segment.

Other income for H1 FY25 was $595,000, down by 14% compared to $692,000 in H1 FY24. The decline was attributed to lower fair value gain on financial assets at fair value through profit or loss.

Earnings per share is at $0.0112.
