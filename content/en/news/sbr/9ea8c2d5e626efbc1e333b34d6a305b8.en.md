**Pinetree Securities will have access to Alta Exchanges' portfolio of assets.**

[Pinetree Securities](https://sbr.com.sg/hotels-tourism/news/velocity-ventures-pinetree-securities-kto-boost-tourism-tech), part of Hanwha Group, is joining Alta Exchange as a member firm in a newly announced partnership.

This enables Pinetree Securities to list exclusive investment opportunities on the exchange. Pinetree Securities’ investor community could also access Alta Exchange’s curated portfolio of globally sourced alternative assets.

These include high-growth private companies, private equity credit funds, and rare real-world assets like fine whisky and wine.
