**Revenue amounted to $199.1m for the period.**

Thomson Medical Group Limited has incurred a net loss after tax of $12.6m [for the half-year ended](https://sbr.com.sg/healthcare/news/thomson-medical-group-expects-loss-h1) 31 December 2024 (H1 2025).

The group’s H1 2025 revenue stood at $199.1m.

Meanwhile, earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortisation amounted to $39.1m.
