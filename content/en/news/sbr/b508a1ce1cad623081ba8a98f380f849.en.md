**The partnership will support air cargo movements through Hong Kong.**

[SATS](https://sbr.com.sg/transport-logistics/news/sats-wfs-expands-menzies-world-cargo-acquisition-in-netherlands) unit, Asia Airfreight Terminal (AAT), and SATS HK, a Hong Kong Airlines-SATS joint venture (JV) have partnered with DHL Aviation’s DHL Air Capacity Sales (DHL ACS) to enhance Hong Kong’s position as a key air cargo hub.

Under the partnership, AAT will handle cargo operations for key DHL network carriers, whilst SATSHK will provide ground handling services at the Hong Kong International Airport (HKIA).

“By serving DHL's network carriers, we strengthen our capabilities in supporting global air cargo movements through Hong Kong while leveraging our extensive experience and network spanning more than 215 locations in 27 countries worldwide,” Bob Chi, CEO of Gateway Services Asia Pacific at SATS said.
