* Circle's USDC stablecoin rose to a fresh record over $56 billion market cap, surpassing the 2022 peak and recovering from the 2023 banking crisis.
* USDC added more than $10 billion to its supply over the past months, leading over Tether USDT's $4.2 billion growth, Artemis data showed.
* The pace of stablecoin growth picked up in the past weeks after a period of muted action since December, a positive sign for crypto markets amid macro headwinds and consolidating token prices.
